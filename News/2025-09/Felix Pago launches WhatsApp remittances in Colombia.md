Hey Payments Fanatic!
Félix Pago is rolling out its WhatsApp remittance service in its 6th country, Colombia.
Backed by a $75 million Series B, it enters one of Latin America’s biggest remittance markets with partners like Nubank and Mercado Libre.
Through WhatsApp, users can send money by text or voice in seconds. The new “Request a Remittance” feature even lets beneficiaries ask directly for funds with a secure link.
“Colombia is one of the main recipients of remittances in Latin America. A fast, affordable, and accessible solution can transform the experience of thousands of Colombian families,” said Manuel Godoy, CEO & Co-founder of Félix Pago.
