# Float secures $2.6M for South Africa's credit market

> [!info] 2025-09-12 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇿🇦 Float secures $2.6M to transform South Africa’s credit landscape. The capital injection will fuel Float’s expansion in the country, enhance its proprietary technology, and prepare the business for international growth. The round was co-led by Invenfin and SAAD Investment Holdings, with participation from Lighthouse Venture Partners and existing backers, including Platform Investment Partners.
