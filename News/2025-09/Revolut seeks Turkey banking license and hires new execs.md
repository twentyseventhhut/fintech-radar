# Revolut seeks Turkey banking license and hires new execs

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇹🇷 Revolut seeks banking license in Turkey and hires new execs. The London-headquartered FinTech has hired Erkin Aydin as its Country Director in Turkey and Eren Cicek as the Head of Strategy and Operations in the country. Revolut is also looking to obtain a banking license either through an application or by acquiring a local bank.
