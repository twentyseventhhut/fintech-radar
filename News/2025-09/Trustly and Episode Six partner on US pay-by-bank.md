[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Trustly and Episode Six partner on US pay-by-bank adoption. The collaboration is designed to enable U.S. retail merchants to accept pay by bank through their existing card networks, potentially reducing complexity and the need for operational overhauls.
