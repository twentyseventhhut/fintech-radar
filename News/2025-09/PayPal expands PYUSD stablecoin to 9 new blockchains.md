# PayPal expands PYUSD stablecoin to 9 new blockchains

> [!info] 2025-09-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 PayPal expands PYUSD stablecoin to 9 new blockchains through LayerZero integration. The integration utilizes LayerZero’s Stargate Hydra bridge system to provide broader access across multiple cryptocurrency platforms. It creates PYUSD0, a permissionless version that works on Tron, Avalanche, Aptos, Abstract, Ink, Sei, and Stable.
