## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇺 Cryptocurrency Exchange Kraken expands $3.5 billion tokenized stock trading to the European Union. The expansion allows eligible EU-based investors to trade digital representations of popular U.S. stocks through Kraken's mobile app, powered by Swiss firm Backed's tokenization technology.
