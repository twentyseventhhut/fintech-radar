# Mastercard collaborates with Zain Cash on digital payments in Jordan

> [!info] 2025-09-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇴 Mastercard collaborates with Zain Cash to drive digital payments in Jordan. The collaboration features Zain Cash’s Mastercard prepaid, credit card portfolio and SMEs solutions. In addition, Mastercard will support the FinTech company in setting up its Innovation Hub, where it will co-create innovative payment solutions and services.
