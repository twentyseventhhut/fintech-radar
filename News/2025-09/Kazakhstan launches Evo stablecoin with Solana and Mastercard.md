# Kazakhstan launches Evo stablecoin with Solana and Mastercard

> [!info] 2025-09-24 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇰🇿 Kazakhstan launches Evo stablecoin with Solana and Mastercard. The new Evo (KZTE) stablecoin is pegged to the Kazakhstani tenge currency and is issued by sandbox participant Intebix crypto exchange and local lender Eurasian Bank, with assistance from Solana and Mastercard.
