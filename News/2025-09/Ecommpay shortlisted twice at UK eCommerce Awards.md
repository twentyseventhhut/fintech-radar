 Double UK eCommerce Award Shortlisting for Ecommpay 
 Infrastructure
 11.09.2025 01:50 pm 
 
 Inclusive global payments platform, Ecommpay, has been shortlisted in two categories of the UK eCommerce Awards. The groundbreaking Graph Analysis added to the in-house Risk control System is a finalist in the  Payment Solutions Technology category , while   the new Hosted Payment Page solution is up for the  Ecommerce Innovation Award . 
 The UK eCommerce Awards celebrate outstanding online retail websites, platforms, software and campaigns, and recognise the agencies and in-house teams that drive innovation and set the benchmark in the industry. Winners will be announced at a gala dinner and awards ceremony in London at the Hilton Bankside on Wednesday 15 th  October. 
 Ecommpay prioritises client security, constantly improving anti-fraud measures to protect merchants without compromising conversion rates. Its in-house Risk Control System (RCS) was recently fortified with groundbreaking Graph Analysis, enabling the company to analyse fraud trends and stop not just one, but several connected fraudulent operations. 
 The Ecommpay Payouts via Hosted Payment Page solution is another key product launched in the last year to revolutionise the digital payment landscape for e-commerce. An industry first and game-changer for e-commerce businesses, it facilitates payments for ‘member communities’, delivering a swift time-to-market solution to overcome the traditional requirements of payout functionality. 
 Max Ryzhov, Chief Product Officer, Ecommpay commented: “At Ecommpay we are committed to simplifying payments for eCommerce merchants and customers, so it is fantastic to receive a double shortlisting for these industry awards. We are extremely proud of these two new innovations, which help protect customers and deliver a convenient payment solution.”
