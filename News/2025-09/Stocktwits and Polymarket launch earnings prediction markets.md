Stocktwits , an online hub for investors and traders, has teamed up with  Polymarket , a prediction marketplace for participants to invest on potential events. Announced on September 15, 2025, this alliance introduces earnings-related markets directly into the Stocktwits ecosystem, aiming to provide users with insights into market expectations.
By embedding Polymarket’s crowd-sourced probabilities, the partnership seeks to bridge social discussions with actionable trading opportunities, particularly during high-stakes earnings periods.
Stocktwits, boasting a user base exceeding 10 million, serves as a vibrant space where market enthusiasts exchange ideas, analyze trends, and react to news in real time.
Polymarket, on the other hand, operates as a decentralized marketplace where participants wager on outcomes of various events, from political developments to sports results and business milestones.
This collaboration designates Polymarket as the exclusive prediction markets provider for Stocktwits, representing the latter’s most ambitious partnership to date.
The integration focuses initially on earnings announcements for prominent publicly traded firms, transforming abstract uncertainties into quantifiable forecasts.
At the core of this initiative are embedded prediction markets accessible right within Stocktwits’ ticker pages and discussion streams.
Users will encounter live probabilities for questions like whether a company such as Tesla will surpass its earnings targets.
These markets update instantaneously, reflecting collective trader sentiment and offering a transparent gauge of anticipated results.
Beyond basic outcomes, the features extend to “mention markets,” which gauge the likelihood of particular topics or phrases surfacing during earnings conference calls.
For instance, traders might bet on whether terms like “supply chain disruptions” will be referenced by executives.
To enhance usability, Stocktwits will incorporate discovery tools such as tailored watchlists and curated collections spotlighting impending earnings events.
These will display evolving probabilities alongside user-generated content, allowing seamless navigation from conversation to deeper analysis.
A simple tap on a probability figure directs users to Polymarket’s detailed interface, where they can explore order books, review settlement criteria, and place trades if desired.
All markets adhere to predefined resolution rules, relying on verified sources like official company filings and transcripts to determine winners.
This setup promises substantial advantages for the investment community. In an era of information overload, especially around volatile earnings releases, these tools deliver calibrated signals that help users refine their strategies.
Stocktwits’ founder and CEO, Howard Lindzon , emphasized the shift toward reliable indicators in today’s fast-paced environment.
 “We’re in a phase where community trust and superior data insights outweigh mere velocity.” 
Lindzon added:
 “Polymarket’s approach to decoding news and forecasts aligns perfectly with our platform, where countless investors converge to debate and share perspectives. United, we empower them to sift through distractions and zero in on essential odds.” 
Polymarket’s Chief Marketing Officer, Matthew Modabber , highlighted the transformative potential of prediction markets.
Modabber explained:
 “These platforms convert vague unknowns—such as quarterly results—into straightforward, bettable propositions backed by clear valuations. Collaborating with Stocktwits embeds this capability amid the daily interactions of millions, revolutionizing how financial information is digested and acted upon.” 
The rollout kicks off this month for a select group of U.S.-based Stocktwits members, with plans to expand access as earnings seasons intensify.
This move comes amid Polymarket’s surge in popularity, evidenced by notable market launches and high-potential alliances.
For those eager to explore, live earnings probabilities are available on both platforms.
Overall, this partnership underscores a growing fusion of social media and predictive analytics in finance.
By being focused on democratizing access to crowd wisdom, Stocktwits and Polymarket are aiming to elevate investor engagement, fostering more informed decisions in an unpredictable market landscape.
As earnings cycles approach, this tool could become useful for traders seeking an edge through collective intelligence.
