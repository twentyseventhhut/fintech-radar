## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 B3, the Brazilian stock exchange, acquired a 62% stake in Shipay, a startup that offers unified integrations for Pix, wallets, QR codes, e-commerce, ERPs, and POS systems, for US $6.8 million, with an option to purchase the remaining shares by 2030, based on performance.
