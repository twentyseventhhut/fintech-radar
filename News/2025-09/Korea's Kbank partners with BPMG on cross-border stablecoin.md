# Korea's Kbank partners with BPMG on cross-border stablecoin

> [!info] 2025-09-02 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇰🇷 Korea’s Kbank partners with blockchain firm BPMG to launch cross-border stablecoin. The deal is for a blockchain wallet, platform development, and stablecoin consulting. Kbank has 14 million clients and is known as the partner bank for Upbit, the country’s largest cryptocurrency exchange.
