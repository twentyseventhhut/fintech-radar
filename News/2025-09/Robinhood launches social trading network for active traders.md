# Robinhood launches social trading network for active traders

> [!info] 2025-09-11 · 4 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Robinhood unveils powerful new tools for active traders at HOOD Summit 2025. The new features include Robinhood Social, a trading community within the app that showcases live, verified trades and authentic profiles for sharing strategies and following experts. Robinhood Legend users can now access AI-powered custom indicators and scans, equities, and crypto trading ladders.

[This Week in Fintech] Robinhood is getting social. The company announced Robinhood Social, a new feature that will let traders share their positions, comment on each other’s moves, and follow real-time profit and loss updates. A small group of users will get access early next year, with broader rollout to follow. The feature will be free to Robinhood customers (of, course!).

[This Week in Fintech] Read more: Robinhood to Launch Its Own Social Network
