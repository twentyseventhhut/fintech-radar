## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Moove, an African mobility-fintech backed by Uber, is raising capital in a funding round valuing the company at $2 billion to grow its mobility financing operations.

[This Week in Fintech] Mooveeyed $300 Million round at a $2 Billion Valuation. Moovegrew its revenues from about $50 million in 2024, when it was valued near $750 million, to nearly $400 million in annualized recurring revenue by September 2025.
