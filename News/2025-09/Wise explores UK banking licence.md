# Wise explores UK banking licence

> [!info] 2025-09-02 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, The Fintech Blueprint

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Wise is said to be exploring a UK banking license. If successful, the move will allow Wise to offer interest-bearing accounts and potentially issue loans, services currently off-limits under its existing electronic money licence. It would also bring the firm under the remit of the Financial Services Compensation Scheme, offering deposit protection up to £85,000 per customer.
