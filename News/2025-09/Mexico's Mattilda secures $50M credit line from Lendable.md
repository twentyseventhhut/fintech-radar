# Mexico's Mattilda secures $50M credit line from Lendable

> [!info] 2025-09-14 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Mattilda, the Mexican company specializing in school financing solutions, secured a $50 million credit line from Lendable, a global investment firm, to expand its services across LatAm. The company aims to enhance its platform that automates school fee collection and financial management, focusing on institutions serving low- and middle-income families in Mexico, Colombia, and Ecuador.

Mattilda, a Mexican fintech for private schools, secured a $50M credit line from Lendable to grow its school-finance services across Latin America. The deal follows Mattilda’s Series A round in 2023

Founded in 2022, Mattilda provides software and financing that help schools reduce late tuition, handle payments more efficiently, and get upfront funding backed by future tuition. Mattilda focuses on schools serving low- and middle-income families and currently operates in Mexico, Colombia, and Ecuador.

Mattilda will use the funds to launch products, grow its operations in current markets, and pursue acquisitions.
