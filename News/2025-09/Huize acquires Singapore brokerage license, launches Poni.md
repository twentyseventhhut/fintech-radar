## Nasdaq-listed Huize gets MAS license for Poni Insurtech

Share

Add TIA as a preferred source

Huize Holding Ltd., a China-based insurance brokerage and wealth platform, has secured a financial adviser and exempt insurance broker license in Singapore for its international brand, Poni Insurtech.

The licence, effective from July 10, 2025, was granted by the Monetary Authority of Singapore.

Poni Insurtech operates in Hong Kong, Singapore, and Vietnam, and aims to use Singapore and Hong Kong as regional hubs to expand its tech-enabled advisory services across Southeast Asia.

The company’s leadership includes Ron Tam as international CEO and Cassandra Wee as managing director for Singapore.

The company said the new license will allow it to scale its advisory and distribution capabilities in the region.
