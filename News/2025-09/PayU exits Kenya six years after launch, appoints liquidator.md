# PayU exits Kenya six years after launch, appoints liquidator

> [!info] 2025-09-18 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇰🇪 PayU exits Kenya six years after launch and appoints Sonal Tejpal as the liquidator. The decision to shut down was filed under Kenya’s Insolvency Act and published in a local newspaper. As liquidator, Tejpal is now responsible for winding up the company’s affairs and settling any outstanding liabilities.
