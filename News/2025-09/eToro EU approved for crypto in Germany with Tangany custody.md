# eToro EU approved for crypto in Germany with Tangany custody

> [!info] 2025-09-18 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 eToro EU approved for crypto in Germany, and custody remains with Tangany. The firm now allows European users to deposit funds in eight currencies via card or bank transfer. The company announced that all cryptoasset trading for German clients will be conducted directly through eToro EU, ending the use of DLT Finance for trading services on the platform.
