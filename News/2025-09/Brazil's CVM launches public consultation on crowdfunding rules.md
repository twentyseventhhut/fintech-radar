# Brazil's CVM launches public consultation on crowdfunding rules

> [!info] 2025-09-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 The Brazilian Securities and Exchange Commission (CVM) has launched a public consultation with investment firms to review the rules governing crowdfunding. Expected changes include the introduction of new issuer profiles, the inclusion of rural products and agricultural cooperatives, and the removal of the annual revenue cap.
