Zand and Chainlink Labs boost tokenised assets in the UAE
Aurora Munteanu
26 Sep 2025 / 5 Min Read
 Zand , an AI-driven fintech and financial services group, has partnered with Chainlink Labs , a primary contributing developer of Chainlink, an oracle technology provider.
The collaboration aims to optimise the integration of tokenised assets and blockchain-based financial solutions in the UAE financial system. Chainlink will offer the foundational connectivity needed to build compliance-focused digital asset infrastructure that interacts across blockchains and traditional systems.
Optimising tokenised assets
The partnership supports Zand Bank’s digital asset strategy, drawing on Chainlink’s industry experience and solutions, such as Automated Compliance Engine (ACE), Automation, Cross-Chain Interoperability Protocol (CCIP), and VRF, to support financial use cases that improve the UAE’s digital asset landscape.
Zand representatives reported the company’s positive feelings regarding the partnership, stating that Zand and Chainlink are exploring more collaborative opportunities to accelerate digital adoption and efficacy. With a shared vision to deliver innovative, secure, and fast solutions that merge traditional finance and decentralised finance, the alliance will contribute to the digital economy.
The initiative comes as Zand Bank serves as the official banking partner of the DLD during the pilot of the department’s programme to test tokenised property investments using the Prypco Mint platform. DLD carried out this project in May 2025, in partnership with Prypco and under the oversight of several government and financial institutions, including the Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA), the Central Bank of the UAE, and the Dubai Future Foundation (DFF).
Other plans from the UAE regarding crypto
In August 2025 , the Central Bank of the UAE (CBUAE) moved toward the rollout phase of its digital currency, the Digital Dirham. Initially, the UAE tested wholesale CBDC settlement with Saudi Arabia in Project Aber, from 2019 to 2020, before becoming a founding participant in the mBridge initiative conducted by the BIS Innovation Hub together with Hong Kong, China, and Thailand. In 2025, the CBUAE leveraged the platform to process a live cross-border payment in Digital Dirham.

Zand, an AI-powered fintech and financial services group based in the UAE, has entered a **strategic collaboration with Chainlink Labs**, a leading oracle technology provider. The partnership aims to accelerate the development of **tokenized assets** and blockchain-based financial solutions, aligning with the UAE’s growing role as a global hub for digital innovation.

By working with Chainlink, Zand Bank gains access to institutional-grade infrastructure trusted by global financial leaders such as Swift, Mastercard, and UBS. This collaboration is a key milestone in connecting traditional financial systems with secure, scalable, and compliant blockchain infrastructure.

Both parties share a vision of expanding the digital asset economy in the region, making financial systems more transparent, interoperable, and efficient while adhering to strict compliance requirements.

### Investor Takeaway

## How Chainlink’s Infrastructure Supports Zand’s Strategy

Chainlink has facilitated **tens of trillions of dollars** in transaction value across the digital asset economy, building a reputation as the industry-standard oracle platform. Its infrastructure supports **data connectivity, interoperability, compliance, and privacy**, enabling seamless interaction between blockchains and traditional financial systems.

Zand Bank will use Chainlink’s robust suite of services, including:

*   **Automated Compliance Engine (ACE)** – Ensuring regulatory standards are met on-chain.
*   **Cross-Chain Interoperability Protocol (CCIP)** – Facilitating secure transactions across multiple blockchains.
*   **Data Streams and Price Feeds** – Providing real-time, verified market data.
*   **Proof of Reserve** – Enhancing transparency and trust in tokenized assets.
*   **Verifiable Random Function (VRF)** – Generating secure randomness for applications like lotteries or risk management.

These tools give Zand Bank the ability to design financial products that integrate seamlessly with the UAE’s regulated financial environment while leveraging the efficiencies of blockchain technology.

### Investor Takeaway

Chainlink’s suite of compliance-focused tools will enable Zand Bank to build tokenized finance solutions with transparency, interoperability, and trust at their core.

## What This Means For The UAE Digital Asset Ecosystem

The UAE has rapidly established itself as a leader in digital asset adoption, supported by progressive regulation and investment in blockchain infrastructure. This partnership between Zand Bank and Chainlink underscores the country’s ambition to become a hub for **regulated tokenization and digital asset innovation**.

By leveraging Chainlink’s technology, Zand Bank is set to explore opportunities in **on-chain compliance, cross-border settlement, and asset tokenization**. The goal is to create financial applications that not only appeal to crypto-native firms but also meet the standards of traditional institutions and regulators.

The collaboration highlights how partnerships between local financial institutions and global blockchain leaders are reshaping the financial services landscape, paving the way for broader adoption of decentralized technologies within mainstream finance.

### Investor Takeaway

The Zand–Chainlink alliance strengthens the UAE’s competitive edge in digital assets by merging regulated finance with cutting-edge blockchain solutions.
