# Revolut confirms plan to begin Colombia operations in 2026

> [!info] 2025-09-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Diego Caicedo, CEO of Revolut, confirmed plans to begin operations in Colombia in 2026. With the company currently in the process of obtaining regulatory licenses, the constitution license is expected by the end of 2025, and the operating license in 2026. The neobank aims to enter the market with a full suite of services, including payments, transfers, savings, and potentially crypto and credit products.
