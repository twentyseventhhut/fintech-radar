# Brazil's tax authority sets January 2026 fintech reporting rules

> [!info] 2025-09-07 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazil’s Federal Revenue Serviceannounced that Normative Instruction 2278, published on August 28, will take effect in January 2026. The regulation requires payment institutions to report financial transactions, payment account balances, and significant transactions to the tax authority.
