# Visa partners with Ample Earth to power sustainable spending

> [!info] 2025-09-03 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Visa partners with Ample Earth to power sustainable spending. Together, Ample and Visa aim to embed sustainability data into digital banking apps and loyalty programmes and enable banks to better segment customers and unlock new ways to engage, empower, and reward sustainability.
