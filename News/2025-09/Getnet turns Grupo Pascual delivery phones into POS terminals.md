# Getnet turns Grupo Pascual delivery phones into POS terminals

> [!info] 2025-09-24 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇸 Getnet transforms Grupo Pascual’s delivery mobiles into full POS terminals. Get Tap on Phone solution turns Pascual delivery drivers' mobile phones into multifunction payment terminals, eliminating hardware and improving logistical efficiency. The implementation of Get Tap on Phone clearly addresses the need to reduce the complexity and human error in delivery and payment processes.Getnet Transforms Pacual Group
