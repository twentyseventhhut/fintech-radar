[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Nvidia to invest in UK firms, including Wayve and Revolut. Nvidia Corp. plans to invest £2 billion to support the UK’s AI industry, comprising funding to startups and partnerships with venture capital firms. Wayve had said that Nvidia was planning to invest $500 million in the company to accelerate the development and deployment of its AI model.
