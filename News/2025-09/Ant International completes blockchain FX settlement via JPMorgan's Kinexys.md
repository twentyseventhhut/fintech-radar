## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌏 Ant International completes real-time FX blockchain settlement in Asia through Kinexys by J.P. Morgan. The solution provides businesses with uninterrupted access to liquidity across selected global currencies, including USD, EUR, and GBP, enabling them to conduct transactions outside of designated market hours and beyond the limitations of traditional market cutoff times.
