# Revolut pays AUSTRAC $187,000 for reporting failures

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 Revolut pays AUSTRAC $187,000 for reporting failures. Revolut Payments Australia was issued infringement notices of $187,000 for “self-disclosed failures to submit international fund transfer instructions within the timeframe stipulated by the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter Terrorism (AML/CTF) Financing Act”.
