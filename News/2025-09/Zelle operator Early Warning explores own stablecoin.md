# Zelle operator Early Warning explores own stablecoin

> [!info] 2025-09-16 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Zelle Operator joins stablecoin race with Bank-Led Token. Early Warning Services (EWS) is exploring the launch of its own stablecoin for retail bank customers. The initiative would likely begin with a small-scale pilot designed to test infrastructure for issuing and circulating a regulated dollar-pegged token across bank networks.

[This Week in Fintech] 🌍 Zelle operator Early Warning Services (EWS) is exploring stablecoin initiatives. It also hit 2 bn transactions in H1 2025. Zelle is also rolling out SMB tags to direct payments straight to eligible bank accounts without needing an email address or phone number.

[This Week in Fintech] Early Warning Services is investigating launching its own stablecoin for retail bank customers, citing interest among its owners like Bank of America, JPMorgan Chase, Wells Fargo, and PNC.
