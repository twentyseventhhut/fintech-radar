# India's UPI crosses 20 billion monthly transactions in August

> [!info] 2025-09-02 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 UPI crosses 20 billion monthly transaction milestone in August. The platform recorded 20.01 billion transactions in August compared to 19.47 billion transactions in July. The digital payments platform also saw its total transaction value for August at Rs 24.85 lakh crore, marginally lower than the Rs 25.08 lakh crore it recorded in July.
