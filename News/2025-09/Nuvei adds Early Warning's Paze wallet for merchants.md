# Nuvei adds Early Warning's Paze wallet for merchants

> [!info] 2025-09-10 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇦 Nuvei adds early warning’s Paze Wallet for its e-commerce merchants. Consumers using Paze will find their card information and billing addresses preloaded. Consumers will be able to add different shipping addresses and store them in Paze, too. The wallet also features a card-updater capability.
