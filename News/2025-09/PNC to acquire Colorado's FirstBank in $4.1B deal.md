# PNC to acquire Colorado's FirstBank in $4.1B deal

> [!info] 2025-09-10 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 PNC agrees to acquire Colorado's FirstBank in $4.1bn deal. The transaction has been approved by the boards of directors of both PNC and FirstBank Holding Company, consisting of around 13.9 million shares of PNC common stock, and a cash payment of $1.2 billion.

[This Week in Fintech] PNC Financial Servicesagreed to acquire Colorado’s FirstBank in a $4.1 billion cash‑and‑stock deal to expand its presence in Colorado and Arizona, adding 95 branches and around $26.8 billion in FirstBank assets.
