# MetLife acquires 20% stake in LatAm insurtech Klimber

> [!info] 2025-09-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎 Klimber, the LatAm insurtech that provides digital life insurance distribution solutions, announced that MetLife acquired a 20% stake in the company. The deal strengthens Klimber’s position as a regional leader in insurtech while enabling MetLife to expand its digital insurance reach across LatAm. No further financial details were disclosed.
