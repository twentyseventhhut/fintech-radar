[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇿 ANZ pays $1.9 million fine in New Zealand and admits law breach. The Melbourne-based firm will pay NZ$3.25 million ($1.9 million) for the breaches that occurred between December 2012 and May 2023. ANZ has made remediation payments to all impacted current and past customers, the statement said.
