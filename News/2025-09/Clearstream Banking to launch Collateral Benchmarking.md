# Clearstream Enhances Collateral Insights with Market Benchmarking and Advanced Analytics

Reference

Last Updated

01.09.2025

Clearstream has released an enhanced version of its Collateral Insights, designed to help market participants gain full control over their collateral and lending activities. The updated data solution now **combines internal portfolio analytics with anonymized peer benchmarking**, offering a dual perspective that supports smarter, data-driven decisions.

## What’s new?

With the enhanced version, you can now:

*   Compare your activity against anonymized peer groups across volumes, repo rates, asset ratings, and allocations
*   Analyze trends across products, geographies, and asset categories to refine your strategies
*   Combine internal performance with external market context for more confident decision-making
*   Access dynamic end-of-day updates with key performance indicators and advanced analytics

## Benefits

*   Market benchmarking
*   Strategic clarity
*   Operational efficiency
*   Customizable analysis
