# Qoyod and HALA partner to support Saudi SMEs

> [!info] 2025-09-29 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇦 Qoyod and HALA have announced a strategic partnership to support the growth and success of SMEs in Saudi Arabia. By integrating HALA’s advanced payment technologies with Qoyod’s comprehensive business management platform, the collaboration aims to enhance operational and financial efficiency for SMEs.
