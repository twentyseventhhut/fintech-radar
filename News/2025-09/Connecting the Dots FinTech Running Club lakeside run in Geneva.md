FinTech Runners: Geneva
​ 🏃‍♂️ FinTech Running Club – Geneva Lakeside Run 🏃‍♀️ 
​ Join us for a refreshing midday run with the FinTech Running Club in the heart of Geneva! Whether you're a seasoned runner or just getting started, this inclusive event is all about community, movement, and enjoying the stunning lakeside views together.
​ 📍 Meeting Point: The iconic Flower Clock (L'Horloge Fleurie) in Jardin Anglais 
​ 🕧 Start Time: 12:30 PM 
​ 🗺️ Route: We'll run along the scenic Quai Gustave-Ador and Quai de Cologny , with two distance options to choose from:
 ​ 5 km – to Genève-Plage and back 
 ​ 7 km – to Pontons de Cologny and back 
​ 🏃‍♀️ All Paces Welcome! This is an inclusive run: we’ll agree on pace and distance as a group before we start. Whether you're in it for a light jog or a brisk run, there's a place for you.
​ 💬 Post-run catch-up encouraged! Stick around after the run to connect with fellow FinTech enthusiasts and enjoy the lakeside vibes.
​ Join our whatsapp groupchat: https://chat.whatsapp.com/CLQtai05vOLFtqhTwPnzON
