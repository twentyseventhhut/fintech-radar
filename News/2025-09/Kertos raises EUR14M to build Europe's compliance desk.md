## From Counsel to Compliance

### **Announcing Kertos’ Series A to Build Europe’s Compliance Desk**

_by Kilian Schmidt, Founder & CEO, Kertos_

‍

When I started my career as a lawyer and legal counsel, compliance wasn’t exactly a passion — it was a necessity. My days were filled with GDPR data subject requests (DSRs), audits, and endless admin. It wasn’t love at first sight.

But the more I did it, the more one thought kept coming back: _If this frustrates me so much, how many others are stuck in the same loop?_

In Munich, Johannes and Alex were already deep in R&D, leading innovation at a tech lab and running a Company. When fate brought us together, it clicked almost immediately: **legal insight + product vision + engineering power**.

That frustration was the spark for Kertos. We started small: automating the most painful GDPR workflow, the **Data Subject Request (DSR)**. Instead of lawyers or privacy managers drowning in spreadsheets and emails, Kertos handled it end-to-end. It worked — and it made us ask: _What if compliance as a whole could run like this?_

### From a single workflow to the Compliance Desk

Fast forward to today, and Kertos has become an **all-in-one compliance suite built for European SMEs and scaleups**. What started with GDPR has expanded into **ISO 27001, ISO 42001, NIS2, DORA, and TISAX**.

Our mission is simple: **compliance that actually does the work.**

*   No more screenshots, evidence chases, or rewriting policies every quarter.
*   AI agents handle the heavy lifting, with your team in control for approvals.
*   Compliance that scales as you grow — whether you’re 20 or 500 employees.

### What makes Kertos different

*   **Lawyer-founded, EU-native:** We understand the **European regulatory maze** from the inside — and we build specifically for it.
*   **Automation-first:** Evidence, vendor checks, RoPA, policy drafting — all handled automatically.
*   **Peer-to-Peer Trust Graph:** A network where companies can **reuse verified controls** with their customers and suppliers, turning trust into a shared asset.
*   **Beyond SaaS:** Not just another platform — Kertos is your **Compliance Desk**.

### Why this matters now

Europe’s regulatory wave — GDPR, NIS2, DORA, the EU AI Act — is only accelerating. SMEs shouldn’t be buried in paperwork or forced to buy fragmented tools. They need a partner that ensures compliance **runs quietly in the background** so they can build in the foreground.

### The road ahead

With this €14 Milion Series A, we’ll deepen automation, expand the Trust Graph, and continue building the **compliance of the future: peer-to-peer, automated, outcome-driven, and trusted by default**.

To our customers — thank you for proving this vision works. To the Kertos team — thank you for turning a lawyer’s frustration into a product that powers Europe’s innovators.

**Trust is the new golden standard. And we’re here to make it effortless.**

‍

– Kilian

## Dr. Kilian Schmidt

CEO & Co-Founder, Kertos GmbH

[](https://www.kertos.io/en/blog/announcing-kertos-series-a-to-build-europes-compliance-desk#)

Dr. Kilian Schmidt developed a strong interest in legal processes early on. After studying law, he began his career as Senior Legal Counsel and Data Protection Officer at the Home24 Group. After working at Freshfields Bruckhaus Deringer, he moved to TIER Mobility, where, as General Counsel, he was significantly involved in expanding the legal and public policy department - and grew the company from one to 65 cities and from 50 to 800 employees. Motivated by limited technological advances in the legal sector and inspired by his consulting work at Gorillas Technologies, he co-founded Kertos to develop the next generation of European data protection technology.
