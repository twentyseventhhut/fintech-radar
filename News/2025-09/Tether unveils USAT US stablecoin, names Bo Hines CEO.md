[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Bo Hines named CEO of Tether's new US arm as the company unveils USAT stablecoin. Hines, who was previously named as a "strategic advisor" for the largest stablecoin issuer, will oversee the rollout of Tether's new stablecoin, USAT. USAT is designed to be a U.S.-regulated, dollar-backed stablecoin and a complement to USDT, which has about $169 billion in circulation.
