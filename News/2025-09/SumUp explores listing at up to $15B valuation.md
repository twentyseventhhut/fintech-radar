# SumUp explores listing at up to $15B valuation

> [!info] 2025-09-16 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 UK FinTech SumUp explores listing at up to $15bn valuation. The London-based FinTech is weighing up an IPO in either London or New York. They had been discussing the plans with investment banks and were aiming to float in the next year. SumUp is hoping to raise cash to buy up competitors.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Payments firm SumUp is seeking a $15bn valuation in its upcoming IPO.

[This Week in Fintech] SumUp, a UK-based fintech that provides payment terminals and digital services to small businesses, is considering going public next year and aiming for a valuation between $10 billion and $15 billion, highlighting its strong European customer base and recent expansion into lending and invoicing solutions.
