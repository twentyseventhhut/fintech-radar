## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Crypto asset manager CoinShares to go public in the US Via a SPAC Deal. The deal values CoinShares at $1.2 billion pre-money on a pro-forma basis, according to a company statement. CoinShares is best known for its crypto exchange-traded products and has about $10 billion in assets under management.
