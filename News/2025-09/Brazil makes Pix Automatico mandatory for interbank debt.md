# Brazil makes Pix Automatico mandatory for interbank debt

> [!info] 2025-09-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 The National Monetary Council of the Central Bank of Brazilapproved new regulations making the use of Pix Automático mandatory for the authorization and cancellation of interbank debt. The measure will take effect on October 13, and institutions will have until January 1, 2026, to migrate existing contracts and debt authorizations.
