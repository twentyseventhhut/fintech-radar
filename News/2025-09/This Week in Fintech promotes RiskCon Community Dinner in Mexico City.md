# This Week in Fintech promotes RiskCon Community Dinner in Mexico City

> [!info] 2025-09-28 · 1 упоминаний · 2 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Next up, the RiskCon Community Dinner CDMX on October 8, presented by Oscilar, the leading AI risk decisioning platform. Join us for an evening on risk, compliance, and the future of fintech, over great food and drinks at the marvelous Alelí Rooftop (Michelin Guide CDMX). Don’t miss it—RSVP now!

RiskCon CDMX with Oscilar & TWIF
​ You're invited! Join us in CDMX on October 8
​ Calling all credit, fraud, and compliance risk operators and leaders. We are delighted to invite you to an exclusive happy hour and dinner in CDMX with facilitated group discussions centered around credit, fraud, and compliance risk management.
​ We will delve into some key topics shaping our industry, including:
 ​ Innovations in credit underwriting and fraud/AML detection 
 ​ How companies are leveraging device and behavioral signals 
 ​ How companies are utilizing alternative data 
​ Please register below and we will confirm your attendance, as spaces are limited.
​ Not located in CDMX or can't make it? No worries, you can book a demo with us here ! 
​
​ RiskCon CDMX event details: 
 ​ Date: Wednesday, October 8 
 ​ Time: 6:00pm - 9:00pm ​ Happy hour from 6:00pm to 7:00pm followed by dinner and discussion groups 
 ​ Location & Address: ​ Roma Norte, CDMX 
​ RiskCon CDMX is brought to you by Oscilar and This Week in Fintech .
