Ondo Finance and the Ondo Foundation have launched over 100 tokenized U.S. stocks and ETFs on Ethereum, providing 24/7 on-chain access to major companies and funds for qualified international investors.
The Ondo Global Markets platform serves eligible Asia-Pacific, European, African, and Latin American users while excluding U.S. and UK participants. Plans include scaling to over 1,000 tokenized assets by year-end with upcoming support for BNBChain and Solana.
Available securities include individual stocks from companies like Apple and Tesla alongside fixed-income ETFs from major providers. Users can mint and redeem tokenized assets 24 hours a day, five days weekly, with access to traditional exchange liquidity backing.
Leading crypto infrastructure supports the launch, including OKX Wallet, Bitget Wallet, Trust Wallet, Gate, BitGo, Ledger, Morpho, and 1inch. The 1inch Swap API received upgrades enabling jurisdiction-aware access to tokenized real-world assets.
"Many tokenized stock offerings today remain confined to siloed platforms with restricted transferability or suffer from low liquidity," Ondo stated. The Global Markets approach prioritizes accessibility and seamless on-chain access to robust traditional exchange liquidity.
Competition includes Kraken's expanded xStocks support across multiple blockchains and Robinhood's tokenized equities on Arbitrum for EU users. Coinbase is seeking SEC approval for similar U.S. offerings, while the tokenized market could reach $2 trillion by 2030.
Ondo previously announced plans to invest $250 million in real-world asset projects alongside Pantera Capital, bridging traditional finance with digital assets. The company also developed Ondo Chain, a layer-1 blockchain for institutional-grade financial markets.
