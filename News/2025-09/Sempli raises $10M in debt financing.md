[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Colombian FinTech Sempli raises US$10 million in debt. With this new funding, the FinTech company has reached a cumulative total of US$68 million and aims to increase the value of its portfolio by more than 15% by year-end. Keep reading
