# Tide raises $120M at $1.5B valuation led by TPG

> [!info] 2025-09-22 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, TechCrunch — Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 TPG leads funding for UK FinTech Tide at $1.5 billion valuation. The fresh funds will be allocated toward a new line of products, investments in AI, and further international expansion. Continue reading

[TechCrunch — Fintech] U.K.-based fintech Tide has entered the unicorn club with a fresh funding of $120 million led by TPG, as the startup now serves over 1.6 million micro and small enterprises globally — with more than half of them based in India, the company’s largest and fastest-growing market.

[TechCrunch — Fintech] Initially launched in the U.K. in 2017, Tide expanded into India in December 2022 to tap into the country’s vast base of small enterprises — around 60 million micro and small businesses employing over 250 million people, per the recent Indian government data. Since its entry, Tide has onboarded more than 800,000 Indian businesses, which it refers to as “members” — surpassing its U.K. member base of nearly 800,000. In the U.K., where Tide is already profitable, the company serves around 14% of the country’s small and medium business market.
