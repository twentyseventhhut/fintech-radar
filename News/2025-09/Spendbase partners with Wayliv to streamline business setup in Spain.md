# Spendbase partners with Wayliv to streamline business setup in Spain

> [!info] 2025-09-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇸 Spendbase partners with Wayliv to streamline business setup in Spain. As part of the collaboration, Wayliv clients will gain access to Spendbase’s neo-banking services, aimed at simplifying company formation. One of the key requirements for registering a business is opening a bank account, a step that can now be completed through Spendbase.
