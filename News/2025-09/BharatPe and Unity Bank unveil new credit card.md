# BharatPe and Unity Bank unveil new credit card

> [!info] 2025-09-02 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 BharatPe and Unity Bank unveil new credit card. The card is available on the RuPay network and can be linked to UPI, allowing users to make payments across numerous merchants in India. It features a “zero-fee structure”, with no joining charges, annual fees, or processing charges, and customers can prepay their EMIs at any time without facing penalties.
