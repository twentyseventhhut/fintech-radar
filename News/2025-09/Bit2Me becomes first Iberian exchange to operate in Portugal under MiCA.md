[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇵🇹 Bit2Me is the first Iberian exchange to operate legally in Portugal under MiCA. This authorization represents a milestone that allows Bit2Me to grow solidly in the country, offering a safe, accessible, and fully regulated experience for those who want to invest in or use crypto assets, says Leif Ferreira, CEO and co-founder of Bit2Me.
