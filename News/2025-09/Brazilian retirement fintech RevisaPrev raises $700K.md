RevisaPrev raises $700K
 by Araceli Dominguez 
September 18, 2025
 1 minute read
 RevisaPrev , a Brazilian legalfintech specializing in retirement benefits, raised $700K in a round led by Domo VC and Bossa Invest .
RevisaPrev provides free retirement benefit analyses, which lead to services such as benefit applications, reviews, payroll loans, and reimbursement of contributions above the INSS ceiling.
RevisaPrev will use the funds to launch a service that allows retirement benefit advances by covering the contributions needed for clients to qualify for INSS retirement, an option not available through traditional banks. The company is also investing in AI with “Isa,” a virtual assistant that guides clients from initial analysis to post-retirement follow-up.
Founded in 2020, RevisaPrev serves 10,000 insured individuals per month and plans to reach 30,000 by the end of 2025. The 40-person team is expanding into a new office in São José do Rio Preto, designed to accommodate up to 100 employees.
