# Japan Post Bank to roll out tokenized deposits on DCJPY network by 2026

> [!info] 2025-09-02 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇵 Japan Post Bank to roll out tokenized deposits on DCJPY network by 2026. Under the plan, retail depositors will be able to exchange cash balances for DCJPY tokens, which can then be used to purchase tokenized securities targeting yields of 3% to 5%, according to the report.
