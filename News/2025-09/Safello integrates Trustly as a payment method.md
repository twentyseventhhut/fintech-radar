Safello integrates Trustly as a payment method
 Stockholm, 3 September 2025 | Safello, the leading crypto exchange in the Nordics, has integrated Trustly as a new payment method on its platform. The integration enables users to make direct bank transfers when purchasing cryptocurrencies, resulting in a faster and more user-friendly buying experience, particularly for larger transactions. 
 Trustly is built on open banking technology and allows users to transfer funds directly from their bank account to Safello’s platform. For users who have previously used Trustly with other providers, bank details are filled in automatically, making the payment process both quicker and more seamless. 
 Unlike some other deposit methods, there is no upper limit, allowing users to complete larger transactions without manual steps. The agreement with Trustly includes support for usage across seven European markets.
