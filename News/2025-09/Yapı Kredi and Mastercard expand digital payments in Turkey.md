#### **A Major Innovation From Yapı Kredi and Mastercard: No More Carrying A Wallet**

Yapı Kredi, which stands out with its pioneering applications in digital banking, has taken the mobile payment experience one step further with Mastercard's digital payment solution, Masterpass. Currently, Yapı Kredi customers can make payments with their Yapı Kredi cards, accounts, and Shopping Credit through World Pay. With the innovation offered through the Mastercard partnership, they will also be able to use all other bank cards registered with Masterpass. Thus, Yapı Kredi customers can link their Masterpass accounts to World Pay, where digital payment services converge, and view not only their Yapı Kredi cards but also other registered bank cards, enabling them to make payments with TR QR codes at all bank POS terminals. Thanks to this pioneering collaboration, Yapı Kredi customers will be able to complete their transactions in accordance with Yapı Kredi security standards using the card of their choice from any bank, even if the card they wish to use is not with them, thanks to the World Pay and Masterpass infrastructure.

**We continue to shape the future of payment systems.**

As Yapı Kredi, we prioritize developing solutions that focus on customer needs and simplifying the digital payment experience**,** said **Serkan Ülgen, Deputy General Manager of Yapı Kredi _._** He added: _"With the innovations we bring to our sector, we continue to transform our customers' payment habits and shape the industry. As you know, our customers could make payments using their Yapı Kredi credit cards, debit cards, accounts, and even shopping credit through World Pay, accessible via Yapı Kredi Mobile or World Mobile. Thanks to our integration with Masterpass, a first in Turkey, we offer our users the ability to easily link their Masterpass accounts via World Pay and make quick and easy payments with TR QR Code using not only Yapı Kredi cards but also cards from other banks._ _Thus, World Pay continues to expand its payment solution alternatives as a digital wallet that includes our customers' cards from all banks. As Yapı Kredi,_ _we will continue to shape the future of digital payment systems in Turkey and stay one step ahead in the coming period_."

**Avşar Gürdal: "We are proud to launch a new era in face-to-face payments"**

Speaking specifically about the partnership, **Avşar Gürdal, General Manager of Mastercard Turkey and Azerbaijan,**_said, "As Mastercard, we are committed to developing solutions that guide Turkey's digitalization journey and continuing our investments in this area. With Masterpass, which has been operating for over 10 years and now serves 42 million users, we have transformed the e-commerce payment experience. Today, through our collaboration with Yapı Kredi and World Pay, we are introducing another major innovation in the sector. We are bringing the secure, fast, and seamless Masterpass experience that our users are familiar with to face-to-face payments via TR QR Code; we are opening the doors to a new era where consumers can make payments using all their cards registered with Masterpass via their mobile devices without needing to carry a wallet. We are very proud to be pioneering a new era in mobile payments with this innovation we have brought to life with Yapı Kredi,"_ he said.
