# ClearScore CEO eyes London listing as signature achievement

> [!info] 2025-09-24 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Floating in London would be my ‘signature achievement’, Justin Basini, ClearScore CEO. He stated London feels like the “natural home” for the company instead of choosing to follow fellow UK tech firms and list in New York instead. If an IPO in the capital is pursued, ClearScore is estimated to be valued between £1.5bn and £2.5bn.
