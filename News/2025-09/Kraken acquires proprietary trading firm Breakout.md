If you’re an experienced crypto trader looking to scale your strategy — without putting personal funds on the line — we’ve got something new for you.
 Kraken has acquired Breakout , a prop trading platform that lets you access up to $200,000 in live trading capital and keep up to 90% of your profits. Just pass a one-step evaluation and start trading. There’s no deposit required (other than the cost of the evaluation).
 What is Breakout? 
 Breakout is a crypto-native proprietary trading firm built to empower traders. By demonstrating your skill through a trading evaluation, you can get funded with up to $200,000 in funded capital and keep up to 90% of the profits. Now backed by Kraken’s infrastructure and global platform , Breakout allows traders to size up and scale their edge with zero personal capital at risk .

Sign up at breakoutprop.com/kraken  with the email linked to your Kraken account
