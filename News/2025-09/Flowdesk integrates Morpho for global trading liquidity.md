# Flowdesk integrates Morpho for global trading liquidity

> [!info] 2025-09-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇫🇷 Flowdesk integrates Morpho to support its global trading business. Flowdesk utilizes Morpho’s various markets and robust liquidity to borrow stablecoins against crypto assets, including cbBTC, WETH, and more. The leverage generated against these assets is used across the broader trading business to help facilitate trading activity.
