# OKX fined $2.6 million in Netherlands for registration failure

> [!info] 2025-09-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Crypto Exchange OKX fined $2.6M in the Netherlands for failing to register with the Dutch National Ban. The fine on the company, whose official name is Aux Cayes FinTech Co., relates to the period July 2023 to August 2024, before the introduction of the European Union’s Markets in Crypto Assets regime (MiCA), according to a press release.
