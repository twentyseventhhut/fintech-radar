# PayPal capital markets head David Knox leaves for crypto treasury

> [!info] 2025-09-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 PayPal’s Head of Capital Markets, David Knox, leaves for a crypto treasury. Knox, 36, said he spent about 1-1/2 years at PayPal, where he was also head of finance for credit and financial services. Before then, he was a vice president at SoFi Technologies Inc. and was a director for asset-backed finance at Cantor Fitzgerald.
