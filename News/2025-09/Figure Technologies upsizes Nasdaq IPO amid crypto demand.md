[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Stablecoin issuer Figure Technologies upsizes IPO as crypto interest soars. The company is now marketing the IPO at a price range of $20 to $22 a share, up from its previous range of $18 to $20 a share. It also raised the number of shares to 31.5 million from around 26 million.
