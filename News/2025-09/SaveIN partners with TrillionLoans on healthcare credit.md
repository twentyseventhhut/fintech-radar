SaveIN, Trillionloans Join Forces To Expand Healthcare & Wellness Credit
 
SaveIN, a healthcare-fintech platform, has partnered with Trillionloans, backed by BharatPe, to expand affordable, on-demand credit solutions across healthcare and corporate wellness segments.
Recently, SaveIN launched welUp, an employee wellness platform offering benefits such as dental, mental health, diagnostics, and fertility services. Embedded on-demand credit within welUp addresses India’s significant out-of-pocket healthcare expenses, particularly amid scrutiny over insurance approvals and cashless coverage availability.
Under the partnership, SaveIN and Trillionloans plan to triple their growth over the next year by expanding credit penetration in both consumer healthcare and corporate wellness. SaveIN also collaborates with banks like HDFC, ICICI, and IDFC First, along with non-banking finance partners, to deliver flexible lending products.
Jitin Bhasin, Founder & CEO of SaveIN, stated, “Healthcare and wellness in India remain largely out-of-pocket, leading to delayed or compromised care. SaveIN’s mission is to ensure that every day, as well as aspirational healthcare needs, can be met affordably and transparently. With Trillionloans added to our credit infrastructure, we are scaling our core healthcare financing business and extending access to on-demand credit through welUp for employees across India.”
Sandeep Singh, CEO of Trillionloans, added, “SaveIN has built strong traction in healthcare credit, establishing itself as a trusted partner for consumers. We are excited to drive the next phase of growth by expanding our seamless lending solutions, empowering more consumers to access the credit they need with ease and confidence.”
