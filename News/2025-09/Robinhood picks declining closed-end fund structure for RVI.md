## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] They chose a closed-end fund structure with listed shares on the NYSE. That decision is notable because closed-end funds, while long-standing, aren’t especially popular. The number of such funds has declined for 13 consecutive years—down 40% since 2011.* Historically, they often trade at a discount to their net asset value (NAV) or experience sharp volatility, as seen with Destiny.xyz’s similar fund launched in 2024.
