[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Stripe and Paradigm announce new payments-focused blockchain Tempo, a so-called Layer-1 blockchain built around stablecoins, or cryptocurrencies typically pegged to the U.S. dollar. Layer-1 blockchains like Bitcoin or Ethereum are more demanding to build and operate than “Layer 2s,” which sit atop other blockchains.
