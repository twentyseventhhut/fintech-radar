[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Carlyle acquires Intelliflo to expand WealthTech portfolio. The transaction, which also includes Intelliflo’s US-based subsidiaries, is valued at up to $200 million. Under the terms of the agreement, Carlyle will make an upfront payment of $135 million, expected to close in the fourth quarter of 2025, subject to customary closing conditions.
