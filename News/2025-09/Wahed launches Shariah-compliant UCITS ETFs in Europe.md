# Wahed launches Shariah-compliant UCITS ETFs in Europe

> [!info] 2025-09-24 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Wahed launches Shariah-Compliant UCITS ETFs in Europe. The ETFs will apply a Shariah screen and an additional layer of exclusions based on human rights lists. The Wahed S&P 500 Shariah UCITS ETF will exclude around 230 stocks from the parent index, leaving around 250 holdings.
