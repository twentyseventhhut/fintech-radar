## Quick Summary

The UK CMA is probing Global Payments' $24.25B Worldpay merger to evaluate its impact on market competition, with a decision due by Nov 11.

## UK Competition Authority Probes Global Payments' $24 Billion Worldpay Deal

## CMA's Examination of the Merger

(Reuters) -Britain’s Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) on Tuesday launched a merger inquiry into Global Payments’ $24.25 billion acquisition of Worldpay assessing whether the deal could potentially lessen competition in UK markets.

### Deadline for Phase 1 Decision

The CMA has set a deadline of November 11 to reach its Phase 1 decision.

(Reporting by Ankita Bora in Bengaluru)
