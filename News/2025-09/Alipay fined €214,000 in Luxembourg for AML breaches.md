# Alipay fined €214,000 in Luxembourg for AML breaches

> [!info] 2025-09-04 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇱🇺 Chinese payments giant Alipay fined in Luxembourg for ‘important’ AML breaches. Alipay has been fined €214,000 by Luxembourg’s financial regulator for ‘important’ breaches of AML (anti-money laundering) rules. The regulator said Alipay “committed important breaches to Luxembourg’s AML professional obligations”.

[This Week in Fintech] Luxembourg authorities have fined Alipay Europe €214,000 for anti-money laundering breaches.
