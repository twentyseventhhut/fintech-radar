# Nigerian fintech Paga expands into US market

> [!info] 2025-09-24 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Nigerian FinTech Paga aims at the US market. The Nigerian FinTech firm is partnering with a US bank to enable customers to link their bank accounts in both countries via a single digital wallet. CEO Tayo Oviosu said the plan is to initially target the Nigerian community living in the US, and then reach the wider African diaspora.
