Introducing Groww Cloud: Algo Trading in One Click
 Hello Traders, 
 If you’ve ever tried running your own trading strategies, you know the pain: setting up your dev environment, worrying about internet drops, keeping your laptop on 24/7, or even setting up and managing a cloud server. 
 We get it, Algo trading should be about your strategy, not the headache of infrastructure.
 That’s why we built something new for you. 
Say hello to Groww Cloud, our broker-integrated cloud execution platform that makes running algos faster, simpler, and more reliable.
 What is Groww Cloud? 
Groww Cloud lets you run trading algorithms directly on Groww’s infrastructure. No external servers, no unstable Wi-Fi, no whitelisted IPs, no complicated setups. Just focus on writing your strategy, and let Groww handle the rest.
With Groww Cloud traders are set to experience significantly reduced latencies as strategies are directly deployed in broker infrastructure reducing any network latencies or trader system performance issues.
Think of it as your personal trading server that’s always on, always close to the exchange, and always ready to execute.
 Feature Highlights 

 Web-Based Code Editor Write and edit your trading strategy right from your browser. 


 Strategy Scheduling & Live Deployment Schedule your strategies to auto-run on set times or days, or trigger manually. Deploy in one click. 


 Real-Time Logs Monitor performance, track errors, and keep an eye on your algo while it runs. 

 Why Groww Cloud? 

 Faster execution : Reduce latency and slippage. 


 Reliable uptime : Strategies run throughout market hours on Groww infra. 


 Zero infra setup : No AWS/GCP complexity. Deploy in one click. 

 How to Get Started? 
 Getting started is quick and simple: 
 Access Groww Cloud here 
 Click on ‘Generate API Key’ . This will create your API key that will be used in your strategy to log in to your Groww account. 

 Create a Strategy . Visit the strategies tab, and click ‘Add new strategy’.  
 


 Save your Code . Paste your code, and hit ‘Save & deploy’. 


 Schedule your execution.   Set a schedule if needed, and hit deploy. 
 

 That’s it. no extra setup, no servers to manage. 
 Ready to Try It Out? 
 Groww Cloud is now live. Get Started by subscribing to the APIs, and run your strategies now. Find API Documentation here 
 — Team Groww
