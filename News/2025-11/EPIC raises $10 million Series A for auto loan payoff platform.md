Dallas-based financial technology company EPIC has secured $10 million in Series A funding, according to Dallas Innovates. The investment round was led by FM Capital, with participation from Automotive Ventures and other strategic industry investors. EPIC, a loan payoff clearinghouse for the automotive ecosystem, plans to utilize the capital for market expansion and platform innovation. The company's platform streamlines loan payoffs and title releases for dealers, lenders, and insurers by replacing manual processes with a secure, digital system. EPIC intends to further extend its reach into the powersports and RV markets.
