# Pibank preps credit card to challenge Nubank in Colombia

> [!info] 2025-11-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Pibank is preparing to launch a credit card and compete with Nubank in a new segment. Pibank holds 70% of Banco Pichincha’s deposits and plans to release the card in 2026 or early 2027. The neobank offers high-yield savings rates and is positioning itself around simple, transparent products, setting the stage for its next growth phase.
