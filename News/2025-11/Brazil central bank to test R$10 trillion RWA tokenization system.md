# Brazil central bank to test R$10 trillion RWA tokenization system

> [!info] 2025-11-27 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 The Central Bank will authorize tests of a system that could generate R$10 trillion in RWA tokens in Brazil. With the approval of the Central Bank, the system is now undergoing supervised testing. This phase will last up to 120 days and includes simulations of the issuance, registration, settlement, and monitoring of digital promissory notes.
