Redacto raises Rs 12 Cr in seed round led by PeerCapital
 
Redacto has raised Rs 12 crore (around $1.4 million) in a seed round led by PeerCapital and Antler India, with participation from angel investors. 

 
 Follow Us
Redacto has raised Rs 12 crore (around $1.4 million) in a seed round led by PeerCapital and Antler India, with participation from angel investors. 
The proceeds will be used to strengthen the company’s AI systems, expand its product and engineering teams, and increase enterprise adoption.
Founded in 2025 by Shashank Karincheti and Amit Kumar, Redacto provides an AI-based data privacy and governance platform for enterprises. It helps organisations automate privacy compliance, manage sensitive data, and monitor third-party data handling. 
Redacto is working with payment companies and NBFCs and is in discussions with banks, fintechs, and insurance firms preparing for Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act compliance.
The platform offers data mapping, consent management, vendor risk checks, breach workflows, and regulatory reporting through a single system.
Redacto’s product suite includes a Privacy Engine for automated data discovery, ConsentFlow for user permission management, VendorShield for monitoring third-party risk, and TrustCentre for compliance sharing. It provides a central view of an organisation’s data and privacy status.
Redacto competes with Indian and global privacy-tech and data governance platforms such as SecurWeave, Privado, TrustCheckr, Scrut Automation, Luciditi, and Seclore, which offer data security, privacy, and compliance automation solutions.
