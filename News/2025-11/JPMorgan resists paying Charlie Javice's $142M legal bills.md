# JPMorgan resists paying Charlie Javice's $142M legal bills

> [!info] 2025-11-24 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 JPMorgan doesn’t want to pay Frank founder Charlie Javice’s legal bills. JPMorgan says it has been billed $142 million for the legal defense of Frank founder Charlie Javice and executive Olivier Amar, who were convicted of defrauding the bank after its $175 million acquisition of the startup. Now seeking to overturn a judge’s order requiring it to cover their fees, JPMorgan claims the bills included excessive charges.
