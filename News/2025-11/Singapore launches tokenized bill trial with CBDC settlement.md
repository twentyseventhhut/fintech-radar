## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 Singapore launches tokenized bill trial with CBDC settlements. These bills will be settled using central bank digital currency (CBDC) and aim to test the viability of tokenization in real-world applications. The trial will involve primary dealers, with more details set to be released in 2026.
