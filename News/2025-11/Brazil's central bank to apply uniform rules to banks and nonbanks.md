# Brazil's central bank to apply uniform rules to banks and nonbanks

> [!info] 2025-11-18 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 The Central Bank will apply the same risk assessment and control rules to banks and non-banking institutions. This shift is part of the evolution of the supervisory process. It occurs at a time when the regulator has 1,800 authorized institutions. In addition, there will possibly be "100 to 150" applications related to virtual assets.
