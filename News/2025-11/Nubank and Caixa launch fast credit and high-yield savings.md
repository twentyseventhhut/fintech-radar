## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 New Nubank loan and Caixa Turbo promise fast credit and returns of up to 120% of the CDI rate directly in the app. The proposal combines practicality and control, allowing customers to choose amounts, terms, and payment start date, while the Turbo Box offers an alternative source of income that can reach 120% of CDI for those who want to set short- and medium-term goals without complications.
