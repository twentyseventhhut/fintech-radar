BharatPe has named Harshita Khanna as its new Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO), signalling a renewed focus on talent strategy and leadership development as the fintech firm sharpens its growth agenda.
An accomplished HR leader with nearly two decades of experience, Khanna has worked across industries spanning financial services, telecommunications, and consulting. Prior to this appointment, she served as Chief People Officer at Home Credit India and has held senior roles with organisations such as Alcatel-Lucent, CSC, and Aon Hewitt.
At BharatPe, Khanna will lead the company’s human capital strategy—driving leadership capability, employee experience, and organisational transformation to support the firm’s next phase of expansion in India’s competitive fintech landscape.
Known for her people-first approach and focus on data-led HR transformation, Khanna is expected to play a key role in shaping a performance-driven culture while strengthening BharatPe’s position as an employer of choice in the digital finance sector.
