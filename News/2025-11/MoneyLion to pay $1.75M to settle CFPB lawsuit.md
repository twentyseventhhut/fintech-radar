# MoneyLion to pay $1.75M to settle CFPB lawsuit

> [!info] 2025-11-27 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] MoneyLion will pay $1.75 million to settle a CFPB lawsuit alleging it charged service members more than the 36% interest-rate cap allowed under the Military Lending Act.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 MoneyLion to pay $1.75M to settle CFPB lawsuit accusing it of bypassing the Military Lending Act’s 36% interest cap through mandatory membership fees tied to loans for service members. The settlement bans the FinTech from exceeding the cap in any form and follows CFPB claims that borrowers were forced to keep paid memberships and sometimes couldn’t cancel them.
