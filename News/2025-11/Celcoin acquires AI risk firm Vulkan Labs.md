# Celcoin acquires AI risk firm Vulkan Labs

> [!info] 2025-11-19 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Celcoin expands its credit business with the acquisition of Vulkan Labs. With this integration, Celcoin has access to Vulkan's proprietary decision engine, a no-code platform that orchestrates multiple data sources and automates real-time decision-making.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Celcoin, the Brazilian digital infrastructure company, acquiredVulkan Labs, the San Francisco-based startup specializing in AI-driven risk decision engines, to double down on its credit business. Celcoin will integrate Vulkan’s proprietary no-code decision-engine platform, which orchestrates multiple data sources in real time and offers advanced dashboards.
