# Fusepay goes live with Seychelles PSP license

> [!info] 2025-11-21 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇨 Fusepay goes live after securing the Central Bank of Seychelles licence to operate as a Payment Service Provider. With cheque issuance set to end for individuals in 2025 and fully cease by 2027, Fusepay aims to modernise business payments across Seychelles and nearby islands.
