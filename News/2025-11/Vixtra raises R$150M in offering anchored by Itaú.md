# Vixtra raises R$150M in offering anchored by Itaú

> [!info] 2025-11-19 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Vixtra raises R$150 million in offering anchored by Itaú. Co-founder and co-CEO Leonardo Baltieri said the new fund will accelerate the company’s expansion and meet rising demand for fast, competitive foreign-trade credit, adding that having Itaú anchor the operation signals strong confidence in Vixtra’s business model.
