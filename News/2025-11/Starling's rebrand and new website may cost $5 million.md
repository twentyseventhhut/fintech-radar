## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Starling has a new brand, and a new website might cost it $5m. The digital bank unveiled a rebrand in September with a more vibrant colour palette, new card designs, a fresh logo, and the removal of “Bank” from the company’s name. It has kept its website at the domain starlingbank.com, while its social media handles also have yet to change at the time of writing.
