# PicPay launches Visa global account with AstroPay

> [!info] 2025-11-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 PicPay announces a global account with the Visa brand and a partnership with AstroPay. Brazil’s PicPay has launched a Global Account in U.S. dollars and euros, enabling users to make international purchases and transfers directly from its app. Developed with AstroPay, the Visa-branded service offers competitive exchange rates, up to 4% annual returns.
