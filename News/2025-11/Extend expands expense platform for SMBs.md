_Businesses can now connect all of their company cards to Extend for centralized visibility and expense automation._

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Extend, a leading spend and expense management platform for SMBs, announced earlier today that it was expanding its reach to support virtually any credit card from all three major card networks.

This marks a significant step forward for small and mid-sized businesses, giving finance teams a centralized view of all their company cards and an intuitive app that streamlines expense tracking, reporting, and reconciliation through AI-powered capabilities.

“Businesses shouldn’t have to overhaul their financial infrastructure just to gain visibility and efficiency,” said Andrew Jamison, CEO and Co-Founder of Extend. “Our mission has always been to make smarter spend management accessible to every business—no matter who they bank with. By enabling nearly any bank credit card to connect with Extend, we’re eliminating the barriers that keep companies tied to manual, outdated processes.”

Extend’s expanded functionality gives small and mid-sized businesses, in particular, an easier path to modern, AI-powered expense management. Many rely on multiple card programs or manual workflows that make reconciliation and reporting time-consuming and error-prone. With Extend, they can now:

*    Connect all company cards—across any bank—in one place 
*    Automate expense tracking, categorization, and reconciliation 
*    Capture and match receipts automatically 
*    Continue using preferred credit cards, bank relationships, and rewards programs 

Companies whose banks partner directly with Extend can unlock an even deeper set of spend controls and automation built on top of their existing cards and powered by virtual cards—simplifying how finance teams manage spending and employee expenses across their organization.

“This update is a pivotal step in our mission to democratize modern payment experiences,” Jamison added. “Whether you’re a startup juggling multiple cards or a growing business ready to streamline spend visibility, Extend now works for you—right out of the box. This is another step toward making spend and expense management effortless and universal.”

**About Extend**

Extend is an AI-powered spend and expense management platform that helps businesses control card spend and simplify essential expense workflows—without switching banks, applying for new cards, or deploying complex software. Thousands of companies use Extend to create and manage virtual cards, automate expense reporting, and gain real-time visibility into team and vendor spend. Powering billions in transactions, Extend partners with the financial institutions businesses already trust. Learn more at www.paywithextend.com or follow Extend on LinkedIn.
