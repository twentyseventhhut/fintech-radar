# 3S Money Appoints Cyber Security Expert Don Randall MBE as Non-Executive Director

No text provided

3 minute read

Don will provide strategic guidance on strengthening cyber security and data practices, ensuring that the company continues to champion client safety and trust.

)

Authors

London, 20 November 2025 – 3S Money, the Fintech scale-up reshaping cross-border business payments, is excited to announce the appointment of Don Randall MBE as a non-executive director.

In his new role on the 3S Money Board, Don will provide strategic guidance on strengthening cyber security and data practices, ensuring that the company continues to champion client safety and trust.

With decades of experience in cyber security and regulatory frameworks, Don is a valuable addition to the Board. His distinguished career spans 26 years with the City of London Police, where he specialised in fraud and counter terrorism, followed by a 13-year tenure at JPMorgan Chase. In 2013, he served as the first Chief Information Security Officer at the Bank of England. As a recognised leader in cyber security, Don was also elected Chairman of the Global Banks Heads of Security Group in 2013. Don was awarded an MBE for his contributions to law enforcement and for fostering collaboration between the public and private sectors in 2007.

3S Money’s clients benefit from multi-currency business accounts, local IBANs, and personalised support from dedicated account managers – real people, not chatbots. In 2025, the business expanded into two new markets, including Hong Kong and Paris, both fully licensed and regulated, providing peace of mind for its clients.

**About 3S Money**

Founded in 2018, 3S Money provides online business accounts that empower international corporates with fast, secure, unlimited payments, and dedicated account management service. We aim to create a fairer financial world by giving every business owner access to the tools they need to send, receive, and exchange global payments anytime, anywhere.

Headquartered in London, we operate from offices in Amsterdam, Dubai, Hong Kong, Luxembourg, Riga, and now Paris. Our team serve clients in 12+ languages.
