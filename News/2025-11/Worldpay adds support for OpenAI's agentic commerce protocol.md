# Worldpay adds support for OpenAI's agentic commerce protocol

> [!info] 2025-11-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Worldpay adds support for OpenAI’s agentic commerce protocol. As the company noted in a news release, this is an open standard for AI commerce, enabling AI agents, people, and businesses to collaborate on purchases. With ACP, users of OpenAI’s ChatGPT will be able to shop directly from participating Worldpay merchants using Instant Checkout.
