# PicPay blames Apple for blocking contactless Pix on iPhone

> [!info] 2025-11-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 PicPay blames Apple for the lack of contactless Pix payment on the iPhone. According to the FinTech company, Apple holds significant economic power in relevant markets, allowing it to impose onerous obligations on card issuers and digital wallets. This would prevent the offering of Pix via contactless payment, unlike what happens on Android.
