# PayTrace partners TreviPay on B2B net-terms financing

> [!info] 2025-11-25 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 PayTrace has partnered with TreviPay to enable B2B merchants to extend net-terms financing and invoicing capabilities to commercial buyers through TreviPay’s Universal Acceptance solution. The integration leverages the Mastercard acceptance network, aiming to streamline trade credit processes and reduce friction in B2B transactions.

We’re thrilled to announce our partnership with TreviPay, a global leader in B2B payments and trade credit solutions. This partnership is designed to help B2B merchants unlock access to TreviPay’s Universal Acceptance solution, a seamless way to offer net terms financing and invoicing to commercial buyers by utilizing Mastercard’s acceptance network.

### B2B Growth Starts with Smarter Payments

B2B merchants are under pressure to do more than just sell. They need to build loyalty, streamline operations, and meet the evolving expectations of business buyers. These buyers are increasingly shaped by their B2C experiences and expect the same level of convenience, flexibility, and speed when making purchases for their companies.

Yet many merchants still rely on outdated payment processes that create friction at checkout, slow down reconciliation, and limit purchasing power.

That’s where PayTrace and TreviPay come in. Our partnership makes Universal Acceptance possible for PayTrace merchants, giving them the tools to offer flexible B2B payment options.

### Providing Payment Flexibility for B2B Merchants and Business Customers

At PayTrace, we’re committed to helping our merchants get paid faster, and that means enabling smarter, more flexible ways to get paid. Our partnership with TreviPay brings Universal Acceptance to your business, transforming how you serve your B2B customers.

Universal Acceptance allows you to offer:

*   Net terms financing through all card accepting channels
*   Fast deployment across all channels and locations
*   SKU-level invoicing delivered directly to buyers’ AP/ERP systems
*   Automated AR and collection functions
*   Streamlined reconciliation and expense matching
*   Upfront payment while TreviPay handles collections and risk

### Driving Loyalty and Business Growth

B2B buyers are loyal, high-value customers, but they expect a payment experience that matches their needs. With Universal Acceptance, you can:

*   Increase average order value (AOV) by offering flexible financing
*   Streamline reconciliation with automated invoice matching
*   Improve buyer loyalty by offering their preferred payment method
*   Accelerate speed to market with virtually no integration work
*   Offer 30-day net terms financing to qualified buyers

Whether you’re in retail, hospitality, logistics, or manufacturing, Universal Acceptance helps you meet the expectations of modern B2B buyers, without overhauling your existing systems.

### What Our Teams Are Saying

“By delivering TreviPay’s Universal Acceptance to PayTrace merchants, we are simultaneously driving business growth and empowering our merchants to offer net-terms financing and invoicing options that meet the evolving needs of B2B buyers,” said Tom Lizzio, Director of Strategic Partnerships at PayTrace.

“TreviPay’s Universal Acceptance is about meeting buyers where they are,” said John Jakobe, Director, Strategic Business and Corporate Development at TreviPay. “Through our partnership with PayTrace, we’re helping merchants offer a frictionless B2B payments experience that supports business buyers’ preferences for paying their invoices.”

### Ready to Get Started?

If you’re a PayTrace merchant already accepting Mastercard, you’re just steps away from offering net terms and integrated invoicing through TreviPay. It’s fast, easy and designed to help unlock B2B business growth.

Request a demo or contact our B2B sales experts to learn how TreviPay’s Universal Acceptance can transform your B2B payment experience.

_The B2B payment experts, PayTrace specializes in accounts receivable automation and credit card processing, making accepting secure payments affordable and easy. With solutions for ACH, interchange optimization, surcharging, and more, the PayTrace platform is built on the solid foundation of a B2B payment gateway that’s trusted by 40,000 merchant businesses._
