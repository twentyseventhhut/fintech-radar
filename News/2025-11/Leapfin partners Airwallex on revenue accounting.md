Leapfin Partners With Airwallex: Smarter Revenue Accounting for Global Businesses
Finance leaders know that when payment operations scale globally, accounting complexity scales even faster. More currencies. More systems. More reconciliation work. That’s why Leapfin and Airwallex are partnering to make it easier for high-growth businesses to unify their revenue data, accelerate reconciliation, and act with confidence.
The partnership brings together Airwallex’s world-class financial infrastructure and Leapfin’s AI-powered revenue accounting platform to help global businesses unify payments, accounting, and revenue recognition. Together, the two companies give finance teams the automation and accuracy they need to scale without compromise.
Turning Airwallex payments into accounting-ready data
With Leapfin’s native Airwallex integration , raw payment transactions are automatically transformed into clean, standardized accounting data. Finance teams no longer need to wrestle with spreadsheets or manual mappings. Instead, every transaction flows through Leapfin’s purpose-built revenue data transformation engine and gets turned into standardized Universal Accounting Records – so you can reconcile faster and report with accuracy.
One reliable source of truth for revenue
Leapfin consolidates complete Airwallex payment data alongside the rest of your revenue stack – billing systems, marketplaces, subscription platforms, and more – into a single source of truth. That means:

 Faster reconciliations . Payment data is automatically combined with related activities, giving you an audit-ready view without the manual effort. 


 Global currency normalization . Leapfin automatically converts multi-currency data, removing another layer of friction for global businesses. 


 ERP-ready reporting . Summarized and standardized records can be posted directly into NetSuite or any other ERP, saving time and cutting down on costs. 

Real-time revenue visibility, powered by AI
Leapfin’s AI-powered revenue recognition ensures that Airwallex payment data isn’t just reconciled – it’s actionable. Finance and Accounting teams gain real-time revenue insights, leveraging Leapfin's native AI agent, Luca,  for easy trend analysis, anomaly detection, and decision-ready reporting.
Why the Leapfin + Airwallex partnership matters
Together, Leapfin and Airwallex give finance leaders confidence that their payment and revenue data is complete, accurate, and accessible. Businesses can scale globally without scaling spreadsheet chaos. Controllers, VPs of Finance, and CFOs get the clarity they need to close faster, answer stakeholder questions with certainty, and focus on growth.
Early joint customers are already realizing the benefits – a consolidated view of real-time revenue data, accurate foreign-exchange reconciliation, faster month-end closes, and greater confidence in audit-ready financials.

About Leapfin
Leapfin is AI-powered revenue recognition and reconciliation software that helps companies accurately account for and report on revenue by standardizing large volumes of siloed, inconsistent operational data into GAAP- and IFRS-ready financials and automating manual accounting tasks. Leapfin powers Finance teams at OddsJam, GoodRx, Reddit, Mozilla, SeatGeek, and other high-growth global businesses. Learn more at www.leapfin.com .
