# Junio Payments gets RBI in-principle PPI approval

> [!info] 2025-11-10 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 RBI grants in-principle approval for PPI license to Junio Payments. Junio’s new Prepaid Payment Instruments authorization expands its ability to deliver secure, innovative financial solutions for teens and young adults. The license allows users to scan QR codes, make payments, and transact without a bank account.

[This Week in Fintech] Junio Paymentsreceived in-principle authorisation from Reserve Bank of India (RBI) to issue prepaid payment instruments targeting teenagers' financial education via digital wallet and UPI integration.
