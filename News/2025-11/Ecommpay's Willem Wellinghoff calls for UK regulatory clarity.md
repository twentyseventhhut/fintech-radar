# Ecommpay's Willem Wellinghoff calls for UK regulatory clarity

> [!info] 2025-11-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Ecommpay’s CCO and UK Chair, Willem Wellinghoff, calls for regulatory clarity. While supporting the National Payments Vision, Wellinghoff warned against over-simplifying reforms or merging the FCA and the Payment Systems Regulator, noting their distinct responsibilities, consumer protection versus system efficiency and innovation. He emphasized that a gradual, well-defined approach is needed to maintain effective oversight and avoid jeopardizing the UK’s payments reform agenda.
