# Green Dot to be acquired by Smith Ventures in $1.1B deal

> [!info] 2025-11-25 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Smith Ventures to acquire Green Dot in $1.1b cross-sector megadeal. Under the plan, Green Dot shareholders will receive $8.11 in cash plus 0.2215 shares of a new publicly traded bank holding company that will absorb CommerceOne’s existing operations.

[This Week in Fintech] Green Dot agrees to $1.1B breakup deal, in which Smith Ventures will acquire the company’s non-fintech operations for $690 million in cash, while CommerceOne will acquire Green Dot Bank, including its related assets and operations.❝

[This Week in Fintech] Green Dotbroke up its operations by selling its fintech business to a private equity firm and its bank unit to a separate lender, thereby splitting its embedded-finance and traditional-banking arms into separately owned entities.
