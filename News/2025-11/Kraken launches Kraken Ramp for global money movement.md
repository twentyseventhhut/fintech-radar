We’ve always believed that seamless access to crypto is the foundation for accelerating global adoption. Today we’re excited to announce Kraken Ramp .
With just one developer-friendly API, Kraken Ramp allows any platform to integrate buy/sell flows directly into their product. No redirects, no compliance headaches, no operational complexity — just instant global crypto access built on Kraken’s trusted infrastructure.
 Why Kraken Ramp? 
The crypto economy is rapidly expanding. Kraken Ramp enables businesses to spin-up seamless global money-movement rails, relying on Kraken’s peerless track record and expertise to come to market fast. 
Built on the same infrastructure that powers one of the world’s most trusted and liquid exchanges, Kraken Ramp brings together:
 Trusted & compliant infrastructure – Licensed and secure rails with industry-leading fraud prevention 
 Global payment coverage – 24+ payment methods supported including cards, ACH, PIX, SEPA, Apple Pay, Google Pay and more 
 Deep liquidity & competitive pricing – Access to Kraken’s institutional-grade pools for reliable execution and tight spreads 
 Broad asset & network support – 400+ assets supported across 100+ blockchains 
 Seamless integration – A unified API + SDK for rapid deployment with minimal engineering lift
 Who is Kraken Ramp for? 
 Kraken Ramp was designed to power the next generation of crypto adoption across industries:
 Fintechs & banks – Add crypto buy/sell flows without new licenses or compliance overhead 
 Wallets & protocols – Onboard users instantly in-app, grow TVL and reduce friction 
 Exchanges & marketplaces – Expand asset coverage and global payment reach 
 GameFi & Web3 – Enable seamless in-game purchases with local fiat methods 
 Developers & partners – Launch faster worldwide with a single integration
 A true partnership for growth 
With Kraken Ramp, businesses can focus on growth and innovation while Kraken manages the heavy lifting of compliance, licensing, fraud prevention and payment operations. This reduces risk, lowers costs and accelerates time to market.
By partnering with Kraken, you’re not just integrating an API — you’re building on a brand trusted by institutions and investors worldwide .
 The future of onboarding 
Crypto adoption depends on creating frictionless, trusted and global access points for users. Kraken Ramp is our step toward making crypto as easy to access as any other digital service — whether you’re buying bitcoin in a mobile wallet, cashing out rewards from a game or expanding a fintech’s payment offering.
Kraken Ramp makes it possible.
 Ready to get started? 
Developers and businesses can integrate Kraken Ramp today and begin delivering seamless crypto access to their users.
