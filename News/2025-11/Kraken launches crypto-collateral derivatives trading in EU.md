# Kraken launches crypto-collateral derivatives trading in EU

> [!info] 2025-11-05 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇺 Kraken launches regulated crypto-collateral futures trading in the EU. The new feature allows traders to post crypto assets instead of converting them to fiat, unlocking faster access to liquidity and reduced transaction costs. Available immediately across more than 150 perpetual futures markets.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇪🇺 Kraken will allow traders to post crypto collateral in the EU via its MiFID derivatives platform.
