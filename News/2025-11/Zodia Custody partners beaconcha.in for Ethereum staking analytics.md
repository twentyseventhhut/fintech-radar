Zodia Custody Partners with beaconcha.in to Enhance Ethereum Staking Analytics
Zodia Custody partners with beaconcha.in for real-time Ethereum validator analytics via API, ensuring maximum transparency for institutional staking investors.
Share article:
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
London, UK – 21 November, 2025 — Zodia Custody, a leading institutional digital asset custodian backed by Standard Chartered, Northern Trust, SBI Holdings, Emirates NBD, and National Australia Bank (NAB), has announced a strategic partnership with beaconcha.in, operated by bitfly explorer gmbh, a premier provider of Ethereum blockchain analytics.
Advancing Institutional Ethereum Staking
This partnership integrates beaconcha.in’s real-time data API, covering all 1.1 1 million Ethereum validators, into Zodia Custody’s institutional staking services. With Ethereum’s price at approximately $2.400, this collaboration enhances Zodia Custody’s ability to provide clients with critical insights into validator performance, staking activity, and network health, ensuring maximum transparency. It empowers institutional investors to optimize Ethereum staking strategies, maximize returns, and mitigate risks such as slashing penalties by providing them with real time data for quick decision making.
 “We are thrilled to partner with beaconcha.in, leveraging their industry-leading Ethereum analytics to deliver secure, data-driven staking solutions with the highest level of transparency for our clients,” said Thomas Ozenda, Technical Business Analyst, Zodia Custody. 
Empowering Clients with Robust Data
 “Zodia Custody’s commitment to transparency aligns perfectly with our comprehensive Ethereum validator data,” said Axel Schuster, CFA, CFO of bitfly explorer gmbh. “This partnership supports our mission to provide strong growth and innovation in the ETH-based ecosystem.” 
Comprehensive Staking Insights
beaconcha.in’s analytics, covering approximately 1 million validators and 34.5 million staked ETH (valued at roughly $100 billion at current market prices), equips Zodia Custody’s clients with the tools to make informed decisions,solidifying their position in the Ethereum staking landscape with unparalleled transparency.
