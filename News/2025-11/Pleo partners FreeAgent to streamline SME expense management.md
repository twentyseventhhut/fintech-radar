[This Week in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Spend management fintech Pleo has partnered with accounting provider FreeAgent to streamline expense management for SMEs.

## Первоисточники

### freeagent.com
<https://www.freeagent.com/blog/pleo-integration-live/>
*226 слов · direct*

We’re excited to announce that FreeAgent now integrates with Pleo, our preferred expense management partner, a user-friendly solution designed for businesses of all sizes to maximise efficiency. 
Pleo helps businesses save time by eliminating the hassle of chasing receipts through smart company cards, out-of-pocket reimbursements and real-time receipt capture.
 Beyond the chase: embracing efficiency  
By integrating FreeAgent with Pleo, businesses can:
manage, approve, pay and track all supplier invoices from a single platform
speed up expense reconciliation through a streamlined process
manage spending with customisable limits, approval workflows, budget tracking, and real-time analytics
seamlessly integrate non-payroll data into FreeAgent
This integration ultimately simplifies businesses’ accounts, leading to a much smoother financial workflow. By centralising processes, they’ll gain greater control and visibility over all business spending, including expenses and invoices, ensuring more insightful financial management. 
 A powerful combination   
The integration between Pleo and FreeAgent is designed to break down frustrating data silos - making reconciliation and month-end bookkeeping a breeze. With their Pleo company cards, reimbursements, and invoices all directly syncing up with their FreeAgent accounting data, businesses will get much better control over exactly where their money is going.
 Ready to get started?  
You can now connect FreeAgent to Pleo . If you’re interested in learning more about Pleo, check out their website .
