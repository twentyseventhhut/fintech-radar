## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 FinTech firm MyBambu shutting down West Palm Beach office, laying off all employees. The company states in the notice that this decision was made after unexpectedly learning that the main and historical source of funding for MyBambu’s operations would end, depriving MyBambu of the funding necessary for running its operations and necessitating the commencement of an orderly wind-down.
