# Zodia Custody expands partnership with Deribit by Coinbase

> [!info] 2025-11-28 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Zodia Custody expands partnership with Deribit by Coinbase. Deribit users leveraging Zodia Custody will now have trades settled in net batches, intraday, with balances automatically reflected in users’ nominated wallets after each settlement cycle.
