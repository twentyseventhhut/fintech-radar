## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇸 Revolut plans to compete in private banking in Spain. The British digital bank is exploring the market for professionals to build its own wealth management division and compete head-to-head in this highly competitive segment. Additionally, Revolut Bank begins the beta phase in Mexico users on the waiting list will have access to initial features.
