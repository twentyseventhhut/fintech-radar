# Groww debuts as India's largest fintech IPO of 2025

> [!info] 2025-11-12 · 4 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, TechCrunch — Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Groww raises nearly $750M in IPO as India’s retail investing boom continues. Groww said it plans to use the fresh capital to expand its cloud and technology infrastructure, intensify marketing efforts, and invest further in its lending and margin trading businesses. It said it has also earmarked funds for potential acquisitions.

[TechCrunch — Fintech] Groww’s listing comes amid a broader pickup in Indian startup IPOs. Eyewear retailer Lenskart made its market debut earlier this week, and payments platform Pine Labs is scheduled to list on Friday (its $440 million IPO was fully subscribed as of Tuesday). Other venture-backed firms, including Physics Wallah and Capillary Technologies, are set to go public in the coming days.

[This Week in Fintech] Groww, an Indian online brokerage platform, raised INR 6,633cr ($748M) in its Initial Public Offering (IPO), closing 29% higher with a market cap of INR 79.5K cr ($9bn).

[This Week in Fintech] Growwdebuted as India's largest fintech IPO of 2025, with the online brokerage platform raising ₹66.3 billion (~$748 million) and shares closing 29% above the issue price.
