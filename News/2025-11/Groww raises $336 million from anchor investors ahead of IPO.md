# Groww raises $336 million from anchor investors ahead of IPO

> [!info] 2025-11-05 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Groww raises over US$336 million from Anchor Investors Ahead of IPO. Shares are priced at ₹95–₹100 (≈US$1.07–US$1.13). The offer opened on November 4th, with allotment on November 10th and listing expected on November 12th, 2025. Proceeds will fund marketing, cloud upgrades, brand initiatives, and capitalisation of subsidiaries Groww Creditserv Technology and Groww Invest Tech.
