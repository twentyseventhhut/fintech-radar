# MoonPay secures NYDFS limited-purpose trust charter

> [!info] 2025-11-27 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 MoonPay joins the “Dual License Club” after securing a NYDFS limited-purpose trust charter. According to a press release, the new license brings further regulatory compliance measures to the firm’s 30 million customers and nearly 500 client and partner companies. MoonPay says it is now fully licensed in the US and regulated in the UK, EU, Canada, and Australia.
