# Revolut to open a store in Greece by year-end

> [!info] 2025-11-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇷 Revolut is set to open a store in Greece by the end of the year. The digital bank has informed the Bank of Greece about the opening of its store and the provision of Greek IBANs to its new customers' accounts, while it is planned that all Lithuanian IBANs of Greek customers will be converted to Greek ones.
