# Brazil's central bank regulates virtual assets, new institution type

> [!info] 2025-11-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazilian Central Bank regulates the use of virtual assets and creates a new type of institution. The set of rules seeks to balance incentives for innovation and security in cryptocurrency transactions. The rules will come into effect on February 2nd, 2026. Companies will now be authorized by the Central Bank, subject to the entire supervisory process.
