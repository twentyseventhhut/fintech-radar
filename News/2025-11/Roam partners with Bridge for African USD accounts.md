# Roam partners with Bridge for African USD accounts

> [!info] 2025-11-21 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Roam partners with Bridge to power global payments for Africans. Through the app, users can open a U.S. bank account, receive USD or stablecoin payments, convert to local currency, and send money back to the U.S., all from one platform.

[This Week in Fintech] Roampartnered with Bridge (a Stripe company) and Maplerad to let Africans open U.S.-dollar bank accounts, receive payments in USD and stablecoins, convert to local currency, and move money cross-border – streamlining global income access for freelancers and remote workers.
