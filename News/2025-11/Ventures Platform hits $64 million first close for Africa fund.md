# Ventures Platform hits $64 million first close for Africa fund

> [!info] 2025-11-10 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Ventures Platform portfolio includes Visa-backed unicorn Moniepoint and Stripe-owned Paystack. The VC welcomed several new and existing LPs, including the International Finance Corporation (IFC), Standard Bank, British International Investment (BII), and Proparco (through its EU-backed Choose Africa VC program). Others include AfricaGrow and Egypt’s Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises Development Agency (MSMEDA). With this fund, the firm said it will also start investing in series A deals.
