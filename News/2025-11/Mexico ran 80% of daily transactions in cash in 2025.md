# Mexico ran 80% of daily transactions in cash in 2025

> [!info] 2025-11-24 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] In 2025 Mexico conducted 80% of its daily transactions in cash, while Argentina and Colombia recorded 70%. This high cash dependence highlights a huge opportunity for LatAm fintechs: areas like digital payments, micro-loans, and financial inclusion could grow significantly by reducing reliance on physical currency and serving unbanked populations.
