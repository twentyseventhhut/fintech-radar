# Tencent launches TenPay Global Checkout for Weixin Mini Programs

> [!info] 2025-11-12 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇳 Tencent launches TenPay Global Checkout, a new payment solution for Weixin Mini Program to enable merchants to accept local payments outside of China. Tencent said in a statement that with a single integration of TenPay, Weixin Mini Program overseas merchants can support multiple payment methods, including digital wallets, local real-time payment networks, and credit and debit cards.
