# PPRO enables Wero payments for CTS EVENTIM in Germany

> [!info] 2025-11-06 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 PPRO enables Wero for CTS EVENTIM. This collaboration will see CTS EVENTIM leverage PPRO’s infrastructure to process Wero payment transactions in Germany for ticket purchases across concerts, theatre shows, and sports events. PPRO’s platform will also support additional Wero payment flows as they are introduced in other markets across Europe.
