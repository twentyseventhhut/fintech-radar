# Aspora to let Indian diaspora pay bills back home

> [!info] 2025-11-17 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, TechCrunch — Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Sequoia-backed FinTech Aspora will let the Indian diaspora pay bills back home. This means non-resident Indians can pay utility bills or recharge their mobile prepaid plans for their family. The startup said that until now, users had to either transfer the money to their Indian accounts or ask someone to handle the bills for them.

[TechCrunch — Fintech] Sequoia-backed fintech platform Aspora, which lets the Indian diaspora send money back to India, is launching a new feature for users to pay bills. This means non-resident Indians (NRIs) can pay utility bills or recharge their mobile prepaid plans for their family.
