 Cashflows Partners With Boodil to Bring Card Payments to UK E-Commerce Merchants in Time for Golden Quarter 
 Payments
 11.11.2025 09:35 am 
 Cashflows, the platform that makes it easy for businesses to accept payments, has partnered with Boodil, a UK-based e-commerce payments technology provider, to enhance the payment experience for Shopify merchants across the UK. 
 The strategic partnership combines Cashflows’ robust acquiring and gateway technology with Boodil’s innovative payment technology, arming Shopify merchants with a comprehensive payment suite that offers card payments and digital wallets. 
 The new joint offering, delivered through the Boodil - UK application, is now fully live and available in time for the high-volume 'Golden Quarter' e-commerce peak season. 
 The new partnership addresses merchant requests for a full payment stack. Designed to streamline checkout processes, reduce cart abandonment, and boost conversion rates, the solution is rapid and low-friction. Merchants complete their onboarding via Cashflows (including KYC and approval) and can activate card payments via the Boodil application in less than a minute, dramatically enhancing their checkout capabilities. 
 By prioritising customer convenience, the solution ensures high transaction success rates, alongside competitive and transparent pricing. 
 Jamie Seabrook, Head of Product Management at Cashflows, said: "From speaking with customers, we saw the opportunity for a robust, integrated Shopify solution. Partnering with Boodil allows us to instantly address this demand, bringing our enterprise-grade acquiring and gateway solutions to a new, dynamic segment of UK e-commerce. This is a significant win that not only expands Cashflows' market reach but also makes our existing merchants very happy with a powerful new sales channel." 
 Sam Owens, Co-founder at Boodil, added: “Our partnership with Cashflows marks a major milestone in our mission to redefine eCommerce payments. By integrating our technology with Cashflows’ expertise, we’re delivering a flexible, customer-centric payment solution that drives conversions and enhances the shopping experience for UK consumers.” 
 For Cashflows, the partnership expands their e-commerce reach within the UK. For Boodil, this alliance strengthens its UK presence by leveraging Cashflows’ deep market expertise to deliver tailored payment solutions that meet the unique needs of British shoppers. This partnership reflects a shared commitment to empowering merchants with the tools they need to succeed in the competitive digital economy, delivering a payment experience that is both secure and effortlessly adaptable.
