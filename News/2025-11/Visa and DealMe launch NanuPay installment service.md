# Visa and DealMe launch NanuPay installment service

> [!info] 2025-11-05 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇻🇳 Visa and DealMe launch NanuPay, the world’s first cross-border card installment service, available in South Korea for Vietnamese cardholders. Visa and DealMe aim to support Vietnam's digital transformation and make international spending simpler, safer, and more affordable.

[This Week in Fintech] Visapartnered with DealMe to launch NanuPay, enabling Vietnamese Visa credit cardholders to select installment plans while shopping in South Korea

About Visa
Visa (NYSE: V) is a world leader in digital payments, facilitating transactions between consumers, merchants, financial institutions and government entities across more than 200 countries and territories. Our mission is to connect the world through the most innovative, convenient, reliable and secure payments network, enabling individuals, businesses and economies to thrive. We believe that economies that include everyone everywhere, uplift everyone everywhere and see access as foundational to the future of money movement. Learn more at Visa.com . 

About DealMe
DealMe was established in 2022 with technology investments from KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology). In June 2023, the company signed an agreement with Lotte Card to process installment payments for international customers visiting South Korea. The Initial Startup Package, a government-supported program organized by the Ministry of Small and Medium Enterprises and Startups and the Korea Startup Promotion Agency, has been crucial in supporting DealMe's investment in the technology needed to implement cross-border credit card installment payments.
