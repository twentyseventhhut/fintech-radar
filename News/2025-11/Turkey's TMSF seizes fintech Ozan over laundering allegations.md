[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇹🇷 Turkish state grabs another FinTech as company seizures continue at a pace. Istanbul's penal court has appointed Turkey’s Deposit Insurance Fund (TMSF) as trustee of local FinTech Ozan, following money laundering allegations against the company, according to the Istanbul Chief Prosecutor’s Office. The move further expands TMSF’s growing control over the country’s FinTech sector.
