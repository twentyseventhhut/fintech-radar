# Solflare and Mastercard launch Solana self-custody debit card

> [!info] 2025-11-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Solflare partners with Mastercard to launch Solana's first self-custody debit card. The new digital card allows users to make instant debit card payments with USDC while maintaining full control of their assets, without preloading or prior conversion. The Solflare card will initially be available to those in the UK and the European Economic Area (EEA).
