 Grupo Cibest S.A. reported that Colombia’s Financial Superintendency has authorized Nequi S.A. Compañía de Financiamiento to operate nationwide as a financing company. The authorization, issued via Resolution No. 2002 dated October 31, 2025 and amended by Resolution No. 2021 dated November 4, 2025, became final and enforceable on November 6, 2025.
Nequi will commence operations once it completes the remaining procedures required to operate as a financing company. Nequi remains part of Grupo Cibest, and this development does not change how customers access or use current products and services.

 Nequi received an operating permit as a financing company from Colombia’s Financial Superintendency, with the authorization finalized on November 6, 2025. This establishes the legal basis to conduct activities aligned with a financing company’s corporate purpose across Colombia.
Operations start only after additional required procedures are completed, so commercial timing depends on completing those steps. The company states that Nequi remains part of Grupo Cibest , and customer access to products and services is unchanged by this announcement.
Subsequent disclosures may detail when operations formally begin and any product scope under the financing company license. The filing provides no financial figures tied to this authorization.
