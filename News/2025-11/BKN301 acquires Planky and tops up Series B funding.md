# BKN301 acquires Planky and tops up Series B funding

> [!info] 2025-11-26 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 London’s BKN301 Group secures €32 million and acquires Planky. Through the acquisition of Planky, BKN301 gains a proprietary AI and data analytics engine that will be fully integrated into its digital banking architecture. The funds will help to deliver smarter, faster, and more personalised digital banking experiences.

[This Week in Fintech] BKN301acquiredPlanky, an AI-driven financial analytics and open-banking specialist, while topping up its Series B with a £10 million credit facility to strengthen its fintech infrastructure stack across Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.

[This Week in Fintech] BKN301 Group raised €32 million (~$37 million) through a combination of a recent €21 million Series B and a credit facility to scale its AI-driven fintech infrastructure platform across Europe.❝
