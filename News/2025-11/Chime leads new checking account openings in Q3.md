# Chime leads new checking account openings in Q3

> [!info] 2025-11-04 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Chime leads in new checking accounts opened in Q3. Neobank Chime has become the most popular destination for consumers opening new checking accounts, according to the most recent J.D. Power report. Of the new checking accounts that were opened in the third quarter, 13% were opened at Chime.
