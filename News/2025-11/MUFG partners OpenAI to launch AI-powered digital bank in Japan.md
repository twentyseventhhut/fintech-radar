## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇵 MUFG partners with OpenAI to launch an AI‑powered digital bank next year in Japan. The partnership will use AI to automate account openings, customer chats, and everyday money management through smartphone apps connected to ChatGPT. The new digital bank will allow people to consult with ChatGPT about savings, asset management, and household spending.
