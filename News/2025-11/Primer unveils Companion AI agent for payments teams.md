 Primer , the unified infrastructure provider for global payments and commerce, has launched Primer Companion, a purpose-built AI agent designed to support merchants navigating complex international payment ecosystems. 
The company introduced Primer Companion to address what it identifies as a significant gap in payments management, according to FF News . Many businesses operate with under-resourced teams that must oversee large payment volumes, monitor risks, and identify revenue opportunities in real time.
Primer aims to equip these teams with intelligent tooling that can augment capacity and streamline operational decision-making.
Its platform integrates data from across the payments ecosystem, providing merchants with tools to enhance authorisation rates, reduce costs and create flexible payment workflows.
Primer Companion brings intelligence into the payment stack by understanding the specific language and logic of a merchant’s environment.
Unlike general-purpose AI tools, it reasons rather than simply responds. The product serves contextual recommendations within the Primer platform, provides a real-time conversational interface, automates approved actions, and embeds strict safety and privacy principles throughout its design.
The AI agent is powered by extensive global payments datasets, capturing more than 400 data points for every transaction. This data depth enables Primer Companion to generate highly tailored insights, helping merchants optimise authorisation performance, lower costs, and accelerate rollout strategies across markets.
Primer Companion is part of a wider slate of product developments being unveiled during the company’s Primer Showcase. The event, which streams on 19 November, will introduce further enhancements across the payment lifecycle. Registration for the showcase is currently open.
Primer co-founder and CEO Gabriel Le Roux said, “With Primer Companion, we’re addressing one of the biggest gaps in payments.”
Speaking further, Primer co-founder and CEO Gabriel Le Roux said, “Speaking to merchants globally, payments are one of the most critical yet under-resourced functions in any business. Often, just one or two people are responsible for managing millions – even billions – in payments, constantly scanning for risks and opportunities. By combining Primer’s data and expertise with generative AI, we’re giving those teams the equivalent of a hundred teammates – surfacing contextual insights and automating actions so they can focus on what truly drives growth.”
Le Roux continued, “Primer Companion represents a paradigm shift for payment and finance leaders – as it rolls out across the Primer platform to cover more of the payment lifecycle, its potential to transform how teams operate is enormous. This marks the next step towards our vision to provide a connected view across the entire money movement journey – creating intelligent, end-to-end infrastructure for the next era of commerce.”
