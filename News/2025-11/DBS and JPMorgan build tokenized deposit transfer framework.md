## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌍 JP Morgan's Kinexys business is working with Singaporean bank DBS on an interoperability framework for tokenised deposit transfers. The US bank also issued its deposit token on Coinbase's Base blockchain, a first for a US bank.

[This Week in Fintech] DBS and J.P. Morganpartnered to create an interbank tokenised deposit transfer framework enabling instant cross-border blockchain payments. Hear the inside story on our Money Code podcast, featuring Naveen Malella, Global Co-Head of Kinexys at J.P. Morgan.
