Binance Japan and PayPay Launch PayPay Money Linkage Service
- Enabling smooth crypto asset purchases on Binance Japan’s spot trading (exchange) via PayPay Money – PayPay Points also supported -
Binance Japan Inc. (Head Office: Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo; General Manager: Takeshi Chino; “Binance Japan”) and PayPay Corporation (Head Office: Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo; President & Representative Director, CEO, Corporate Officer: Ichiro Nakayama; “PayPay”) today launched a new integration service with PayPay Money*1. Through this integration, users of Binance Japan’s crypto asset spot trading (exchange; mobile and web versions) can now purchase*2 crypto assets using funds deposited from PayPay Money, and can top up their PayPay Balance with the proceeds from selling crypto assets as PayPay Money.
In addition to PayPay Money, users can also use PayPay Points*3 to fund crypto asset purchases. By presetting “Use for payments” in the PayPay app, users can combine PayPay Money and PayPay Points for transactions.
Until now, crypto asset purchases and withdrawals on Binance Japan’s crypto asset spot trading were limited to Japanese yen transfers via domestic bank accounts or deposits and withdrawals through external exchanges or wallets.
*1 PayPay Money is a type of PayPay Balance. PayPay Money Lite is not eligible for this service. For details on PayPay Balance, please refer here . For details on the balance limit of PayPay Money, please refer here .
*2 Crypto asset spot trading (Exchange) and services such as Simple Earn are not eligible. Users cannot deposit PayPay Money solely as an account balance in Binance Japan, nor top up their PayPay Balance from Japanese yen already held in the account.
*3 PayPay Points are points primarily granted through payments in the PayPay app and cannot be withdrawn or transferred. Limited-time PayPay Points cannot be used.
With this new integration, users can execute both deposit and withdrawal procedures with a single click when buying or selling crypto assets on Binance Japan’s crypto asset spot trading (exchange), enabling a smooth trading experience available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year*4, starting from as little as JPY 1,000.
To activate the PayPay Money integration, users are required to enable the linkage with PayPay Money once via the “PayPay” icon displayed within Binance Japan’s crypto asset spot trading (exchange; mobile and web versions).
*4 Excluding system maintenance hours.
As the Japanese subsidiary of Binance – which operates one of the world’s largest blockchain ecosystems and crypto asset infrastructures*5 – Binance Japan began offering services in Japan in 2023.
Drawing on advanced technology and a broad product lineup cultivated worldwide, Binance Japan offers a secure, easy-to-use trading environment for users in Japan. It serves a wide spectrum – from first-time users to experienced traders – and is gaining support as a service that helps make crypto assets more accessible.
*5 Binance operates the world’s largest crypto asset exchange, serving 290 million users worldwide.
This initiative builds on PayPay’s investment in Binance Japan. 
Going forward, PayPay and Binance Japan will continue to strengthen their collaboration to further enhance the convenience of both PayPay and Binance Japan users.

 ■ Overview of the PayPay Money Linkage Service 
– Eligible users: Users who satisfy all of the following conditions:
 
 1. Identity verification completed on the Binance Japan app, 
 2. Identity verification completed on PayPay, 
 3. Consent to account linkage 
 

– Availability: 24 hours a day, 365 days a year (in principle)
– Supported purchase orders: Spot purchases via Binance Japan “Exchange” (mobile and web versions)
– Deposit fee: Free
– Withdrawal fee: JPY 110
– Deposit minimum: JPY 1,000
– Deposit limits: JPY 1,000,000 per 24 hours; JPY 2,000,000 per 30 days
– Withdrawal minimum: JPY 1,000
– Withdrawal limits: JPY 1,000,000 per 24 hours; JPY 2,000,000 per 30 days 
 　* Subject to “PayPay” usage limits. 
 　* Withdrawals are unavailable if the PayPay Money balance limit is exceeded.
– Cancellation of account linkage: Users may cancel at any time.
To use the Binance Japan app, please download or update to the latest version (iOS 3.6.2 or later, Android 3.6.4 or later) before use.
