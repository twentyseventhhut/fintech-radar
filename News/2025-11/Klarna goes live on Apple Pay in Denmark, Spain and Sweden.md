# Klarna goes live on Apple Pay in Denmark, Spain and Sweden

> [!info] 2025-11-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Klarna is now available on Apple Pay in Denmark, Spain, and Sweden, with France to follow. Eligible customers can split purchases into three monthly installments, or pay up to 30 days later, always interest-free. Customers can also spread the cost of higher-value items over a longer period, with competitive interest rates starting from 0%.
