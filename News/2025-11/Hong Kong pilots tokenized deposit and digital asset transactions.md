Hong Kong has started the pilot phase of Project Ensemble, moving from a sandbox to live testing of tokenized deposits and digital asset transactions.

The Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) said the pilot will operate through 2026 and initially focus on tokenized money-market fund trades and real-time treasury management.

Project Ensemble aims to integrate tokenization into banking through the e-HKD, with interbank settlement first supported by the HKD Real Time Gross Settlement system.

The HKMA said future upgrades will support 24/7 settlement in tokenized central bank money.

This move comes as regional financial centers increase efforts in tokenized settlements.
