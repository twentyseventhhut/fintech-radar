# QNB taps TransferMate for embedded B2B payments

> [!info] 2025-11-05 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇶🇦 QNB taps Irish paytech TransferMate for embedded B2B payments tech. The partnership will see TransferMate's embedded B2B payments infrastructure integrated into QNB's platform, providing the bank's corporate customers with "access to a broader network of international multicurrency collections and local accounts capabilities", QNB says.
