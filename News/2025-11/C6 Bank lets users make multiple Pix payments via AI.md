# C6 Bank lets users make multiple Pix payments via AI

> [!info] 2025-11-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 C6 Bank users can now make multiple Pix payments with the help of AI. The customer needs to provide the amounts and payment method keys, confirming the data afterward. It is also possible to share this information via an image. According to the bank, the new feature is already available for individuals and businesses.
