## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Colombia's financial superintendency approves the integration of Scotiabank and Davivienda. With the integration of Scotiabank´s business, Davivienda´s total assets are projected to reach approximately US$60 billion, representing growth of nearly 40%. Regionally, assets are expected to increase by around 30% in Colombia, 9% in Costa Rica, and 180% in Panama.
