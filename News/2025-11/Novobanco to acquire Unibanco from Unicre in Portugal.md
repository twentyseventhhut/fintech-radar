# Novobanco to acquire Unibanco from Unicre in Portugal

> [!info] 2025-11-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇵🇹 Novobanco notifies the Competition Authority that it will acquire Unibanco from Unicre. The deal, effective from November 5, 2025, transfers all Unibanco assets and operations, focused on consumer credit and credit card services, to Novobanco, strengthening its position in Portugal’s retail financial market.
