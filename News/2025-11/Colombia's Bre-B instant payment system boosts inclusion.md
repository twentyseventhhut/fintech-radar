# Colombia's Bre-B instant payment system boosts inclusion

> [!info] 2025-11-12 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Colombia’s Bre-B Instant Payment System Transforms Transactions and Financial Inclusion. Bre-B is an instant payment system that enables transfers using simple “keys” like phone numbers or emails. It allows payments for services, transport, and salaries directly from mobile apps. ACI Worldwide noted that the system will let companies and freelancers send and receive payments instantly, improving efficiency and financial inclusion.
