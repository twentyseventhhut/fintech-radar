[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇪 SWIFT’s ISO 20022 cutover approaches as blockchain connections point to the next phase. The cutover will retire the long-standing MT format for core payment instructions and replace it with a unified framework built for richer, more consistent data. SWIFT has also tested connections with blockchain networks to explore cross-border transfers, CBDC payments, and asset tokenisation.
