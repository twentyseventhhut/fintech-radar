# Stakk signs three-year contract with SoFi

> [!info] 2025-11-21 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 Stakk signs a 3-year contract with US FinTech powerhouse SoFi. Under the terms of the Agreement, Stakk will deliver SoFi a solution encompassing Stakk IQ, specifically its mobile image capture, image authentication, OCR, and document/data orchestration capabilities, powering certain deposit acceptance features for SoFi.
