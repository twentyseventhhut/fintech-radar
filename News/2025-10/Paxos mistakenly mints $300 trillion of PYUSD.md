# Paxos mistakenly mints $300 trillion of PYUSD

> [!info] 2025-10-17 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Paxos mistakenly issues $300 trillion of PayPal stablecoin. Paxos immediately identified the error and burned the excess PYUSD, with the company stating there is no security breach and customer funds are safe. The erroneous transaction was visible on Etherscan and was quietly reversed within minutes.
