## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇦 Gulf Startup Tabby nabs $4.5 billion valuation in secondary sale. The new valuation is up from the $3.3 billion price tag Tabby garnered in a $160 million financing round just eight months ago. The deal allowed firms, including HSG and Boyu Capital, to buy shares from existing investors, according to a statement.
