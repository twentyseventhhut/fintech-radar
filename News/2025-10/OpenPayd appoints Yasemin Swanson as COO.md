# Ex-ClearBank executive named COO at OpenPayd

## Yasemin Swanson has held senior roles at numerous banking and fintech groups

Payments and banking platform OpenPayd has appointed Yasemin Swanson as the firms new chief operations officer (COO).

Swanson will be responsible for scaling OpenPayd’s operational infrastructure as it looks to expand globally and secure additional regulatory milestones.

The new OpenPayd executive joins the company from clearing and embedded banking company ClearBank, worth around $1bn, where she held a handful of roles, including head of operations, chief operations officer and chief business officer.

Swanson has also held senior roles at Barclaycard, GoCardless and Vocalink, a fintech group owned by Mastercard.

“OpenPayd is breaking down barriers in global payments, and I’m thrilled to help build the operational backbone for a truly borderless financial future,” Swanson said.

“I’m eager to work with our talented team and develop a world-class operational framework that drives scalability, regulatory compliance, and customer trust – ultimately enabling OpenPayd to expand its reach and impact without losing the agility that defines its culture.”

Swanson’s background includes know your customer and anti-money laundering monitoring.

“We are delighted that Yasemin has joined OpenPayd as our Chief Operations Officer,” said Iana Dimitrova, chief executive of OpenPayd.

“We are confident that her experience will enable us to continue fostering a high-performance culture that aligns with our ongoing growth and innovation goals.”

OpenPayd appoints Yasemin Swanson as Chief Operations Officer 
OpenPayd strengthens Senior Leadership Team by appointing ex-ClearBank Executive as Chief Operations Officer. 
Posted on October 6, 2025
 OpenPayd Editorial Team 
 London, UK, 6 October 2025 – OpenPayd, a leading provider of financial infrastructure, today announced the appointment of Yasemin Swanson as its Chief Operations Officer. Swanson joins from ClearBank, where she was Chief Business Officer.   
 In her new role, Swanson will be scaling OpenPayd’s operational infrastructure to support global expansion and the continued development of payment innovations that meet and exceed regulatory standards.  
 Swanson brings a wealth of experience in operational excellence, KYC, and AML monitoring, with a proven track record of developing and enhancing operating models across the financial services sector. During her tenure at ClearBank, she was responsible for developing and implementing the bank’s target operating model, as well as ensuring compliance with evolving regulatory requirements. She delivered the first phase of the operating model transformation and successfully implemented the APP Fraud Reimbursement Scheme.   
 Swanson’s previous roles also include Digital and Operations Director at Barclays Payments. Responsible for a 300+ person global team, Swanson drove improvements in speed, compliance and customer satisfaction across the card issuing and acquiring operations and the onboarding arm of the business.   
 On the appointment of Swanson, Iana Dimitrova, CEO of OpenPayd said, “We are delighted that Yasemin has joined OpenPayd as our Chief Operations Officer. We are confident that her experience will enable us to continue fostering a high-performance culture that aligns with our ongoing growth and innovation goals.”  
 Yasemin Swanson, Chief Operations Officer at OpenPayd, said: “OpenPayd is breaking down barriers in global payments, and I’m thrilled to help build the operational backbone for a truly borderless financial future. I’m eager to work with our talented team and develop a world-class operational framework that drives scalability, regulatory compliance, and customer trust – ultimately enabling OpenPayd to expand its reach and impact without losing the agility that defines its culture.”       
 About OpenPayd   
 OpenPayd is building the universal financial infrastructure for the digital economy. Their rails-agnostic platform enables any business to move and manage money globally – across fiat and digital assets – through a single, powerful API.  
 OpenPayd provides embedded finance solutions, including FX, domestic and international payments, Open Banking, and stablecoin on/off ramps – delivering full interoperability between traditional finance and digital assets. With one of the most comprehensive banking networks in the market, OpenPayd enables real-time money movement, everywhere.  
 Trusted by global brands including eToro, OKX and B2C2, they process more than €130 billion in annual volumes for over 800 businesses. OpenPayd is the infrastructure layer powering the next generation of financial services.  
 For more information, please visit www.openpayd.com .
