# Moving from payments coordination to payments orchestration

### Manage the full payments lifecycle with one platform

Merchants require a centralized modern payment platform to keep up with savvy shoppers who demand a frictionless experience with the latest alternative payments, regardless of how they shop or pay.

Download this whitepaper to learn how retailers are tackling this and other issues with centralized payment orchestration solutions:

*   The top benefits of centralizing the entire payment and fraud ecosystems
*   How to find the right payments orchestration partner
*   What you need to know about executing a transition to orchestration

Use this knowledge to gain a competitive edge to maximize conversion, drive customer loyalty, and prevent fraud with one seamless integration.
