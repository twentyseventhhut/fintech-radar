Summary of this article

*   IREDA sanctioned loans worth ₹33,148 crore in April–September FY26, up 86% from a year earlier.

*   Loan disbursements rose 54% to ₹15,043 crore in the same period.

*   Outstanding loan book reached ₹84,445 crore as of September 30, up 31% year-on-year.

*   CMD Pradip Kumar Das said IREDA will continue scaling up support for clean energy projects aligned with India’s green goals.

*   IREDA, under the Ministry of New & Renewable Energy, is a state-owned NBFC financing renewable energy projects.

State-owned IREDA has sanctioned loans worth ₹33,148 crore in April-September FY26, up 86% from ₹17,860 crore in the same period a year ago.

In a statement on Wednesday, the company said its loan disbursements have also recorded healthy growth of 54% to ₹15,043 crore, up from ₹9,787 crore in H1 of FY 2024-25.
