PagoNxt Payments automates €150M+ in Annual Cross Currency Invoice Payments for Gesban 
Paying suppliers worldwide should be effortless. Yet for more than a decade, Gesban — Santander’s backbone for supplier invoice payments — faced a different reality: every cross-currency transaction meant approvals, manual checks, and reconciliations that took around 15 minutes per operation. Complexity slowed teams, raised costs, and kept global efficiency out of reach. Until automation changed the game. 
By integrating Gesban’s SAP system with PagoNxt’s Payments platform, everything transformed. Today, cross-currency payments flow seamlessly end-to-end — from approval to FX execution to reconciliation — with zero manual intervention. What once took 15 minutes is now completed in seconds, securely and at scale.

 The Impact   
- Full automation of cross-currency invoice payments, eliminating manual steps. - Processing times reduced from 15 minutes to seconds.   - 2–3% FX margin improvement, enabling larger volumes at better rates.   - Reduced operational risk by eliminating human error.   - Global reach, supporting USD, GBP, BRL, JPY, CHF, PLN and more.   - Already processed €13M in pilot (around 165 payments with one entity).   - Scaling to 1,700–2,000 annual payments, equivalent to €150M+. - Expansion already in progress: Gesban México has started adopting the model, with 10 additional Santander entities expected to follow.
