# GoCardless signs Clay as a customer

> [!info] 2025-10-27 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Clay selects GoCardless to streamline loan disbursements and repayments at scale. Clay now uses GoCardless to unify its entire payment process onto a single, scalable platform, leveraging GoCardless’s Direct Debit capability to collect repayments and Outbound Payments, the FinTech’s newly launched disbursement product, to pay out loans.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Payments provider GoCardlesssigned AI credit and debt-as-a-service company Clay as a customer.
