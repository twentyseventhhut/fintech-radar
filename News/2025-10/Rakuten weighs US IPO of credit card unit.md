## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Rakuten jumps on reports that it is weighing a US IPO of its credit card unit. Rakuten Group shares rose as much as 6.7%, the most since April 10, after Reuters reported the Japanese e-commerce firm is considering listing its credit card business in the US. The company is in the early stages of considering a US IPO.
