Barclays and Visa Extend Partnership to Bring Customers Next Gen Payment Experiences and Shape the Future of Payments 
29/10/2025
Partnership will help drive growth in issuing and acquiring across Retail, Corporate, Business, and Private Bank segments and includes supporting Retail issuing in the US 
New agreement paves the way to unlock the potential of emerging payment experiences for customers, from account-to-account (A2A) payments to agentic commerce
 London, 29 October 2025 – Barclays and Visa have announced a new, expanded strategic partnership and multi-year agreement, marking a powerful next chapter in their six-decade relationship. This milestone reinforces a foundation of innovation, trust, and shared ambition, with a renewed commitment to delivering exceptional outcomes for consumers, businesses, and the broader economy. 
The agreement spans issuing and acquiring across Barclays’ Retail, Corporate, Business, US and Private Bank segments, enabling secure, seamless, and future-ready payment experiences for millions of consumers and merchants. Visa will continue to support Barclays issued card credentials in the UK, while also driving growth in US Retail issuing and expanding commercial issuing into new markets. 
 As part of the expansion, Barclays and Visa will explore new opportunities to innovate across a broad spectrum of payment types, channels, and technologies. Visa will provide Barclays with access to enhanced services supporting both issuing and acquiring, whilst enabling Barclays to leverage the extensive range of Value Added Services (VAS) that Visa offers. 
This renewed partnership reflects a shared commitment to meeting evolving customer and business needs. It builds on decades of co-investment and innovation, from pioneering card programmes to embedded finance and today’s AI-powered solutions, underscoring deep strategic alignment and a joint ambition to drive inclusive growth and digital transformation across the economy. 
 Vim Maru, Chief Executive of Barclays UK, said: 
“This agreement represents the next chapter in our long and successful partnership with Visa. Together we are investing in the future of payments and making it easier, faster and more secure for people and businesses to pay and get paid, wherever they are in the world. From pioneering the UK’s first credit card to enabling new forms of digital and account-to-account payments, Barclays and Visa have both maintained a longtime focus on the evolving ways that customers connect with their money. Extending this relationship allows us to continue innovating and providing trusted solutions for millions of customers.” 
 
 Antony Cahill, Regional President & Chief Executive Officer, Visa Europe, said: “We’re proud to extend our partnership with Barclays—a relationship between two iconic brands built on trust, innovation, and a shared commitment to serving consumers, businesses, and the economy. Barclays has invested in innovations that customers love, from introducing the first credit card in the UK market to enabling the first contactless transaction. In a world being reshaped by AI, digital transformation, and evolving customer expectations, this agreement brings together the full strength of Visa’s capabilities—from issuing and acquiring to loyalty and open banking — delivering payment experiences that are not only trusted and resilient, but also ready for what’s next.”
