# Canada’s Wealthsimple valued at C$10 billion in new equity round

> [!info] 2025-10-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇦 Canada’s Wealthsimple valued at C$10 billion in new equity round. The capital raise announced is co-led by San Francisco-based Dragoneer Investment Group and GIC in Singapore. The round includes both a C$550 million primary offering and a secondary offering of up to C$200 million.
