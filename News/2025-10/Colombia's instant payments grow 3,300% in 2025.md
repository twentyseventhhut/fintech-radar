# Colombia's instant payments grow 3,300% in 2025

> [!info] 2025-10-01 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Immediate payments from businesses and individuals are growing at a rate of 3,300%. In 2025, real-time payments in Colombia surged, with Transfiya processing over 8.5 million transactions worth 1.7 trillion pesos. The new Bre-B system, built by Banco de la República with support from ACI Worldwide, will enable instant, secure transfers between banks for both people and businesses.
