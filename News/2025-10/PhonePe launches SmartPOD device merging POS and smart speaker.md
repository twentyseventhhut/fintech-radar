# PhonePe launches SmartPOD device merging POS and smart speaker

> [!info] 2025-10-09 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 PhonePe launches next-gen SmartSpeaker with integrated card payments. PhonePe has launched its new SmartPOD, a device merging POS and smart speaker functions, enabling small merchants to accept both UPI and card payments. Designed in India, it supports major card networks and offers features like dual displays and celebrity voice confirmations.
