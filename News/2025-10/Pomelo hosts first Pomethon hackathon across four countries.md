# Pomelo hosts first Pomethon hackathon across four countries

> [!info] 2025-10-02 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇷 Pomelo hosted its first Pomethon, a hackathon with 140 participants across 4 countries and 14 teams. In just two days, teams developed innovative projects and pitched them to a top-tier jury, who judged based on impact, feasibility, and originality. The result: 14 new ideas, key learnings, and one winning team.
