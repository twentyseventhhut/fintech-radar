# Revolut tops UK bank fraud complaints for third year

> [!info] 2025-10-03 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Revolut receives more fraud complaints than any other bank for the third year running. Revolut customers have submitted a total of 3,208 fraud complaints to the Financial Ombudsman Service as of 2025 to date, according to a Freedom of Information request.
