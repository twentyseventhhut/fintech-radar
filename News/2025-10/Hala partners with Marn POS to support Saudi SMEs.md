## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇦 Hala partners with Marn POS to support SMEs in KSA with smarter, more efficient payment solutions. This integration streamlines checkout, offers more payment options, enhances security, reduces errors, and helps merchants manage inventory and sales more effectively, saving time and driving business growth.
