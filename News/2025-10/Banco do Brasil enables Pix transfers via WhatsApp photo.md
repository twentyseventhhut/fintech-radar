# Banco do Brasil enables Pix transfers via WhatsApp photo

> [!info] 2025-10-12 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 The Central Bank of Brasillaunched a new feature that allows customers to make Pix transfers by sending a photo via WhatsApp. Using AI and optical character recognition (OCR), the system automatically identifies Pix keys and payment amounts from handwritten notes, printed materials or receipts.
