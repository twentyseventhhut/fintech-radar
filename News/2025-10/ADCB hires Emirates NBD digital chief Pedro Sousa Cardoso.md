Pedro Sousa Cardoso has announced his departure from Emirates NBD after serving as Chief Digital Officer for three and a half years.
During his tenure, he led the bank’s digital transformation efforts, introducing a new agile operating model and overseeing initiatives aimed at strengthening market position.
 “Our digital transformation was about more than technology – it was about people, strategy, and execution,” 
said Sousa Cardoso.
 “We built a strong foundation for digital growth, improved automation and customer engagement, and delivered measurable value across multiple markets, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, London, Singapore, and India.” 
His time at Emirates NBD saw the expansion of digital initiatives across the organisation, including enhancements to Emirates Islamic and Liv Digital Bank.
Sousa Cardoso expressed gratitude to the leadership team, colleagues, and partners, adding,
 “I am proud of what we have accomplished and look forward to staying engaged in the digital and financial sectors.” 
His departure marks a transition for Emirates NBD as the bank continues its focus on digital development.
