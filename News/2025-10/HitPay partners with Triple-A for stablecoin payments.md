HitPay has partnered with Triple-A to enable stablecoin payments for over 20,000 businesses in Singapore.

HitPay is a Singapore-based payments platform for small and medium-sized businesses, while Triple-A provides cryptocurrency payment solutions and is also based in Singapore.

Both companies are regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) as major payment institutions.

The partnership allows merchants to accept stablecoins for ecommerce and in-store transactions, with instant conversion to fiat currency.

This is aimed at reducing exposure to cryptocurrency volatility and providing compliance with local regulations.

The new feature can be activated by merchants within minutes, according to the companies.

Recent industry data cited by HitPay shows growing interest in cryptocurrency payments among Singapore businesses, with Chainalysis reporting that local merchant services received nearly US$1 billion in cryptocurrency during Q2 2024.
