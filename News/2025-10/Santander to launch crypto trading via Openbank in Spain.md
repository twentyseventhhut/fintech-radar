# Santander to launch crypto trading via Openbank in Spain

> [!info] 2025-10-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇸 Santander to launch crypto trading via Openbank in Spain. The service, which is already available in Germany, will launch in the coming weeks and allow customers to invest in cryptoassets starting from just 1 euro. The service will operate under the European Union’s MiCA regulation, which came into full effect earlier this year.
