## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇪 Creditstar announces new funding facility from Kilde, reportedly increasing the total to $20 million. The facility will now aim to support loan-book growth across Creditstar’s eight European markets and support the group’s funding-diversification strategy, which includes bonds, institutional partnerships, and retail investment offerings.
