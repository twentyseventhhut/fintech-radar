[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇷 Ualá lays off 135 employees in the region: 80% of the cuts affect Argentina. The company explained that the measure is a response to the search for greater regional efficiency and the result of task automation. The layoffs represent 8% of the company's total workforce, which operates in Argentina, Mexico, and Colombia.
