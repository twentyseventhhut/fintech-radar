The Crypto Wealth Report 2025
 
 An essential publication for digital asset investors and those tracking investment migration — from global citizens to wealth managers and private bankers. Worldwide Crypto Wealth Statistics
The wealth stats in the Crypto Wealth Report 2025 are based on in-house wealth tier models, which benchmark billionaires, centi-millionaires, and other high-net-worth individuals using a progressive Lorenz curve distribution, as well as open-source information on large crypto holdings from major crypto platforms such as CoinMarketCap, Binance, BscScan, and Etherscan.
