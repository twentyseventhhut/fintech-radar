# OpenAI launches ChatGPT in Brazil and partners with Nubank

> [!info] 2025-10-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 OpenAI launches ChatGPT in Brazil and partners with Nubank. The agreement provides special benefits for the digital bank's customers: those with a regular Nubank account will receive a free month of subscription, Nubank+ users will receive three months, and Ultravioleta customers will receive a full year of ChatGPT Go at no additional cost.
