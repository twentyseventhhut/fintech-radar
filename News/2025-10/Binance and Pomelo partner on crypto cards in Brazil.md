## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Binance and Pomelo have formed a strategic partnership in Brazil, combining Pomelo’s infrastructure technology with the crypto exchange to launch innovative crypto-powered cards. This collaboration strengthens the connection between the crypto ecosystem and traditional finance, delivering regulated, scalable solutions ready for regional expansion across Latin America.
