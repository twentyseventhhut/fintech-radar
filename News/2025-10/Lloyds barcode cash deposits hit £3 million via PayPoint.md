# Lloyds’ barcode cash paying-in app feature hits £3 million in deposits using PayPoint

Lloyds

Published on: 06 October 2025

2 min read

*   Lloyds latest app feature ‘barcode cash deposit’ has been used to pay in over £3 million at PayPoint locations since its recent launch.
*   Lloyds customers can now also use their debit card to pay-in cash at participating PayPoint locations.
*   Deposits are quick and secure, and the money appears in accounts within minutes.
