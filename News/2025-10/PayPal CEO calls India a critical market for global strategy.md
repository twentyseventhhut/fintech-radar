# PayPal CEO calls India a critical market for global strategy

> [!info] 2025-10-08 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 PayPal’s focus will remain on connecting India to the world rather than competing in the domestic payments market, Alex Chriss, CEO, said. He said India is playing a defining role in the global FinTech ecosystem, from remittances to commerce to technology, calling it a “critical market” for the company’s international strategy.
