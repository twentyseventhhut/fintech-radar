# Punjab National Bank Q2 net profit rises 14% to Rs 4,904 crore

> [!info] 2025-10-20 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Punjab National Bank (PNB), state-owned lender, raised net profit by 14% to INR 4,904cr ($557M) in Q2 2026, with improved asset quality, reduced Non-Performing Assets (NPA), and strong digital transaction growth.
