# Shawbrook plans London IPO at ~£2B valuation

> [!info] 2025-10-07 · 5 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Shawbrook shares jump after London's biggest IPO in two years. The bank sought to raise around 50 million pounds by selling new shares, while its two private equity owners expected to raise around 298 million pounds from the deal, according to its prospectus.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Shawbrook looks to raise over £50M in November IPO. The company is targeting a listing in early November 2025 and expects to raise £50m in net proceeds through new shares. The IPO will be offered to qualified institutional buyers internationally and to retail investors resident in the United Kingdom through a partner network with RetailBook.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Shawbrook turns to Ardea and Goldman Sachs as IPO plans take off. Digital bank Shawbrook has confirmed it plans to float in London in a boost to the City. The lender said it intends to publish a registration document on 6 October for a listing on the London Stock Exchange’s main market.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Specialist lender Shawbrook is looking at listing on LSE in November at a £2bn valuation.

[This Week in Fintech] Shawbrookplans a London IPO in 2025 to fund growth and boost its profile , targeting a potential valuation at around £2 billion (~$2.7 billion).
