## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇸 New era of instant Payments. Are you ready? by PagoNxt. In Episode 3 of PayTalks by PagoNxt, Susana Delgado (Swift), David Hurtado (Microsoft), Mattia Gamberoni (Stripe), and Francisco Huerta (PagoNxt) discuss how instant payments are reshaping global commerce. They explore the shift in customer expectations, the role of AI in security and efficiency, and the need to offer preferred payment methods in a world where speed is no longer optional.
