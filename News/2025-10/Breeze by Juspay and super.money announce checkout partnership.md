## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Breeze by Juspay and super.money announces checkout partnership to serve 500M+ shoppers. With this, brands can tap into a high-intent, high-conversion audience with frictionless checkout flows, seamless reward experiences, and a logistics optimisation suite.

[TechCrunch — Fintech] The partnership, unaccompanied by press releases or social media posts and only mentioned in a blog post on Breeze’s seemingly un-indexed website, comes as Juspay works to rebuild momentum after major payment companies earlier this year sought to reduce how much they work with third-party payment orchestrators.

By the end of that conversation, we knew something magical was about to happen.

**Today, we're announcing SuperMoney Breeze - where India's fastest checkout meets India's largest e-commerce intelligence.**

## When Innovation Meets Scale

Here's the thing about partnerships: most of them are just fancy press releases. But when Breeze's D2C Commerce technology meets Flipkart's decade of mastery, something different happens.

You get SuperMoney Breeze.

Think of it as your regular Breeze checkout, but with superpowers from India's biggest e-commerce ecosystem. We're not just talking about incremental improvements - we're talking about fundamentally changing what's possible for D2C brands.

## The Right Insight Changes Everything

**SuperMoney Breeze is powered by** insights from **500 million shopper journeys**. Every abandoned cart, every successful purchase, every delivery attempt - distilled into intelligence that now powers your checkout.

**The result? Address pre-fill that works 98% of the time.**

No more "Please enter your complete address" friction. No more customers abandoning carts because typing on mobile is painful. Your customers' addresses appear like magic, refreshed with real delivery signals from millions of successful orders.

But that's just the beginning.

## RTO Intelligence That Actually Works

Let's be honest - RTO prediction has been the holy grail of D2C for years. Everyone promises it. Few deliver.

SuperMoney Breeze is different. It's powered by 5+ million daily orders across every pincode in India. Tier 1 cities? Obviously. But Tier 2 and Tier 3? That's where the real magic happens.

The system doesn't just predict RTO risk - it acts on it. Automatic COD restrictions for risky orders. Token payments for suspicious pincodes. Convenience fees that actually discourage bad actors while keeping genuine customers happy.

## Your Brand, Amplified Across India

Here's where things get really interesting. SuperMoney Breeze doesn't just optimize your checkout - it amplifies your reach.

Scratch card rewards on Thank You pages that customers actually want to use. Visibility across Flipkart's media network. SuperCoins redemption at full 1:1 value, turning Flipkart's loyalty ecosystem into your acquisition funnel.

It's like having Flipkart's marketing muscle working for your D2C brand, without diluting your brand identity.

## Logistics That Just Works

Remember when shipping was a nightmare? Multiple partners, inconsistent pricing, random failures that killed customer trust?

SuperMoney Breeze integrates seamlessly with eKart and 3P logistics partners. Competitive pricing that makes your CFO smile. Tracking that actually tracks. Deliveries that actually deliver.

**Because a great checkout experience doesn't end at payment confirmation.**

## Why This Changes Everything

Most checkout solutions optimize for conversion. SuperMoney Breeze optimizes for lifetime value.

Better address data means fewer failed deliveries. Smarter RTO prediction means healthier unit economics. Integrated logistics means happier customers. Flipkart's ecosystem means organic growth opportunities you never knew existed.

**This isn't just better checkout. This is your unfair advantage.**
