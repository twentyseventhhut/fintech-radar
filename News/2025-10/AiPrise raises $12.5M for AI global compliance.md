Key Takeaways










Our Series A
We're witnessing a powerful convergence of events. Companies are expanding globally at a breathtaking pace, and money now moves across borders faster than ever before. The momentum in fintech is electric, fueled by landmark IPOs, foundational stablecoin innovations, and growing regulatory clarity. Tying it all together, the advent of AI is supercharging every stack with a fresh wave of innovation.
Fintech is so back, and it’s more global than ever.
The biggest bottleneck is still onboarding. Especially for businesses where the process can drag on for weeks. Expanding across borders makes things even tougher. Each country has its own rules and requirements, systems rarely talk to each other, and fraudsters are evolving faster than the safeguards. From deepfake identities to entire fake companies, bad actors are finding new ways to slip through, leaving businesses struggling to keep pace.
 This changes today. 
AiPrise is proud to announce our $12.5M Series A led by Headline with participation from Sixthirty and Correlation to revolutionize global compliance. Trusted by 6 publicly traded companies including Fortune 500 companies, we have built a product loved by compliance teams from around the world. This milestone would not have been possible without the early belief of our seed investors - YCombinator , Okta , TwentyTwo , Wedbush and Restive .
For far too long, Compliance Officers have been tasked with the critical responsibility of finding the "needle in a haystack" without the proper tools. The result is a significant amount of their time still being spent on inefficient spreadsheets and disconnected systems.
At AiPrise, we are building the world’s first AI-powered Global Compliance Platform.
There are 3 key ingredients:
 AI - AI Agents to automate the entire compliance lifecycle. Whether it's screening merchant risk, reviewing AML alerts, verifying addresses, or conducting in-depth EDD, we have an agent for the job. The result? Your team can handle 9x the workload without expanding.
 DATA - Stop googling SMBs and start making decisions with confidence. AiPrise provides instant access to real-time data from official global registries, offering an unprecedented 800+ data points on businesses and 100+ on individuals.
 PLATFORM - Bring clarity to complexity. A case management tool to consolidate all compliance activities into a single, visual dashboard. Effortlessly track, audit, and manage customer onboarding from start to finish.
Don't just take our word for it. Our exponential growth is driven by the success of our customers. Over 150 industry leaders trust AiPrise to power their global compliance, from fintech pioneers to the world's largest tech companies.
We're proud to work with leaders like Nium , DLocal , Meta , Pingpong , Grey , Nsave , Bridge , and many more.
 The future of Compliance is agentic and full-stack. This is just the beginning. Lots more to come.
Onwards and Upwards 🚀
Chaitanya & Rushabh
