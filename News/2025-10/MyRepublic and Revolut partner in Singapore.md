MyRepublic, a leading telecommunications provider in Singapore, and fintech super-app Revolut have launched an all-in-one partnership delivering connectivity, finance, travel, and lifestyle services at affordable rates. 
 SINGAPORE , Oct. 9, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- MyRepublic , one of Singapore's leading telecommunications providers, and global fintech super-app Revolut have announced a new partnership aimed at mobile-first digital natives.
With the new MyRepublic™ MOBILE x Revolut plan, users enjoy up to 2 months off their 4G/5G MyRepublic Mobile plan bundled with a 3-month Revolut Premium Trial . This special offer is now stackable with MyRepublic's other promotional mobile plans. All plans are available in SIM and eSIM formats with unlimited data*, free caller ID & incoming calls, and no-contract.

 
 
There are no hidden fees or lock-ins, delivering high-value telco, fintech, travel, and lifestyle services to users at no upfront cost.
The collaboration curates must-have applications and services for the digitally-driven life, particularly for travellers. It provides not just one of the best SIM-only deals in Singapore from just S$9.90 per month, but a host of other travel perks and benefits like international data roaming to popular destinations like Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, and South Korea; no overseas ATM withdrawal fees up to S$700/month; no FX fees up to S$15,000/month; discounted airport lounge access ; and global travel insurance access** via Revolut partners.
Lifestyle benefits for users include free subscriptions to Freeletics, Tinder Plus, NordVPN Standard, Sleep Cycle Premium, and Picsart Pro under the Revolut Premium Trial.
"This is how MyRepublic redefines telco value—by partnering with forward-thinking brands like Revolut to give our customers something extra." Shivendra Singh, Managing Director for Mobile Business
The partnership reinforces MyRepublic's position as a provider of affordable, high value-for-money 4G/5G mobile plans in Singapore. It's the perfect offer for users seeking transparent, no-contract, low-risk mobile offers with integrated travel and lifestyle benefits.
To take advantage of this exclusive offer, customers can sign up for a 4G/5G mobile plan with MyRepublic using the promo code " JUSTRYREVOLUT " .
For more information, please visit https://myrepublic.net/sg/revolut/

 About MyRepublic 
Founded in Singapore in 2011, MyRepublic is a telecommunications company primarily known for providing high speed mobile services. Headquartered in Singapore, it currently operates in Singapore and New Zealand and licences its platform to an operator in Brunei. MyRepublic focuses on offering competitive packages to both consumers and businesses, with a strong emphasis on digital transformation within the telecommunications sector. It is a premium mobile provider known for speed, reliability, and customer-first innovation in Singapore.
