# India to launch RBI-backed digital currency, minister says

> [!info] 2025-10-08 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 India to launch digital currency. India is set to launch its own RBI-backed digital currency, aiming for simpler, faster, and traceable transactions while reducing paper use. Union Minister emphasized that the nation discourages cryptocurrencies lacking sovereign or asset backing, imposing heavy taxes on them due to inherent risks and lack of accountability.
