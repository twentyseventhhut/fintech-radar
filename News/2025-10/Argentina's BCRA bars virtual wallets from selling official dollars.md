# Argentina's BCRA bars virtual wallets from selling official dollars

> [!info] 2025-10-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇦🇷 The Central Bank of Argentina (BCRA) announced that virtual wallets, including Mercado Pago and Cocos Capital, will no longer be allowed to sell official U.S. dollars. The decision follows findings that these companies were conducting currency exchange operations without authorization, with the BCRA clarifying that only banks and licensed exchange houses are permitted to sell official dollars.
