# Robinhood launches futures trading for UK customers

> [!info] 2025-10-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Robinhood launches futures trading for UK customers. It provides customers with the ability to trade index, energy, metals, and foreign exchange futures directly through the Robinhood app and on Robinhood Legend. Customers can now access more than 40 CME Group futures products with low fees, seamless mobile and desktop access, and one-tap ladder execution.
