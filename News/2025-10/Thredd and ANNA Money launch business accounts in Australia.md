SYDNEY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Thredd, the leading next-generation global payments processor, today announced it is powering ANNA Money’s business accounts in Australia. The collaboration delivers instant virtual cards and physical commercial debit cards, with the platform engineered for secure wallet support - so customers can add cards to leading mobile wallets (when available), all backed by Thredd’s real-time authorisation and settlement.

> ANNA Money is investing meaningfully in scaling its Australian operations, with Thredd providing the platform reliability and flexibility needed to accelerate product development and long-term growth.
> 
> Share

The relationship builds on Thredd’s work with ANNA in Australia; the company is consolidating their recently launched Business Account, ANNAOne, onto Thredd’s cloud platform, providing ANNA with reliability, scale and speed to market as it expands in Australia.

ANNA’s Business Account goes beyond payments, giving business owners integrated bookkeeping and tax support, including automated receipt capture and categorisation, and BAS/IAS preparation and filing. By simplifying money admin, ANNA helps small businesses focus on growth rather than paperwork.

“We’re proud to work with ANNA Money Australia to support its vision for small businesses,” said Damien Gough, Head of Asia Pacific at Thredd. “By combining Thredd’s cloud-first processing platform with ANNA’s integrated expense management and tax automation, we’re enabling a smarter, simpler way for entrepreneurs to manage money.”

“At ANNA, we’re building a platform that makes business finances feel effortless,” said Ryan Edwards-Pritchard, CEO, ANNA Money Australia. “Thredd gives us the rails to scale, powering instant virtual cards today and add-to-wallet capabilities as certifications complete. Our focus is making money admin invisible, with ANNA’s AI assistant matching receipts, reconciling spend, drafting BAS and IAS, and flagging anomalies before a human needs to ask. The goal is simple: help small business owners feel supported, not alone.”

ANNA Money is investing meaningfully in scaling its Australian operations, with Thredd providing the platform reliability and flexibility needed to accelerate product development and long-term growth.

**About ANNA Money Australia**

ANNA is a financial super-app for small businesses. We help business owners start and run their companies with tools for formations, payments, bookkeeping and tax - including automated receipt capture and BAS/IAS filing - so money admin feels simple and supported. ANNA operates in the UK and Australia with the vision to be the first name business owners think of when they get started or want to grow. Learn more at annamoney.au

Thredd is the trusted next-generation payments processing partner for innovators looking to modernise their payments offerings worldwide. We process billions of debit, prepaid and credit transactions annually, serving over 100 fintechs, digital banks, and embedded finance providers, from consumer to corporate, based across 47 countries.
