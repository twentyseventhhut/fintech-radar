# Baltimore sues MoneyLion over predatory loan practices

> [!info] 2025-10-09 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Baltimore sues MoneyLion over alleged predatory loan practices. The complaint, filed by the city's Department of Law, claims that the company violated Baltimore's Consumer Protection Ordinance by promoting small, short-term "Instacash Advances" with deceptive terms.

[This Week in Fintech] The City of Baltimore accused fintech MoneyLion of misleading consumers and making loans without a lending license, with a key issue being the use of recommended tips.
