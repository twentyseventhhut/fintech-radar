# vobapay integrates Mastercard Open Finance for A2A payments

> [!info] 2025-10-08 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 vobapay integrates Mastercard Open Finance to enable A2A payments. As part of this collaboration, vobapay will integrate Mastercard’s Open Banking technology into its platform. This will enable companies working with vobapay to offer their customers secure and fast account-to-account (A2A) payments directly at checkout.
