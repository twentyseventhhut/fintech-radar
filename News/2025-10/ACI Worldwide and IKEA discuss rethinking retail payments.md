[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🎤 Rethinking Payments with ACI Worldwide and IKEA. In a dedicated session, Paul Skeldon speaks with Anna Pulante, Global Payments Manager at IKEA, and Dan Coates, Global Omnichannel Payments Solution Evangelist at ACI Worldwide, to explore IKEA’s omnichannel payments approach and the evolving retail landscape.
