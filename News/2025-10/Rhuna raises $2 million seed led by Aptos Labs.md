# Rhuna raises $2 million seed led by Aptos Labs

> [!info] 2025-10-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇷🇴 Rhuna raises $2M seed round led by Aptos Labs to build stablecoin payment infrastructure for entertainment. The new funding will strengthen Rhuna’s payments and settlement rails, expand organizer tooling and integrations (including POS, ticketing, and mobility partners), and accelerate the launch of Rhuna’s consumer app, which brings discovery, access, and wallet-native checkout into one seamless experience.
