# Leeds-based fintech Nochex acquired by PAYSTRAX

## PAYSTRAX will retain the Nochex team, name and brand in the UK

Online payment service provider Nochex has been acquired by PAYSTRAX, a company that specialises in supporting businesses with complex payment needs.

Established in Vilnius in Lithuania and with significant operations in Brighton, the acquisition will allow PAYSTRAX to diversify its portfolio in the UK where the company had its first customers.

Founded in 2001, Nochex is one of the UK’s first online payment service providers and is licensed as an Electronic Money Institution (EMI) by the FCA. The company operates as a payment facilitator and payment service provider, offering a payment gateway orchestration platform.

“Over the years, Nochex has focused on delivering reliable payment solutions and building lasting trust with our merchants,” says Nick Fox, managing director at Nochex.

“Joining PAYSTRAX marks an exciting new phase where we can continue that commitment while unlocking new opportunities for innovation and service. It’s a strong fit for our people and our partners, and I look forward to what we’ll achieve together.”

PAYSTRAX works with more than 800 merchants and retailers across the UK and Europe and intends to retain the Nochex name and brand in the UK, along with the existing team.

“While we continue to grow through our own organic efforts, part of our long-term strategy is to expand externally as well by identifying the right opportunities to complement our business,” says Johannes Kolbeinsson, CEO at PAYSTRAX.

“Nochex has a strong brand heritage, a proven reputation with merchants, and a history of innovation as one of the first online payment service providers in the UK, and we look forward to bringing this legacy into PAYSTRAX and building on it for the future.”

We’re excited to share that PAYSTRAX has officially acquired UK-based fintech company Nochex – one of the UK’s first online payment service providers.   
 This marks an important milestone in our journey to strengthen PAYSTRAX’s presence in the UK and continue building one of the most reliable payment processing networks across Europe.   
 Why Nochex?   
 Although PAYSTRAX was founded in Lithuania, the UK has always been close to our story. Our first customers were based here, and in 2023, we received our UK Payment Institution Licence from the FCA.   
 Today, we work with over 800 merchants and retailers across the UK and Europe, helping them reduce friction, improve acceptance, and embrace digital payments more efficiently.   
 Nochex has been around since 2001 and was one of the UK’s very first online payment service providers. Licensed by the FCA as an Electronic Money Institution, they’ve earned the trust of thousands of merchants across the country.   
 By joining forces, PAYSTRAX and Nochex combine decades of experience in payments. Together, we’ll be able to bring even more value to UK businesses: faster and smoother transactions as well as a wider range of payment options for merchants of all sizes (or verticals).   
 But what does this mean?   
 We’re keeping what already works. The Nochex brand and team will remain in place, continuing to operate under the same trusted name UK merchants already know. Our goal is to build on Nochex’s strong foundation while leveraging PAYSTRAX’s acquiring expertise, technology, and reach across Europe.   
 This is what our co-founder & CEO Johannes Ingi Kolbeinsson said regarding the acquisition:   
 “While we continue to grow through our own organic efforts, part of our long-term strategy is to expand externally as well, by identifying the right opportunities to complement our business. Nochex has a strong brand heritage, a proven reputation with merchants, and a history of innovation as one of the first online payment service providers in the UK, and we look forward to bringing this legacy into PAYSTRAX and building on it for the future.”   
 From the Nochex side, Managing Director Nick Fox added:   
 “Over the years, Nochex has focused on delivering reliable payment solutions and building lasting trust with our merchants. Joining PAYSTRAX marks an exciting new phase where we can continue that commitment while unlocking new opportunities for innovation and service. It’s a strong fit for our people and our partners, and I look forward to what we’ll achieve together.”   
 So what’s next?   
 We’re happy to welcome the Nochex team into the PAYSTRAX family and look forward to what we’ll achieve together. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue expanding and innovating to make payments simpler, faster and more reliable for merchants everywhere!
