HONG KONG , Sept. 30, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- 3 World Global today announced it has raised nearly USD 5 million across its seed and angel rounds within three months of founding. The capital—backed by a mix of strategic and institutional investors—will be used to expand the company's global compliance footprint, advance payment technology R&D, scale its team, and deepen ecosystem partnerships. The funding strengthens 3 World Global's position as a next-generation provider of global financial infrastructure.

 "Our mission is to build seamless, efficient, and compliant global payment and financial infrastructure," said Kit Chu, Co‑Founder and CFO of 3 World Global. "This financing marks a key milestone in our international expansion. By partnering closely with our investors and ecosystem, we will enable enterprises to embed banking‑grade financial services at scale—redefining cross‑border payments and asset management."
3 World Global delivers one-stop embedded financial solutions for innovative enterprises, anchored by three core pillars:
Globally licensed compliance architecture
API-driven, modular technology platform
Multi-asset management and settlement network
At the foundation is a robust cross-border compliance capability designed to provide stable, reliable support for international operations.
"Many fintech offerings still struggle with regional silos, compliance gaps, and fragmented technology—hindering true global scalability," Chu added. "From day one, we have put compliance at the core. We have already secured licenses and qualifications in key markets across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and we are committed to delivering embedded financial products that meet international regulatory standards and support sustainable global growth."
The company provides end-to-end enterprise services—from compliance architecture design and API integration to global operational support. To further catalyze ecosystem collaboration, 3 World Global will launch a Global Partner Program, offering partners expanded access to technology resources, go-to-market support, and capital connections.
For product updates and regional developments, please visit https://www.3worldglobal.com/ or follow upcoming announcements.
 About 3 World Global 
3 World Global is a leading fintech company providing enterprises worldwide with payment, compliance, and asset management solutions through embedded financial infrastructure. Built on its global multi-license compliance network, API-driven technology platform, and multi-asset management and settlement capabilities, 3 World Global enables seamless and efficient financial services for crypto-native businesses, multinational organizations, high-volatility markets, and select high-net-worth sectors. Guided by its core principles of compliance-first, technology-driven innovation, and global connectivity, 3 World Global is the ideal partner for enterprises building the next generation of financial products.
The founding team of 3 World Global brings deep industry expertise and a unique cross-disciplinary perspective. Co-Founder & CFO Kit previously held positions at leading investment banks including Merrill Lynch, J.P. Morgan, and UBS, as well as at top private equity firm Hermitage Capital, where he successfully invested in multiple publicly listed technology companies and global unicorns. He also led strategic resource integration for the NYSE-listed SPAC Copley Acquisition Corp as a sponsor. Together, the team established 3 World Global with the mission of empowering enterprises worldwide to break through the boundaries of traditional finance and achieve freer, more connected business growth through innovative financial infrastructure.
