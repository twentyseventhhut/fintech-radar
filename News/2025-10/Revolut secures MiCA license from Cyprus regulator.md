# Revolut secures MiCA license from Cyprus regulator

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇾 Revolut has secured a MiCA licence from the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission, allowing it to expand its crypto services across the European Union. The licence enables the UK-based challenger bank to offer and market crypto products in 30 countries within the European Economic Area.
