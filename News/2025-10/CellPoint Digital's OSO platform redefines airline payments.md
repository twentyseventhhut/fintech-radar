The One Source for Travel Payment Orchestration
 The control layer for modern travel payments 
 CellPoint Digital’s OSO (One Source Orchestration) Platform is the only travel-native solution that unifies payment flows across all channels, partners, and geographies. From direct bookings and ancillaries to embedded offers and post-booking upgrades, OSO transforms payments into a strategic lever for profitability.
 Built for Tier One travel brands 
Travel payments are uniquely complex – fragmented by region, channel, system, and provider. OSO simplifies this complexity by centralising your payment infrastructure into one intelligent, purpose-built control layer.
Designed for global scale, OSO handles the highest volumes with enterprise-grade performance, security, and flexibility. It is trusted by the world’s most demanding brands to support hyper-growth, legacy migration, and real-time optimisation across every booking and payment channel.
Supports 168+ payment methods including card networks and local APMs
Connects to 220+ PSPs and acquirers with intelligent routing and failover
Operates across 100+ currencies in over 70 countries
Built on Active-Active infrastructure with Single Tenancy environments for full control
 Orchestrate every payment across the OOSD journey 
Most payment providers focus on authorisation. OSO goes further – orchestrating every transaction with intelligence, redundancy, and logic tailored to your business objectives.
From the moment a product is offered to final settlement, OSO dynamically routes payments to maximise conversion and reduce cost. It automatically reroutes failed payments, detects fraud in real-time, and manages complex multi-party settlements across bundled orders.
Intelligent routing based on issuer, geography, and cost
Local acquirer routing to reduce cross-border fees and improve acceptance
Auto-failover across PSPs for always-on performance
Real-time fraud decisioning with adaptive risk logic
Native settlement splits for multi-party Offer-Order bundles
 Embedded intelligence. Unified Control. 
The true power of orchestration lies in visibility. OSO gives travel merchants a real-time view into every transaction – surfacing hidden costs, missed revenue, and performance gaps across all methods, markets, and providers.
With embedded analytics and benchmarking tools, OSO replaces fragmented spreadsheets and dashboards with a single source of truth for operational and commercial decision-making. Whether you’re optimising for margin, speed, or compliance – OSO gives you the insight to act with precision.
Unified analytics across issuers, acquirers, methods, and regions
Performance benchmarking to identify opportunities and risks
Decline recovery logic, surcharge tracking, fraud bypass
APIs for automated reporting, reconciliation, and workflows
 Enterprise Infrastructure, Global Compliance 
At global scale, infrastructure matters. OSO is built for high availability, low latency, and maximum regulatory compliance – across every region you operate in.
Whether you’re updating PSPs in production or navigating data sovereignty laws in new markets, OSO provides the control and confidence to scale without risk. Its cloud-native architecture ensures zero-downtime deployments, seamless blue-green rollouts, and complete jurisdictional coverage.
Active-Active deployments to eliminate single points of failure
Blue-Green deployments for zero-downtime upgrades
Local data residency zones to meet country-specific regulations
Fully compliant with PCI-DSS, GDPR, PSD2, LGPD and more
 Built for airlines, hotels and travel merchants 
Every travel business has a different starting point – legacy PSS, multi-channel distribution, fragmented acquiring setups. OSO meets you where you are and accelerates where you need to go.
Its modular architecture supports diverse business models across airlines, hotel groups, mobility providers, OTAs, and aggregators. Whether you’re re-platforming, expanding to new markets, or launching new products – OSO adapts to your needs.
For Airlines: Full OOSD support, including PSS and NDC alignment
For Hotels: Embedded commerce with partner and channel flexibility
For OTAs and Retailers: Real-time integration with GDS, DMC, and partner ecosystems
