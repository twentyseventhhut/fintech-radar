St. Cloud, Minn.-based Stearns Bank is acquiring Centerville, S.D.-based One American Bank, according to a Sept. 26 social media post from Performance Trust Capital Partners.

Financial details of the acquisition have not been disclosed.

Based 40 miles south of Sioux Falls, One American Bank had $385 million in assets, $283.5 million in net loans and leases and $259.5 million in deposits. As of June 30, Stearns Bank had $3.5 billion in assets, $2.8 billion in deposits and $3 billion in net loans and leases.
