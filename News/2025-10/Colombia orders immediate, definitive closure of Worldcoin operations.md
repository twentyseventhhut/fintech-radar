[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Colombia orders immediate, definitive closure of Worldcoin operations. The SIC investigation determined that this quid pro quo, offering money for biometric data, invalidated the legality of the consent obtained. The agency found that the economic incentive “conditioned the will” of data holders, preventing them from making a “free, spontaneous, or voluntary” decision as required by Colombian law.
