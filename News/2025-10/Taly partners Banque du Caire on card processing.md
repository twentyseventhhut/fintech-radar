 Banque du Caire   has announced a new strategic partnership with taly For Digital Payments, one of Egypt’s leading digital payments enablers, marking a major milestone in its ongoing journey toward digital innovation and financial inclusion.
Building on a successful collaboration that has spanned the past two years, the expanded partnership aims to advance Banque du Caire’s credit card processing operations, introducing more secure, seamless, and innovative payment solutions for customers across Egypt. The agreement underscores both organizations’ shared vision of accelerating Egypt’s digital transformation and strengthening the country’s rapidly evolving fintech ecosystem.
Banque du Caire stated that this milestone reflects its commitment to driving digital finance, enhancing operational efficiency, and delivering world-class digital experiences. By leveraging taly’s advanced payment technologies and expertise, the bank aims to offer smoother, faster, and more reliable card services that meet the growing needs of modern consumers.
In line with Egypt’s national strategy for financial inclusion, the collaboration reinforces Banque du Caire’s position as a leading innovator in digital banking. The bank emphasized that partnerships like this are key to broadening access to digital financial services, supporting economic growth, and helping more individuals and businesses participate in the formal financial system.
This latest development signals a strong step forward in Banque du Caire’s mission to integrate advanced technology into its core banking operations, reaffirming its role as a catalyst in Egypt’s digital economy transformation.
