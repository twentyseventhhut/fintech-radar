[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 StraitsX raises US$10 million from UQPAY to fuel Asia stablecoin expansion. The new funding will enable StraitsX to expand its stablecoin-to-fiat capabilities, broaden cross-border use cases, and grow its network of enterprises and institutions. The company said it aims to advance programmable, real-time settlement solutions.
