# UBS merges mobile banking and Circle One into wealth app

> [!info] 2025-10-24 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇭 UBS merges mobile banking and Circle One into a unified wealth app. The integration combines trading, portfolio management, and investment insights into a single platform designed to enhance client accessibility and convenience. The move reflects UBS’s broader push to align its digital offerings with changing client behaviour.
