# Binance pays $283 million, denies causing crypto crash

> [!info] 2025-10-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Binance pays $283,000,000 reimbursement to customers, denies causing the bitcoin and crypto market crash. Binance says it will also compensate users affected by delays in internal transfers and Earn product redemptions. The exchange is also pledging to implement new optimizations in an ongoing effort to continuously improve system performance and risk controls.
