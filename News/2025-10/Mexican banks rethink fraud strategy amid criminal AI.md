# Mexican banks rethink fraud strategy amid criminal AI

> [!info] 2025-10-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇲🇽 In response to fraud, banks rethink strategy in the face of criminal AI. By 2025, at least 50% of financial frauds are expected to be linked to AI attacks, according to estimates by ACI Worldwide. Given this scenario, Cleber Martins, the company's Head of Payments Intelligence and Risk Solutions, warned that banks will have to rethink their strategy to stay one step ahead of cybercriminals.
