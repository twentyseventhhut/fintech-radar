Smart Web2.0 + Web3.0 trading app marks new era for Asian digital finance 
 HONG KONG , Oct. 2, 2025 /PRNewswire/ --  Eddid Financial ( "the Group") announced today that its flagship trading app, Eddid ONE, has become the first TradFi + Web 3.0 trading APP in Hong Kong to offer subscription and redemption service for tokenised Real World Assets ("RWA") . The new feature is now available to professional investors, who can subscribe and redeem various RWA products directly through the app.
 Stablecoin Support for RWA Transactions  
The enhanced Eddid ONE enables professional investors to subscribe and redeem RWA products, and trade stocks, futures, forex, funds, cryptocurrencies, as well as stablecoins, all within a single APP. Such RWA products will support subscription and redemption using stablecoins such as USDT, significantly improving capital efficiency and cross-market flexibility. This addresses a key pain point for investors who previously needed to manage traditional securities and digital assets across multiple platforms, delivering a streamlined, all-in-one solution. 
 Robust Digital Finance Licensing Framework 
Eddid Securities and Futures, a subsidiary of the Group, is one of the first brokerages in Hong Kong to receive upgraded licenses for digital asset operations. The firm recently received its another round of license uplift, with the SFC further expanding its Type 1 and Type 9 licenses to authorize distribution of tokenised securities and RWA products, as well as management of investment funds with up to 100% digital asset holdings. The Group is among the few financial institutions holding SFC Type 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 9 regulated activities licenses as well as financial licenses across multiple global jurisdictions. This comprehensive licensing portfolio provides a solid regulatory foundation for Eddid ONE's RWA trading capabilities, enabling professional investors to safely and efficiently capture Web3.0 opportunities.
 Leading RWA Expertise and Industry Research 
Eddid Financial has assembled the region's top digital finance talent. Beyond its virtual asset team, the investment banking, asset management, finance, and operations teams have all been deeply engaged in the digital finance sector for long time, seamlessly combining extensive traditional finance experience with cutting-edge digital technology and precise market insights. The Group's research team consistently publishes influential in-depth research reports, earning high market recognition. Last month, the team published "RWA Core Guide: From Fundamental Theory to Global Practice," systematically analyzing RWA core architecture, global case studies, and key regulatory pathways, providing investors with comprehensive professional guidance.
 Global Network Advantage Building International RWA Ecosystem 
With professional teams in Hong Kong SAR , Singapore, Japan, the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom, the Group leverages its global network advantage to transcend geographical boundaries, connecting quality assets and investors worldwide to build a truly international RWA investment ecosystem. Having successfully obtained a capital markets license in Singapore this year, the Group will use this as a springboard to focus on expanding the Southeast Asian RWA market, bringing more investment opportunities to regional investors.
 Expanding Premium RWA Product Portfolio 
"Eddid ONE becoming Hong Kong's first TradFi + Web 3.0 trading platform to offer subscription and redemption for RWA reflects our long-term commitment to digital asset innovation," said Dr. Jerry Tse, CEO of Eddid Financial. "We've methodically built our capabilities, from cryptocurrency trading and virtual asset ETFs to stablecoin infrastructure and now RWAs. At every step, we've prioritized delivering compliant, reliable services that meet the needs of sophisticated investors."
Dr. Tse added, " We're actively sourcing RWA products with stable cash flows and high-quality underlying assets, including tokenised precious metals, bonds, and real estate. We're also evaluating innovative projects that will provide our clients with direct access to the convergence of multi-trillion-dollar traditional asset markets and blockchain technology."
About Eddid Financial
Anchored in Hong Kong, Eddid Financial is an all-encompassing financial group centered around fintech and dedicated to integrating latest technologies into its enterprise DNA. The diversified businesses of Eddid Financial range from retail to institutional and include but are not limited to fintech, internet finance, wealth management, asset management, investment banking, and digital assets. Eddid Financial is committed to providing one-stop financial services and products to customers through high-quality investment solutions. Members of the Group hold a variety of licenses and memberships across key financial markets. These include Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission (SFC) regulated activities ("RA") licenses for types 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 9; SEHK and HKCC participant (OTP-C broker number: 0974 and 0977), Insurance Broker Company license; Trust or Company Service Provider License in Hong Kong. Additionally, our fully owned U.S. broker-dealer subsidiary, Eddid Securities USA Inc., maintains approved membership with the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA), the National Futures Association (NFA), the Securities Investor Protection Corporation (SIPC), and the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC (NQX), and is a registered with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) in the United States. Our Singapore subsidiary, Eddid Financial Singapore  Pte . Ltd., holds the Capital Markets Services License (License No.: CMS101839) issued by the Monetary Authority of Singapore ( MAS ).
