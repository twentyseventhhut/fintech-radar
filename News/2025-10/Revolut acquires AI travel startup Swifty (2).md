[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 Revolut acquires AI travel agent startup Swifty, incubated at Lufthansa Innovation Hub. This acquisition complements Revolut’s development of its AI financial assistant, building on the lifestyle offering. Swifty's AI agent is designed to autonomously handle the entire travel booking process through a simple conversational interface.
