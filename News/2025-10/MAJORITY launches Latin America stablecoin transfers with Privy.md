MAJORITY launches stablecoin transfers to millions across Latin America with Privy
Behind MAJORITY’s stablecoin wallet: Privy, Circle, and Solana enabling instant, borderless money movement
Debbie Soon
Oct 23, 2025
Today, MAJORITY announced the launch of its stablecoin-enabled wallet, unlocking free, instant, and secure transfers between the U.S., Colombia, and Mexico. Privy is proud to power the underlying wallet infrastructure that makes this possible, giving MAJORITY users the ability to hold, send, and receive USDC seamlessly on Solana.
With Privy, MAJORITY can create and manage onchain wallets directly within its app experience, while users enjoy the benefits of stablecoin speed and transparency, without any crypto complexity. Built on Solana’s high-performance network and integrated with Circle, this rollout enables millions of people to move money across borders instantly and at no cost.
For MAJORITY’s global community, this marks a major leap forward. Everyday banking is connected to real-time blockchain settlement, all within a regulated, user-friendly environment.
Banking for the global migrant community
Founded in Sweden, MAJORITY was created by a diverse team that has spent the last two decades serving global migrant communities across fintech, payments, and telecom. Their mission is simple yet profound: to build better societies by empowering migrants, the world’s most resilient and ambitious people, to thrive financially wherever they are.
Today, 258 million people live outside their country of origin, with nearly 50 million in the US alone. Many of them face barriers to banking access, which MAJORITY addresses by combining checking accounts, remittances, and international calling through a single trusted digital platform. 
Stablecoins is the next part of that journey, and helps to connect MAJORITY customers with family and friends through instant, borderless transfers.
Stablecoins in the real world
This launch marks a pivotal moment for both fintech and blockchain adoption. With MAJORITY, stablecoins are solving a deeply human problem: sending money home.
Privy’s wallet infrastructure enables MAJORITY to embrace blockchain responsibly and at scale, demonstrated through:
 Instant, fee-free transfers between the US, Colombia, and Mexico 
 Onchain wallets managed seamlessly within the MAJORITY app 
 Secure custody and key management handled through Privy’s infrastructure 
 Transparent, compliant settlement on Solana via Circle’s USDC 
Together, these layers bring stablecoins into everyday financial life, and make global money movement feel as natural as sending a text.
What’s next
Starting today, MAJORITY’s Stablecoin Wallet sign-up is available across the Americas. Customers in Colombia and Mexico can now hold, receive, and send USD instantly to users across Latin America and cash out into local currencies at near-zero cost.
In the coming months, MAJORITY plans to expand coverage across the Americas, connecting more communities through fast, affordable, and borderless money movement. Privy is proud to continue supporting their vision of building an open and inclusive global financial system.
