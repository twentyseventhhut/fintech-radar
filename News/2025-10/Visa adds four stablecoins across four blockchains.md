# Visa adds four stablecoins across four blockchains

> [!info] 2025-10-30 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] Visa is doubling down on stablecoins. CEO Ryan McInerney announced that the company will soon support four stablecoins across four separate blockchains.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Visa adds four stablecoins on four chains as stablecoin spend surges. Visa’s latest move aims to increase the ways of settling payments and moving money across its network. Visa’s CEO Ryan McLnerney stated that the stablecoins will represent two currencies that Visa can accept and convert to 25 traditional fiat currencies. Additionally, Visa earnings top estimates as consumers spend on cards. Visa reported strong fiscal fourth-quarter results, with adjusted net income rising 7% to $5.8 billion ($2.98 per share) and revenue up 12% to $10.7 billion, both exceeding estimates.
