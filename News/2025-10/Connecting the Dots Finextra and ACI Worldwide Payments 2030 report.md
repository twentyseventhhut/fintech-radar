## A clear roadmap to future-proofing your modernization strategies

The Payments 2030 report, developed by Finextra in partnership with ACI Worldwide, reveals how financial institutions are navigating the next era of payments.

Based on a global survey of 162 senior leaders across banks, fintechs, and payment providers, the report uncovers:

*   The **accelerating shifts** towards cloud-based and hybrid payment infrastructures
*   The **rise in A2A payments** over the next 5 years
*   The **global race to enable instant payments** to meet 24/7 customer demand
*   How **compliance pressures** are shaping innovation strategies
*   **Regional differences** in readiness, investment, and regulatory response

With expert commentary from Deutsche Bank, The Clearing House, and the US Faster Payments Council, this report offers actionable insights for decision-makers preparing for the future of payments.
