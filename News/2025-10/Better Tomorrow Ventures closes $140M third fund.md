# Better Tomorrow Ventures closes $140M third fund

> [!info] 2025-10-03 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Better Tomorrow Ventures closes $140M and remains bullish on FinTech. Sheel Mohnot, co-founder of BTV, believes FinTech remains a strong investment opportunity, citing the sector’s vast untapped potential in financial services that are still not fully digitized. He aims to accelerate this transformation.

[This Week in Fintech] Better Tomorrow Venturesraised a $140 million third fund, planning to invest in 30 to 35 companies, with check sizes ranging from $500,000 to $3.5 million.
