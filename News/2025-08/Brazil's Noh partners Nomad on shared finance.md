# Brazil's Noh partners Nomad on shared finance

> [!info] 2025-08-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Noh, the Brazilian startup focused on shared finance, announced a strategic partnership with Nomad to support financial planning for couples traveling together. Through Noh’s "shared boxes" feature, users can access pre-designed templates for trip planning, with the first itineraries covering Orlando, Latin America, and Europe.
