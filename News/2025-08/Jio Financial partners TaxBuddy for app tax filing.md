Jio Financial Services Ltd (JFSL) has unveiled a powerful new feature on its JioFinance app—a tax planning and filing module, developed in collaboration with TaxBuddy. Launched on August 11, 2025, this addition aims to simplify the complexities of income tax return (ITR) filing, making the process smarter, more affordable, and accessible to Indian taxpayers.
Feature Overview
1. Tax Planner
A standout tool designed for proactive financial planning:
 Deduction Mapping : Automatically suggests common deductions like 80C and 80D.
 Regime Comparison : Helps you evaluate and choose between old and new tax regimes for maximum savings.
 Tax Projections: Forecasts future liabilities, offering insights to manage finances proactively.
2. Tax Filing
Comes with two pricing tiers,
 Self-Service: Fully guided process, starting at just ₹24—ideal for straightforward filings like ITR‑1.
 Expert-Assisted: Includes professional review and personalized advice, priced from ₹999.
Added Functionalities
 Return Status Tracker: Users can monitor filing progress and check refund statuses within the app.
 Tax Alerts: Receive notifications on pending tasks or tax-related communications.
Benefits
 Ultra-Affordable: Most users can file for as low as ₹24—significantly cheaper than traditional CA services.
 User-Friendly: Guided step-by-step experience makes tax filing accessible even to first-time users.
 Efficient & Error-Resistant: Automated checks help avoid missed deductions and submission errors.
 Tech-Driven For Year-Round Planning: Not just a filing tool—it’s a finance companion that helps plan taxes proactively throughout the year.
Things to Know
 Limited Scope at ₹24: This package is likely restricted to simple ITR‑1 returns (e.g., salaried income, Form 16 only) with no complex income elements like capital gains or rental income. Expert review isn’t included.
 Data Privacy Considerations : While the tool offers convenience, users should ensure compliance and data security when sharing sensitive information digitally.
 Complex Cases May Need CA Support : If your tax situation involves multiple income types, audits, or scrutiny risks, the expert-assisted tier or traditional services may be more suitable.
