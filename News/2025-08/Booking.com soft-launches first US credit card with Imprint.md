# Booking.com soft-launches first US credit card with Imprint

> [!info] 2025-08-08 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Booking.com soft-launched its first credit card in the U.S., the Booking.com Genius Rewards Visa Signature Credit Card, which should help in its push for direct bookings. Rather than offering points and miles, the Genius rewards card, which is powered by Imprint, issues travel credits.
