# Bitstamp partners BBVA for retail crypto trading in Spain

> [!info] 2025-08-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇸 Bitstamp, by Robinhood, partners with BBVA to provide Bitcoin and Ethereum trading for retail customers. Bitstamp is serving as one of the first liquidity providers for BBVA’s new offering, now available to all eligible retail customers in Spain. This enables clients to buy, sell, and hold cryptocurrencies directly through the BBVA app.
