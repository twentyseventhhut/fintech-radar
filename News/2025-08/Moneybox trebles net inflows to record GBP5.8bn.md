> [!info] 2025-08-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Moneybox’s wealth focus helps treble inflows to a record high. The investment platform‘s net inflows more than tripled to £5.8bn, from £1.7bn the year prior, a jump of over 241%. Assets under management also more than doubled to £11.7bn as the FinTech’s wealth segment boomed.
