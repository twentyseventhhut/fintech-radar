# CORFO promotes pilot venture fund for Magallanes region

> [!info] 2025-08-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇨🇱 CORFO (Corporación de Fomento de la Producción) is promoting a pilot Venture Capital Investment Fund for the Region of Magallanes and the Chilean Antarctic, led by its Investment and Financing Management (GIF). The fund is still in the design phase and aims to bring together public and private stakeholders to channel investments into strategic sectors defined by the Regional Productive Development Committee.
