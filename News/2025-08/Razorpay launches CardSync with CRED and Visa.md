## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Razorpay partners with CRED and Visa for the CardSync payment solution to enable one-tap payments across online stores. CardSync automatically updates saved card details when users receive a new or replacement card, reducing payment failures and improving user experience.

[This Week in Fintech] RazorpaylaunchedCardSync, a comprehensive card tokenisation solution for seamless and secure digital payments.
