# Nubank weighs bank investment to win Brazil central bank licence

> [!info] 2025-08-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Nubank evaluates an investment by a bank to obtain a license from the Central Bank. The move gained momentum after the Central Bank opened a public consultation to redefine the classification of financial institutions, which could limit Nubank's scope of operations. Additionally, Nubank may be considering a return to Argentina. A report from BTG suggests that this could have happened through acquiring Brubank, which serves around 4 million clients. Nubank itself has not confirmed these plans.
