# Triodos Bank appoints new CTO and CIO

> [!info] 2025-08-08 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Triodos Bank appoints new CTO and CIO. The Dutch bank has appointed former ABN Amro exec Suzanne Schilder as CTO, and promoted Barbara van Duijn to CIO. The appointments will come into effect after the bank's extraordinary general meeting on 26 September 2025.

The Supervisory Board of Triodos Bank N.V. appointed Suzanne Schilder as Chief Transformation Officer (CTO) and Barbara van Duijn as Chief Information Officer (CIO), the Supervisory Board also reappointed Jacco Minnaar as Chief Commercial Officer (CCO) for a second term. Their appointment follows the notification of the intended appointments at the Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) of Shareholders held on the 26th of September.
The Executive Board now consists of Marcel Zuidam (CEO and Chair), Kees van Kalveen (CFO), Marjolein Landheer (CRO), Jacco Minnaar (CCO), Suzanne Schilder (CTO) and Barbara van Duijn (CIO).
