# Colombia's Bre-B to add millions to financial system

> [!info] 2025-08-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 $5.1 million will enter the financial system over the next three years. According to Sonia Gómez, Director of Solutions Consulting at ACI Worldwide, the new infrastructure will allow for real-time transfers and payments between different banks, digital wallets, and financial institutions. Meanwhile, a key player in this space is Colombia's Bre-B. According to estimates by ACI Worldwide, it could add at least 5 million new users to the financial system and generate an economic boost of more than $280 million.
