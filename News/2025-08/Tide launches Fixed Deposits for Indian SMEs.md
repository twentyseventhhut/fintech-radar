# Tide launches Fixed Deposits for Indian SMEs

> [!info] 2025-08-06 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Tide empowers SMEs to earn more with the launch of Fixed Deposits on its platform. Tide has introduced Fixed Deposits (FDs) on its platform, enabling SMEs to earn up to 8.84% interest per annum. This offering allows businesses to invest idle capital with tenures from 7 days to 60 months, starting at just ₹1,000.
