# China asks brokers to stop endorsing stablecoins

> [!info] 2025-08-15 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] China asked brokers to stop endorsing stablecoins to cool speculation and hype following Hong Kong’s new regime. This aligns with the government’s concern that stablecoins could become vehicles for scams, illegal fundraising, and herd-driven investment surges that may destabilize the financial system.
