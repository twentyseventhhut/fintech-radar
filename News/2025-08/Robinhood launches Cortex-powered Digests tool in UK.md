# Robinhood launches Cortex-powered Digests tool in UK

> [!info] 2025-08-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Robinhood launches stock-insights "Digests" tool. The tool is the first UK product launched by Robinhood Cortex, Robinhood’s investment assistant, which it launched in the US earlier this year. The tool, powered by Cortex, produces summaries or digests, as Robinhood calls them, of why a particular stock is moving in the market.
