**ACY Securities**, a leading global multi-asset CFD broker, today launched instant crypto withdrawals in the ACY Client Portal to give clients and affiliates faster, simpler access to their money. Built directly from client feedback, the upgrade delivers a smoother payout experience, clearer visibility, and more fee-free withdrawal flexibility so traders and partnerscan move capital with confidence.

With instant crypto withdrawals, eligible accounts can send funds directly to their crypto wallet as soon as the request is approved, subject to standard withdrawal requirements. The portal shows the available instant amount upfront, removing guesswork and reducing follow ups.

To make payouts even more convenient, the monthly allowance for fee-free crypto withdrawals has increased from three to five. The feature supports USDT and works alongside the ACY Trading Platform,MT4,MT5, and Copy Trading.

**Ashley Jessen**, COO of ACY Securities, said, “We listened to our clients and affiliates. They wanted speed, clarity, and a frictionless way to access their money. Instant crypto withdrawals deliver exactly that, with the available instant amount shown upfront in the portal and payouts flowing straight to a client’s crypto wallet. By lifting fee-free crypto withdrawals from three to five per month, we are giving traders and partners more flexibility to manage cash flow, take profits, and scale opportunities with confidence. This is another step in our commitment to technology, liquidity, and performance.”

The bottom line for clients, affiliates, and signal providers:

*   **Faster access to funds.** Instant crypto withdrawals move approved funds straight to a client’s crypto wallet, cutting wait times and giving traders control.
*   **Clear, immediate eligibility.** The ACY Client Portal shows the instant eligible amount upfront, so clients can act quickly without back-and-forth.
*   **Five fee-free withdrawals each month.** More flexibility to take profits, rebalance, and manage cash flow.
*   **Power combo for affiliates and Copy Trading.** Pair instant payouts with our Basic accounttype that triples your rebates per lot on Gold,FX, and Oil. Affiliates and signal providers can access commissions, performance fees, and subscription revenue faster, supporting recruitment, retention, and growth. And we have halved the requirements for affiliates to start getting paid.

Instant withdrawals are available to eligible accounts and remain subject to standard withdrawal requirements.

**Key instant payout rules include:**

*   Up to 30% of equity balance available for instant payout
*   1 instant payout transaction per 24-hour period
*   USD 5,000 daily maximum cumulative payout amount
*   Only the amount shown in the Client Portal will be processed instantly
*   Any amount above the 30% instant limit will be actioned manually through the standard withdrawal process
*   Automated instant payout service operates over the weekend

The feature is rolling out across eligible regions and segments, including retail clients, forex affiliate partners, and Copy Trading participants.

To view all deposit and withdrawal options, including instant crypto withdrawals, visit:https://acy.com.au/en/deposit-withdrawal-methods/

**FAQs:**

**What is the instant withdrawal limit?**

Instant crypto withdrawals are capped at up to 30% of the client’s equity balance, subject to standard withdrawal requirements.

**How many instant withdrawals can a client make?**

Each trader can perform up to 1 instant payout transaction within a 24-hour period.

**What is the maximum daily instant payout amount?**

The daily limit and maximum cumulative instant payout amount is USD 5,000.

**What happens if a client wants to withdraw more than the instant eligible amount?**

We will only process instantly up to the amount shown in the Client Portal, which is based on the client’s current equity. Any amount above the 30% instant limit will not be processed instantly and will instead be actioned manually through the standard withdrawal process.

**Does the instant payout feature work on weekends?**

Yes. The automated instant payout service also operates over the weekend.

**What does the Client Portal show?**

The ACY Client Portal shows the instant eligible amount upfront, helping clients understand how much they can withdraw instantly.

**Which cryptocurrencies are supported?**

The feature supports USDT.

**How many fee-free crypto withdrawals are available each month?**

Clients can make up to five fee-free crypto withdrawals each month.
