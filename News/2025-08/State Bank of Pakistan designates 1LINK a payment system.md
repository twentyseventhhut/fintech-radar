# State Bank of Pakistan designates 1LINK a payment system

> [!info] 2025-08-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇵🇰 State Bank declares 1LINK a designated payment system. The SBP has further strengthened oversight of the country’s payment ecosystem, recognizing 1LINK’s role as Pakistan’s only interbank network operator. The platform facilitates ATM withdrawals, bill payments, interbank fund transfers, and a wide range of digital financial services used by millions nationwide.
