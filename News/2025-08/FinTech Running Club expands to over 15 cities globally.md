# FinTech Running Club expands to over 15 cities globally

> [!info] 2025-08-14 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 The FinTech Running Club has expanded globally, marking a significant evolution from its origins. Originally launched as a casual run in Barcelona in November 2024, the initiative has grown into an international movement, now active in over 15 cities worldwide. It brings together individuals from diverse cultures and backgrounds who share a common interest in both FinTech and outdoor activity.
