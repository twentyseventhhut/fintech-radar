Zip becomes first Australian card issuer to launch latest Google Wallet experience on iOS
Wednesday, 13 August 2025
 Zip Pay and Zip Plus customers in Australia will now have access to their Zip Visa Card in
even more places 
 Zip Co (ASX: ZIP), the digital financial services company 
offering innovative, people-centred products, has collaborated with Google to become 
the first Australian card issuer to bring the benefits of Google Wallet to iOS, and 
among the early adopters globally to launch the latest Google Wallet experience 
across both iOS and Android.
In one seamless step, Zip Pay and Zip Plus customers can now access their Zip Visa Card across the entire Google ecosystem in one click - Chrome Autofill, Google Pay Online, and Google services - making it even easier to spend anywhere Visa is accepted, globally and in-store.
The move comes as more Australians are embracing the ease and security of digital wallets, with nearly 40%* of all card transactions in Australia being made with a digital wallet.
The new feature enables a customer’s Zip Visa Card on both Android and iOS systems. With no extra steps in the existing card provisioning flow, their Zip Visa Card will also be added to all their compatible devices for use across Google.
iOS customers will see a brand new “Add to Google Wallet” button and with just a couple of taps, their Zip Visa Card will be added across their Google account.
“We are proud to have been invited by Google to be a pilot partner of this new feature and the first Australian card issuer to go live with the iOS experience,” said Lauren McCormack, Zip Chief Product Officer, ANZ. “This rollout reflects our commitment to making everyday payments faster, easier and more accessible for all of our customers, and unlocking financial potential together. With the rise of smart payments, our goal is to support our customers with flexible payment options.”
Naomi Gaskell, Manager, Payments, at Google Australia added: "Making everyday payments just got easier for Aussies with the launch of a simple way to add Zip cards to Google Wallet. We’re thrilled that Zip customers will be among the first to experience the added convenience and flexibility when using their Zip cards online in Google Chrome or other Google services.”
*According to the Reserve Bank of Australia Payments System Board 2024 Report - page 20
