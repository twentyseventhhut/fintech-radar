 Tata Digital dominates ONDC credit market with one million applications
 
The company now holds the largest share of applications, users and disbursals on the ONDC Network
 By BL Mumbai Bureau 
Share
Tata Digital has emerged as the leading player in credit disbursement on the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC), processing over one million personal loan applications within three months. The company now holds the largest share of applications, users and disbursals on the ONDC Network.
The Tata Group subsidiary operates a loan marketplace on its Tata Neu platform featuring 14 lending partners, including two integrated through the ONDC Network. This multi-lender approach provides customers with competitive pricing and customised loan options for both salaried and self-employed borrowers, including first-time credit users.
 Gold loans have shown particularly strong uptake on the platform, according to Gaurav Hazrati, President of Financial Services at Tata Digital. The entire loan process operates digitally without paperwork, with near-instant disbursals.
“ONDC Network is transforming how credit is accessed and delivered in India,” said Vibhor Jain, Acting CEO and COO at ONDC. The network allows fintechs to connect with multiple lending partners through a single integration while giving consumers transparent access to various loan options.
The collaboration represents a significant step in India’s digital financial inclusion efforts, leveraging public-private partnership to expand credit access to underserved segments. Tata Digital operates as a Lending Service Provider that works with multiple lenders to offer various loan products to customers.
