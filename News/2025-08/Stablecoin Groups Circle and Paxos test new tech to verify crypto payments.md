# Stablecoin Groups Circle and Paxos test new tech to verify crypto payments

> [!info] 2025-08-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Stablecoin Groups Circle and Paxos test new tech to verify crypto payments. The firms partnered with Bluprynt to use cryptography and blockchain technology to provide issuer verification when stablecoins are released by a company. The pilot provided a way to trace back a token to the verified issuer, using Bluprynt’s technology.
