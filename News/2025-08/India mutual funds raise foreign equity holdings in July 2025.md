Foreign equity holdings by mutual funds increase by Rs 3,100 crore in July
Increase in holding
The foreign equity holdings by mutual funds increased by Rs 3,101 crore on monthly basis to Rs 51,705 crore in July against Rs 48,604 crore in June. Here is a detailed breakup of the fund houses which increased their financial holdings, according to the monthly data by Prime Database.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
PPFAS Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 1,119 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 12,305 crore in June to Rs 13,425 crore in July. The portfolio includes Alphabet, Microsoft Corporation, Meta Platforms, and Amazon.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 976 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 6,509 crore in June to Rs 7,485 crore in July. The portfolio had 150 such stocks in the portfolio such as Nvidia, Apple, Meta Platform, Thomson Reuters, and Tesla.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 633 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 13,266 crore in June to Rs 13,899 crore in July. The portfolio had 550 such stocks which includes Netflix, Tesla, NetApp, Airbnb, Alphabet, Apple, Meta Platforms.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
Mirae Asset Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 191 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 4,341 crore in June to Rs 4,532 crore in July. The portfolio had 82 such stocks which includes Netflix, Adobe Systems, Tesla, Nvidia, Amazon, Alphabet, Apple, Meta Platforms.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
Franklin Templeton Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 140 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 462 crore in June to Rs 602 crore in July. The portfolio had 44 such stocks which included Microsoft, Alphabet, Meta Platforms, Hyundai Motors, and Apple.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
Nippon India Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 75 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 2,133 crore in June to Rs 2,208 crore in July. The portfolio had 171 such stocks which included Anthem Inc, Adobe Systems, Hitachi, Alphabet, Amazon, Meta Platform, and Microsoft.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
Axis Mutual Fund
The fund house increased its foreign equity holding by Rs 25 crore on a monthly basis from Rs 989 crore in June to Rs 1,051 crore in July. The portfolio had 45 such stocks which included Netflix, Alphabet, Meta Platforms, Microsoft, Amazon, and Salesforce.com.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
Reduction in holding
Around six fund houses reduced their foreign equity holdings which includes DSP Mutual Fund, Edelweiss Mutual Fund, Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund, SBI Mutual Fund, Sundaram Mutual Fund, and Kotak Mutual Fund.
Image Source: ETMarkets.com
