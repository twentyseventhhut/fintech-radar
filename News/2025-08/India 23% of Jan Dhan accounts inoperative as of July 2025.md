## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Governmentreported 130M out of 560M i.e. 23%, Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) accounts were inoperative as of July 31, 2025, with Uttar Pradesh having the highest number of inactive accounts.
