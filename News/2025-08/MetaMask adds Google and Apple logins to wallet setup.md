# Introducing MetaMask Social login

Simplified, secure crypto wallet creation and recovery.

*    Gabriela Helfet

3 minutes

August 26, 2025

*   Copy link 

What do an _awkward shrimp tornado_ and a _feral chaos waffle_ have in common? Both may be parts of your 12 word seed phrase.

Crypto doesn’t have to be complicated. That’s why we’ve made it easier than ever to manage a MetaMask wallet with our new Social login feature: use your Google or Apple account to create, backup, and restore a wallet.

### How MetaMask Social login works

Traditionally, you need a 12 word Secret Recovery Phrase (SRP) to manage non-custodial crypto wallets. This route gives you more control and security over your digital assets than traditional financial accounts. But keeping track of your SRP can be cumbersome.

Social login simplifies the wallet creation and management process. Get started in two steps:

1.   Sign in with your Google or Apple ID.

2.   Create a unique, secure password.

You’re in! You can now use Social login.

Your SRP is then generated under the hood, and can be recovered later using the same social account and password that was used to set-up your wallet.

**Remember:**Secure password creation and management for Social login is essential. **If you lose this password, your wallet cannot be recovered for you.**For privacy best practices, head to our MetaMask Support article.

### Web2 familiarity meets web3 security

No single entity, not even MetaMask, can access all of the pieces needed in order to retrieve your SRP, preserving the self-custodial nature of your wallet. Only your social credentials combined with your unique password can ultimately unlock the SRP on a local device. For a technical deep dive on how the process works, check out our Social login technical guide.

Social login is an alternative, familiar way to control your digital assets that is seamless and simple, without compromising on security. Your wallet and assets remain fully in your control, with fewer obscure words to manage if you so choose.

**Ready to get started?****Download MetaMask****and set-up your wallet instantly with Social login.**

**Looking for extra help? We got you. Head to****MetaMask Support****for more info on how to use Social login.**
