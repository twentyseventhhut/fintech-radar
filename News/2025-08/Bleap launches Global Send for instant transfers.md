Bleap Launches Global Send
Unified Money Transfers Powered by Stablecoins and Local Rails
 Unified Money Transfers Powered by Stablecoins and Local Rails 
 London - Bleap, the self-custodial fintech platform built on stablecoins, today announced the launch of Global Send, a major new feature that simplifies international payments into a single, seamless experience. 
 With Global Send, users can send money instantly from their Bleap wallet to anyone in the world using just a phone number or any payment address, including a crypto wallet, payment link, or traditional bank account. 
 Behind the scenes, Bleap’s Send Resolver automatically finds the fastest, cheapest, and most efficient rail for every transaction - whether it’s a blockchain transfer, peer-to-peer stablecoin payment, or a local fiat settlement like SEPA Instant. 
 “We believe money should move like messages - instantly, globally, and without friction,” said Guilherme Gomes, co-founder of Bleap. “That’s what we’re building with Global Send. One app, one interface, one simple way to send money anywhere in the world.” 
 A New Model for Remittances 
 Global Send introduces a new approach to stablecoin-powered remittances. Instead of relying on slow and expensive correspondent banking networks, Bleap uses stablecoins for the cross-border leg, settling locally via trusted regional payment rails. 
 This hybrid model enables instant, low-cost international transfers - without compromising on compliance, speed, or user experience. 
 Bleap has already rolled out EUR support and is preparing to launch MXN, COP, BRL, PEN, and ARS in the coming months. 
 “We are already testing our MXN rails,” said João Alves, Bleap co-founder. “You can now send money from anywhere in the world to Mexico in under 3 seconds - at 10x lower cost than what traditional fintechs are charging.” 
 Why Global Send Matters 
 Infrastructure-agnostic: Users don’t need to understand how money moves - Bleap’s resolver handles the routing. 
 Globally inclusive: Anyone can onboard with just a phone number - no bank account or crypto knowledge needed. 
 Unified UX: A single self-custodial app that works across fiat and crypto ecosystems. 
 Global Send is another step in Bleap’s mission to build a global, self-custodial financial OS - replacing outdated infrastructure with a faster, programmable, and borderless alternative, powered entirely by stablecoins. 
 About Bleap 
 Founded by two former Revolut executives, Bleap is a self-custodial financial app that connects a stablecoin wallet to a Mastercard debit card - offering 2% cashback, 3% yield on EUR, 8% on USD, and free global transfers. Partnering with Mastercard, Bleap is reimagining the financial stack: no intermediaries, no fees, and full user control. With Bleap, the future of money is fast, global, and built on the blockchain.
