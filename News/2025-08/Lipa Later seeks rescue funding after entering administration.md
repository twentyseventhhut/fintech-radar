# Lipa Later seeks rescue funding after entering administration

> [!info] 2025-08-04 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Lipa Later sought $5 million from a UK lender two weeks after entering administration.

[This Week in Fintech] Lipa Later which raised $16.6 Million in total funding, was founded in 2018. In December 2023, it announced a surprise $1.9 Million acquisition of Sky Garden, a struggling e-commerce platform.By March 2024, Lipa Later had run out of road. Staff were unpaid, creditors were circling, and fundraising efforts had stalled.
