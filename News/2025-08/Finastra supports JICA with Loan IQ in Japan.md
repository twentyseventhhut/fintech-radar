# Finastra supports JICA with Loan IQ in Japan

> [!info] 2025-08-19 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇵 Finastra supports JICA with Loan IQ to transform the private-sector investment finance system in Japan. With Loan IQ at its core, the system enables end-to-end management of international private sector loan transactions and supports loans denominated in Japanese yen, U.S. dollars, euros, and local currencies.
