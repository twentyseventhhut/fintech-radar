About Event

​Lace up your sneakers and get ready to hit the pavement with the Fintech Running Club! Whether you’re a startup hustler, a big bank pro, a media maven, or just a fintech fanatic, this is your chance to connect with the community in a fun, energetic way. We’re bringing together runners of all levels for a casual jog (or walk!) and some lively networking, no suits required.

​Come as you are, meet new friends, and swap stories about the latest in fintech while getting those steps in. The exact route and meeting spot will be announced soon, so stay tuned. Let’s make some moves, on the road and in the industry!

Location

Irish Hunger Memorial

North End Ave &, Vesey St, New York, NY 10280, USA

We will group by the Irish Hunger Memorial close to Brookfield Place. We will go up to around pier 45 and turn back south to finish close to where we started.
