[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Klarna automatically enables thousands of WooCommerce stores, tackling checkout drop-offs head-on via Stripe auto-enablement. By making Klarna available by default, Woo merchants can now meet modern checkout expectations straight out of the box.
