Meet the Top Voices in Risk of 2025 
Taktile's Top Voices in Risk program honors standout individuals who are transforming how risk management is understood and practiced in financial services: through bold leadership, deep expertise, community impact, and visionary thinking.
 Innovation 
Honoring those pioneering new systems, tools, and technologies in risk.
 Impact 
Celebrating those advancing financial inclusion and driving positive societal change.
 Progress 
Spotlighting those pushing boundaries and moving the industry forward.
 Leadership 
Recognizing those shaping teams, cultures, and the next generation of leaders.
 Celebrating the global voices redefining risk 
Over the past months, Taktile invited nominations from across the global financial community, resulting in a diverse pool of candidates spanning industries, regions, and risk domains. Winners were selected across four categories:
Innovation
They’ve pioneered new systems, technologies, or approaches that redefine how risk is measured, managed, and operationalized.
 Impact
They’ve advanced financial inclusion, equity, or broader societal good through risk-driven initiatives and action.
Progress
They’ve challenged conventional thinking and pushed the boundaries of risk: introducing new ideas, frameworks, and industry-shaping change.
Leadership
They’ve built strong teams, mentored emerging talent, and shaped the next generation of leaders in risk.
