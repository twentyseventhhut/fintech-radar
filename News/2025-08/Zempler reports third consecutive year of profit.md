## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Challenger bank Zempler reports third consecutive year of profit. Customer deposits grew 15% to £626m, while Zempler recorded a slight increase in the number of business customers to just over 170,000 open accounts. Interest income was up from £34m to £37m over the year, while total income increased by more than seven per cent to £68.2m.
