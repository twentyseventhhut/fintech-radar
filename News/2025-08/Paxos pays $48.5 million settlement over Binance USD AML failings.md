## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Paxos pays $48.5m to settle NY charges over Binance USD. New York regulators accused Paxos of failing to conduct sufficient due diligence on its partner, Binance. Under the terms of the settlement, Paxos will pay a $26.5 million fine and invest $22 million to strengthen its compliance program.
