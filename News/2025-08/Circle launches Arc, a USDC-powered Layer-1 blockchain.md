# Circle launches Arc, a USDC-powered Layer-1 blockchain

> [!info] 2025-08-14 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Circle announces Arc, an open layer-1 blockchain. Fully compatible with the Ethereum Virtual Machine, Arc will use USDC as its native gas token, enabling transaction fees to be paid directly in the stablecoin. It will power the next generation of stablecoin-based payments, foreign exchange, and capital markets infrastructure.

[This Week in Fintech] CirclelaunchedArc, a new USDC-powered Layer-1 blockchain designed to support stablecoin-based financial infrastructure with sub-second settlements and privacy controls. Stripe also deployed its own blockchain, dubbed “Tempo,” to assess whether openness can endure when built atop branded rails, with Paradigm’s Matt Huang appointed as its first CEO.
