# Konfio moves to behavioral biometrics over one-time passwords

> [!info] 2025-08-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Konfío, the Mexican unicorn specializing in businesses, is revising its user authentication system by moving away from one-time passwords (OTPs) and adopting behavioral biometrics—tracking how users interact with their devices (typing patterns, phone angle, etc.) to enhance security and reduce fraud risk such as phishing and SIM swapping.
