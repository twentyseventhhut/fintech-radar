# Ryt Bank debuts as Malaysia's AI-powered digital bank

> [!info] 2025-08-15 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇲🇾 Ryt Bank debuts as Malaysia’s first AI-powered digital bank. It can carry out tasks such as bill payments, fund transfers, and spending insights through natural conversation in Bahasa Malaysia, English, and Manglish. Mandarin support is expected to be added by September, but that is only for the app and not Ryt AI.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇲🇾 Malaysia's Ryt Bank selects Episode Six to power card services ahead of launch. Episode Six's card issuing and ledger infrastructure will provide Ryt Bank with instant card issuance capabilities, as well as personalised spending controls and real-time authorisations.
