# B3 acquires Control of Shipay for R$37 million

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 B3 acquires Control of Shipay for R$37 million. B3 is purchasing 62% of the company for R$37 million. According to a market filing, the deal also includes the option to acquire the remaining stake by 2030, with the price contingent on the achievement of certain performance targets.
