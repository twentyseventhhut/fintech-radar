Summary of this article

*   BLS International Services awarded ₹2,055.35 crore work order by UIDAI to establish and run district-level Aadhaar Seva Kendras (ASKs).

*   ASKs will provide appointment and walk-in Aadhaar enrolment, updates, and other citizen services.

*   BLS responsible for end-to-end setup and management of district-level ASKs under UIDAI supervision.

*   Company operates in visa, passports, consular, citizen, e-governance, attestation, biometric, e-visa, and retail services since 2005.

BLS International Services on Tuesday said it has been awarded a work order amounting to ₹2,055.35 crore from Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) for being the service provider for establishing and running district-level Aadhar Seva Kendra.
