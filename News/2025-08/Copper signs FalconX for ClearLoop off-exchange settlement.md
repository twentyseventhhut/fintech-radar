FalconX Expands Prime Connect with Copper ClearLoop Integration, Enhancing Off-Exchange Settlement for DMA Clients

FalconX continues to strengthen the foundation of its Direct Market Access (DMA) product with the latest expansion of Prime Connect – now integrating with Copper’s ClearLoop network. This integration unlocks off-exchange settlement for clients trading on Bybit, furthering FalconX’s commitment to delivering institutional-grade infrastructure that prioritizes capital efficiency, operational simplicity, and fund security.
DMA has long been a cornerstone of FalconX’s institutional offering, providing a single, unified access to global exchanges with capital efficiency. Prime Connect further enhances this by enabling clients to trade while maintaining control of their assets through trusted third-party custodians, eliminating the need to post collateral on the exchange.
Custody assets off-exchange with Copper ClearLoop while trading on Bybit
Avoid counterparty risk tied to exchange-held funds
Access up to 5x leverage through FalconX’s integrated credit and risk solutions
Consolidate margin and risk across venues 
Maintain seamless workflows via FalconX 360
Gain near real-time visibility into off-exchange balances and settlements
 This integration with FalconX enables institutional clients to access Copper’s ClearLoop network and MPC custody while significantly reducing counterparty risk,” said Adam VandenBoogaard, Head of Revenue – Americas at Copper. “By combining FalconX’s execution and credit infrastructure with our off-exchange settlement network, we’re helping bring the security and capital efficiency of traditional market structure to digital assets.” 
This milestone marks the second production-grade deployment of Prime Connect, following FalconX’s recent launch with Ceffu for Binance. By connecting Copper and Ceffu through the same infrastructure, FalconX is building a unified settlement layer across exchanges, laying the foundation for faster, safer, and more capital-efficient execution.
As more Prime Connect integrations go live, clients will gain greater flexibility to move capital across venues, with funds never leaving custody.
To learn how FalconX DMA and Prime Connect can support your trading workflow, contact your FalconX team or sales@falconx.io .
