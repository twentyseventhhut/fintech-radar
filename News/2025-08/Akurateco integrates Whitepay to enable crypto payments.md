 Akurateco Announces New Integration with Whitepay to Enable Crypto Payments 
 Payments
 11.08.2025 09:45 am 
 Akurateco , a cutting-edge white-label payment software provider, has announced a new integration with Whitepay, a leading cryptocurrency payment gateway. This collaboration allows all Akurateco customers – ranging from payment providers to acquirers and enterprise merchants – to start accepting crypto payments from their end users, offering new levels of flexibility at checkout. 
 With support for over 200 cryptocurrencies and compatibility with any crypto wallet globally, Whitepay provides a secure, efficient, and user-friendly crypto processing experience. The integration simplifies access to mass payouts, allowing businesses to send funds to partners, contractors, and employees in crypto, while benefiting from automatic conversion to stable assets to mitigate volatility risks. Clients can also take advantage of transparent, low fees, automatic balance conversion without relying on external exchanges, and quick API integration for effortless setup. 
 “We are always focused on providing our clients with the widest possible choice of payment methods,” said Andrew Riabchuk, Founder of Akurateco. “The integration with Whitepay enables our merchants and PSPs to embrace the crypto economy and open new opportunities for growth.” 
 Known for its secure infrastructure and intuitive interface, Whitepay strengthens Akurateco’s offering by unlocking seamless access to the growing world of cryptocurrency payments. This integration aligns with Akurateco’s mission to empower global businesses through payment innovation.
