[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Galaxy, Jump, and Multicoin seek $1 billion for buying Solana. The three firms aim to create a digital asset treasury company by taking over an unidentified publicly traded entity, with Cantor Fitzgerald LP as lead banker for the deal. A deal would bring together three of crypto’s biggest names, wielding a reserve more than double the size of the biggest existing one focused on Solana.
