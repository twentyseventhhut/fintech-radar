# Monato raises $13 million seed for Mexico embedded finance

> [!info] 2025-08-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Monato, the Mexican embedded-finance fintech enabling companies to create and manage accounts, disperse payments, increase offerings, and extend credit via APIs, raised a $13 million Seed round led by Monashees and FinTech Collective. IGNIA Partners, Clocktower Ventures, Polymath Ventures, and Angel Ventures also participated in the round.
