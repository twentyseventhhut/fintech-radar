[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇪 UAE closes in on launch of Digital Dirham. The CBUAE has already put in place the core infrastructure and can now issue, redeem, and distribute the Digital Dirham to licensed financial institutions through a custom-built issuance platform utilising DLT.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇪 UAE Central Bank set to launch Digital Dirham in phases.
