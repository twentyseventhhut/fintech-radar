# Bitpanda names Lukas Enzersdorfer-Konrad co-CEO

> [!info] 2025-08-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇹 Lukas Enzersdorfer-Konrad replaces Paul Klanschek as Co-CEO of Bitpanda. Klanschek takes a new role as a Member of the Board of Directors. Lukas Enzersdorfer-Konrad, currently Deputy CEO, will now assume the CEO role alongside Eric Demuth, maintaining Bitpanda’s dual leadership structure.
