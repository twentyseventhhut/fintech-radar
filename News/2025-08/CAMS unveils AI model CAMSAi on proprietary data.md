 CAMS unveils CAMSAi, an AI strategy based on proprietary datasets 
 
With pre-trained models on CAMS’ proprietary datasets and deep financial domain focus, CAMSAi helps institutions improve service quality, operational agility, and compliance
 By BL Chennai Bureau 
Share
 Financial infrastructure company CAMS on Tuesday launched CAMSAi, a domain-trained AI stack for the company built on three decades of capital market data and machine-learning research. The company has formally branded its AI-led efforts as CAMSAi and products will be rolled out from it in the future.
CAMSAi products are designed to meet the unique needs of India’s BFSI sector, with built-in capabilities that are regulatory compliant and adhere to data localization and investor protection mandates.
With pre-trained models on CAMS’ proprietary datasets and deep financial domain focus, CAMSAi helps institutions improve service quality, operational agility, and compliance
CAMSAi tackles the challenges that generic AI cannot handle including the multi-intent interactions, multilingual handwriting, compliance clauses and real-time fraud vectors specific to the BFSI and capital markets segments.
“Institutions now process petabytes of structured and unstructured data; AI must automate not just the routine 80 per cent, but the stubborn 20 per cent edge cases,” a statement from the company said. “CAMSAi fuses Natural Language Processing, computer vision and AI led real-time analytics to deliver compliant decisions in milliseconds.,” it added.
“With CAMSAi, we are ushering in a new era of intelligent automation for India’s capital market ecosystem. It will enable institutions to operate smarter, respond faster, and exceed customer expectations while staying compliant.” said Anuj Kumar, MD, CAMS.
