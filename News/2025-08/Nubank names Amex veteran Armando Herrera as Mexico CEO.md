# Nubank names Amex veteran Armando Herrera as Mexico CEO

> [!info] 2025-08-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Nubank appoints Amex vet Armando Herrera as Mexico CEO. In his new position, Herrera will focus on preparing Nu Mexico to operate as a fully licensed bank. He joins Nubank from small business lending platform Konfío, where he has served as GM of financial products and payments since January 2024.
