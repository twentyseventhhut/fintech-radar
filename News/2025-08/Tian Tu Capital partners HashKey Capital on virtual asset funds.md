Hong Kong, August 18, 2025 – HashKey Capital Limited ("HashKey Capital") is pleased to announce the signing of a strategic collaboration memorandum with Tiantu Asset Management Company Limited (“Tiantu AM”), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Tian Tu Capital Co., Ltd. (Stock Code: 1973). This collaboration aims to explore the potential joint establishment of investment funds in the virtual asset space, with opportunities for additional forms of strategic cooperation in the future. 
 As both HashKey Capital and Tiantu AM are licensed asset managers in Hong Kong with virtual asset authorization under their Type 9 licenses, the collaboration will be conducted in strict full compliance with applicable laws and regulations. HashKey Capital recognizes the growing integration of virtual assets and blockchain technology into mainstream business operations and sees significant potential for growth in the virtual asset and cryptocurrency sectors. The company aims to leverage its expertise to support Tian Tu Capital in expanding its asset management offerings in this rapidly evolving space. 
 " Through this partnership, HashKey Capital will leverage its deep expertise in the virtual asset space to provide input and investment insights, enabling Tian Tu Capital to expand its investment product offerings, diversify its investor base, and strengthen its capabilities in the growing virtual asset sector. This collaboration underscores HashKey Capital’s leading role in shaping and advancing the digital asset ecosystem across Asia and globally"  said Vivien Wong, Partner, Liquid Funds at HashKey Capital. 
 Tiantu Capital’s strategic partnership with HashKey Capital reflects a shared vision of leveraging complementary expertise to explore new growth opportunities. As a leading private equity investor in China’s consumer sector, Tiantu brings deep industry knowledge, a systematic investment approach, and experience backing top consumer brands. Li Qi, Executive Director of Tiantu AM says: " By collaborating with HashKey Capital, Tiantu can integrate innovative digital asset and blockchain capabilities into its investment strategy, enhancing value creation for portfolio companies and investors while enabling strategic expansion into virtual asset space by its Hong Kong asset management business. " 
   
 HashKey Capital is an entity of HashKey Group, a leading digital asset financial services group with operations across Hong Kong, Singapore, Japan, and Bermuda. As one of Asia’s largest crypto fund managers and an early corporate investor in Ethereum, HashKey Capital has managed over US$1 billion in client assets  since its inception. The company continues to support institutions, founders, and talent in accelerating innovation within the blockchain and digital asset industry. 
 About Tian Tu Capital 
 Tian Tu Capital is a joint stock company incorporated in the People’s Republic of China and listed on the Main Board of the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong. The Company manages capital for institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals, investing through its funds under management and directly with its own capital. Tiantu AM, its wholly-owned Hong Kong subsidiary, is primarily engaged in investment management and holds a Type 9 (asset management) license under the Securities and Futures Ordinance. 
   
 About HashKey Capital 
 HashKey Capital is a global digital asset and blockchain leader helping institutions, founders and talents advance the blockchain industry. 
 As one of the largest crypto fund managers and being the earliest corporate investor in Ethereum, HashKey Capital has managed over US$1 billion in client assets since its inception. Leveraging its unparalleled expertise, HashKey Capital's venture investments team oversees a diversified portfolio of over 600 pioneering projects across institutional services, infrastructure, data, AI, consumer services/ technology and more. 
 On the liquid funds front, HashKey Capital manages a suite of digital asset products, including an actively managed fund and the Bosera HashKey Bitcoin ( 3008.HK ) and Ether ( 3009.HK ) spot ETFs, which are listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (HKEX). 
 With our deep knowledge across the blockchain ecosystem, HashKey Capital has built a robust network connecting founders, investors, developers, and regulators.
