[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇻🇳 Vietnam greenlights crypto-to-fiat Sandbox Trial in Da Nang. Da Nang has cleared Basal Pay, a crypto-to-fiat conversion platform developed by AlphaTrue Solutions JSC, to operate in the city’s controlled FinTech sandbox. AlphaTrue designed Basal Pay to comply with global standards.
