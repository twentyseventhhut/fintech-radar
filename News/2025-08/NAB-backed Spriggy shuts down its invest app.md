# NAB-backed Spriggy shuts down its invest app

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 NAB and Cannon-Brookes-backed pocket money FinTech Spriggy is shutting down its invest app, promising an all-in-one reboot. In a message, the company thanked users for their support over the past four and a half years and reflected on its mission to create a simple, jargon-free investment tool for parents.
