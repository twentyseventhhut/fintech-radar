# RUUT: New player set to enter UK digital banking market

## London's Algbra Labs and Turkish group Moka United are joining forces to launch the service

FintechFri 22 Aug 2025

RUUT, a new digital banking brand is set to enter the UK market following a strategic partnership between British and Turkish fintechs Algbra Labs and Moka United.

Moka, a subsidiary of İşbank, one of the largest banks in Turkey, will launch the new banking brand with Algbra, a fintech-as-a-service company that will power RUUT through its tech stack.

The partnership was agreed as part of Moka United’s international expansion plans and the broader goal of İşbank to provide tech-focused financial services beyond Turkey.

“RUUT is an exciting digital banking initiative, and we are delighted to provide the platform infrastructure to support its UK launch,” said Zeiad Idris, chief executive of Algbra Labs.

“This collaboration reflects the growing demand for values-led, high-compliance, and scalable financial technology.”

The two firms also hope to expand the UK-Turkish financial corridor, which currently facilitates over £28bn in annual trade and remittance.

“This partnership with Algbra Labs is a strategic milestone in our journey to expand globally,” said Moka United and RUUT chief executive Halim Memiş.

“Through RUUT, we aim to deliver inclusive, user-friendly, and trusted digital banking experiences — beginning in the UK. Algbra’s technological excellence and shared ethical approach make them the ideal long-term partner.”

### algbra.com
<https://www.algbra.com/news/algbra-labs-and-moka-united-the-fintech-subsidiary-of-isbank-announce-strategic-partnership-to-launch-ruut-in-the-uk/>

**London, 21 August 2025** - Algbra Labs, the leading UK-based Fintech-as-a-Service (FaaS) provider, has announced a strategic partnership with Moka United, the fintech subsidiary of İşbank - Türkiye’s largest private bank. The collaboration supports the UK launch of RUUT, a next-generation digital banking brand developed by Moka United as part of İşbank’s global fintech strategy.

This partnership represents a key step in Moka United’s international expansion and İşbank’s broader vision to deliver inclusive, technology-driven financial services across borders. Powered by Algbra Labs’ full-stack FaaS platform, RUUT will offer a seamless, secure, and scalable digital banking experience to customers in the United Kingdom.

The collaboration is a powerful signal of Algbra’s leadership in using our Fintech-as-a- Service (FaaS) capabilities to enable diaspora-focused digital banking across the globe. Every Algbra partnership is a catalyst for innovation and a reaffirmation that the company Is commited to not just building platforms, but rather redefining what inclusive modern banking looks like.

The multi-phased agreement includes:

*   Deployment of a partner banking platform enabling RUUT to operate client accounts;
*   Launch of customer-named accounts, with RUUT acting as an authorised electronic money distributor;
*   Support for RUUT to become a fully licensed electronic money institution, with Algbra Labs continuing to provide the core banking and payments infrastructure.

The partnership aims to deliver a fully operational platform within six months. In the medium term, both parties plan to further develop the UK-Türkiye financial corridor, which currently facilitates over £28 billion in annual trade and remittance flows. Türkiye continues to be a significant trading partner for the UK, with bilateral trade in goods and services surpassing £26 billion in 2024, while the UK is among Türkiye’s top five export markets. In addition, the UK is home to a growing Turkish diaspora of close to one million people, whose economic, social, and cultural ties further strengthen the corridor. Expanding digital financial infrastructure between the two countries will unlock new opportunities for trade, remittances, and investment.

**Zeiad Idris, CEO of Algbra**, said:

“We are proud to partner with Moka United, the fintech arm of Türkiye’s most established bank. RUUT is an exciting digital banking initiative, and we are delighted to provide the platform infrastructure to support its UK launch. This collaboration reflects the growing demand for values-led, high-compliance, and scalable financial technology.”

**Halim Memiş, CEO of Moka United and RUUT**, said:

“This partnership with Algbra Labs is a strategic milestone in our journey to expand globally. Through RUUT, we aim to deliver inclusive, user-friendly, and trusted digital banking experiences — beginning in the UK. Algbra’s technological excellence and shared ethical approach make them the ideal long-term partner.”

The collaboration further reinforces Algbra Labs’ position as a premier Fintech-as-a-Service provider, enabling banks and fintechs to launch and scale digital offerings without compromising on compliance, speed, or innovation.
