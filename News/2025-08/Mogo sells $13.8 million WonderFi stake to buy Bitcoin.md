# Mogo sells $13.8 million WonderFi stake to buy Bitcoin

> [!info] 2025-08-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇦 Mogo sells $13.8 million of WonderFi stake to buy Bitcoin. It aims to hold remaining shares until Robinhood’s purchase closes, which is expected in the second half of 2025. In its announcement, Mogo said it was using the sale to boost its Bitcoin investment to roughly $2 million after making a $1 million contribution to Bitcoin exchange-traded funds (ETFs) in June.
