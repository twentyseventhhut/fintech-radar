## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Weaver Services, an Indian housing finance platform, raised INR 1,484cr ($170M).

[This Week in Fintech] Weaver Services, backed by Gaja Capital, acquired 75% stake in Centrum Housing Finance for INR 600 crore ($73M) at 2x enterprise valuation.
