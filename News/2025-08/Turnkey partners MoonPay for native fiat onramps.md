Turnkey now supports fiat onramp via MoonPay
Turnkey has partnered with MoonPay to support native fiat onramps, enabling users to purchase crypto without leaving your app.
Fiat onramps can be tricky to implement securely and compliantly, but Turnkey makes it simple. Developers can embed fiat–to-crypto purchases directly into their wallet UI — no redirects, no modals, no extra SDKs.
What is a fiat onramp?
A fiat onramp lets users exchange traditional currency (like USD, EUR, or GBP) for crypto. It’s often the first step in a user’s crypto and wallet journey. A smooth onramp is essential to maximize conversion, especially for mobile-first and mainstream audiences.
Previously, most apps would redirect users to third-party sites or pop-up modals for fiat-to-crypto purchases. This approach breaks trust, slows onboarding, and increases user dropoff. Native onramps solve these issues by keeping the experience fully contained in the app.  
Why it matters
With this MoonPay integration, Turnkey customers can own the entire purchase experience, securely, natively and with minimal integration work.
Turnkey handles the complexity of building a compliant purchase flow while giving you full control over UX, security and policy logic. 
Run a fully in-app, secure purchase flow
Reduce dropoff with a seamless user experience
Integrate quickly with a single API
Choose from multiple onramp providers based on your use case
A seamless funding experience, built in
The native onramp makes it easy for users to fund their wallets instantly, securely, and globally. With MoonPay, users can now buy crypto in over 160 countries using payment methods such as:
Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Maestro)
Bank transfers (ACH, SEPA, and more)
Digital and mobile first payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Venmo, PayPal)
Localized methods (PIX, Yellow Card, Open Banking, and others) 
Purchases run inside the same secure session as signing, with verifiable authentication and policy enforcement, with no extra steps required.
