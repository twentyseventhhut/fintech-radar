# Indian fintechs raise $119.8 million in July

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Indian FinTechs raise USD 119.8 Mn. Indian FinTechs raised a total of USD 119.8 million in July 2025, marking a continued decline from USD 156.9 million in June, USD 210.7 million in May, and USD 238.9 million in April. InCred topped the charts with a USD 46.8 million raise, followed by Credit Wise and Navi.

FinTech Funding June 2025: Indian FinTechs raise USD 157 Mn
 Indian FinTechs raised a total of USD 156.9 million in June 2025, registering a decline from USD 210.7 million in May and USD 238.9 million in April. FlexiLoans led the chart with a USD 45.2 million raise, followed by Popclub and GoKwik. Out of the nineteen deals, three were seed-stage, while the rest spanned Series A to growth rounds.
FinTech Funding May 2025: Indian FinTechs raise USD 210 Mn
Indian FinTechs raised a total of USD 210.7 million in May, across thirteen deals, compared to USD 238.9 million in April. FinTech unicorn CRED bagged USD 75 million in its series G funding, followed by Propelld that secured USD 30.8 million in Series B.
FinTech Funding April 2025: Indian FinTechs raise USD 238.9 Mn
Indian FinTechs raised a total of USD 238.9 million, across fifteen deals, compared to USD 180.8 million in March. Bengaluru based Juspay managed to bag USD 60 million in its series D funding, followed by Scapia that secured USD 40 million in Series B.
