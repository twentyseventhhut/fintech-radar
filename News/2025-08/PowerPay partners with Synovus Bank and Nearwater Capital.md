# PowerPay partners with Synovus Bank and Nearwater Capital

> [!info] 2025-08-19 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 PowerPay partners with Synovus Bank and Nearwater Capital. The partnership is expected to provide PowerPay with the capital needed to continue scaling its originations and serve its growing network of over 12,000 contractors, merchants, and medical professionals nationwide.
