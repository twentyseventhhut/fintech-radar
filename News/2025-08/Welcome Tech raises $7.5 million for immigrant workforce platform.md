LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Welcome Tech, the leading digital platform serving immigrant workforces and the employers who rely on them, today announced the close of a **$7.5 million funding round**. The capital will accelerate the development of Welcome Tech’s proprietary AI infrastructure and expand its workforce engagement platform, which connects immigrant workers to essential services – including jobs, healthcare, and financial tools – at the highest quality and lowest cost.

> Welcome Tech Raises $7.5M to Scale AI-Powered Workforce Platform. Funding to accelerate personalized services for immigrants and expand partnerships with U.S. employers across labor-strained industries.
> 
> Share

This funding milestone follows a breakout year for the company. In the last 12 months, Welcome Tech has:

*    Tripled its enterprise partnerships 
*    Achieved over 200% year-over-year revenue growth in its subscription business 
*    Grown to over **4.5 million registered members**, making it the most trusted digital platform for immigrant households in the U.S. 

_“This quarter has marked a meaningful turning point – from closing a highly successful capital raise to launching strategic enterprise partnerships that are now fueling thousands of new subscriber enrollments,” said **Amir Hemmat**, Co-Founder and CEO of Welcome Tech. “This new funding positions us to reach profitability, scale partnerships, and deliver digital-first solutions that unlock economic access for the immigrant workforce that powers our country.”_

**Backed by Mission-Aligned Capital**

The **$7.5M round** was led by institutional investors focused on innovation and impact, including: **TTV Capital, Mubadala Capital, Westbound Equity Partners, CityRock Ventures Partners, Next Legacy Partners**, and **BTN Ventures**.

_“Welcome Tech combines strong business fundamentals with a powerful social impact,” said **Oliver Libby, Managing Partner at CityRock Ventures.** “We believe in their platform and its ability to transform this vital part of the labor market and deliver meaningful solutions to help literally millions of hard workers and their employers across some of the most crucial sectors in the American economy.”_

**Scaling Across Workforce-Critical Sectors**

Welcome Tech’s platform is being rapidly adopted across industries experiencing acute labor shortages—including **hospitality, construction, logistics, manufacturing, and financial services**. Enterprise partners such as **Harri.com, Esusu, Stark Carpet, Instant Financial**, and **Enstructure** are leveraging the platform to recruit eligible workers, offer culturally relevant benefits, and improve workforce retention.

_“TTV Capital invests in companies solving real-world problems – and Welcome Tech is doing just that with a platform that is purpose-built for immigrants. The team has built a powerful, tech-forward solution that aligns directly with our thesis around inclusive financial innovation. We are proud to support Amir and the team as they scale this next phase of growth,” said **Gardiner Garrard, Co-Founder and Managing Partner, TTV Capital**_

**AI-Powered Access and Personalization**

The newly raised capital will support the expansion of Welcome Tech’s AI capabilities. These include automating onboarding, personalizing multi-language service delivery, and intelligently matching members to job and benefit opportunities – enhancing access, reducing cost, and delivering best-in-class user experiences at scale.

_“Westbound backs founders solving hard problems in overlooked markets – and Welcome Tech is a standout example. Amir’s and Raul’s vision for building infrastructure that supports underserved workforces is both socially urgent and commercially smart. We are thrilled to support this next chapter,” said**Will Bumpus, Partner, Westbound Equity Partners**_

_“Welcome Tech is building an amazing platform serving communities of great need and capacity that have historically been overlooked,” said **BTN Ventures general partner Sean Sebastian.** “We’re privileged to be part of their journey enabling millions of immigrant families to fully realize their piece of the American dream.”_

**About Welcome Tech**

Welcome Tech is the digital platform built for immigrants, offering access to essential services including **healthcare, jobs, education**, and **financial tools**. With over **4.5 million registered members** and a growing base of enterprise partners, Welcome Tech enables inclusive economic participation while helping employers recruit, retain, and serve an increasingly diverse workforce.
