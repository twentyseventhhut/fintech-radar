[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇵🇭 Philippine central bank orders e-wallets to drop gambling links. Financial firms have 48 hours to take down icons redirecting to Internet betting, according to Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas Deputy Governor. Senators are tackling proposals to either restrict or ban online gambling amid concerns over debt and addiction.
