# Solfacil authorized by Brazil's central bank

> [!info] 2025-08-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Solfácil, the Brazilian solar solutions ecosystem that includes a fintech arm, was authorized by the Central Bank of Brazil to operate as a financial institution. This designation enables Solfácil to raise funds from the public through Bank Deposit Receipts (CDBs), Bank Deposit Certificates, and Bills of Exchange.
