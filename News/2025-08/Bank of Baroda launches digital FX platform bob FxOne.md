bob FxOne : Introduction
Introducing a powerful digital solution for Bank of Baroda’s customers, an intuitive portal designed to streamline forex and derivative transactions. This application empowers bank’s customers to instantly book exchange rates and book rates for derivative deals in a seamless, user-friendly, and cost-effective manner.
 Customers can: 

 Book exchange rates for their trade & forex transactions.

 Book rates for derivative transactions and hedge currency risk effectively.

 *Product / Service Disclaimer: 
Information provided here about bob FxOne is preliminary and may change at the sole discretion of the Bank without notice. Please contact your relationship branch for details.

bob FxOne : Features
 Real-time FX Rate Access: bob FxOne provides customers with instant and continuous access to live Forex rates for multiple currency pairs.
 Simplified Management of Forex Exposure & Tracking: Customers can efficiently manage their forex exposure and currency risks through bob FxOne without the need for phone calls or branch visits.
 Instant Deal Confirmation: Upon booking transactions, the system immediately generates token, thus providing instant confirmation.
 Comprehensive Transaction Booking: Customer can book exchange rates for Cash, Tom, Spot, Forward, Bills and Fx Option derivative transactions seamlessly, with transparent and competitive pricing.

bob FxOne : FAQs
 1. What is bob FxOne? 
It’s a powerful digital solution for Bank of Baroda’s customers. It’s an intuitive portal designed to streamline forex and derivative transactions.
 2. Who can avail bob FxOne services? 
For details contact Bank of Baroda’s Branch / Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 3. How do I avail bob FxOne services? 
Kindly contact Branch / Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 4. What are the charges for bob FxOne services? 
Currently, bob FxOne access is offered completely free of charge.
 5. What type of bookings does bob FxOne offer? 
Booking of Cash, Tom, Spot, Forward, Bills & FX Option is allowed on bob FxOne platform.
 6. What are the allowed currency pairs? 
The currency pairs available in bob FxOne are determined based on the assessment of Bank of Baroda. The Bank reserves the right to modify the list of permitted currency pairs at its sole discretion from time to time.
 7. Is bob FxOne replacing booking through other modes? 
Purpose of bob FxOne is to complement the current modes of deal booking i.e. through branch or over a call with dealing room. Post booking the rate in bob FxOne, customer needs to follow existing process.
 8. Can multiple users from single corporate book deals on bob FxOne? 
Yes, multiple users from single corporate can login and book deals simultaneously.
 9. How do I opt out of bob FxOne? 
Kindly contact Branch / Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 10. How safe is bob FxOne? 
bob FxOne features enhanced security with the integration of Two-Factor Authentication and Captcha, alongside multiple layers of protection to ensure robust system safety.
 11. I notice some discrepancies in the deal blotter within the bob FxOne application. 
Kindly get in touch with Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 12. How should I proceed if I don't receive a deal confirmation after booking? 
Kindly avoid rebooking the deal and reaching out to your relationship branch.
 13. My login screen is not loading & is stuck at some percentage. 
Kindly try clearing the cache or accessing the application in incognito mode. Further, if still issue persists kindly contact the Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 14. Rates are not visible on the tiles on landing page of bob FxOne application. 
Deal booking for customers is allowed only within market hours. If the issue is being faced during specified market hours, kindly get in touch with the Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 15. How should I proceed if I am not able to login? 
Kindly get in touch with Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch or proceed to take rate over call as per earlier process.
 16. When will I get confirmation of the deal? 
A pop-up notification will appear after deal booking. Moreover, the deal blotter & bell icon in the portal can be used to see booking status.
 17. How should I proceed if a deal is booked erroneously? 
We recommend getting in touch with the Specialized Treasury Branch as no cancellation of the deal would be allowed at your end.
 18. How will I get to know if access to bob FxOne portal is available to me? 
Onboarded users would receive an onboarding mail with necessary details.
 19. What is the process to disable or revoke my portal access? 
Kindly get in touch with Specialized Integrated Treasury Branch.
 20. Can I utilize the booked deal? 
Platform only provides provision to book deals. For utilization or any other needs, kindly get in touch with the related branch.
