# India GST collections grow 7.5% to INR 1.95 lakh crore in July

> [!info] 2025-08-09 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] India's Goods and Services Tax (GST) collections grew 7.5% to INR 1.95 lakh crore ($22bn) in July, with domestic source collections growing 6.7% and import-based collections increasing 9.7%.
