# Deutsche Bank to restrict mobile TAN online banking access

> [!info] 2025-08-26 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 Deutsche Bank warns customers of imminent online banking access restrictions. The move comes as part of Deutsche Bank’s ongoing digital transformation, aiming to replace outdated and vulnerable methods with modern, more secure solutions. For customers who still rely on the traditional mobile TAN procedure, time is running out.

Starting next month, customers of Deutsche Bank will be confronted with a change. Affected users are users of the mobileTAN method used for online banking. So far, customers have been able to use this security method to obtain a transaction number (TAN) via SMS, in order to, among other things, approve transactions. The procedure will be stopped from August 25, 2025, as the financial institution announces on its website. If customers do not act, the worst case scenario is that they could lose online access to the account.

To prevent this from happening, Deutsche Bank customers need to get involved. We will tell you what to do below.

### photoTAN method

Deutsche Bank turns off mobileTAN in August

Picture: picture alliance / dpa Deutsche Bank customers will have to use the photoTAN method in the future. For this to work, an activation letter † is required . The activation letter can be ordered from the bank's online banking section via the Internet browser and, according to the financial institution, should be sent to the postal address within a few days. The activation letter is valid for 30 days after it has been sent and allows you to activate a device using the photoTAN method. If you want to activate more than one device, such as an additional smart phone, you must also activate

If the photoTAN process is already actively used, Deutsche Bank recommends using the process directly in online banking for the next assignment. This gives the user the certainty that he can still use the accounts without the mobileTAN process. If customers use online banking for more than one branch / account number, the institution says that an activated online banking procedure is sufficient. With the end of the mobileTAN process, the activated photoTAN process will become valid for all of the branch / account numbers in online banking.

Note: Another Deutsche Bank information page states that the activation letter for the photoTAN process can be requested last time on August 15, 2025, from online banking (not the app). If the photoTAN process has not been activated before August 21, the "BestSign" security method can be configured after August 25, 2025.

### Enable photoTAN - this is how it works

To activate the photoTAN process, you need the said activation letter. If you have it handy, you are ready to go. What you need now is the corresponding photoTAN app for your handheld, which you can download either from the Google Play Store for Android or from the App Store for iPhone.

You will also need to provide your personal online banking PIN. If you do not have it handy, you can re-order it [](https://www.deutsche-bank.de/pk/service-und-kontakt/services/fragen-antworten/online-banking/wie-bestelle-ich-eine-neue-online-banking-pin.html).

### Setting up Credit Card App

Customers using a Deutsche Bank credit card are currently able to track transactions using the "MyCard" app. The app is also to be turned off in August. From August 25 all important functions of the app will be available in the Deutsche Bank app.

The MyCard app has an option to store digital cash register receipts. According to the provider, these have to be saved by August 22nd, 2025.
