# Indian Non-banking financial companies (NBFCs) encountered funding pressures as bond yields increased

> [!info] 2025-08-31 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Indian Non-banking financial companies (NBFCs) encountered funding pressures as bond yields increased 15 basis points and bank lending slowed, forcing large issuers like Bajaj Finance to delay INR 8,000cr (~$1bn) in bond offerings in FY26.
