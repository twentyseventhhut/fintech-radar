# Bankrupt Linqto to sell stakes in private firms

> [!info] 2025-08-21 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Bankrupt FinTech Linqto Inc to sell hard-to-get stakes in private firms. Linqto did not list which securities it would try to liquidate, but said they would come from a portfolio worth more than $500 million. It had acquired the stakes for itself and its customers before the online investment platform shut down and filed for bankruptcy.
