## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 India's UPI system faces backlash as small vendors receive GST notices based on digital transaction data. This has led to a shift back to cash transactions, with vendors fearing tax scrutiny. Concerns rise that aggressive formalisation efforts may push the informal economy further underground, undermining the goals of financial inclusion and transparency.
