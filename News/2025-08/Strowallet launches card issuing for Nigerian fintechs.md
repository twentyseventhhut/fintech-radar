# Strowallet launches card issuing for Nigerian fintechs

> [!info] 2025-08-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇬 Strowallet launches card issuing solution for Nigerian FinTechs and MFBs. The new platform allows partners to issue Physical Naira ATM Cards for cash withdrawals and in-store payments, Virtual Naira Cards for secure local online transactions, and Virtual Dollar Cards for international payments, subscriptions, and e-commerce.
