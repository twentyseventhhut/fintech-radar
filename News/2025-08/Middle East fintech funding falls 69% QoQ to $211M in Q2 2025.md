# Middle East fintech funding falls 69% QoQ to $211M in Q2 2025

> [!info] 2025-08-27 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Middle Eastern FinTech funding plummeted by 69% QoQ in Q2 as investors grew cautious. Q2 2025 market compared to the previous quarter experienced a slowdown, where deal volume fell by 36% from 25 in Q1 2025 to 16 in Q2 2025, while total funding dropped by 69% from $685m in Q1 2025 to $211.1m in Q2 2025.
