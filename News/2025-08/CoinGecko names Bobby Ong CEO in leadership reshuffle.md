# CoinGecko Strengthens Leadership with New CEO, President, and CTO

 4.2 

 | by 

CoinGecko -  Updated August 18 2025 

(L-R: Cedric Chan, TM Lee, Bobby Ong)

We’re excited to announce a series of leadership transitions at CoinGecko, marking an important step in our journey from a founder-led startup to a globally trusted crypto data infrastructure provider. These changes will help us scale our enterprise offerings, expand our developer ecosystem, and continue delivering reliable, high-quality data to millions of users worldwide.

* * *

## Bobby Ong Appointed CEO, TM Lee Transitions to President

Our co-founder and COO, **Bobby Ong**, is stepping into the role of Chief Executive Officer, succeeding fellow co-founder and former CEO,**TM Lee**, who will transition to President.

After more than a decade as COO, Bobby has played a pivotal role in shaping CoinGecko’s business strategy, scaling operations, and expanding our global presence. Under his leadership, we’ve grown into a platform relied upon daily by millions of users worldwide, with our crypto data API powering exchanges, wallets, and financial institutions across both web3 and traditional finance.

> "TM and I started CoinGecko with a shared vision to empower the decentralized future. It’s been a privilege to build alongside him from day one, cultivating a user-first culture rooted in transparency and integrity. These values will continue to guide us forward."
> 
> — Bobby Ong, CEO of CoinGecko

As President, TM will advance our long-term product vision and lead zero-to-one R&D initiatives, driving foundational innovation to keep CoinGecko at the forefront of market leadership and shape the next decade of crypto.

## Appointment of New CTO and Head of Product

**Cedric Chan** will be appointed Chief Technology Officer, where he will lead CoinGecko’s technical roadmap and infrastructure development.

Since joining as Head of Engineering in 2021, Chan has strengthened CoinGecko’s reliability, security and compliance controls. These improvements have propelled CoinGecko’s API growth to surpass 677 billion requests in the past year, with thousands of customers onboarded since launch. Chan also established key functions and leadership roles in data, site reliability, and software testing, creating the framework to scale and institutionalize technical operations.

> "Under Cedric’s leadership, CoinGecko has delivered enterprise-grade reliability and scalability, achieving a 99.95% annual uptime SLA, accelerating recovery times, and unlocking greater data accessibility across the organization."
> 
> — Bobby Ong, CEO of CoinGecko

As CTO, Cedric will lead our technical roadmap and infrastructure development, forge key technology partnerships, embed AI into operations, and design the next generation of scalable data systems to meet the rising demand for real-time, high-fidelity crypto data.

In parallel, **Xingyi Ho** has been appointed Head of Product, transitioning from his role as Head of Growth. Since joining CoinGecko in 2022, Xingyi has pioneered new growth functions, including product growth and analytics, growth marketing, and SEO. In his new role, he will drive product strategy across our offerings and ensure our roadmap stays aligned with evolving user needs.

## What’s Next for the CoinGecko Community

With these leadership changes, we’re stepping into an exciting new chapter — one where we’ll push the boundaries of innovation, expand our capabilities, and uphold our commitment to being your trusted crypto companion and the most comprehensive source of crypto data worldwide.

As always, thank you for being a part of our community. Here’s to the road ahead!
