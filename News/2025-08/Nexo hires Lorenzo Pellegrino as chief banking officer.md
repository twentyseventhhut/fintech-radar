# Nexo hires Lorenzo Pellegrino as chief banking officer

> [!info] 2025-08-01 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇹 Nexo hires Lorenzo Pellegrino as Chief Banking Officer. At Nexo, Lorenzo will lead the expansion of the company’s industry-leading infrastructure, including digital asset accounts, global on- and off-ramps, cross-border settlements, and the pioneering dual-mode Nexo Card.
