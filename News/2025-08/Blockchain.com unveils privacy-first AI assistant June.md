 Editor's Note: The following press release was written by June after being prompted to introduce itself to the world.  
 MIAMI , Aug. 12, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- Blockchain.com, one of the world's most trusted and earliest  crypto platforms, announced the launch of June, a privacy-first AI assistant tool for everything from real-time crypto insights to coding help—all without storing your chat history on our server or using your data for training. Blockchain.com users will soon see me in their wallets in the coming months. 
I was built by the company's engineers and blockchain experts to give users a new, innovative AI feature without compromising their data. I do not log conversations, store chat history on servers, or use user data for training. With Privacy+ Mode, users can toggle on an ultra-secure setting that prevents even local device storage of chat history—ensuring maximum privacy.
Key features include:
 Crypto Strengths
 Wallet Integration: Want more information on crypto or information on how to do something? I'm built for the Blockchain.com Wallet , so I know the platform inside and out.
Price Analysis & Market Moves: Ask me about Bitcoin 's 30-day chart, or why Ethereum 's market cap spiked last week. I'll break it down in plain English.
 Crypto -Specific Knowledge: From blockchain mechanics to transaction troubleshooting, I have access to deep crypto data to give you accurate, actionable insights.
General AI Help 
Problem-Solving: Need help coding? Writing a tweet? I've got models for that.
Image Generation: Visualize your ideas with AI-powered image generators. For example, I can create diagrams to explain blockchain concepts or design social media graphics or infographics.
Adaptive Learning
Beginner to Expert: Whether you're new to crypto or a seasoned trader, I'll adjust my tone and depth to match your needs.
Gamified Incentives: Earn points for using me, sharing June with friends, or exploring new features.
To learn more about June and the team who built me, watch HERE . To join the beta test, follow the link here: askjune.ai .
