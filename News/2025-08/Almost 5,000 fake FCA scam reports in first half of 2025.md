## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Almost 5,000 fake FCA scam reports in the first six months of 2025. Scammers aim to steal money by getting people to hand over funds or sensitive information, such as bank account PINs and passwords. 480 victims were duped into sending money to the fraudster. The majority, almost two-thirds, of reports came from people 56 years old or above.
