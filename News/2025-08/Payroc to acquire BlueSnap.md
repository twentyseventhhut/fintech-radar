# Payroc to acquire BlueSnap

> [!info] 2025-08-03 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Payroc to purchase payment orchestrator BlueSnap. The acquisition aims to expand Payroc's service offerings by incorporating BlueSnap’s global and enterprise capabilities. Payroc said the integration will facilitate the acceptance of various payment methods, including card-not-present transactions and electronic checks.

[This Week in Fintech] Payrocsigned a definitive agreement to acquire BlueSnap, a payment orchestration and AR‑automation platform, enhancing its global and enterprise payment capabilities; the transaction is expected to close in Q3 2025, subject to approvals.
