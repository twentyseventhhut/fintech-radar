## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Dutch online bank Bunq fined for failures in money laundering controls. DNB said Bunq was fined in May for "serious shortcomings" in four cases between January 2021 and May 2022, in which it had failed to properly investigate and report signs of possible financial crimes.
