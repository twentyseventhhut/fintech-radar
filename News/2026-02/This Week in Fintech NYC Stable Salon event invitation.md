Stablecon Salon Modern Treasury Edition: Stablecoins as a First-Class Rail
​ Stablecoins aren't a crypto strategy – they’re a payments strategy.  
​ Join Modern Treasury, Paxos, and Circle for a Stablecoin Salon on what it actually takes to operate stablecoins as a first-class payment rail alongside ACH, wires, RTP, and FedNow.
​ Panel:
 ​ Dimitri Dadiomov | President & Co-Founder, Modern Treasury 
 ​ Nick Robnett | Head of Crypto Business, Paxos​ 
 ​ Keith Mandell | Sr. Director Enterprise Development, Circle
