# PayPay plans Nasdaq IPO at $19.6B valuation

> [!info] 2026-02-13 · 3 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇵 Japan's PayPay app eyes Nasdaq in $19.6bn IPO in March. The company will submit registration documents to the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission this month. It will then sound out institutional investors through a book-building process based on an indicative price range before setting the final offer price.

[This Week in Fintech] PayPay, Japan's digital payments company, planned its Initial Public Offering (IPO) on Nasdaq at a valuation of $19.6bn in March 2026.

[This Week in Fintech] PayPayfiled for a US IPO, as the SoftBank-backed Japanese payments app has over 70 million users (ticker: PAYP).
