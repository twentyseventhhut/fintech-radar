## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Chrysalis looks to drop managers as UK FinTech fund sells assets. The board plans to divest Chrysalis’ holdings and return cash to shareholders over the next few years, it said. As part of the move, the board is looking to end the current advisory arrangement with its investment manager, which receives £4.5 million ($6.1 million) per year.
