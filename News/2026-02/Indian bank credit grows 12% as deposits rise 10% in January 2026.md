# Indian bank credit grows 12% as deposits rise 10% in January 2026

> [!info] 2026-02-23 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Indian banks saw credit growth of 12% YoY to ₹22.3 lakh crore ($245bn) while deposits grew 10% to INR 23 lakh crore ($253bn) as of Jan, 2026 with the incremental credit-deposit ratio reaching 97%.
