MariBank links remittances to Weixin Pay via TenPay Global
 
 Users can send salaries and savings via MariBank Overseas Transfers. 
 MariBank has partnered with TenPay Global , Tencent’s cross-border payment platform, to offer remittances to Chinese e-wallet Weixin Pay.
MariBank is the first Singaporean digital bank to offer this service, it said in a press release on 13 February 2026.
Users can send salaries and savings directly to their own or close relatives’ Weixin Pay accounts through MariBank Overseas Transfers, the digital bank’s remittance service.
The bank promises highly competitive exchange rates and even zero transfer fees on a promotional basis.
The remittance service follows MariBank observing “significant growth” in its Chinese Yuan volumes, which the bank attributed to competitive exchange rates and a S$0 transfer fee promotion.
MariBank Overseas Transfers allows users to transfer funds to over 40 countries in 20 currencies almost instantly or within one to three business days.
