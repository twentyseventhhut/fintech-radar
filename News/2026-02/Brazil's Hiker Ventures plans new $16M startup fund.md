# Brazil's Hiker Ventures plans new $16M startup fund

> [!info] 2026-02-09 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Hiker Ventures, the Brazilian venture capital firm backed by M&A boutique Araújo Fontes, plans to launch a new $16 million fund in 2026. The fund aims to build a portfolio of 15–20 startups, with a sector-agnostic strategy and a focus on fintech infrastructure, healthcare, and AI-driven productivity solutions.
