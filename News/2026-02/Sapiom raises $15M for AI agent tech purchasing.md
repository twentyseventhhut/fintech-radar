# Sapiom raises $15M for AI agent tech purchasing

> [!info] 2026-02-05 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, TechCrunch — Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Sapiom raises $15M to help AI agents buy their own tech tools. The platform aims to simplify how nontechnical creators connect AI-built apps to paid external services like SMS, email, or payments by enabling seamless authentication and micropayments.

[TechCrunch — Fintech] Last summer, Zerbib launched Sapiom, a San Francisco startup developing the financial layer that allows AI agents to securely purchase and access software, APIs, data, and compute — essentially creating a payment system that lets AI automatically buy the services it needs.
