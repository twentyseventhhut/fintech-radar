# Ruvo raises $4.6M to modernize Brazil-US remittances

> [!info] 2026-02-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Ruvo raises US$4.6 million to modernize remittances between Brazil and the US. The company operates like a US dollar account, allowing Brazilians to receive payments, make transfers, spend, and invest as if they were based in the United States. The platform brings together Pix, stablecoins, bank transfers (ACH/wire), and Visa in a single application.
