# RedotPay explores US IPO at $4 billion valuation

> [!info] 2026-02-25 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 HK-based stablecoin payments firm RedotPay is said to consider a $1 billion US IPO. RedotPay is working with JPMorgan Chase & Co., Goldman Sachs Group Inc., and Jefferies Financial Group Inc. on a listing that could take place in New York as soon as this year.

[This Week in Fintech] Hong Kong-based stablecoin payments fintech RedotPay reportedly began exploring a U.S. initial public offering at a $4 billion valuation, working with major banks to target a $1 billion raise.
