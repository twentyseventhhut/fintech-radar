# Tether aided Turkey freezing over $500M in assets

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇹🇷 Crypto giant Tether aided Turkey in a billion-dollar crackdown. Turkish authorities have frozen over $500 million in assets linked to Veysel Sahin, who is accused of operating illegal betting platforms and laundering proceeds. Tether carried out the freeze at Turkey’s request as part of broader efforts to combat crypto-related crime.
