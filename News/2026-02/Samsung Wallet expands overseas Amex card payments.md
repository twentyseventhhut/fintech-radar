Samsung Electronics announced on the 3rd that it will add American Express (Amex) cards to cards that can be paid overseas through "Samsung Wallet" from the 4th.
Existing Samsung Wallet has supported Mastercard and Visa payments overseas, and Amex card users can now use overseas payments through Samsung Wallet without a physical card.
Payment through Samsung Wallet is available at Amex merchants worldwide that support NFC (contactless payment).
Based on cooperation with Amex, Samsung Electronics plans to expand the scope of Samsung Wallet's global payment support and further strengthen its overseas market competitiveness.
"We plan to strengthen the global usability of Samsung Wallet and provide differentiated services to premium card users through the addition of overseas payment support for Amex cards," said Chae Won-seok, head of Samsung Electronics' MX division digital wallet team.
