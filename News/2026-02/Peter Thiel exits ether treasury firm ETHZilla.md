## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Tech billionaire Peter Thiel exits Ether Treasury firm ETHZilla. Entities managed by Thiel now report owning zero shares in the company, the filing released late Tuesday shows. ETHZilla is a digital asset treasury, a company that primarily holds cryptocurrencies, and raises funds by selling investors stocks and bonds.
