# Slash Announces Collaboration with Visa DPS to Power Scalable, Reliable Card Issuing Platform

Slash announces its collaboration with Visa DPS to power a more reliable, scalable card issuing platform with stronger controls and clearer data for growing teams.

San Francisco, CA, Feb. 25, 2026, **Slash announced it has brought greater reliability, customization, and scale to its card program through the implementation of Visa DPS**, a leading card processing and digital payment solution. As part of its collaboration with Visa, a world leader in digital payments, the move gives Slash customers stronger controls and clearer data, with the capacity to scale alongside expanding transaction volume.

Since implementing Visa DPS in November 2023, Slash has delivered faster, more accurate reporting and a better cardholder experience. Additionally, Slash’s customer base has expanded from predominantly early operators and sole proprietors to teams across multiple industries. That growth requires a card-issuing stack designed for scale and resilience. Visa DPS provides modern APIs and granular authorization controls that help Slash deliver consistent performance without compromising on security or visibility. Enhanced Visa DPS analytics with daily interchange provide customers with clearer accounting and reconciliation.

By working with Visa , Slash is able to extend additional benefits to its customers, including:

**• Improved dispute resolution** through Visa Dispute Management Service.

**• Richer transaction insights** with data from Visa Business Data Solutions.

**• Support for enhanced rewards programs** through Visa Card Program Enrollment APIs.

These improvements ensure that Slash customers experience a secure, reliable card1 issuing and banking platform2 with better visibility into their financial operations.

_“At Slash, we want to work as close to the metal as possible. Processing directly with Visa gives us easier access to improved economics and the broader suite of Visa products, allowing us to provide the best experience to our cardholders,” said Victor Cardenas, Slash CEO._
