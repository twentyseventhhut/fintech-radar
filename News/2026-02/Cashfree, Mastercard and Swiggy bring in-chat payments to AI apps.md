# Cashfree, Mastercard and Swiggy bring in-chat payments to AI apps in India

## Cashfree Payments launched Cashfree Here with Mastercard and Swiggy, enabling UPI and card payments within AI chats like ChatGPT and Claude, unveiled at India AI Impact Summit 2026.

ADVERTISEMENT

ByAnshulFebruary 17, 2026, 4:08:07 PM IST (Published)

2 Min Read

[](https://www.cnbctv18.com/impact-shorts/cashfree-mastercard-swiggy-bring-in-chat-payments-ai-apps-in-india-impact-summit-ws-el-19852018.htm)

[](https://www.google.com/preferences/source?q=cnbctv18.com "CNBCTV18 on Google")[](https://api.whatsapp.com/send?text=Cashfree%2C%20Mastercard%20and%20Swiggy%20bring%20in-chat%20payments%20to%20AI%20apps%20in%20India%20https%3A%2F%2Fwww.cnbctv18.com%2Fpersonal-finance%2Fcashfree-mastercard-swiggy-bring-in-chat-payments-ai-apps-in-india-impact-summit-ws-el-19852018.htm "Cashfree, Mastercard and Swiggy bring in-chat payments to AI apps in India")

Cashfree Payments on Tuesday, February 17, launched “Cashfree Here”, an in-chat payments extension developed in collaboration with Mastercard and Swiggy, enabling businesses to accept UPI and card payments directly within AI applications such as OpenAI’s ChatGPT and Anthropic’s Claude.

 Unveiled at the India AI Impact Summit 2026, the solution allows users to complete transactions inside conversational interfaces without being redirected to external payment pages. The company said the extension addresses a key gap in AI-led commerce, where discovery and recommendations happen within chat, but payments typically require switching platforms.

 The offering supports UPI and card payments embedded natively into AI conversations built on OpenAI’s Apps SDK and Anthropic’s MCP framework. For card transactions, Mastercard enables passkey-based biometric authentication, replacing traditional OTP-based verification with device-level biometric validation.

 For consumers, the integration could mean completing purchases—such as food delivery or other services—within the same AI chat window where they search and decide.

 For businesses, the company said it reduces integration complexity and may help improve conversion rates by lowering checkout friction.

 Cashfree said the system is PCI-compliant and built on its existing payments infrastructure. The launch follows its earlier rollout of “Agentic Payments”, which allows AI agents to manage payment conversations on behalf of merchants.

 Founded in 2015, Cashfree processes about $80 billion in transactions annually for over one million businesses. It is authorised by the Reserve Bank of India to operate as a payment aggregator for domestic and cross-border payments.

 As conversational AI gains traction across commerce use cases, embedding payments directly into chat interfaces could become a key development in India’s digital payments landscape.

Cashfree Payments Rolls Out In-Chat Payments to AI Apps with Mastercard and Swiggy 
 Cashfree Payments on Tuesday, February 17, introduced “Cashfree Here”, an in-chat payments extension built with Mastercard and  Swiggy  that allows users to complete UPI and card payments directly inside AI chat applications, as per news reports.   
 AI Chat Platforms Get Native Checkout Layer   
 The new solution was unveiled at the India AI Impact Summit 2026 and is designed to let users finish transactions within conversational interfaces instead of moving to separate checkout pages.   
 The company said this targets a structural gap in AI-driven commerce flows, where product discovery and decision-making happen inside chat, but payments usually require users to exit the interface.   
 Cashfree Here supports embedded UPI and card payments inside AI environments developed on OpenAI’s Apps SDK and Anthropic’s MCP framework, enabling merchants to plug payments into chat-based journeys.   
 Security, Use Cases and Business Impact   
 For card transactions, Mastercard provides passkey-based biometric authentication, replacing OTP flows with device-level biometric validation for user approval.   
 For customers, this means purchases such as food orders and service bookings can be completed within the same AI chat window where queries and recommendations occur.   
 For businesses, Cashfree said the embedded model simplifies integration and can improve checkout completion by reducing payment friction. The system runs on the company’s existing payments stack and is PCI-compliant.

 The launch adds a native payments layer to conversational AI commerce, extending Cashfree’s push into AI-led transaction flows.
