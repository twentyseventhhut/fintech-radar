[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Bank of America finally discloses XRP exposure. A recent SEC Form 13F filing suggests Bank of America has indirect exposure to XRP through shares in a Volatility Shares product labeled as an “XRP ETF.” While it does not show direct XRP custody, the disclosure highlights institutional involvement via regulated exchange-traded instruments.
