Aeon Bank CEO Raja Teh Maimunah to step down on March 31 

KUALA LUMPUR: Aeon Bank (M) Bhd has announced that its chief executive officer Raja Datin Paduka Teh Maimunah will be stepping down effective from March 31.
Raja Teh joined the bank three years ago and steered Aeon Bank through its inception as a digital bank.
Under her leadership, the bank became Malaysia's first digital Islamic bank, successfully building its cloud-native core infrastructure and securing the necessary regulatory approvals for a full-scale public launch.
"On behalf of the board, I would like to thank Raja Teh for her visionary leadership. Taking a bank from a conceptual stage to a fully functional, live digital ecosystem is a monumental task.
"She has built a rock-solid foundation for Aeon Bank, and we are now well-positioned to scale our operations and continue our mission of financial inclusion," said Aeon bank chairman and independent non-executive Tomokatsu Yoshitoshi.
Raja Teh said her tenure at the bank was a "profound honour and a once-in-a-lifetime privilege"
"We have made history together by establishing the nation's first digital Islamic bank. I am incredibly proud of the infrastructure we have built and the talented team we have assembled. I leave with full confidence that Aeon Bank will continue to thrive," she said.
