# Araxi to acquire 80% of Pay@ Group for R1 billion

> [!info] 2026-02-19 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇿🇦 Araxi has agreed to acquire an 80% stake in Pay@ Holdings and its affiliate IHPL for R1 billion through its subsidiary African Resonance. Araxi said that the acquisition of the Pay@ Group will significantly strengthen its payment capabilities, enabling the group to deliver an expanded, more competitive offering to customers.
