## TerraPay Partners with Banco Ganadero to Enable 24/7 Real-Time Cross-border Payments Processing in Bolivia

February 20, 2026|News

TerraPay, a global money movement company, has announced a strategic partnership with Banco Ganadero S.A., one of Bolivia’s leading financial institutions, to enable 24/7 real-time payments processing across the country.

Through this collaboration, Bolivians living abroad can send funds to Bolivia quickly, securely, and with real-time availability. Payments can be credited to any bank in Bolivia through the ACH system, as well as to Banco Ganadero accounts, mobile wallets, and YoloPago, Banco Ganadero’s digital wallet, offering multiple and convenient payout options.

Remittances play a critical role in Bolivia’s economy and in supporting millions of families nationwide. With nearly two million Bolivians residing abroad, this partnership strengthens key remittance corridors including Spain, the United States, Chile, Argentina, Switzerland, and Brazil, ensuring faster and more reliable access to funds for recipients in Bolivia.

“Remittances continue to play a vital role in Bolivia’s economy, with officially recorded inflows reachingUS $1.29 billion in 2024, according to World Bank‑based estimates and this is only increasing as we begin 2026. At a time when thousands of Bolivians abroad rely on fast, dependable ways to support their families back home, our partnership with Banco Ganadero delivers exactly what the market needs – real‑time payments, instant availability across all banks via ACH, and seamless payouts to mobile wallets” – said Hassan Chatila, VP, Global Head of Network at TerraPay.

By combining TerraPay’s global interoperability with Banco Ganadero’s strong domestic reach, we are making cross‑border transfers significantly faster, more transparent, and more accessible for Bolivian communities worldwide.”

Remittances account for an estimated 2–3% of Bolivia’s GDP, underlining their importance to the national economy. This partnership reflects the shared commitment of TerraPay and Banco Ganadero to innovation, financial inclusion, and the continuous improvement of cross-border payment services.

### About Banco Ganadero S.A.

Banco Ganadero S.A. is a leading financial institution in Bolivia, authorized by the Superintendency of Banks and Financial Institutions in 1994. The bank actively promotes domestic savings, facilitates financing, and supports the development of national enterprises across sectors including services, commerce, industry, agriculture, and foreign trade. Its modern banking style and conservative policies ensure strength, solvency, and customer satisfaction.

### About TerraPay

TerraPay simplifies global money movement, providing a single connection to one of the most expansive cross-border payment networks, regulated across multiple markets. Our network enables payments to receiving and sending countries worldwide, reaching a vast network of mobile wallets, bank accounts, and cards.​

​On a mission to create a borderless financial world, TerraPay makes money transfers instant, reliable, transparent, and fully compliant for its partners, connecting them to 3.7Bn+ mobile wallets, 7.5Bn+ bank accounts, in 156+ countries.We work behind the scenes as the trusted partners for some of the world’s most innovative financial players, from banks and digital wallets to MTOs, corporates and fintech platforms.​

​Since its founding, TerraPay has built a global interoperable wallet network that advances financial inclusion, even in hard-to-reach markets. TerraPay is headquartered in London, with offices in cities including, Dubai, Milan, Miami, Singapore, Bogota, Johannesburg, Kampala, Bangalore.
