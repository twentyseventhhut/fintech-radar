Merchant Rentals Partners with FreedomPay to Deliver Flexible Financing Solutions for Merchants
 
 3rd February, 2026: Merchant Rentals, part of the PayPoint Group, has announced a new partnership with FreedomPay, a global leader in Next Level Commerce™ solutions. 
 By collaborating with Merchant Rentals, a leading provider of flexible financing and rental options for point-of-sale (POS) payment terminals, FreedomPay can now offer merchants access to tailored leasing and financing options for POS technology, meeting growing demand for flexibility and affordability in the retail and hospitality sectors.
 Through this partnership, FreedomPay expands its offering to merchants, powered by Merchant Rentals’ market leading expertise in short-term hire and long-term finance. 
The agreement, signed in December, underscores Merchant Rentals’ commitment to supporting introducers and their clients with innovative financing solutions.  The partnership is already delivering results, with the first joint client now live across more than 1,000 terminals. 
 This relationship represents significant growth for Merchant Rentals and reinforces its position as a trusted provider of flexible payment technology solutions.
