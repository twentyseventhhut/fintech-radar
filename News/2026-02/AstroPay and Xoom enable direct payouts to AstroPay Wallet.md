AstroPay and Xoom Enable Direct Payouts to the AstroPay Wallet
AstroPay and Xoom Bring Payouts Directly to the AstroPay Wallet
LONDON, February 10th, 2026 – AstroPay , a global digital wallet and payments platform, today announced a new collaboration with Xoom, a PayPal service, to enable Xoom users to send money directly to the AstroPay wallet. The new payout option expands AstroPay’s access to fast and affordable cross border remittances, starting with Colombia , with plans to expand to additional countries across Latin America and Europe.
With this integration, Xoom users can choose the AstroPay wallet as their destination directly from the Xoom website or app. Funds are delivered quickly, providing a convenient solution for users who rely on digital wallets to receive, store, and spend money locally.
 “This integration with Xoom represents a major milestone for AstroPay and validates our role as a trusted global wallet for cross border payments,” said Diego Steinberg, COO at AstroPay. “By enabling Xoom payouts to the AstroPay wallet, we are making it easier for millions of users to access their money quickly while supporting the growing demand for efficient remittance solutions. ”
The integration went live on February 4th, 2026, with the first phase focused on remittance corridors in Colombia. Additional markets in Latin America and Europe are expected to follow as the partnership expands.
 “For Xoom users, flexibility and speed are essential,” said Arturo Picicci, Vice President of Xoom, a PayPal service. “By adding AstroPay as a payout option, we are giving our customers another fast and reliable way to receive funds where and how they need them.” 
The partnership marks AstroPay’s first major launch within the remittance vertical and strengthens its position as a scalable payout solution for global money transfer companies.
Key benefits of the integration include:
Fast payouts to the AstroPay wallet
Low-cost cross-border remittances
One integration to reach millions of AstroPay users
Scalable infrastructure for global payout expansion
This collaboration reinforces AstroPay’s commitment to simplifying global money movement and supporting partners that serve cross border users worldwide.
A pioneer in digital remittances, Xoom is a fast and easy way to send money, pay bills and reload phones for friends and family in approximately 160 countries globally. With Xoom, customers can access a fast and reliable way to send money, by simply downloading the Xoom or PayPal mobile app, or visiting Xoom.com or PayPal.com .
