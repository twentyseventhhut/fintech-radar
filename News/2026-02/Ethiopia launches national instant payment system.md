## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇹 Ethiopia launches national instant payments. EthSwitch now connects 32 banks, 12 MFIs, three PSO and three PII, providing modern card-switch hosting, centralized automated reconciliation, and modern card and shared e-wallet services to its members.

[This Week in Fintech] Ethiopia activated its new National Instant Payment System (IPS), a 24/7 interoperable gateway designed to enable immediate account-to-account transfers.
