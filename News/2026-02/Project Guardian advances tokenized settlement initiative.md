GUARDIAN
 

Guardian is a collaborative initiative between policymakers and the financial industry to enhance liquidity and efficiency of financial markets through asset tokenisation.
It serves as a cross-border sandbox program that brings together an ecosystem of financial institutions, industry associations, central banks and regulators. The initiative actively supports industry trials involving multiple currencies and across various financial products, including tokenised funds, bonds, stablecoins and bank liabilities.
 
Objectives
 
 
 Establish standardised protocols and processes across asset classes (AWM, Fixed Income, FX) to ensure seamless interoperability in token issuance, trading, and settlement across different platforms and jurisdictions.
 Develop comprehensive risk management and regulatory frameworks that address custody, settlement finality, compliance requirements and maintaining robust security measures and operational resilience.
 Foster cross-border implementations, enabling efficient settlement and clearing across jurisdictions while maintaining regulatory alignment.

Use Cases
Multiple industry trials have been conducted under Guardian across asset and wealth management, fixed income, and foreign exchange. These efforts have resulted in a number of live use cases, demonstrating how asset tokenisation can be integrated with existing market infrastructure. The live implementations offer tangible evidence of commercial viability, operational resilience, and alignment with regulatory requirements. Examples include Ant Group, Apollo, DBS, Franklin Templeton, Hamilton Lane, OCBC and UBS Group.

Members
Guardian brings together a diverse group of industry participants, including custodians, fund managers, banks, market infrastructure providers, and industry associations, to collectively shape the future of digital finance. Through active participation in workstreams and live pilots, members contribute to the advancement of industry standards, scalable use cases, and next-generation shared ledger infrastructure.

Guardian Publications
To support the development of foundational infrastructure for asset tokenisation, Guardian has developed a set of frameworks for designing open and interoperable digital asset networks, models for cross-ledger integration, and practical guidance for implementing tokenisation across asset classes.
Developed in close collaboration with financial institutions, industry associations, and international standard-setters, these frameworks and corresponding publications serve as reference materials to guide market participants, in navigating legal, operational, and technical considerations as they adopt tokenised financial services.

 Important Notice 

 Members of the public should note that: 
MAS and companies participating in MAS' initiatives will not ask for your personal banking information, security login credentials or private keys to digital wallets.
MAS advises the public to be alert and wary of unsolicited calls or messaging which offer deals that seem too good to be true, request personal information, give instruction to transfer funds to a third party account or offer investments into projects.
If you encounter any suspected scams related to digital assets involving MAS, please DO NOT transfer any funds, and report the matter to MAS .
For impersonation scams involving institutions regulated by MAS or phishing scams, please make a police report via the Police Hotline at 1800-255-0000, or submit it online at www.police.gov.sg/iwitness .
