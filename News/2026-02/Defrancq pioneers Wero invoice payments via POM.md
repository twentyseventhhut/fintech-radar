Defrancq pioneers Wero invoice payments via POM
 Antwerpen, 12 February 2026 — Defrancq is the first company in Belgium to enable customers to pay invoices via Wero, the new European payment solution and successor to Payconiq. To make this possible, Defrancq is working together with Belgian payment service provider POM. A key advantage compared to other payment methods is that Wero allows higher transaction amounts. Until recently, the use of Wero in Belgium was limited to person-to-person payments. Thanks to recent developments, Wero is now also available for payments to businesses.
 “At POM, we have spent the past months intensively preparing for the arrival of Wero. By getting everything technically and operationally ready in advance, we were able to bring the first merchant live as soon as the first banks were ready.” 
Tom Totté
Co-Founder of POM
At present, Wero payments to businesses are available for customers of ING and KBC. The other Belgian banks will follow later this year.
 “At Defrancq, we embrace Wero as the logical next step in digital payments. Especially for higher invoice amounts, Wero offers clear added value thanks to higher payment limits and a smooth, secure payment experience. Being able to offer this today via POM fits perfectly with our vision of innovation and customer focus.” 
Jérome Bridelance
Operations Director at Defrancq
 “ Wero is designed to make European payments simpler, stronger and more independent. Its expansion beyond person-to-person payments into business and invoice transactions represents a key step in scaling a trusted European payment solution that delivers tangible value to merchants and consumers across Europe. ”  
Martina Weimert
CEO of EPI
With this first go-live, POM once again confirms its pioneering role in the payment sector and, together with Defrancq, takes an important step towards the future of digital and European payments.
 About POM 
 POM (short for peace of mind) develops digital solutions for invoicing, payments and accounts receivable management. POM offers a SaaS platform that enables organizations to automate their entire invoice-to-cash process, including the issuance of invoices, the facilitation of payments via payment links and QR codes, and the follow-up of outstanding amounts through machine learning and AI-driven workflows.
 About EPI 
 EPI (or European Payments Initiative) is supported by 16 European banks and payment service providers. Beyond the shareholders, 27 institutions in Europe are now members of EPI. They have joined forces with a common goal: to offer a unified mobile payment service, to all European companies and citizens, Wero . EPI intends to enable European consumers and merchants and to carry out all types of retail transactions simply, via a resolutely sovereign digital wallet. Find out more at epicompany.eu and follow us on  LinkedIn .  
 About Wero 
Based on instant account-to-account (A2A) payments, Wero further streamlines payments in Europe by eliminating intermediaries in the payment chain and the associated additional costs. Wero already supports payments between individuals, and will shortly unveil payments to professionals (P2Pro). Wero is currently launching online and mobile purchases in Germany. Point-of-sale payments will also be offered in 2026, as well as value-added services such as integration of merchant loyalty programs and recurring payments and subscription management. Find out more at wero-wallet.eu and follow us on  Instagram and  Facebook
