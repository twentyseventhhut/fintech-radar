México Expansion Series by BGBG and Stripe @ Mexico Fintech Week 2026
​ Calling international founders, investors, and operators exploring Mexico. 
​ The México Expansion Series presented by BGBG and Stripe , brings together global builders and local operators to unpack why Mexico has become one of the most compelling fintech expansion markets today.
​ Designed as a practical, operator-led conversation, the focus is on real execution — from regulation and go-to-market to on-the-ground building beyond the headlines.
​ The main conversation: Mexico’s Next Fintech Chapter 
​ Mexico’s fintech ecosystem has moved from early experimentation to global relevance. These three panels trace that evolution — from how the market was built, to why global players are entering now, to how new financial models are being created and scaled.
​ Panel 1 — Winning Mexico : Lessons from the operators who helped shape the banking and wallet ecosystem over the past decade.
 ​ Iván Canales - Former General Manager Mexico, Nu 
 ​ Juan Guerra - CEO México, Revolut 
 ​ Moderator : Nik Milanović - CEO, TWIF & Stablecon 
​ Panel 2 — Why Mexico, Why Now? : Why global fintech leaders are choosing Mexico as a core expansion market in 2026.
 ​ Jørn Lyseggen - CEO, BMONI (Nigeria - Africa) 
 ​ Olivia Hu - VP International Strategy & Investments, Lian Lian (China - Asia) 
 ​ Lukas Zorich - CTO & Co-fundador, FIntoc (Chile - LATAM) 
 ​ Moderator : Rene Salazar - Head of LATAM, Stripe 
​ Panel 3 — The Next 100 Million Frontier: A deep dive into credit as a case study, and how new asset-backed models are being built at scale.
 ​ Nicolás Schiaffino - VP & Country Manager - PayJoy 
 ​ Moderator : Salvador Leal - Global Head of Comms - Bitso   
​ Agenda  
 ​ 5:00 pm – Arrival & welcome networking 
 ​ 6:30 pm – Curated conversations 
 ​ 8:00 pm – Networking & cocktails 
​ Location 
 ​ Stripe HQ, Mexico City - Torre Punta Reforma - Reforma 180 - Floor 29, Juárez, Cuauhtémoc, 06600, Mexico City, Mexico 
 ​ At the Lobby, find the check-in desk at the main entrance to get access to Floor 29.  
 ​ Paid parking will be available directly in front of the venue. 
​ Meet our Partners 
​ We are proud to be supported by BGBG and Stripe , two pillars of the modern financial ecosystem. BGBG provides the essential legal and regulatory framework for navigating the complex Mexican fintech landscape, while Stripe delivers the world-class payments infrastructure needed to scale globally.
