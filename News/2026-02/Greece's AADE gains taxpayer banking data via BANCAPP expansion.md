## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇷 AADE gains access to Revolut, Viva, and foreign banks via BANCAPP. Greece’s Independent Public Revenue Authority (IPA) has expanded its BANCAPP system to require not only Greek banks but also foreign bank branches, payment institutions, and electronic money institutions operating in Greece to submit taxpayer banking data.
