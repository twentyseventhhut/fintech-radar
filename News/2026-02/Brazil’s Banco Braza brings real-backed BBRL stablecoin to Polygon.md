# Brazil’s Banco Braza brings real-backed BBRL stablecoin to Polygon.

> [!info] 2026-02-26 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazil’s Banco Braza brings real-backed BBRL stablecoin to Polygon. With this step, BBRL is no longer confined to a single blockchain setup and can now be used on Polygon for transfers and other on-chain transactions. The move to Polygon enables faster and lower-cost transactions, particularly for cross-border payments and business use cases.
