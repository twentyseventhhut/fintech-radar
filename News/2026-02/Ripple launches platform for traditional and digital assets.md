Ripple Launches Platform for Managing Traditional and Digital Assets
Ripple announced the launch of a corporate treasury platform that brings together the management of traditional and digital assets within a single interface.
FinTech company Ripple, one of the largest providers of institutional blockchain solutions, unveiled Ripple Treasury, a comprehensive platform designed to deliver full control over liquidity, settlements, and investments in both fiat currencies and digital assets.
Ripple Treasury aims to reduce system fragmentation caused by traditional treasury platforms not supporting digital assets, while crypto services often fail to meet corporate finance requirements. The solution provides unified access to the management of bank accounts, digital assets, stablecoins, and tokenized instruments. Ripple Treasury supports direct API integrations with digital assets, enabling real-time tracking of balances, transactions, and market positions within standard treasury workflows.
At a global level, the platform is intended to transform corporate treasuries into a strategic liquidity management tool. Key benefits of Ripple Treasury include:
real-time, unified visibility of financial positions;
reduced operational and FX costs;
release of trapped capital;
creation of a competitive advantage through early adoption of tokenized assets and programmable payments.
The solution is built on the GTreasury treasury management system, which Ripple acquired in 2025. Ripple Treasury combines over 40 years of GTreasury’s expertise in corporate treasury with Ripple’s blockchain infrastructure, designed for institutional use and regulatory compliance. The platform addresses several core challenges faced by corporate treasuries, including:
idle working capital;
international settlements with clearing times of 3–5 business days;
FX risks during the settlement period;
hidden correspondent banking fees;
lack of transparency in the status of cross-border payments.
Through Ripple Treasury, companies gain access to collateralized overnight repos using the RLUSD stablecoin, tokenized money market funds, including BlackRock’s BUIDL, as well as Ripple’s MMF portal built on the XRP Ledger (XRPL), enabling instant investment and redemption. This approach allows capital to be deployed on weekends and outside banking hours while maintaining instant liquidity and full audit control.
For cross-border payments, Ripple Treasury offers settlements in RLUSD with near-instant completion instead of 3–5 days. Currency conversion occurs only at the destination point, reducing FX risk exposure from several days to just seconds. The solution also lowers FX costs through transparent pricing and automated FX rules tailored to specific payment corridors.
In the supplier payments segment, Ripple Treasury enables clearing within 3–5 seconds regardless of banking hours or holidays. This eliminates the need to pre-fund overseas accounts, freeing up millions of dollars in frozen working capital, while improving payment status transparency and strengthening relationships with counterparties.
Ripple continues to actively expand the institutional capabilities of the XRPL blockchain ecosystem. In April 2025, the company acquired prime broker Hidden Road to expand the institutional use of the RLUSD stablecoin. In September 2025, a native lending protocol and compliance tools were launched on XRPL. In November 2025, Ripple introduced the Ripple Prime digital brokerage platform in the U.S. and acquired custodial provider Palisade.
