Key takeaways
Stake raised $31 million in an oversubscribed Series B led by Emirates NBD
The capital will accelerate growth across Saudi Arabia and the USA
New funding will drive product innovation in real estate tokenisation and digital property ownership via StakeOne
At Stake, our mission has always been clear: to democratise real estate investment. We believe that geographic borders shouldn't limit your ability to build wealth, and that world-class real estate should be accessible to everyone, not just the institutional elite.
Today, we are thrilled to announce a massive leap forward in that journey. Stake has successfully raised $31 million in an oversubscribed Series B funding round. 
This milestone brings our total funding to date to USD 58 million , reinforcing our position as the leading digital real estate investment platform in the region and a rising force in the global fintech landscape.
A powerhouse of partners
This funding round was led by Emirates NBD , a premier banking group in the MENAT region. Their leadership, alongside a distinguished group of regional and international investors, underscores the industry's confidence in our regulated, technology-first approach.
We are honored to welcome and continue our journey with:
 Emirates NBD (Lead Investor)
 Mubadala Investment Company (MENA Venture Capital Fund)
 Middle East Venture Partners (MEVP) 
 Property Finder 
 STV NICE 
 Wa’ed Ventures 
 GFH Partners 
 Ellington Properties 
 Why this matters for our investors 
This capital injection isn't just about growth; it’s about scale, innovation and access. Here is how we are putting this investment to work for you:
 1. Deepening our roots in Saudi Arabia 
Saudi Arabia is our most strategic growth market. Since becoming the first CMA-regulated platform to open the Kingdom's property market to global investors in late 2024, the response has been incredible.
 3 Real Estate Funds successfully closed in KSA.
 SAR 416 Million channelled into the local sector.
 6,930+ International Investors already participating in the Saudi success story.
2. Global diversification
In October 2025, we officially expanded into the U.S. industrial real estate market . Why? Because it is one of the most resilient asset classes in the world. This funding allows us to scale our cross-border model, giving you seamless access to income-generating assets in stable, high-growth global markets.
3. Digitising the entire lifecycle
We aren't just a marketplace; we are the infrastructure.
 StakeOne: We recently launched StakeOne to digitise full property ownership and after-sales management for premium Dubai assets.
 Tokenisation & Liquidity: In collaboration with Property Finder and under In-Principle Approval from VARA , we are advancing regulated tokenisation. This will bring unprecedented liquidity and flexibility to real estate—allowing you to trade fractional exposure like never before.
By the numbers: Our momentum
Our growth over the last three years reflects the trust you have placed in us:
 130%+ GMV CAGR.
 100%+ Revenue CAGR.
 2 Million+ Users on the platform.
 211 Nationalities represented across 181 countries .
The road ahead
As Manar Mahmassani, Co-Founder and Co-CEO of Stake , puts it: "We are committed to being a long-term partner to the market, helping channel capital into high-quality opportunities while supporting the ambition to broaden foreign investment to the masses." 
Whether you are an investor in Riyadh, Dubai, London or the US, Stake is building the bridge to your next great investment. We are just getting started.

FAQs
The round was led by Emirates NBD , the leading financial services brand in the UAE with a presence in 13 countries and over USD 316 billion in total assets. The investment was made through their Innovation Fund , which targets best-in-class technologies that deliver strategic value.
 Mubadala Investment Company , an Abu Dhabi-based sovereign investor managing a USD 330 billion portfolio , participated in the round. They focus on generating sustainable financial returns and supporting the global integration of the UAE economy.
Stake is backed by the region’s most prominent tech investors, including:
 STV : The largest independent technology investment firm in MENA.
 Middle East Venture Partners (MEVP) : A firm managing over USD 300 million with a portfolio of 70+ companies.
 Wa’ed Ventures : A USD 500 million firm wholly owned by Aramco to drive economic diversification in Saudi Arabia.
Major real estate players have joined as strategic partners:
 Property Finder: The MENA region’s pioneering property portal that connects millions of seekers with real estate professionals.
 Ellington Properties: Dubai’s leading design-led developer, known for award-winning projects like Ocean House and One River Point.
 GFH Partners: A global real estate investment manager with approximately USD 7 billion in assets under management across the U.S., U.K., and GCC.
