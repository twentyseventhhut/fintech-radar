BrightCHAMPS and Triple-A partner to enable stablecoin payments for global learners
The Partnership
India, January 27, 2026: BrightCHAMPS , an award-winning global edtech company reshaping K-12 education through STEM-accredited academics and future-ready courses, has announced a partnership with leading global stablecoin financial institution Triple-A . As part of this collaboration, BrightCHAMPS will now accept all major stablecoins such as USDT and USDC as fee payment options across all 30+ countries, including the US, Canada, the UK, the UAE, and Southeast Asia.
This association has been introduced to offer a secure and seamless payment experience for both new and existing BrightCHAMPS customers. By enabling stablecoin payments, BrightCHAMPS aims to reach a wider global audience and simplify enrollment for international families. The integration also allows for faster payment settlement and significantly reduces cross-border payment friction commonly associated with traditional international transactions.
Commenting on the announcement, Ravi Bhushan, Founder and Chief Executive Officer of BrightCHAMPS , said, “Stablecoins are shaping the future of global payments, and enabling stablecoin payments is a natural step in making BrightCHAMPS truly global. Our partnership with Triple-A will allow families across geographies to access our programs more easily, while helping us reach a new generation of digital-first learners as we continue building future-ready education pathways.” 
 Eric Barbier, Chief Executive Officer of Triple-A, said: “Democratizing access to education is a global priority. Achieving this at scale requires enabling payment methods people trust to maintain purchasing power across borders. We’re proud to support BrightCHAMPS in accepting stablecoins and expanding access to education worldwide.”
This tie-up will ensure that all stablecoin transactions are processed through a secure and compliant payment gateway, which will enable BrightCHAMPS subscribers to access competitive exchange rates aligned with leading global cryptocurrency exchanges, while allowing the company to receive settlements in a stable and efficient manner.
 About BrightCHAMPS 
BrightCHAMPS is a global edtech company that delivers online, offline, and hybrid STEM-accredited classes in Coding, Robotics, AI, Financial Literacy, Entrepreneurship, Communications, Maths, and Science in 30+ countries across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and MENA. BrightCHAMPS was founded in 2020 and has raised $63 million from marquee investors like GSV Ventures, BEENEXT, Premji Invest, and 021 Capital.
For more information, visit: https://brightchamps.com/  
For media queries, reach out at pr@brightchamps.com  
 About Triple-A 
Triple-A is a global payment institution licensed in the United States, Europe, and Singapore, enabling businesses worldwide to pay and get paid in both stablecoins and local currencies. It empowers businesses to reach over 700M digital currency owners, boost revenue, and optimise costs through stablecoin payments, while eliminating volatility, custody, and compliance risks. Triple-A's commitment is to build a more efficient, inclusive global payment ecosystem by bridging the gap between local and digital currencies.
For more information, visit: https://www.triple-a.io/
For media queries, reach out at pr@triple-a.io
