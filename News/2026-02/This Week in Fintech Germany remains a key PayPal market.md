[This Week in Fintech] In Europe, Germany specifically has long been a strong market for PayPal. It's the most popular e-commerce payment method in the country — ahead of card payments. Last year PayPal launched in-store payments in Germany, which operates on the Mastercard network, and uses PayPal’s own contactless capability rather than Apple Pay or Google Pay.
