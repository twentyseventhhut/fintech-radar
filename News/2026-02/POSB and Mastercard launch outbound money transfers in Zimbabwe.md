# POSB and Mastercard launch outbound money transfers in Zimbabwe

> [!info] 2026-02-09 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇿🇦 POSB collaborates with Mastercard to launch a new outbound money transfer service. The service aims to provide a fast, secure, and cost-effective way for customers in Zimbabwe to send money directly to bank accounts, mobile wallets, and cash abroad. Additionally, Riyadh Air and Mastercard take off together to redefine the global travel experience through multiple touchpoints. The partners will develop a portfolio of innovative consumer products, including Riyadh Air-branded credit and prepaid cards built for the next generation of travelers.
