**African tech startup funding rebounded in 2025 following a couple of years of decline, as the sector began to recover from the “funding winter”.**

A total of 178 startups raised over US$1.6 billion over the course of the year.

For the 11th consecutive year, Disrupt Africa is releasing data on the tech investment ecosystem across Africa, contained in the _African Tech Startups Funding Report 2025_, once again available for FREE.

The report includes:

*   Detailed information on funding activity in four African countries;

*   Figures on the number of deals per location, and average deal sizes;

*   Data on growth in funds and deals over the past 10 years;

*   Highlights of key deals across continent;

*   Sector-specific breakdowns across 8 sectors;

*   Tracking of acquisitions in 2025

*   The full list of 178 funded tech startups from across Africa, detailing location, sector, when funding was secured and, where we are able to disclose, the investors and approximate amount raised. Any funding amounts disclosed confidentially do not appear in the list.
