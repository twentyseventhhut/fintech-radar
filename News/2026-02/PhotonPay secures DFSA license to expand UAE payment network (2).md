# PhotonPay secures DFSA license to expand UAE payment network

> [!info] 2026-02-10 · 1 упоминаний · 2 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇪 PhotonPay secures new license by Dubai Financial Services Authority to expand global payment network in the UAE. The company said the milestone strengthens its compliance-focused approach and enhances its ability to support businesses operating across the Middle East with improved local payment access, onboarding, and cross-border settlement tools.
