> [!info] 2026-02-27 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 India and Israel sign pact to link UPI systems in major digital payments push. The agreement will connect India’s Unified Payments Interface with Israel’s domestic payments framework. The move is aimed at making digital transactions between the two countries faster, cheaper, and more seamless.
