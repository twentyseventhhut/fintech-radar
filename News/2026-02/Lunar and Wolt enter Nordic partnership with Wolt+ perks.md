Lunar and Wolt enter major Nordic partnership: Lunar’s bank customers have zero delivery fee
As the first Nordic bank, Lunar has entered into a major Nordic partnership with the delivery platform Wolt to make everyday life easier for its customers. As part of the collaboration, all Lunar customers on the highest tier plans gain access to Wolt+, which includes unlimited delivery at 0 kr.
The digital Nordic challenger bank Lunar and the delivery service Wolt are launching a broad partnership across Sweden, Denmark, and Norway. Through the collaboration, all Lunar customers on the highest tier plans will gain access to Wolt+, which offers unlimited delivery at 0 kr, exclusive offers, and much more.
Lunar becomes the first Nordic bank to enter into a partnership with Wolt. The collaboration strengthens Lunar’s value proposition while opening up a new distribution channel for Wolt, making it possible to offer Wolt+ to Lunar users across the Nordics.
Wolt+ will become a permanent part of Lunar’s Unlimited plan. Lunar’s business customers on Limitless will also receive Wolt+ with zero fee deliveries. In addition, customers on Light, Standard and Plus will be able to redeem between three and nine months of Wolt+ at no cost starting 11 February. The partnership will roll out simultaneously in Denmark, Norway and Sweden, with local adaptations.
 Making everyday life simpler 
The partnership is based on a shared ambition to simplify everyday life through digital solutions that are easy to use.
“Collaborations like this create tangible value in a service many people already use. Zero fee deliveries may sound like a small thing, but it can mean fewer errands and more time when life is busy—whether it’s breakfast, dinner, or essentials you need quickly,” says Johan Grönvall, CEO of Wolt Sweden.
“Our partnership with Wolt is a natural fit that creates real value for both our private and business customers. With Wolt+ included, we give customers unlimited delivery at zero cost, and a little more time back in everyday life. It can be as simple as skipping the trip to the supermarket rush and having groceries delivered instead. It’s the small things that make a big difference day to day, and they reflect the kind of banking experience we’re building—a bank that works for you and makes your everyday life easier, not the other way around,” says Mette Gade, Chief Product Officer of Lunar.
From Lunar’s side, the partnership is positioned as part of the bank’s ambition to build a subscription offering focused on everyday usefulness for both private individuals and businesses.
 Offers also available to Wolt partners 
In addition to benefits for Lunar customers, the partnership includes special Lunar packages for Wolt’s restaurant, retail and courier partners. The aim is to give local businesses and self-employed partners on Wolt’s platform access to solutions that can simplify payments, operations and administration.
