# Peter Schmeichel and Mastercard promote Click to Pay in UK

> [!info] 2026-02-12 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Peter Schmeichel teams up with Mastercard to help Brits ditch manual card entry. For Click to Pay, this means UK shoppers will no longer have to enter their 16-digit card number when paying online, something 9 in 10 revealed they had done last year, and these details will no longer be shared with merchants.
