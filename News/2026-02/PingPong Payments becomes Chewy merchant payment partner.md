Global Payment Platform PingPong Forms Partnership With Chewy

Global payment platform PingPong has announced its official partnership with Chewy, the largest pet retail platform in the United States. This partnership aims to fully support merchants in cross-border payments, working together to build a high-speed channel that empowers Chinese merchants to enter the North American pet market. Through this collaboration, PingPong will offer merchants a one-stop solution that includes onboarding consultation, qualification pre-assessment, and comprehensive cross-border payment services, facilitating a seamless process from platform registration and product listing to order payment collection.
