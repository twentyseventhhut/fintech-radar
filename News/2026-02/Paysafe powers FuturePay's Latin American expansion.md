# Paysafe powers FuturePay's Latin American expansion

> [!info] 2026-02-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌎 Paysafe enables FuturePay’s Latin American expansion with a local payments suite. To support its Latin American market entry, FuturePay has integrated Paysafe’s SafetyPay solution to enable merchants to offer customers in the region bank transfers, online cash, and other local payment methods, including Pix for Brazil.
