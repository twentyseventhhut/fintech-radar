Plum introduces worldwide travel protection powered by Qover
 Plum’s Max plan now includes automatic worldwide travel coverage for users and their family traveling with them. 
 Coverage includes medical emergencies, lost baggage, delays and cancellations, accessible through the Plum app. 
 Program powered by Qover’s orchestration platform and backed by Aspire and Lloyd’s, with a smooth, digital-first experience for claims and travel certificates. 
Plum, the smart money app, is expanding its offering with worldwide travel protection. The feature gives customers and their families immediate peace of mind whenever they travel. 
Backed by Aspire and Lloyd’s, the automatic travel protection is included with Plum’s Max subscription plan in the UK. Claims are run through Qover’s AI-powered digital process, reducing friction and giving customers a smoother, quicker experience when they need it most.
Customers and their travelling family members are protected for medical emergencies, lost baggage, delays and cancellations. All information is accessible from the Plum app, where customers can view their cover, download travel certificates and manage claims through a simple digital journey. Behind the scenes, Qover orchestrates the tech, operations (care, claims and complaints), insurance services and risk carrier management needed to run an insurance program at scale, so Plum doesn’t have to build that complexity itself. This lets Plum launch and grow this feature faster while staying focused on helping people take control of their money.
Quentin Colmant, CEO & Co-founder of Qover said: ‘Plum is a great example of a fintech that genuinely puts customer value first. By embedding protection directly into the Max experience, they’re giving users peace of mind in an effortless way. We’re proud to help Plum bring this experience to life and deliver everyday value to their community.’
Rashmi D’Souza VP of Product & Design at Plum said: ‘Travelling, especially with family, is one of the key moments when finances and peace of mind intersect. People are able to save for flights and hotels through Plum’s automation goals, but then need to sort out the admin when it comes to booking those trips. By including insurance in our Max plan, Plum can provide extra protection during those important moments. It helps ensure our customers can maintain their financial resilience should the unexpected happen so they don’t have to start saving again from scratch while also strengthening the overall value of the subscription. It differentiates Plum from standard savings or trading apps, and reinforces the app as a holistic financial companion that supports them every step of the way. Working with Qover means we can offer this in a digital, reliable way that fits naturally into the Plum experience.’
