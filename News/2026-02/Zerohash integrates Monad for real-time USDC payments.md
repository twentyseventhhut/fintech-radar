# Zerohash integrates Monad for real-time USDC payments

Stablecoins are moving from niche to mainstream. As adoption accelerates, the infrastructure behind them has to keep up. That's why we've integrated Monad and USDC.Monad into the zerohash platform—unlocking fast, scalable, and cost-efficient stablecoin settlement for real-world financial use cases.
Monad was designed as a high-performance blockchain built for financial applications that demand throughput, determinism, and low latency. With Monad now enabled within zerohash, our partners can launch stablecoin-powered payment flows without managing blockchain infrastructure themselves.
Real-Time Payments, Without the Complexity
Through our APIs and SDKs, platforms can now access Monad and USDC.Monad while we handle the heavy lifting:
Node operations
Liquidity management
Custody
Regulatory licensing
Settlement workflows
That means payment providers, fintechs, and financial platforms can focus on product and user experience while we abstract away the technical and compliance complexity. Partners like Kalshi, Gusto, and Public can use this integration to power:
Real-time account funding
Cross-border payments
B2B settlement
On-chain commerce use cases
All with scalable, high-speed settlement rails underpinning the experience.
Building on High-Performance Infrastructure
“Stablecoin adoption is accelerating—as is the performance and scalability of the underlying technology,” said Edward Woodford , Founder and CEO of zerohash. "By supporting Monad, we're giving our platform partners access to a high-speed, highly scalable blockchain, combined with our regulated, battle-tested payments infrastructure. Together, this helps move stablecoins forward as the global rail for real-time money movement."
From the Monad Foundation's perspective, performance alone isn't enough. Real-world deployment requires operational and compliance foundations.
“Payments infrastructure needs to be both fast and dependable to support real-world adoption,” said Raj Parekh , Head of Stablecoins and Payments at Monad Foundation. “Monad delivers high throughput and low latency without sacrificing determinism, and zerohash brings the compliance, settlement, and operational foundation required by serious financial platforms.”
A Broader Shift in Financial Infrastructure
This integration reflects a larger trend across financial services: upgraded settlement rails that operate continuously, globally, and with near-instant finality. Stablecoins are increasingly being used not just for trading, but for payments, payroll, remittance, and commerce. As that shift continues, performance and regulatory clarity both matter.
Our goal is simple: give platforms a single integration to launch compliant, global stablecoin products—without forcing them to become blockchain infrastructure companies. With Monad now live on zerohash, partners have another high-performance option to power the next generation of payments.
