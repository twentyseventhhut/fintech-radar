 Manama, Bahrain: ila Bank, powered by Bank ABC, proudly introduces two innovative credit cards—ila World and ila World Elite—designed to elevate customers’ lifestyles with exceptional benefits, rewards, and digital convenience. These new offerings reinforce ila Bank’s commitment to delivering modern, personalized banking experiences that blend global privileges with local relevance.
 The Black Card (ila World Elite Credit Card) 
Designed for customers seeking an elevated banking and lifestyle experience, the ila World Elite Credit Card prioritises high‑value everyday benefits: up to 2% cashback on spending, complimentary valet parking across key destinations in Bahrain via ParkPoint and Valetch, and an exclusive 15% on your total bill at The Ritz-Carlton restaurants & Spa Services, Bahrain. Cardholders also receive a 50% discount on ila Premium, unlocking preferential rates, reduced fees, and enhanced banking privileges.
Additionally, the World Elite benefits include complimentary access to 1,300+ airport lounges worldwide for the cardholder and one guest, including the Pearl Lounge at Bahrain International Airport, fast‑track security at select airports, and a 24/7 VIP lifestyle concierge supporting travel bookings, dining reservations, event access, and personalised luxury planning. The card further provides travel medical insurance coverage of up to USD 500,000 and travel inconvenience protection. 
 The White Card (ila World Credit Card) 
The ila World Credit Card introduces a new tier of everyday premium banking, offering customers a range of global travel and lifestyle benefits alongside seamless digital convenience.
Customers can enjoy no annual fees and up to three supplementary cards at no additional cost, enabling family members to share the card’s benefits—alongside flexible rewards with a choice of airline miles, up to 1.5% cashback on eligible spending, or ila Tokens. Cardholders can benefit from access to 1,300+ airport lounges worldwide, including the Pearl Lounge at Bahrain International Airport, as well as travel medical insurance coverage of up to USD 500,000 and travel inconvenience protection. Mastercard travel and lifestyle privileges are also included, such as complimentary annual airport transfers, car rental discounts, and offers across 250+ partner merchants.
Commenting on the launch, Nada Tarada, Head of Business and Customer at ila Bank , said:
 “The introduction of ila World and ila World Elite marks an important step in expanding our premium product offering. These cards are designed to redefine customers everyday experiences whether you seek flexible rewards and premium travel perks with ila World, or unparalleled luxury, global privileges, and elite lifestyle benefits with ila World Elite. As we continue to innovate, our focus remains on delivering banking experiences that reflect our customers’ ambitions and evolving needs.” 
Both cards are are available to eligible customers through the ila app, enabling customers to manage spending, set up standing payments, apply for supplementary cards, and enjoy interest-free installment plans—all with a few taps. Applications can be completed swiftly via the app, bringing a world of upscale privileges within easy reach.
 Perfect Timing for a Lifestyle Upgrade 
Customers who apply for ila World Elite Credit Card or ila World Credit Card before May 31, 2026 will enjoy 0% interest on all purchases for their first 3 billing cycles.

ila Bank launches ila World and ila World Elite credit cards
Iulia Musat
02 Mar 2026 / 5 Min Read
Bahrain-based ila Bank, powered by Bank ABC, has introduced two new credit cards, the ila World and the ila World Elite, available to eligible customers through the ila app.
Both cards are positioned to serve distinct customer segments, with the ila World targeting everyday premium banking and the ila World Elite aimed at higher-value lifestyle and travel benefits.
Customers who apply for either card before 31 May 2026 will receive 0% interest on all purchases for their first three billing cycles.
Card features and benefits
The ila World Credit Card carries no annual fee and allows up to three supplementary cards at no additional cost. Rewards can be configured as airline miles, up to 1.5% cashback on eligible spending, or ila Tokens. The card includes access to over 1,300 airport lounges worldwide, among them the Pearl Lounge at Bahrain International Airport, travel medical insurance coverage of up to USD 500,000, travel inconvenience protection, and a range of Mastercard travel and lifestyle privileges, including complimentary annual airport transfers, car rental discounts, and offers across more than 250 partner merchants.
The ila World Elite Credit Card offers up to 2% cashback on spending, complimentary valet parking at select locations in Bahrain through ParkPoint and Valetch, and a 15% discount on dining and spa services at The Ritz-Carlton Bahrain. Cardholders also receive a 50% discount on ila Premium, which provides preferential rates, reduced fees, and enhanced banking privileges. The card includes the same lounge access and travel insurance coverage as the ila World, alongside fast-track security at select airports and access to a 24/7 concierge service covering travel bookings, dining reservations, event access, and personalised planning.
Both cards are managed through the ila app, where customers can monitor spending, set up standing payments, apply for supplementary cards, and access interest-free instalment plans.
Talking about the launch, Nada Tarada, Head of Business and Customer at ila Bank, noted that the two cards are designed for different customer profiles, one focused on flexible rewards and travel perks, the other on broader lifestyle and premium privileges, and that the rollout reflects the bank's continued focus on personalised banking experiences.
