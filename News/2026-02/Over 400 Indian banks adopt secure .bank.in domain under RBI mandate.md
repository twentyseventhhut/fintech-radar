# Over 400 Indian banks adopt secure .bank.in domain under RBI mandate

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Over 400 Indian banks shift to secure ‘.bank.in’ domain to boost cybersecurity, complying with RBI mandate. This new top-level domain (TLD) is exclusive to verified banking institutions, making it easier for customers to distinguish genuine bank websites from fraudulent or phishing sites that often mimic official portals to steal credentials or carry out financial fraud.
