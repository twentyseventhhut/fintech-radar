Bleap Raises $6 million Seed Round
Bleap raises a $6 million seed round led by Blossom Capital to build a self-custodial financial account on modern rails, scaling payments, stablecoins, and global money movement across Europe and LATAM.
 Bleap raised $6m to keep rebuilding money on modern rails 
 Bleap has raised a $6 million seed round, led by Blossom Capital , to continue building a better foundation for how money should work. The round follows a $2.3 million pre-seed raised in late 2024 and marks an important step in scaling the platform. 
 Since the pre-seed, we’ve grown to more than 20,000 users and processed over $30 million in transactions in 2025 alone. That traction has reinforced what we believed from the start: people want financial tools that work globally, instantly, and without unnecessary intermediaries. 
 Bleap was founded by Joao Alves and Guilherme Gomes after years building products at Revolut. While user experiences improved across the industry, the systems underneath remained slow, permissioned, and fragmented. “The interfaces got better, but underneath, money is still moving on systems built decades ago. That’s where most of the friction comes from” , said Alves. 
 Transfers still take days, fees remain opaque, and access to funds is often constrained by rules rather than technology. We built Bleap to change that. 
 We’re building a self-custodial financial account that brings everyday money tools into one place. From a single balance, users can spend globally with no FX markups, earn cashback paid directly in stablecoins, move money instantly across borders, and earn yield, all while keeping full control of their funds. 
 Since the pre-seed, we’ve expanded beyond payments. Users can already buy digital assetns with no fees, and we’re soon rolling out new, fee-free trading features directly inside the app. Over time, Bleap is being built to support everything that becomes tradable, from digital assets today to tokenized real-world assets in the future. 
 Blockchain isn’t an add-on for us, it’s the settlement layer. Stablecoins sit at the centre, enabling predictable spending, instant remittances, and global access by default, while the experience remains familiar and simple. 
 This seed round will help us scale across Europe and Latin America, accelerate product development, and keep building toward our long-term vision. Not another finance app, but the account money moves into as finance itself evolves. 
 That’s what we mean by our slogan, Beyond Money.
