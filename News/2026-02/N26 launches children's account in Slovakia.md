# N26 launches children's account in Slovakia

> [!info] 2026-02-17 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇰 N26 launches account for children aged seven and up in Slovakia. This is a children's debit card designed for children aged seven to 17. According to the bank, the child has their own card, while the parent has full control over the account and payments and can set limits or rules for the card's use.
