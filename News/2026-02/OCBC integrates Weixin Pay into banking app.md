# OCBC app adds Weixin Pay QR payments

**Customers will see rates in real-time before making a transaction.**

OCBC Singapore can now use their apps to scan and pay in Weixin Pay merchant QR codes.

OCBC promises a competitive exchange rate with no additional fees, with customers able to see rates in real-time before making a transaction, according to a press release on 9 February 2026.

WeixinPay, alongside Alipay+, dominate China’s mobile payment market and make up over 90% of transactions there, according to OCBC. China is also the top destination for OCBC customers using the bank’s scan and pay app overseas, with 1 in 5 overseas scan and pay transactions made in China.

OCBC customers can now make QR payments across 57 destinations, including mainland China, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Hong Kong, Japan, and South Korea.
