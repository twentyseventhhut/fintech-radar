# Kissht's NBFC arm wins Crisil ratings upgrade to A-minus

> [!info] 2026-02-23 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] The NBFC arm of IPO-bound Kissht, received a Crisil Ratings upgrade to A-/Stable from BBB+/Stable. It had Assets Under Management (AUM) growing to ₹5,533cr ($610M) as of Sep 30, 2025.
