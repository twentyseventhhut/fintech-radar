# Binance Pay brings crypto payments to 650,000 SA merchants

> [!info] 2026-02-06 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Binance Pay brings crypto payments to 650,000 SA merchants through a strategic partnership with Scan To Pay. Through Scan to Pay’s integration with Moneybadger, Binance Pay users can now pay with over 100 cryptocurrencies at participating merchants, ranging from fashion retailers to essential bill payments, using the familiar QR-code payment experience.
