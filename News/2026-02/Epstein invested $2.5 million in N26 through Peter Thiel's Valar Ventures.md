[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 Epstein invested $2.5 million in N26 through Peter Thiel's Valar Ventures. Epstein had committed $40 million to two Valar funds, which between 2015 and 2016 contributed to N26’s early funding rounds, including its $11 million Series A, while Valar invested about $14.6 million in total; N26 has since raised nearly $1.8 billion overall.
