FDI Flow in Banking Sector Declines from $898 Mn in FY23 to $115 Mn in FY25

Foreign direct investment (FDI) equity inflow in the banking sector has declined from USD 898 million in FY23 to USD 115 million in FY25, Parliament was informed on Tuesday.

Total FDI inflow includes equity inflow, equity capital of unincorporated bodies, re-invested earnings, and other capital, Minister of State for Finance Pankaj Chaudhary said in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha.

FDI is considered as a major source of non-debt financial resource for the economic development, he said.
