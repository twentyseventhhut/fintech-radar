# Amex debuts flexible payment option for small businesses

> [!info] 2026-02-09 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 AmEx debuts flexible payments for small businesses. The new offering is aimed at giving businesses more control over their cash flow needs with an instant line of credit. Flexible Payment Option (FPO) is available for AmEx Business Gold and Platinum cardholders, giving them the option to repay their statement balances in full or pay in portions over time and with interest.
