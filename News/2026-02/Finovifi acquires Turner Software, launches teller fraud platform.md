 Finovifi Acquires Turner Software Technologies, Inc. and Launches FrontLine Sentry — A Real-Time Teller Fraud Prevention Platform for Community Banks 

 
 Read Time 3 mins | 
 
 Written by: Karly Field 
 
 
 
 BIRMINGHAM, Ala. —February 19, 2026 — Finovifi, a provider of AI-driven fraud prevention, compliance technology and core processing solutions for community financial institutions, today announced the acquisition of Turner Software Technologies, Inc., the developer of the Teller21 teller automation platform. The acquisition brings more than 50 community bank clients into the Finovifi ecosystem and advances the company’s strategy to embed real-time fraud detection directly into teller operations.
Turner Software founder and president Jerry Turner will join Finovifi as Senior Vice President of Engineering, partnering with Finovifi CTO Terry Ray to lead product development and platform innovation.
“Joining Finovifi is the next chapter in a journey that started with one goal: helping community banks operate with speed and confidence at the teller line,” said Turner. “Integrating Teller21 with Finovifi’s fraud and compliance technologies creates something truly powerful. FrontLine Sentry will give banks real-time protection at the exact moment it matters most.”
 Strengthening a Long-Standing Teller & Core Integration 
Teller21 has been tightly integrated with the MBS by Finovifi core platform for many years, supporting streamlined teller workflows and transaction processing for community banks. Following the acquisition, Finovifi will further enhance that integration by embedding its flagship check fraud detection engine, FraudSentry, directly into the teller workflow.
This evolution delivers fraud intelligence at the point of transaction — combining teller automation, core connectivity, and real-time risk analysis within a unified operational environment.
Finovifi President Russell Taylor emphasized the strategic importance of this advancement:
“Turner Software has built a highly respected community banking teller platform. By uniting Teller21 with our real-time fraud intelligence, we’re giving banks a defense system that operates where fraud happens — right at the front line, in real time.”
 
 
 Introducing FrontLine Sentry 
 FrontLine Sentry is Finovifi’s real-time teller fraud prevention platform designed specifically for community banks. 
Unlike traditional teller systems that rely primarily on post-transaction monitoring, FrontLine Sentry delivers fraud intelligence before a transaction is completed — enabling institutions to stop fraudulent checks at the window rather than reconcile losses afterward.
FrontLine Sentry empowers tellers to:
Scan checks and receive instant fraud risk analysis
Identify suspicious items before funds leave the institution
Reduce back-office fraud investigations and operational cleanup
Strengthen compliance visibility at the point of transaction
By embedding FraudSentry’s check fraud detection technology directly into teller operations, FrontLine Sentry transforms the teller line into an active fraud prevention checkpoint rather than a passive processing function.
For more information about FrontLine Sentry, visit: https://finovifi.com/frontline-sentry 
 Expanding a Unified Fraud & Compliance Strategy 
FrontLine Sentry integrates with Finovifi’s broader fraud and compliance ecosystem to create layered protection across the institution:
 FraudXchange — Dark Web Intelligence for Community Banks FraudXchange monitors dark web marketplaces, criminal forums, and illicit data sources to identify stolen checks and compromised payment information before criminals exploit them. Banks receive early warning notifications that support proactive stop payments and targeted customer outreach.
 BSA Guardian — Real-Time AML Monitoring and Automated SAR Preparation BSA Guardian analyzes transaction behavior in real time, flags suspicious patterns, streamlines case management, and automates Suspicious Activity Report preparation — reducing manual compliance workload while improving regulatory readiness.
Together, these solutions provide coordinated fraud defense spanning teller transactions, digital activity, and external criminal networks.
 
 About Turner Software Technologies, Inc. 
Founded in 2004, Turner Software Technologies, Inc. has served community banks nationwide with its advanced teller automation platform, Teller21. Designed to eliminate paper tickets, reduce transaction errors, and strengthen compliance controls, Teller21 is trusted by more than 50 financial institutions.
Learn more at www.turnersoftwaretech.com .
 About Finovifi 
Finovifi empowers community financial institutions with AI-driven fraud prevention, compliance automation, core processing solutions, and digital banking technology. With decades of industry expertise and a focus on practical innovation, Finovifi delivers solutions that are real-time, reliable, and purpose-built for the operational realities of community banking.
Learn more at www.finovifi.com .
