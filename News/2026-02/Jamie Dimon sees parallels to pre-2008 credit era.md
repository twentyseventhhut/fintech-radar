# Jamie Dimon sees parallels to pre-2008 credit era

> [!info] 2026-02-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Jamie Dimon, JPMorgan Chase & Co. CEO, sees parallels to the pre-crisis era. Dimon said he's starting to see parallels to the era before the 2008 financial crisis, when a rush to make loans ended disastrously. He expects the credit cycle to eventually sour again, though he is not sure when, and said he sees a couple of people doing "dumb things" to create net interest income.
