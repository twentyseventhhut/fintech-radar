[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Leona Health, the Mexico-based healthtech startup, raised a US$14 million seed round led by Andreessen Horowitz. The company builds software that helps doctors in Latin America manage patient communications, including high volumes of WhatsApp messages. The round also included participation from General Catalyst, Accel, and angel investors.
