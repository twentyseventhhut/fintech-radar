# Brazil’s Banco Master fallout mounts as FinTech gets liquidated

> [!info] 2026-01-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazil’s Banco Master fallout mounts as FinTech gets liquidated. The Central Bank of Brazil has liquidated Will Financeira SA Crédito, citing insolvency and its ties to the failed Banco Master SA. The move highlights the broader fallout from Banco Master’s collapse, which was liquidated in November, while Will Bank had initially been kept outside the process.
