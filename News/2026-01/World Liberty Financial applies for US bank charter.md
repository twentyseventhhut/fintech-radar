[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Trump Crypto Venture World Liberty applies for Bank Charter. World Liberty Financial’s subsidiary WLTC Holdings has lodged a “de novo” application with the US Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) for a national trust bank charter.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Trump Firm to start new cryptocurrency for shareholders. The company said the new token will be allocated to investors in a partnership with crypto exchange Crypto.com, according to a statement.
