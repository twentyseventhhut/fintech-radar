Gemini Predictions™ Unveils Contracts for Sporting Events
Gemini Predictions™ is now offering sporting event contracts for the College Football Playoff semifinals and the NFL's Wild Card Round. And for a limited time, eligible users in the US can earn up to $250 for hitting specific trading benchmarks.
Gemini customers can now buy contracts for the following games:
 College Football Playoff Semifinals 
Miami vs. Ole Miss 
Indiana vs. Oregon 
 Wild Card Round 
Houston vs. Pittsburgh 
Los Angeles Chargers vs. New England 
San Francisco vs. Philadelphia 
Buffalo vs. Jacksonville 
Los Angeles Rams vs. Carolina 
Gemini Predictions™ offers event contracts that are simple yes or no questions on future events.
Gemini customers in the United States can trade events contracts using USD in their Gemini Exchange account on web and iOS, with Android to follow shortly.
Adding sporting event contracts marks Gemini’s latest step in building our one-stop super app where customers can trade crypto, stake, earn crypto credit card rewards, stablecoin rewards, and more.
