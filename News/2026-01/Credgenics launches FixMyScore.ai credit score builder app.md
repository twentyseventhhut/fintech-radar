 Credgenics forays into consumer segment 
Business Standard
January 15, 2026
 Debt collections fintech Credgenics has forayed into a consumer category with the rollout of credit-score improvement and repayment platform FixMyScore.ai on Wednesday. 
- AJINKYA KAWALE
 The company is targeting 100,000 users by March, which it further expects to grow in the range of three to four million by the end of the next financial year (FY27), Rishabh Goel (pictured), co-founder and chief executive officer (CEO), Credgenics, told Business Standard.
“The target is to reach 100,000 users by March. We have anticipated the number to be in the range of three to four million by the next financial year-end,” he said.
