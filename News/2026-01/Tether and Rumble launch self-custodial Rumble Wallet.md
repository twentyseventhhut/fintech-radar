# Tether and Rumble launch self-custodial Rumble Wallet

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇦 Tether and Rumble launch Rumble Wallet, bringing self-custodial crypto payments to millions of creators and users.
