# Tether-backed Oobit adds Phantom Wallet for Visa payments

> [!info] 2026-01-15 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 Tether-Backed Oobit expands Visa payments by adding Phantom Wallet. This update allows Phantom users to make real-time crypto payments through Visa-supported merchants without transferring assets to custodial services. According to Oobit, the platform’s “DePay” payment layer enables instant crypto-to-fiat conversions at the point of sale.
