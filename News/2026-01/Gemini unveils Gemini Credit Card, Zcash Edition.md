JAN 27, 2026
Gemini Unveils Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition
Today, we’re excited to announce the launch of the Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition.
The latest Gemini Credit Card builds on our line of popular crypto-back credit cards, which includes the Bitcoin, XRP, and Solana Editions.
The Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition gives users the opportunity to endorse the Zcash ecosystem. It includes custom branding, highlighting the token’s privacy-focused ethos while delivering the same streamlined user experience Gemini customers know well.
With the Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition, customers can stack Zcash, an established privacy-focused token that’s gained notoriety for offering users the ability to send shielded crypto transfers. And Gemini Credit Card holders who held Zcash rewards for at least one year saw rewards appreciate by 912.2%. 1 
The Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition has no annual fees, no foreign transaction fees and automatically deposits crypto rewards directly into your exchange account. 2 It offers up to 4% back on Gas at the pump, EV charging, transit & rideshare on up to $300 in spend per month (then 1% on all other qualifying purchases that month), 3% back on dining, 2% back on groceries, and 1% back on other purchases.
The Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition is available now for eligible U.S. customers.
Additional Benefits
The Gemini Credit Card: Zcash Edition has no annual fee, no fee to receive crypto rewards, and no fee on foreign transactions. Rewards are delivered, and customers can alternate between crypto rewards as frequently as they want. Plus, Gemini Credit Cardholders receive discounted access Peacock Premium, as well as other discounts to Instacart, Lyft, and more through the Mastercard World Elite program.
