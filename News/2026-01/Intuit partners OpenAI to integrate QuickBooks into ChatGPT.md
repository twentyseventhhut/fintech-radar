## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] For example, it said that Intuit has partnered with OpenAI to integrate apps like QuickBooks, QuickBooks Payroll, TurboTax, and others directly into ChatGPT. This means business users will be able to link their Intuit accounts and ask ChatGPT to help with financial tasks, including payroll management and payroll-related questions, without leaving the ChatGPT interface.
