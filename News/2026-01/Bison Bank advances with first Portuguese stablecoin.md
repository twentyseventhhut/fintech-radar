# Bison Bank advances with first Portuguese stablecoin

> [!info] 2026-01-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇵🇹 Bison Bank advances with first Portuguese stablecoin. Bison Bank plans to issue Portugal’s first bank-backed stablecoin, expand asset tokenization, and become the country’s first cryptobank, enabled by the EU’s MiCA framework. The bank aims to launch the services in the first half of the year, according to CEO António Henriques.
