# TWIF announces Mexico Fintech Week 2026

> [!info] 2026-01-15 · 3 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] From February 23rd to 27th, we'll be in Mexico City for Mexico Fintech Week, culminating in the FinTech México Festival, organized by FinTech México alongside their own FTMX Week.

[This Week in Fintech] Produced and created by TWIF alongside partners and community leaders, Mexico Fintech Week is a community-driven platform for curated, in-person conversations bringing together founders, operators, investors, and builders shaping the region’s fintech ecosystem.

[This Week in Fintech] Throughout the week, Mexico City becomes a hub for local and international fintech voices, with discussions spanning payments, banking, lending, crypto, and financial infrastructure. Global platforms, including the Stablecon Roadshow, are part of the program.This isn’t about sitting through a conference — it’s about being where conversations actually happen: events, working sessions, and moments where founders, operators, and investors connect.
