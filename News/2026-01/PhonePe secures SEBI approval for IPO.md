## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 PhonePe India IPO set to see Walmart cut stake as Tiger Global and Microsoft exit. PhonePe, which competes with Google Pay and Paytm in India, had received regulatory approval for its stock market listing ‌after confidentially filing for an IPO in September.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 PhonePe has secured Sebi approval for its IPO. The $14.5-billion startup plans a $1.5-billion OFS, with Walmart, Tiger Global, and Microsoft set to sell shares. Strong payments dominance and rising revenues underpin investor confidence.

[This Week in Fintech] PhonePeobtained regulatory clearance for Initial Public Offering (IPO) targeting mid-2026 listing with 100% offer for sale.
