# Mastercard and Moneythor add payment enrichment for First Abu Dhabi Bank

> [!info] 2026-01-21 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇪 Mastercard and Moneythor provide payment enrichment for First Abu Dhabi Bank. Through this collaboration, FAB’s customers now see real merchant names and logos, organised spending categories, intuitive summaries of transactions, and forward-looking cashflow forecasts. Additionally, BoxCommerce and Mastercard launch a Prepaid Card Program to give SMEs direct, instant access to their earnings. Through this collaboration, the ecosystem approach not only strengthens SME access to financial tools but also paves the way for future innovations.
