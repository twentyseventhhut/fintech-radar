# PayPal is partnering with Nigerian FinTech Paga to finally let Nigerians receive

> [!info] 2026-01-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇬 PayPal is partnering with Nigerian FinTech Paga to finally let Nigerians receive international payments and fully access its global payments network after two decades of limited service. Under the partnership, Nigerian users can link their PayPal accounts to their Paga wallets and receive payments from more than 200 countries.
