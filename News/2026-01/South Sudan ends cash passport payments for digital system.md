[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇸 South Sudan ends cash payments for passports, launches digital system to curb leakages. Under the new setup, applicants pay digitally through an on-site commercial bank, with receipts automatically linked to the immigration system, allowing documents to be processed without staff handling cash.
