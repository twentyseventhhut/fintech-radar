PB Fintech Unveils Pensionbazaar To Focus On Retirement Planning
 # PB Fintech 
 # Pensionbazaar 
 # Pension 
PB Fintech on Tuesday introduced Pensionbazaar, a new platform focused on retirement planning, as part of its efforts to expand into long-term financial solutions. The initiative was inaugurated by S. Ramann, Chairperson of the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority, signalling closer engagement between digital financial platforms and the pension ecosystem.
Positioned as a dedicated retirement marketplace, Pensionbazaar is designed to enable end-to-end planning for individuals looking to build long-term financial security. The platform seeks to address a structural gap in India’s financial landscape, where retirement planning is often postponed or inadequately prioritised. Industry data cited at the launch indicated that more than 90 per cent of individuals above the age of 50 regret not building a larger retirement corpus, while future expenses related to inflation, healthcare and lifestyle are frequently underestimated.
PB Fintech said Pensionbazaar will bring together multiple retirement-oriented offerings on a single platform. These include pension and savings products such as the National Pension System, annuities and pension-linked unit-linked insurance plans, along with health insurance solutions tailored for post-retirement needs. The objective, the company said, is to make retirement planning more accessible and structured for individuals at different life stages.
Addressing the gathering, Ramann emphasised the importance of early and sustained participation in pension products. He said long-term financial security requires disciplined savings over several decades and highlighted the need for individuals to remain invested in pension systems such as the NPS for extended periods.
PB Fintech’s management said the initiative aligns with broader demographic trends, with India’s population aged 60 and above projected to nearly double by 2050. Pensionbazaar, the company said, aims to support individuals in preparing for longer post-retirement lives by combining financial planning with risk protection.
Yashish Dahiya, Chairman and Group CEO of PB Fintech, said retirement planning has historically taken a back seat to short- and medium-term financial goals in India. He added that Pensionbazaar has been created to encourage earlier engagement with retirement planning through a structured and easy-to-navigate platform.
