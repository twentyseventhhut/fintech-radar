Bitget Wallet launches zero-fee USDC on-ramp with Alchemy Pay
Bethiah Negussie
23 Dec 2025 / 5 Min Read
 Bitget Wallet has launched a zero-fee USDC on-ramp with Alchemy Pay , supported by Coinbase 's stablecoin subsidy programme.   
 The El Salvador-based crypto wallet provider has introduced a zero-fee on-ramp for USDC purchases through its partnership with Alchemy Pay. The service eliminates transaction fees and network charges whilst offering market-referenced foreign exchange rates and one-click checkout via Apple Pay and Google Pay. The zero-fee structure operates through Alchemy Pay's stablecoin subsidy programme, which receives funding from Coinbase to expand USDC access in growth markets.   
 The on-ramp is available directly through Bitget Wallet's Buy Crypto portal and supports users across Asia Pacific, Latin America, and Africa. The service integrates global payment channels including Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard, and Visa, alongside domestic payment rails and local bank transfers. Smaller-value purchases settle instantly.   
Stablecoin accessibility and integration   
 The zero-fee on-ramp forms part of Bitget Wallet's broader stablecoin infrastructure. The wallet, which serves over 80 million users, provides more than 80 payment methods to buy and sell crypto across over 100 markets. The platform enables stablecoin-based gas fees on several major blockchains and supports USDC usage for card payments in over 50 markets, QR transactions, bank transfers, and in-app spending. Users can allocate USDC to yield-generating options through integrated decentralised finance (DeFi) protocols, with rates reaching up to 10% annually.   
 Bitget Wallet provides crypto wallet services with integrated trading, payment, and DeFi functionality. The platform maintains a USD 700 million protection fund for user security.   
 Alchemy Pay, founded in 2017, operates a payment gateway connecting cryptocurrency with fiat currencies. The Singapore-based company supports fiat payments in 173 countries through its on-ramp and off-ramp solutions, Web3 digital banking services, and tokenised real-world asset platform.
