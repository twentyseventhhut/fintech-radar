# Amazon to end Amazon One palm payments

> [!info] 2026-01-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Amazon to stop Amazon One palm payments. Amazon will end its Amazon One palm-recognition payment system and remove all palm readers from physical locations by June 3, 2026, citing low customer adoption. All customer data linked to the service will be securely deleted after it ends.
