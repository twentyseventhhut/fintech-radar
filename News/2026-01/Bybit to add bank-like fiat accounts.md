# Bybit to add bank-like fiat accounts

> [!info] 2026-01-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Crypto exchange Bybit to become more bank-like with new accounts. Bybit plans to start offering banking services with the launch of accounts that will let customers hold balances in US dollars and other fiat currencies. It also plans to launch a new custody product for institutional investors, which will primarily support banks.
