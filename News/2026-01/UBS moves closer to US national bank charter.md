SWISS bank UBS is a step closer to getting a national bank charter for its US franchise after receiving a first conditional approval, the company said on Thursday.

“This milestone marks a significant step toward securing final approval of our National Bank Charter, reinforcing UBS’ commitment to growth and strengthening our position as the premier global wealth manager in the US,” a UBS spokesman said.
