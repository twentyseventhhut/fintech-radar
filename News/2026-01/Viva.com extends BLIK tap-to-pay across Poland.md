 Viva.com Extends BLIK Code Payments to In-Store Checkout via Tap on Any Device, Covering Both Online and In-Person Payments Across Poland 
 Payments
 29.01.2026 09:05 am 
 Viva.com, the first Tech Bank in Europe for businesses, has rolled out support for BLIK 6-digit code payments on its Tap on Any Device technology, enabling Polish merchants to accept, and software partners to embed, one of the country’s most popular payment methods through a fully software-based checkout experience. The rollout delivers a future-proof, locally relevant solution that seamlessly connects in-store and online commerce, enabling scale across sectors and European markets. 
 Now available via the Viva.com Terminal app powering its leading Tap on Any Device technology, the new functionality allows BLIK 6-digit code payments to be accepted, quickly and securely, on any device - from smartphones and tablets to kiosks and vending machines.  
 This development builds on the strong market momentum of BLIK. As of Q3 2025, nearly 20 million users in Poland actively use BLIK. In the same quarter alone, 188.5 million payments were made at physical terminals using BLIK, averaging over 2 million in-store transactions per day, with a growing preference for secure, mobile-first payments.  
 “This launch is a major step forward for merchants and our ISV ecosystem,” said Yannis Kavaklis, Country Manager Poland for Viva.com. “By integrating BLIK 6-digit code acceptance into our Tap on Any Device platform - alongside all major international and domestic schemes - we give software partners the ability to deliver fully embedded, omnichannel payments without the constraints of hardware or multiple integrations. As BLIK continues to expand beyond Poland, Viva.com is ready to support that growth through our unified, pan-European infrastructure.” 
 “We are pleased to see another international partner recognize the strong potential of BLIK. By expanding our acceptance network and introducing innovative solutions like BLIK Code Payments for in-store checkout via Viva.com’s Tap on Any Device, we make payments simpler for consumers while providing merchants with a fast, flexible way to accept BLIK, without any additional hardware,” said Adam Kokoszkiewicz, Sales Department Director at BLIK. 
 Through a single integration, Viva.com’s Tap on Any Device platform enables acceptance of over 40 payment methods, in 24 countries, including all major international card schemes - Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, Diners Club - alongside local champions like BLIK, and popular alternative payment methods, like KLARNA. For ISVs, this means they can build rich, unified checkout experiences across channels without the complexity of fragmented infrastructure. 
 Viva.com’s all-in-one business platform offers access to a complete suite of payment and banking tools, including financing options and deposit accounts, as well as real-time settlement, offline payments, fiscalisation, dynamic currency conversion, 0% transaction fees when using the Viva.com Business Debit Card. The platform is built for scalability and speed, helping merchants and software partners deliver modern commerce at every touchpoint.

Viva.com unlocks BLIK payments on any device for Polish merchants
Claudia Pincovski
30 Jan 2026 / 3 Min Read
 Viva.com has expanded its Tap on Any Device technology to support BLIK 6-digit code payments, allowing Polish merchants to accept the payment methods through a fully software-based checkout. 
 The update also enables ISVs and software partners to embed BLIK acceptance directly into their applications, removing the need for dedicated payment hardware. 
 By supporting BLIK on any smart device, from smartphones and tablets to self-service kiosks and vending machines, the solution delivers a scalable and locally relevant payment experience that can be rolled out across industries and European markets. 
 BLIK adoption continues to accelerate in Poland 
 BLIK has become a dominant force in Poland’s payments landscape, driven by high smartphone penetration and consumer demand for fast, mobile-first checkout experiences. 
 Industry data shows that mobile payments now account for a growing share of point-of-sale transactions in Poland, with double-digit year-on-year growth. BLIK’s 6-digit code model has gained traction due to its simplicity, strong authentication flow, and compatibility with both online and in-store environments. For merchants, this translates into faster checkout times, reduced dependency on cards, and higher customer preference alignment. 
 By integrating BLIK into its Tap on Any Device ecosystem, Viva.com enables merchants and ISVs to respond directly to these market trends. Software partners can deploy fully embedded, omnichannel payment experiences without managing fragmented integrations or investing in proprietary hardware. 
 Beyond BLIK, Viva.com’s Tap on Any Device platform supports more than 40 payment methods across 24 European countries. These include global card schemes such as Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, and Diners Club, as well as local and alternative payment methods like Klarna. This unified infrastructure allows businesses to standardise payments across markets while remaining locally relevant. 
 Viva.com’s broader all-in-one business platform complements its payments offering with banking services, including real-time settlement, offline payments, fiscalisation, financing solutions, deposit accounts, and dynamic currency conversion. With features such as zero transaction fees when using the Viva.com Business Debit Card, the platform is designed to support merchants and software partners building modern commerce experiences.

 About BLIK 
BLIK is a common standard for mobile payments. The option to use BLIK in mobile banking applications is now available to virtually every customer of domestic payment institutions offering a mobile app. The company Polski Standard Płatności (PSP) is responsible for developing the system. PSP continuously enhances BLIK’s functionality to make the system as user-friendly and versatile as possible. PSP’s shareholders are Alior Bank, Millennium Bank, Santander Bank Polska, ING Bank Śląski, mBank, PKO Bank Polski, and Mastercard.
 About Viva.com   
Viva.com, the 1st Tech Bank in Europe for Businesses, revolutionises omnichannel payments acceptance, offers fiscalisation, card issuing, loans and deposit accounts in 24 European countries. Viva.com is the first to pioneer and holds the top position in the Tap on Any Device technology, enabling payments acceptance on any device or even without a device. Connecting directly to all local payment schemes, alternative payment methods and payment systems across the continent, further strengthens Viva.com’s value proposition. Reflecting its holistic approach to business needs, today Viva.com is leading the transition to All-in-One business solutions, powered by its full suite of financial services and a dynamic ecosystem featuring over 450 tech partner-innovators in the software and hardware space.
