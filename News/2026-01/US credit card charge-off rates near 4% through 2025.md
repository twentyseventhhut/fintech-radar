# US credit card charge-off rates near 4% through 2025

> [!info] 2026-01-20 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Despite strong labor markets through much of 2024 and 2025, indicators of consumer stress remain elevated. Charge-off rates on credit card loans at all commercial banks were around 4% through 2025, significantly higher than the pandemic-era lows. Delinquency rates have risen significantly since 2022.
