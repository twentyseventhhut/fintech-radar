# Netherlands set to scrap bonus caps for some banking and FinTech staff

> [!info] 2026-01-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Netherlands set to scrap bonus caps for some banking and FinTech staff. The proposal would lift caps on variable pay for most bank and FinTech staff while keeping limits in place for top material risk takers, and still requires further approval from the chamber and the Senate.
