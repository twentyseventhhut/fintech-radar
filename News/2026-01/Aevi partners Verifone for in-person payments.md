Aevi and Verifone partner to simplify and scale in-person payments globally 
 

 Aevi news
 

 2 Minute Read
 

 Last updated 07/01/2026
 
 
 Alex Benjamin
 
[New York, 7th January 2026] – Aevi, a leader in in-person payment orchestration, and Verifone , a leading global payments technology provider, today announced a strategic partnership that streamlines how enterprises deploy, manage, and scale in-person payments across regions and meets the demands of modern commerce.
The partnership combines Verifone’s global device portfolio and operational services with Aevi’s cloud-based, in person payment platform. Together, the companies address the fragmentation, proprietary integrations, and operational complexity that have historically slowed international in-person payment expansion.
What this partnership delivers
 One global integration across all Verifone devices and use cases . A single integration through Aevi’s acquirer-agnostic orchestration platform enables merchants to deploy Verifone devices across EMEA and North America. The solution supports all major in-person use cases, from in-lane and counter to mobile and unattended, without duplicating integrations market by market
 Multi acquire orchestration capabilities and access to local payment methods across Europe . Aevi’s cloud-based payment gateway gives merchant access to local and alternative payment options across Europe, advanced payment flows, and a modular ecosystem of solution, all delivered without adding integration or support complexity.
 Operational efficiency through Verifone’s white-glove services. Merchants benefit from Verifone’s end-to-end deployment, onboarding, installation, logistics, and managed services through one partner. This approach standardizes rollout quality, accelerates expansion timelines, and reduces operational overhead for multi-regional merchants.
 Brand-agnostic estate management for multi-vendor environments . Aevi’s terminal management system enables enterprises to gain the flexibility to manage and evolve in-person payment estates spanning multiple hardware brands, reducing lock-in while supporting new use cases as business needs change.
“The Verifone partnership is about complimenting each of our strengths and accelerating innovation across a rapidly evolving payment industry that historically relies on proprietary infrastructure. This partnership greatly increases our collective ability to meet the demands of our customers in an increasingly complex environment of in person payment acceptance."
Alex Benjamin, General Manager, Aevi
"With Aevi, we are expanding the power and flexibility of Verifone’s device ecosystem. Together, we’re giving merchants a global solution that removes fragmented integrations and regional barriers so they can scale faster and operate with far less complexity."
Rajeev Yerukalapudi, EVP, Global Head of Strategy & Partnerships at Verifone
