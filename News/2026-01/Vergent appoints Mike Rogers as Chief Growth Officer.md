RIDGELAND, Miss.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--**Vergent**, a complete, end-to-end lending platform, has announced the appointment of **Mike Rogers** as **Chief Growth Officer**. Rogers brings more than 25 years of senior leadership experience across fintech, payments, and financial technology, with a proven track record of driving revenue growth, scaling go-to-market strategies, and building diversified, sustainable business models.

> As Vergent continues to grow, Mike will play a critical role in expanding our market presence, strengthening client relationships, and ensuring long-term, sustainable revenue growth.
> 
> Share

In his new role, Rogers will be responsible for company-wide revenue growth with a focus on delivering industry changing consumer user experience, customer value and service delivery. His responsibilities include ownership of diversified transaction revenue growth, new client acquisition, expansion across existing clients, and execution of Vergent’s go-to-market strategy. He will work closely with Sales, Marketing, Product, and Client Operations leadership to align growth initiatives with platform capabilities and market demand.

Rogers brings deep expertise in building and scaling revenue engines across lending, payments, bill pay, and financial services technology. Most recently, he served in senior growth and revenue leadership roles at companies including Solutions by Text, Constant.ai, and ACI Worldwide, where he led commercial strategy, partnerships, and market expansion efforts. Over the course of his career, Rogers has supported or led multiple high-value M&A transactions, built high-performing sales and partnership organizations, and driven significant increases in recurring and transaction-based revenues.

“Mike’s experience building diversified revenue models and scaling go-to-market execution makes him an ideal leader for this role,” said **Scott Putnam**, CEO of Vergent. “As Vergent continues to grow, Mike will play a critical role in expanding our market presence, strengthening client relationships, and ensuring long-term, sustainable revenue growth.”

“I’m excited to join Vergent at a pivotal stage of its growth,” said Rogers. “Vergent has built a powerful lending and servicing solutions platform with significant opportunity ahead. My focus will be on expanding adoption, diversifying revenue streams, and executing a go-to-market strategy that supports both our clients’ success and Vergent’s long-term growth objectives.”

**About Vergent**

Vergent is a complete, end-to-end lending platform that streamlines the full loan lifecycle, from origination to servicing, payments, communications, and collections. Trusted by lenders across the U.S., Canada, South Africa, Australia, and Central America, Vergent delivers scalable, cloud-based technology used by more than 20,000 daily users and supports over $1 trillion in loans serviced. Founded in 2006, Vergent empowers lenders with flexible configuration, automation, and seamless integrations to operate efficiently and grow responsibly. Learn more or schedule a free demo at **www.vergentlms.com**.
