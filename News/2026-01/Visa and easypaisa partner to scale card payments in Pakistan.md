# Visa and easypaisa partner to scale card payments in Pakistan

Visa, a global leader in digital payments, and easypaisa Digital Bank have signed a strategic agreement to strengthen their collaboration with a focused objective of expanding card-based digital transactions across Pakistan and international.
The agreement formalizes the partnership between Visa and easypaisa Digital Bank, reflecting a shared commitment to increasing the issuance and usage of Visa-branded debit and credit cards. The collaboration aims to enable secure, seamless, and reliable card transactions across both physical and digital merchant channels.
 
 
 
 

As part of this collaboration, easypaisa Digital Bank plans to enhance the overall cardholder experience through the launch of premium debit card, credit card and cross border initiatives, while promoting increased usage of cards for international transactions across all channels, including POS, e-commerce, and ATM. The partnership also includes joint initiatives in marketing, customer activation, merchant enablement, training, and technology enhancements.
Commenting on the collaboration, Jahanzeb Khan, President & CEO, easypaisa digital bank, said, “This partnership with Visa allows us to leverage its global payment network, advanced transaction processing capabilities, and robust security standards to deliver seamless and secure payment experiences, drive greater card usage, and expand merchant acceptance across the country for our ambition to unlock opportunities for all and make cashless Pakistan a reality”.
Umar S. Khan, Country Manager Pakistan & Afghanistan, Visa, added, “Pakistan’s shift to digital is accelerating, and easypaisa’s scale shows what’s possible as they serve roughly one in every five Pakistanis. By pairing that momentum with Visa’s global network and advanced security, we can help more consumers and merchants move from cash to digital payment experiences complementing the national ecosystem and widening participation.”
Through this agreement, both organizations reaffirm their commitment to supporting Pakistan’s evolving digital payments ecosystem, advancing financial inclusion, and contributing to the development of a secure, scalable, and cash-lite economy.
With a customer base representing one in every five Pakistanis, a 31% female user base, and over 4.5 billion transactions processed in 2025, valued at over PKR 15 trillion, approximately 13% of Pakistan’s GDP, easypaisa digital bank continues to set new benchmarks for digital banking and financial empowerment across Pakistan.
