# Juniper builds out TSI portfolio with Tagit acquisition

Tagit, based in Singapore, will join a portfolio already including SysArc Infomatix and Airport Information Systems.

[](https://www.fintechfutures.com/author/tyler-pathe)

Tyler Pathe,Reporter, FinTech Futures
January 21, 2026

Tagit Singapore, a digital banking solutions provider, has been acquired by Juniper Group, a Spanish operating subsidiary of Vela Software and Constellation Software Inc (CSI). The financial terms of the deal have not been disclosed.

Founded in 2004, Tagit serves banks and government clients across Asia, the Middle East and Africa with its flagship Mobeix digital banking platform. The platform features open-API systems for customer onboarding, payments, account management, and retail, corporate, and family banking.

Current clients include Saraswat Co-operative Bank, Baiduri Bank, and Axis Bank, among others, with Tagit processing $100 billion+ in transactions annually.

Following the acquisition, Tagit will operate under TSI Group, an India-headquartered division acquired by Juniper in 2020 which supplies organisations with coding, testing, analysis, and debugging solutions, as well as other IT services. Juniper says the addition of Tagit will enable TSI to "strengthen its value proposition".

The TSI portfolio currently comprises lending solutions provider SysArc Infomatix and airport operations management system Airport Information Systems.

The purchase also includes the Tagit team, which has been led by former SunGard VP Sandeep Bagaria as CEO since 2012.
