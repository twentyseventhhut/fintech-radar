# eToro adds DKK accounts in Denmark

> [!info] 2026-01-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇰 eToro adds DKK accounts in Denmark after expanding Nasdaq Nordic data. The company said Danish users can now deposit, hold, withdraw, and invest directly in DKK. Users can choose whether to fund trades using their DKK or USD balance, rather than converting to USD by default.
