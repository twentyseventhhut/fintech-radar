# India sees ~3-4% GDP consumption stimulus, says HDFC AMC

> [!info] 2026-01-31 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] India is seeing a multi-pronged consumption stimulus of ~3–4% of GDP over FY25–FY28 from GST and income-tax cuts, repo rate cuts, welfare spending, and the 8th Pay Commission. Source: HDFC Asset Management
