# Wero payments move closer in Luxembourg

> [!info] 2026-01-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇱🇺 Wero payments move one step closer in Luxembourg. Luxembourg is expected to join the Wero network in June 2026, with five major retail banks: Spuerkeess, BGL BNP Paribas, Banque Internationale à Luxembourg, Post Luxembourg, and Banque Raiffeisen. Wero is Europe’s attempt to build a continent-wide alternative to dominant global payment platforms.
