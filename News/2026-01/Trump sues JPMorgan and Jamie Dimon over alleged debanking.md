# Trump sues JPMorgan and Jamie Dimon over alleged debanking

> [!info] 2026-01-23 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Donald Trump files lawsuit against JPMorgan and Jamie Dimon. The case, which was filed in Miami, Florida, on 22 January, is being brought on behalf of the US president and several of his companies. The US president had said that he would sue the bank for “incorrectly and inappropriately” debanking him.

[This Week in Fintech] Donald Trump sued JPMorgan Chase and CEO Jamie Dimon for $5 billion, alleging that the bank engaged in "political debanking" by closing his accounts following the January 6 Capitol riot.(JPMorgan denied claims of ideological discrimination, asserting that the bank does not close accounts based on political or religious beliefs despite the President's legal threats.)
