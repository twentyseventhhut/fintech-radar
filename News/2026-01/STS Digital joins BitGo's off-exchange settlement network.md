NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BitGo, the digital asset infrastructure company, announced that STS Digital has joined Go Network Off-Exchange Settlement as an exchange partner, expanding institutional access to secure trading and off-exchange settlement for spot and derivatives. Regulated by the Bermuda Monetary Authority, STS Digital delivers principal liquidity across spot, options, and bespoke structured products for institutional market participants globally.

Through this integration, institutional clients can trade with STS Digital while keeping assets protected in regulated, qualified custody with BitGo Bank and Trust, National Association, a federally chartered digital asset bank with oversight from the U.S. Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC). Assets remain secured off-exchange at all times in cold storage, segregated from BitGo’s own assets, and insured up to $250M, reducing counterparty and commingling risk while preserving access to institutional liquidity.

Post-trade settlement is automated by Go Network’s infrastructure rails and also occurs within regulated custody, allowing institutions to streamline digital asset operations while maintaining continuous access to their assets and full visibility into the trading and settlement lifecycle. By keeping assets secure with BitGo, institutions can also access BitGo’s full suite of services including OTC trading, financing, staking, and Go Network Delivery-vs-Payment settlement, to support a broader range of digital asset activity.

By separating custody from execution, this partnership brings a familiar institutional market structure to digital assets, bridging traditional finance and digital markets while empowering institutions to modernize trading, risk management, and capital deployment strategies.

“We are pleased to welcome STS Digital to Go Network,” said Brett Reeves, Head of Go Network and European Sales. “Go Network was built to provide institutions with a better market structure, one that separates trading and custody, allowing them to operate within a framework that prioritizes asset protection, segregation, and regulatory oversight without sacrificing advanced trading capabilities. Partnerships like this help institutions build their digital asset strategies on a foundation they can trust.”

"This integration with BitGo’s Go Network is a meaningful step forward for institutional digital asset markets,” said Gideon Hyams, Chairman of STS Digital. “By enabling clients to access STS Digital’s options trading platform while keeping assets securely custodied with BitGo, we are reinforcing a market structure that prioritizes security, transparency, and regulatory alignment. Separating execution from custody is essential for institutional adoption, and this partnership allows institutions to trade with confidence while maintaining full control over their assets.”

Together, BitGo and STS Digital are delivering a higher standard of protection and accountability to digital asset markets and supporting institutional participation in digital assets through regulated infrastructure.

**About BitGo**BitGo is the digital asset infrastructure company delivering custody, wallets, staking, trading, financing, stablecoins, and settlement services from regulated cold storage. Since 2013, BitGo has focused on accelerating the transition of the financial system to a digital asset economy. BitGo maintains a global presence and multiple regulated entities, including BitGo Bank & Trust, National Association, a federally chartered digital asset bank. Today, BitGo serves thousands of institutions, including many of the industry's top brands, financial institutions, exchanges, and platforms, and millions of investors worldwide.

**About STS Digital**STS Digital is a regulated principal trading firm specialised in digital-asset derivatives and providing institutional-grade market access. Clients can trade vanilla and exotic options, spot, and structured products through a unified platform spanning UI, API, and voice channels. Built by derivatives veterans and licensed by the Bermuda Monetary Authority, STS Digital delivers deep liquidity, competitive pricing, and robust risk-management to ensure a seamless trading experience.
