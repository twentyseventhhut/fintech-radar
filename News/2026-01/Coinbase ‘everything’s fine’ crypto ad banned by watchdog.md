# Coinbase ‘everything’s fine’ crypto ad banned by watchdog

> [!info] 2026-01-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Coinbase ‘everything’s fine’ crypto ad banned by watchdog. Coinbase has had a cryptocurrency advertising campaign banned by the UK’s Advertising Standards Authority. The ads, which used satire to link crypto to cost-of-living pressures, were found to misleadingly imply digital assets could offer a solution, and have been ordered not to appear again.
