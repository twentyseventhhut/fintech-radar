[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Magie snags $5 million and brings financial services to businesses' WhatsApp. This capital supported the validation of the product for the end consumer and now finances the expansion of the corporate offering and the business account, within the strategy of growing via WhatsApp.
