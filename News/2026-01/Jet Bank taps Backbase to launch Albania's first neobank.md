Jet Bank and Backbase partner to launch Albania’s first digital-only bank
Strategic partnership enables a six-month path from setup to launch for the new digital bank
 Tirana, 19 January 2026 - Backbase, the leading AI-powered banking platform, announces its collaboration with Jet Bank to power the launch of Albania's newest digital-only bank . The partnership positions Jet Bank to deliver a fully digital, customer-first banking experience built on a unified engagement platform designed for speed, scalability, and continuous innovation.
As a greenfield digital bank, Jet Bank faced a critical challenge: launching fast without compromising stability, security, or the ability to evolve. Traditional banking infrastructure wasn't an option. The bank needed a foundation that could support rapid market entry while enabling ongoing product innovation and regulatory discipline.
Jet Bank chose the Backbase AI-native Banking OS to unify customer interactions across mobile and web channels - coordinating journeys across core banking, card processing, and third-party services from a single execution layer.
The collaboration enabled Jet Bank to move from setup to User Acceptance Testing in approximately three months, with full operational capability as a licensed digital bank within as little as six months. From launch, the platform supports monthly product releases, allowing the bank to introduce new features and services based on real customer usage without disrupting existing journeys.
Our ambition is to set a new standard for digital banking in Albania by building a bank that feels intuitive, reliable, and relevant in everyday use. This partnership with Backbase gives us the digital foundation to launch quickly while maintaining the trust and discipline expected from a licensed bank. We're not just launching an app - we're building a platform that can continuously evolve with our customers. ‍ Fatbardha Rino, CEO at JET Bank
The Banking OS delivers end-to-end banking capabilities across customer onboarding, lending, investing, and Conversational Banking - all running on one shared execution layer. Backbase delivered the solution through a managed hosting environment, working hands-on with Jet Bank throughout to ensure rapid delivery and production readiness.
A key factor in Jet Bank's decision was Backbase's marketplace-driven approach, which allows the bank to easily introduce new products and third-party services within a consistent digital experience. This capability is critical for a digital bank planning to expand its offering over time through both core banking services and innovative propositions via partnerships.
Jet Bank represents the future of digital banking - fast to market, built for continuous innovation, and designed around customer needs from day one. Their strategic choice to partner with a single, unified platform rather than assembling disconnected channels demonstrates the discipline required to build a truly scalable digital bank. We're proud to support their vision and help them bring world-class digital banking to Albania. Robert Mihaljek, Regional Vice President at Backbase
Beyond launch, the partnership will focus on ongoing optimization and expansion. Jet Bank will refine customer journeys based on real usage, roll out additional features in iterative releases, and strengthen digital servicing capabilities. The platform's composable architecture enables the bank to introduce new capabilities, including personalization and AI-driven services, without disrupting core systems.
In the medium term, Jet Bank aims to establish itself as a trusted daily banking partner in Albania by expanding its digital product portfolio based on customer needs and behaviors. Long term, the bank's goal is to grow into a scalable regional digital banking platform capable of launching innovative, market-first products and expanding beyond Albania while maintaining agility and regulatory discipline.
This collaboration also highlights the strength of the partnership model between Jet Bank, Backbase, and implementation partners. The initial phases were carried out by Backbase to meet strict timeframes, while simultaneously onboarding Facilization - Jet Bank's partner for core banking implementation - to support subsequent phases. This coordinated delivery model reduced execution risk and enabled smooth, efficient rollout.
 About JET Bank 
Jet Bank is Albania’s first fully digital, mobile-native bank, delivering secure, simple, and transparent banking through a seamless app-based experience. Built to offer modern financial services that emphasize speed, clarity, and customer control, it operates to the highest standards of security, compliance, and governance. 
Owned by Jet Holding B.V., Amsterdam, Jet Bank brings proven European banking standards to the local market, supporting the long-term evolution of digital banking in Albania.
