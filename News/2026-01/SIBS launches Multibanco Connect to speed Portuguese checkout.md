# SIBS launches Multibanco Connect to speed Portuguese checkout

MULTIBANCO Connect
 SIBS introduces MULTIBANCO Connect – an innovative solution set to transform the payments landscape by integrating point-of-sale systems (ePOS/ECR) with intelligent payment terminals (SmartPOS). This new solution eliminates the need for manual entry of purchase amounts on the payment terminal, delivering a more efficient and seamless checkout experience in which transaction data — such as the purchase value and the customer’s tax identification number — is shared automatically. 
 MULTIBANCO Connect offers multiple benefits for both merchants and customers, including a significant reduction in waiting lines of up to 40% and increased accuracy in value entry by minimizing the risk of human error. These advantages translate into an improved shopping experience, supported by faster and more reliable transactions. The solution also incorporates advanced functionalities through its service application, which operates alongside the MULTIBANCO SmartPOS App, enabling a flexible, cable-free connection that adapts to different business models. In addition, seamless integration with the MB WAY ecosystem allows customers to automatically share their data (fiscal number and email). 
 With a fast certification and installation process, MULTIBANCO Connect can be implemented in just four steps, making it an agile solution for sectors such as hospitality, retail, healthcare and wellness (hospitals, clinics and the pharmaceutical industry), public services, and culture and entertainment (cinemas, theatres, live events and monuments), among others. These sectors can benefit from more optimized and standardized payment operations. 
 With this new solution, SIBS reinforces its leadership position in the digital transformation of the payments sector, providing merchants and consumers with tools that simplify processes and create value in an increasingly demanding market.
