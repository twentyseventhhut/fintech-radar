# Startuplinks and Cuantico VP form Latin American venture alliance

> [!info] 2026-01-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎Startuplinks, a VC community and research platform, and Cuantico VP, a B2B media and market intelligence firm, announced a strategic alliance to reduce information asymmetry in the region’s venture ecosystem. The initiative plans to channel up to US$10 million over the next ten years through multiple mechanisms, including alternative investment approaches.
