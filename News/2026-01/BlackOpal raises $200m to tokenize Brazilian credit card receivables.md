# BlackOpal raises $200m to tokenize Brazilian credit card receivables

> [!info] 2026-01-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 BlackOpal secures $200m to tokenize Brazilian credit card receivables. BlackOpal has launched GemStone, designed to provide emerging-market yields without direct credit risk. GemStone acquires receivables through true sale with ownership registered via Brazil’s central registry and automated collections through Visa and Mastercard rails.
