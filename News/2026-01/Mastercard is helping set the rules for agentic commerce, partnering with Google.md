## The new rules of the road for agentic commerce

### From standards to scale: Mastercard joins Google on new protocol, works to bring Agent Pay to Microsoft Copilot Checkout and grows Start Path to turbocharge AI-powered commerce.

Pablo Fourez

Chief Digital Officer, Mastercard

Sherri Haymond

Global Head, Digital Commercialization, Mastercard

Speed without trust is chaos. We’re able to zip along freeways precisely because we have shared expectations that help us anticipate moves, interpret signals and understand intent.We’re quietly coordinating, following rules and using an unspoken language that makes the whole system move efficiently. Without those rules — or when some people ignore them — the system breaks down.

It’s the same with payments. When we make a payment in store or online,we know what to expect, and this breeds trust in the digital ecosystem. That trust is the result of decades of innovation, engineering and collaboration within a rules-based framework.

The next wave of digital commerce is agentic, where intelligent agents help consumers and companies discover, compare and complete purchases seamlessly. And it’s coming at us fast. A few years ago, AI agents were the stuff of science fiction. At last week’s NRF, the National Retail Federation’s annual trade show in New York, it was clear the agentic commerce ecosystem is already moving from experimentation to real commercial infrastructure.

Also last week, we joined forces with Google on its Universal Commerce Protocol, an open protocol that enables interoperability between AI agents and merchants. Protocols like these are essential to scaling agentic commerce, which is why we continue to collaborate across the value chain, including Google’s Agent Payments Protocol and Agent2Agent Protocol, OpenAI’s Agentic Commerce Protocol, and more to ensure these platforms meet the same standards for clear user intent, secure credentials and verifiable agent identity.

These protocols protect merchants from fraud,provide transparency and strengthen authentication for issuers, and deliver confidence and convenience for consumers—without adding friction.It also makes payments more personal with real-time, tailored experiences for every user.

Our own tools are powered by these same principles of trust and responsibility. Welaunched Mastercard Agent Paylast year to deliver smarter, more secure and more personal payments experiences to consumers,merchants and issuers, and now we're actively working with Microsoft to bring Mastercard Agent Pay toCopilot Checkout.We’re also continuing to work with leaders likeOpenAI,Cloudflare andPayPalto further advance secure and intent-driven agentic commerce solutions.

We’re fueling the future of agentic commerce, and we’re not slowing down.These large-scale industry partnerships are critical,but we also need to engage with innovative newcomers who can bring the cutting-edge technology, products and services that make agentic commerce essential for people and businesses.

To accelerate adoption and ensure that our network continues to support that rapid growth, we’re expanding Start Path, Mastercard’s award-winning startup engagement program, to advance agentic commerce and services.

Since its inception in 2014, Start Path has welcomed over 500 companies across more than 60 countries into its portfolio, including many on the cutting edge of AI. Next generation technology companies enabling agentic payments and commerce ecosystems — as well as current Start Path portfolio companies — can unlock a wide range of benefits through the program. Start Path is a gateway to new connections within Mastercard’s global network of partners and customers, which helps accelerate adoption for novel offerings in new markets and distribution channels. Companies can access bespoke opportunities to collaborate on new products and services tailored for agentic environments and advanced capabilities built for agentic experiences.

You can learn more about how to apply to Start Pathhere.Applications for the next cohort open now.

Intelligent agents can accelerate the pace of commerce only if the foundation beneath them is predictable,secure and universally understood.That’s why we’re building the standards, partnerships and capabilities that allow innovation to move at full speed without losing the coordination that keeps the system flowing.

Learn more about Mastercard’s vision for agentic commercehere.
