Adyen nominates Herna Verhagen as Supervisory Board member and Chair
Piero Overmars to retire from Adyen’s Supervisory Board
Download the image above to use for your publication.
January 20, Amsterdam - Adyen N.V. announces that the Supervisory Board has nominated Herna Verhagen for appointment as a member of the Supervisory Board, with effect from the Annual General Meeting of Shareholders to be held on May 28, 2026 (Adyen’s Annual General Meeting).
Piero Overmars, who has served multiple terms on the Supervisory Board since January 2017, will not stand for re-election as Chair of the Supervisory Board upon the expiry of his current term. Subject to her appointment, the Supervisory Board intends to elect Herna Verhagen as Chair following the retirement of Piero Overmars. 
Herna Verhagen brings more than 20 years of experience in executive and non-executive leadership roles. She previously served as Chief Executive Officer of PostNL N.V. and has been a member of the Supervisory Board of ING Groep N.V. since 2019 and of Royal Philips N.V. since 2022.
“We are very pleased to nominate Herna Verhagen for appointment to our Supervisory Board,” said Caoimhe Keogan, Vice Chair of the Supervisory Board and Chair of the Nomination and Remuneration Committee. “Herna brings extensive experience from both executive and non-executive roles. We are confident that the Supervisory Board will benefit from her broad knowledge and judgment as Adyen continues to scale its platform.” Keogan continues: “We thank Piero Overmars for his commitment and his role as an outstanding Chair, and express our deep appreciation for his significant contributions during his tenure.”
Herna Verhagen: “Adyen is a company that has grown rapidly by building technology at the core of everything it does. I am impressed by the company’s entrepreneurial energy, technological depth, and the enthusiasm with which its teams continue to scale a truly global platform. I look forward to contributing to Adyen’s long-term growth.”
The proposed appointment has been approved by the Dutch Central Bank ( De Nederlandsche Bank ) and will be submitted for approval to shareholders at Adyen’s Annual General Meeting. Piero Overmars will step down from the Supervisory Board toward the end of 2026.
 About Adyen 
Adyen (ADYEN:AMS) is the financial technology platform of choice for leading companies. By providing end-to-end payments capabilities, data-driven insights, and financial products in a single global solution, Adyen helps businesses achieve their ambitions faster. With offices around the world, Adyen works with the likes of Meta, Uber, H&M, eBay, and Microsoft.
