# Stream raises $90M Series D for workplace finance

> [!info] 2026-01-21 · 3 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] Stream just raised a $90M Series D to scale its workplace finance platform and expand further into the US.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 UK FinTech Stream raises $90m to tackle £31B in lost pensions and scale in the US. Stream plans to use the new capital to grow its pensions and long-term savings offerings in the UK and accelerate its expansion in the US through new partnerships and integrations.

[This Week in Fintech] Stream (formerly Wagestream)raised$90 million in series D to expand its product offerings, including pensions and long-term savings in the UK, and accelerate growth in the US through new strategic partnerships and integrations.
