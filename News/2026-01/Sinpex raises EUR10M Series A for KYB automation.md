# Sinpex raises EUR10M Series A for KYB automation

> [!info] 2026-01-20 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] Sinpex just raised a €10M Series A to scale its AI-powered KYB automation platform. Europe prepares for a much stricter AML regime coming into force in 2027. The platform unifies the full KYB lifecycle, from onboarding and UBO identification to ongoing reviews and audit-ready reporting.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 KYB platform Sinpex secures €10 million Series A. The new capital will be used to accelerate growth, strengthen Sinpex’s position as the category-defining Know Your Business (KYB) automation platform in Europe, and support expansion in key international markets, such as France and the Netherlands.
