 payabl. Appoints Breno Oliveira as Chief Product Officer 
 People Moves
 20.01.2026 08:45 am 
 payabl., a leading European financial technology provider, has appointed Breno Oliveira as Chief Product Officer (CPO). 
 In his new role, Oliveira will lead payabl.’s product strategy and execution, overseeing the continued development of the company’s modular platform and its flagship unified interface, payabl.one. His appointment reflects payabl.’s clear, uncompromising focus on product-led growth, platform scalability, and delivering consistent, reliable money flow for merchants across markets and verticals. 
 Oliveira joined payabl. in 2022, with the role and responsibilities steadily expanding since then. Initially joining the company to develop its core banking, he progressed to become payabl.’s  Head of Product, leading teams across multiple product areas and markets. 
 During his tenure, Oliveira has played a central role in delivering a number of key product milestones, including the launch of payabl.’s in-house cloud-native core banking platform, its award-winning card issuing capabilities, mobile SDK, payabl.one, and expanded payment method support such as American Express, SEPA Direct Debit, and Wero. These launches have strengthened payabl.’s end-to-end offering and its ability to support complex merchant needs at scale. 
 As CPO, Oliveira will focus on strengthening payabl.’s presence in the UK and across the EU, expanding the company’s mobile capabilities, broadening the card issuing portfolio and card network coverage, and ensuring that product development remains closely aligned with real merchant needs.  
 Commenting on the appointment, Ugne Buraciene, Group CEO of payabl., said: “Breno has been instrumental in shaping our product direction over the past few years. He combines deep technical understanding with a strong sense of commercial reality, and crucially, he knows what products and services merchants want and how to build them. His leadership will be key to ensuring we continue to scale without compromising the quality of service that merchants know payabl. for.” 
 Breno Oliveira, Chief Product Officer at payabl. added: “We are entering a new phase of growth and international expansion at payabl. My focus is to ensure that payabl. builds products that are reliable, well-designed, and grounded in real merchant needs. That means combining robust infrastructure with an exceptional user experience, and solving problems properly so our clients can stay focused on running their businesses.”
