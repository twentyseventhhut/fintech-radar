# Revolut to seek own US banking license, drops acquisition

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Revolut scraps plans to buy US lender in favour of push for standalone licence. The company has held talks with US officials about seeking a licence through the Office for the Comptroller of the Currency in hopes of a quick process amid the Trump administration's deregulatory push, according to the report.

[This Week in Fintech] Revolut shifted its U.S. strategy by abandoning plans to acquire an existing bank, opting instead to apply for its own de novo banking license to gain more control over its American operations.
