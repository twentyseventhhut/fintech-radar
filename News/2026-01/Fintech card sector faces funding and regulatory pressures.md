## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] The fintech card sector has faced significant challenges over the past two years, including higher funding costs, stricter underwriting, and increased regulatory scrutiny. Several high-profile partnerships have struggled, including the unwinding of the Bilt and Wells Fargo relationship, which highlighted the complexity of combining novel use cases with traditional bank compliance and economics.
