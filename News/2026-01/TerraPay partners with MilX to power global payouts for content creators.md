## TerraPay Partners with MilX to Power Global Payouts for Content Creators

January 28, 2026|News

TerraPay, a global money movement company, today announced its strategic partnership with MilX powered by Air Media Tech/Influencers Group, marking TerraPay’s official launch of the payouts for content creators and the gig economy. This milestone reinforces TerraPay’s vision of powering borderless payments for emerging digital communities worldwide.

The creator economy is booming, valued at $253 billion in 2025 and projected to surpass $2 trillion by 2035, growing at a CAGR of 23.3%. With over 207 million active creators globally, platforms face mounting challenges in delivering fast, compliant, and cost-effective cross-border payouts. TerraPay’s partnership with MilX addresses these challenges head-on, enabling creators to receive earnings instantly in local currencies across 70+ markets via bank accounts, wallets, and cards.

This partnership empowers creators to access their earnings quickly and securely, while supporting platforms in scaling globally. With the creator payment solutions market projected to grow from $4.5 billion in 2024 to $18.5 billion by 2033, this collaboration positions TerraPay and Air Media Tech at the forefront of innovation in the creator economy.

“Partnering with MilX allows us to give creators what they’ve lacked for far too long – instant, secure access to their earnings in local currencies, straight into their bank accounts or digital wallets, without FX friction or hidden fees. By combining MilX’s creator ecosystem with TerraPay’s global payout infrastructure, we’re enabling faster scaling for the platform and true financial freedom for creators everywhere.” – Valli Ardalan, SVP and Head of Europe, TerraPay.

“We’re excited to partner with TerraPay to further strengthen our global payout capabilities. This collaboration helps us deliver even more seamless, borderless payouts — enabling creators to focus on growing their audiences while we take care of the payments.” – Paul Lekhnovsky, CEO, MilX.

### About MilX (Air Media Tech)

MilX is a financial service and ecosystem designed specifically for YouTube creators to effectively manage and access their income. It offers fast, flexible, and convenient payouts — including daily access to earnings, multiple transfer options, and the ability to unlock future revenue — so creators can focus on growth without waiting for traditional YouTube payment cycles.

Founded by Air Media Tech, a licensed YouTube partner with over 15 years of experience helping creators build, distribute, and grow content, MilX helps thousands of creators around the world unlock their earnings in ways that fit their needs.

MilX supports multiple payout options – including bank transfers, cards, e-wallets, and crypto – across dozens of countries and currencies. Creators can choose how and when to receive their funds, access earnings daily.

### About TerraPay

TerraPay simplifies global money movement, providing a single connection to one of the most expansive cross-border payment networks, regulated across multiple markets. Our network enables payments to receiving and sending countries worldwide, reaching a vast network of mobile wallets, bank accounts, and cards.​

​On a mission to create a borderless financial world, TerraPay makes money transfers instant, reliable, transparent, and fully compliant for its partners, connecting them to 3.7Bn+ mobile wallets, 7.5Bn+ bank accounts, in over 150+ countries.We work behind the scenes as the trusted partners for some of the world’s most innovative financial players, from banks and digital wallets to MTOs, merchants and fintech platforms.​

​Since its founding, TerraPay has built a global interoperable wallet network that advances financial inclusion, even in hard-to-reach markets. TerraPay is headquartered in London, with offices in cities including Bangalore, Dubai, Bogotá, Dares Salaam, Kampala, and Singapore.

TerraPay , the global money movement company, has officially launched its payout solution for content creators and the gig economy through a strategic partnership with financial service MilX . 
The collaboration marks a significant expansion for TerraPay into the booming creator sector. The new service addresses a persistent pain point for digital creatives: the difficulty of accessing earnings quickly and cost-effectively across borders.
The creator economy is projected to be valued at $253billion in 2025, with over 207 million active creators globally. However, platforms often struggle to deliver compliant cross-border payments efficiently.
Through this partnership, creators using MilX—a service powered by Air Media Tech designed specifically for YouTubers—will be able to receive earnings instantly in local currencies across 70+ markets. The integration supports payouts to bank accounts, digital wallets, and cards, aiming to eliminate foreign exchange friction and hidden fees.
 Valli Ardalan , SVP and head of Europe at TerraPay, commented: “Partnering with MilX allows us to give creators what they’ve lacked for far too long – instant, secure access to their earnings in local currencies… without FX friction or hidden fees. By combining MilX’s creator ecosystem with TerraPay’s global payout infrastructure, we’re enabling faster scaling for the platform and true financial freedom for creators everywhere.”
The move positions both companies to capitalize on a creator payment solutions market that is projected to grow from $4.5billion in 2024 to $18.5 billion by 2033.
 Paul Lekhnovsky , CEO of MilX, added: “We’re excited to partner with TerraPay to further strengthen our global payout capabilities. This collaboration helps us deliver even more seamless, borderless payouts — enabling creators to focus on growing their audiences while we take care of the payments.”
MilX currently offers creators features such as daily access to earnings and the ability to unlock future revenue, moving away from traditional monthly payment cycles. By leveraging TerraPay’s network—which connects to over 3.7 billion mobile wallets and 7.5 billion bank accounts worldwide—the platform aims to scale these capabilities globally.
