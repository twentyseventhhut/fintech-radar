# Nomura taps Mark McMillan to lead electronic FX

> [!info] 2026-01-19 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Nomura taps former Stanchart Executive Mark McMillan to lead electronic FX. In his new position, McMillan will oversee Nomura’s electronic FX trading business and the associated sales teams that distribute the platform to clients. His new post spans e-trading and sales, which allows Nomura to align platform development, pricing, and distribution under one senior manager.
