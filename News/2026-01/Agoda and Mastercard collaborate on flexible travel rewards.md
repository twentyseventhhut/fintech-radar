# Agoda and Mastercard collaborate on flexible travel rewards

> [!info] 2026-01-15 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 Agoda and Mastercard collaborate to modernize loyalty programs with flexible travel rewards. Through this collaboration, banks connected to Mastercard’s rewards ecosystem can embed the Agoda-powered travel redemption solution into their own loyalty programs, where points can be redeemed instantly and applied directly to Agoda’s global inventory of accommodations and flights.
