# Revolut Business launches GlobalHire employer-of-record service

> [!info] 2026-04-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Revolut Business launches its latest new bet, GlobalHire, to accelerate international expansion for British businesses. By integrating Employer of Record (EoR) functionality, Revolut is taking the concept of embedded finance to a new operational frontier: international talent acquisition and compliance. Additionally, the company bets on Aussie SMEs as it pushes beyond travel cards. And it’s betting that gaps in the local banking system will be something it can fill, especially for SMEs.
