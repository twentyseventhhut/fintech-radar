# Uzbekistan tightens digital banking verification rules from April 22

> [!info] 2026-04-06 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇿 Uzbekistan tightens digital banking verification rules. From April 22, it will introduce stricter registration and verification requirements for digital banking services, aiming to enhance security across mobile apps and online platforms. The new system introduces additional verification stages for user registration and bank card linking.
