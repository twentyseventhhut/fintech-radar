 Enfuce Joins Mastercard Product Express to Fast-Track Business Card Programme Launches Across Europe 
 Payments
 21.04.2026 09:15 am 
 Enfuce, a leading European card issuing and payment processing provider, will join Mastercard Product Express to support business card issuance across the European region. 
 The collaboration extends Mastercard’s Product Express offering in Europe to include business card programmes, enabling financial institutions, fintechs and non-financial institutions (non-FIs) to launch new solutions with greater speed and efficiency. 
 Product Express is Mastercard’s ready-to-go global enablement platform, designed to streamline the card issuance process, reduce complexity, and accelerate time to market. 
 The platform brings together pre-integrated partners, standardised processes, and streamlined onboarding, helping reduce development timelines and operational overhead. 
 As a certified partner of the Product Express platform, Enfuce will provide a simplified pathway to launch fully compliant, scalable business card solutions through its configurable technology stack, which offers access to instant virtual-card issuance, advanced spend controls, and full capabilities across debit, credit, and prepaid categories. 
 Denise Johansson, CEO and Co-Founder of Enfuce, said: “Enfuce’s integration with Mastercard Product Express is more than just a faster way to launch cards, it’s a smarter way to scale. With our cloud-native, API-first platform, partners can instantly issue cards, tailor interactions, and embed compliance from day one. This partnership proves that speed, compliance, and innovation can go hand in hand, turning card issuing into a true growth engine for financial institutions and fintechs.” 
 Through the collaboration, Enfuce and Mastercard aim to empower financial institutions, fintechs and non-FIs to deliver solutions that address evolving business needs, helping organisations operate more effectively and grow with confidence. 
 Tulsi Narayan, Executive Vice President, Commercial and New Payment Flows Business Europe, Mastercard, says: “We’re pleased to welcome Enfuce to our Product Express platform in Europe, enabling faster, more streamlined launches of business card programmes. By making it easier to bring these solutions to market, we’re helping businesses access tools that improve financial control, transparency and efficiency in managing everyday business spend. This is especially valuable for SMEs, helping them operate more efficiently, unlock new opportunities for growth, and thrive in the digital economy.” 
 The integration of Enfuce to Mastercard Product Express builds on a long-standing relationship with Mastercard, which includes their Card as a Service platform and 2-in-1 debit and credit card.

# Enfuce Joins Mastercard Product Express to Fast-Track Business Card Programme Launches Across Europe

April 21, 2026 | HELSINKI, FINLAND

Enfuce, a leading European card issuing and payment processing provider, will join Mastercard Product Express to support business card issuance across the European region.

The collaboration extends Mastercard’s Product Express offering in Europe to include business card programmes, enabling financial institutions, fintechs and non-financial institutions (non-FIs) to launch new solutions with greater speed and efficiency.

Product Express is Mastercard’s ready-to-go global enablement platform, designed to streamline the card issuance process, reduce complexity, and accelerate time to market.

The platform brings together pre-integrated partners, standardised processes, and streamlined onboarding, helping reduce development timelines and operational overhead.

As a certified partner of the Product Express platform, Enfuce will provide a simplified pathway to launch fully compliant, scalable business card solutions through its configurable technology stack, which offers access to instant virtual-card issuance, advanced spend controls, and full capabilities across debit, credit, and prepaid [](https://www.mastercard.com/news)categories[[1]](https://www.mastercard.com/news#_msocom_1).

Denise Johansson, CEO and Co-Founder of Enfuce, said: “Enfuce’s integration with Mastercard Product Express is more than just a faster way to launch cards, it’s a smarter way to scale. With our cloud-native, API-first platform, partners can instantly issue cards, tailor interactions, and embed compliance from day one. This partnership proves that speed, compliance, and innovation can go hand in hand, turning card issuing into a true growth engine for financial institutions and fintechs.”

Through the collaboration, Enfuce and Mastercard aim to empower financial institutions, fintechs and non-FIs to deliver solutions that address evolving business needs, helping organisations operate more effectively and grow with confidence.

Tulsi Narayan, Executive Vice President, Commercial and New Payment Flows Business Europe, Mastercard, says: “We’re pleased to welcome Enfuce to our Product Express platform in Europe, enabling faster, more streamlined launches of business card programmes. By making it easier to bring these solutions to market, we’re helping businesses, access tools that improve financial control, transparency and efficiency in managing everyday business spend. This is especially valuable for SMEs, helping them operate more efficiently, unlock new opportunities for growth, and thrive in the digital economy.”

The integration of Enfuce to Mastercard Product Express builds on a long-standing relationship with Mastercard, which includes their Card as a Service platform and 2-in-1 debit and credit card.

For more information about Mastercard Product Express, visit: https://www.mastercard.com/global/en/business/industry-segment/fintech/program/product-express.html

About Enfuce

Founded in 2016, Enfuce is a female-founded and led global payments partner solving payments complexity for ambitious brands, banks and fintech leaders.

Enfuce Joins Mastercard Product Express to Fast-Track Business Card Programme Launches Across Europe
 Collaboration expands Mastercard Product Express in Europe, enabling faster and more efficient business card programme launches.   
21 April 2026 – Enfuce, a leading European card issuing and payment processing provider, will join Mastercard Product Express to support business card issuance across the European region.
The collaboration extends Mastercard’s Product Express offering in Europe to include business card programmes, enabling financial institutions, fintechs and non-financial institutions (non-FIs) to launch new solutions with greater speed and efficiency. 
Product Express is Mastercard’s ready-to-go global enablement platform, designed to streamline the card issuance process, reduce complexity, and accelerate time to market. 
The platform brings together pre-integrated partners, standardised processes, and streamlined onboarding, helping reduce development timelines and operational overhead.
As a certified partner of the Product Express platform, Enfuce will provide a simplified pathway to launch fully compliant, scalable business card solutions through its configurable technology stack, which offers access to instant virtual-card issuance, advanced spend controls, and full capabilities across debit, credit, and prepaid categories. 
 Denise Johansson, CEO and Co-Founder of Enfuce, said: “Enfuce’s integration with Mastercard Product Express is more than just a faster way to launch cards, it’s a smarter way to scale. With our cloud-native, API-first platform, partners can instantly issue cards, tailor interactions, and embed compliance from day one. This partnership proves that speed, compliance, and innovation can go hand in hand, turning card issuing into a true growth engine for financial institutions and fintechs.”
Through the collaboration, Enfuce and Mastercard aim to empower financial institutions, fintechs and non-FIs to deliver solutions that address evolving business needs, helping organisations operate more effectively and grow with confidence.
 Tulsi Narayan, Executive Vice President, Commercial and New Payment Flows Business Europe, Mastercard, says: “ We’re pleased to welcome Enfuce to our Product Express platform in Europe, enabling faster, more streamlined launches of business card programmes. By making it easier to bring these solutions to market, we’re helping businesses, access tools that improve financial control, transparency and efficiency in managing everyday business spend. This is especially valuable for SMEs, helping them operate more efficiently, unlock new opportunities for growth, and thrive in the digital economy.”
The integration of Enfuce to Mastercard Product Express builds on a long-standing relationship with Mastercard, which includes their Card as a Service platform and 2-in-1 debit and credit card.
