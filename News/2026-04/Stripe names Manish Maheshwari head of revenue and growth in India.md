# Stripe names Manish M as Head of Revenue and Growth in India

_Aims to support global-first and AI-native businesses from India in scaling internationally_

*   byTeam PITCH
*    April 09, 2026 

[](https://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=https://www.pitchonnet.com/on-the-move/stripe-names-manish-m-as-head-of-revenue-and-growth-in-india-39585.html)[](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?&url=https://www.pitchonnet.com/on-the-move/stripe-names-manish-m-as-head-of-revenue-and-growth-in-india-39585.html&text=Stripe%20names%20Manish%20M%20as%20Head%20of%20Revenue%20and%20Growth%20in%20India)[](https://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle?mini=true&url=https://www.pitchonnet.com/on-the-move/stripe-names-manish-m-as-head-of-revenue-and-growth-in-india-39585.html&title=Stripe%20names%20Manish%20M%20as%20Head%20of%20Revenue%20and%20Growth%20in%20India)[](mailto:?subject=Stripe%20names%20Manish%20M%20as%20Head%20of%20Revenue%20and%20Growth%20in%20India%20-%20pitchonnet&body=Hi,%0A%20I%20thought%20you'd%20like%20this:%0A%0A%20https://www.pitchonnet.com/on-the-move/stripe-names-manish-m-as-head-of-revenue-and-growth-in-india-39585.html)

Stripe, the programmable financial services company, has announced the appointment of Manish M as Head of Revenue and Growth for India.

Manish M brings more than two decades of experience across technology, internet platforms, and startups. He has held leadership positions at Twitter, Flipkart, and Intuit, and has also founded startups, including Fanory.ai, an AI-driven creator monetisation platform.

At Stripe, Manish M will concentrate on supporting businesses building for global markets, particularly a new wave of AI companies that are expanding internationally from the outset.

"As more businesses from India build for global audiences, they will require seamless infrastructure to manage payments, subscriptions, usage-based billing and revenue operations across borders," said Paul Harapin, Head of Asia Pacific & Japan at Stripe.

"Manish brings deep experience across the Indian technology space, and we're excited to have him lead our efforts to enable these businesses."

"India is one of the most exciting ecosystems for building global companies today," said Manish M. "Founders here are building for global markets from day one, especially in Al and digital-native sectors. Invideo is just one example of that. I am thrilled to lead Stripe's efforts to provide businesses with the foundational infrastructure they need to scale and monetise globally."

Stripe has also been strengthening its presence in Bengaluru, which has emerged as one of its largest and fastest-growing global hubs. The company continues to invest in engineering, operations, and go-to-market teams in India.

Stripe Names Manish Maheshwari To Drive Revenue And Growth Strategy In India
 
 # Stripe India 
 # Manish Maheshwari 
 # revenue growth 
 # fintech 
 # AI startups 
 # global scaling 
 # leadership appointment 
Stripe has appointed Manish Maheshwari as Head of Revenue and Growth for India, as it sharpens its focus on supporting businesses building for global markets from the country.
In his new role, Maheshwari will drive Stripe’s growth strategy in India, with a particular emphasis on enabling digital-first and AI-led companies to scale internationally. His mandate includes strengthening revenue operations and helping businesses manage cross-border payments, subscriptions and billing infrastructure.
Maheshwari brings over two decades of experience across technology, internet platforms and startups. He has previously held leadership roles at Twitter, Flipkart and Intuit, and has also built ventures of his own, including an AI-driven creator monetisation platform.
Commenting on the appointment, Paul Harapin, Head of Asia Pacific and Japan at Stripe, said, “As more businesses from India build for global audiences, they require seamless infrastructure to manage payments and revenue across borders. Manish brings deep experience across the Indian technology ecosystem, and we are excited to have him lead this effort.”
Maheshwari said India is emerging as a strong base for globally oriented companies, particularly in AI and digital-native sectors. “Founders today are building for global markets from day one. I look forward to enabling them with the infrastructure needed to scale and monetise effectively,” he noted.
The appointment comes as Stripe continues to expand its footprint in India, with Bengaluru emerging as one of its largest and fastest-growing hubs, spanning engineering, operations and go-to-market functions.
