Growth on Tap: Agentic AI Edition
​ ​ This is an official event of #NYFTW26. 
​ Join This Week in Fintech and Alloy for an evening of conversation, connection, and cocktails at the intersection of AI and financial services.
​ ​As machine learning and agentic AI reshape the fraud and risk landscape, the question for fintech and banking leaders is no longer whether to act, it's how to do it right.
