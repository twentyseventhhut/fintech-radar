Empowering Global Commerce: PhotonPay Expands Local Account Capabilities Across Southeast Asia and Africa
HONG KONG, Apr 20, 2026 -- PhotonPay , the next-generation global payment infrastructure, today announced the launch of its local account capabilities in the Philippines and Kenya , further strengthening its presence across high-growth emerging markets. Businesses can now seamlessly collect in Philippine Peso (PHP) and Kenyan Shilling (KES).
 Delivering Frictionless, Real-Time Local Collections 

 Direct Clearing Network Integration: Through connectivity to domestic clearing rails — InstaPay and PesoNet in the Philippines, and PesaLink in Kenya, transactions can be processed in near real-time. This direct access also removes intermediary banks, reducing both costs and delays. 


 Built for Local Payment Preferences: Buyers can pay in their local currency with familiar methods, which removes the need for FX conversion. At the same time, businesses are no longer required to establish local bank accounts, avoiding complex onboarding processes and operational overhead. 


 Unified Liquidity Management: Multi-currency funds are managed through a unified, intelligent dashboard featuring 24/7 real-time FX services , providing businesses with maximum flexibility in global treasury and liquidity management. 


 Institutional-Grade Regulatory Assurance: Every transaction is safeguarded by PhotonPay's rigorous, multi-jurisdictional compliance framework. This ensures that all capital movements adhere to the stringent AML and regulatory standards worldwide. 


 "The launch of PHP and KES local capabilities marks a pivotal step in our mission to build an inclusive and efficient global payment rail," said Chao Xu, VP of Product at PhotonPay . "By connecting global businesses and local clearing networks in the Philippines and Kenya, we are not just providing accounts, we are delivering the infrastructure necessary for businesses to scale fearlessly in high-growth markets."
