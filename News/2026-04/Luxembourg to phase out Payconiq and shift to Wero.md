# Luxembourg to phase out Payconiq and shift to Wero

> [!info] 2026-04-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇱🇺 Luxembourg to phase out Payconiq by the end of September and shift to Wero. Users will need to download the Wero app and complete an onboarding process to link their bank account before they can make or receive payments. The transfer from one system to another will not be automatic.
