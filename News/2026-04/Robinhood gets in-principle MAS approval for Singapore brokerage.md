# Robinhood gets in-principle MAS approval for Singapore brokerage

> [!info] 2026-04-24 · 4 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 Robinhood secures in-principle approval to launch brokerage in Singapore. By establishing a local footprint, Robinhood aims to provide investors in Singapore with the tools and access they need to participate in the global economy, furthering our mission to democratize finance for all.

[This Week in Fintech] Robinhood $HOOD ( ▲ 1.04% ) received in-principle approval from the Monetary Authority of Singapore. The IPA covers securities trading, exchange-traded derivatives, custody, product financing, and collective investment funds. It's conditional, so MAS can still pull it before the full license is issued, and no launch date has been set. Singapore will serve as Robinhood's Asia-Pacific headquarters.
