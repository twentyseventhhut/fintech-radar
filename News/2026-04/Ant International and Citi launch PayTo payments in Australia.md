# Ant International and Citi launch PayTo payments in Australia

> [!info] 2026-04-27 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 Ant International is collaborating with Citi to launch a PayTo payment solution in Australia, to enable real-time, secure account-to-account payments for SMEs. Integrated into its Global Account Service, the offering aims to improve cash flow, reduce costs, and streamline B2B transactions.

[This Week in Fintech] Ant International and Citi in Australia partnered to launch PayTo-enabled real-time, secure, and low-cost account-to-account payments to improve cash flow for Australian SMEs.
