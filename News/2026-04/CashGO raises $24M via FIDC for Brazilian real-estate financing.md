# CashGO raises $24M via FIDC for Brazilian real-estate financing

> [!info] 2026-04-08 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 CashGO, the Brazilian financial platform focused on real estate companies, raised $24 million via a FIDC (Credit Rights Investment Fund) structure backed by institutional investors including Augme Capital, Arx Investimentos, and RBR Asset Management, which invest in receivables tied to its credit operations.❝
