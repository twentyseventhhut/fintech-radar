# Klarna to become available through Swedbank Pay in the Nordics

> [!info] 2026-04-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇪 Klarna will soon be available through Swedbank Pay, giving its merchant network across the Nordics access to Klarna’s payment options. The integration expands Klarna’s reach in Sweden, Norway, Denmark and Finland, with rollout starting in Sweden later this year and in other markets from early 2027.
