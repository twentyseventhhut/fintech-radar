# Trump administration overhauls banking rules to combat debanking

> [!info] 2026-04-10 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] The Trump administration overhauled banking regulations to combat "debanking," targeting rules that previously allowed lenders to cut ties with controversial or high-risk clients. Others would simply call these rules “KYC,” or “AML.” It is unclear how much politically-targeted debanking actually occurred in the US.
