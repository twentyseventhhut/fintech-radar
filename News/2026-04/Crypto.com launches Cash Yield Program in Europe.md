Crypto.com Launches Cash Yield Program in Europe
 The Cash Yield Program Allows Eligible Users to Seek Potential Returns on EUR Balances, with up to 3% p.a. Boosted Promotional Rate for a Limited Period 
 April 9, 2026 - Crypto.com today announced that eligible users in selected EEA countries can now access EUR Cash Yield through their Crypto.com App, with assets allocated to the program invested in money market funds managed by Fidelity International. 
Strengthening Crypto.com’s offering, App users can transfer funds from their EUR account into a new Cash Yield account to earn yield with no minimum balance requirements, no caps on total earnings and no lockup period. 
“To be able to offer our users the ability to earn on Euro balances with no lock up periods,  allowing funds to be accessible to invest or spend when needed, is a really important development for the EU crypto market and an exciting boost to our financial services offering on our App,” said Eric Anziani, President and Chief Operating Officer of Crypto.com. “With this new program our users can trade and earn yield in one seamless and secure platform and it is yet another way we’re enhancing our products beyond digital assets trading.”
Assets allocated to the Cash Yield program are invested in EUR-denominated low-volatility net asset value money market funds managed by Fidelity International through Crypto.com’s authorised and regulated EU entities. The program offers access to interest from invested Euros in money market funds. Returns are accrued daily and paid out monthly in EUR. Users also get the possibility to convert their interest payouts into CRO to receive an additional total reward rate of up to 3% p.a. for a limited time depending on a users’ Level-Up tier and subject to eligibility criteria.  
“The Cash Yield program provides complete flexibility over how our users choose to grow and manage their funds,” said Karl Mohan, EVP, Financial Services, General Manager International of Crypto.com. “We’re proud to introduce this product to the EU market with Fidelity International, the industry-leading investment specialists, adding to the many ways our users can interact securely through our world-class App.” 
“Fidelity International is excited to extend its digital distribution footprint in Europe with Crypto.com, enabling investors to access Fidelity’s world-class money market fund solutions from their crypto app. Fidelity’s funds deliver unique and valuable products to users, as part of Fidelity’s mission to bridge traditional and digital finance,” said Emmanuelle Pecenicic, Head of Digital Assets Distribution at Fidelity International. “Crypto.com has moved beyond the digital asset platform label and is now offering eligible users a regulated, secure and easy way to enhance their wealth creation opportunities.” 
Crypto.com offers one of the industry’s most comprehensive financial technology destinations, with products and services spanning crypto, cards and much more.
Find out more about Cash Yield here: https://crypto.com/eea/financialservices/cashyield
