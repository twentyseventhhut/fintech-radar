# This Week in Fintech: Rain's mission for everyday digital dollars

> [!info] 2026-04-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Rain is on a mission to make digital dollars work like everyday money for everyday people, no matter where they live or what bank they use. Today, moving money across borders is still too slow, too expensive, and too dependent on decades‑old infrastructure that was never designed for an internet-scale world. Rain believes people shouldn’t have to learn new jargon or understand “stablecoins” to benefit from instant, low-cost payments; they should just see that their money arrives faster, more reliably, and with fewer hidden fees.
