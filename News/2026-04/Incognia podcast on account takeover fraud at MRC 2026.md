# Incognia podcast on account takeover fraud at MRC 2026

> [!info] 2026-04-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🎤 Why Your "Secure" Account is a Fraudster’s Playground (Live at the Incognia Booth!). In this special episode at MRC 2026 in Las Vegas, Jordan speaks with André Ferraz, CEO of Incognia, and Steven Coats from Ticketmaster about the evolution of account takeover (ATO) attacks. The conversation highlights how fraud has shifted toward high-value accounts, why traditional 2FA methods are increasingly vulnerable, and how location-based identity and “smart friction” can dramatically reduce fraud while maintaining a seamless user experience.
