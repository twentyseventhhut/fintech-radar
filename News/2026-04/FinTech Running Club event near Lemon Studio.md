FRC Nairobi x Lemon Studio
​ FRC and Lemon Studio are bringing together people from FinTech, tech/finance, and beyond to connect, run, and have fun here in Nairobi. Our runs are super casual and open to everyone, from first-timers to seasoned runners. No pressure, just awesome vibes and good people. We'll get coffee after the run on Rain! 
​ Run details
​ Meeting point: Marsha P. Johnson Memorial Fountain off of Christopher St on the West Side Highway
​ Distance: 3.1 miles (5km)
​ Route: We’ll head north past Pier 66 up to 29th Street and back. Afterward, we’ll walk over to a coffee shop (Hungry Ghost) to grab a drink with the Rain team!
​ Pace: We’ll have three pace groups, each led by a designated pace leader: 8 min/mile, 9 min/mile, and 10 min/mile.
