Bitget Wallet Integrates Polymarket Ahead of 2026 FIFA World Cup
 Bitget Wallet has launched a new World Cup prediction feature in partnership with Polymarket, giving its 90 million users easier access to on-chain prediction markets . The launch comes as interest in World Cup prediction markets rises ahead of the 2026 FIFA World Cup.
The new feature brings Polymarket’s prediction markets into Bitget Wallet. Users can make event predictions from their self-custodial wallet while using AI tools for market insights and analysis.
According to Bitget Wallet, the 2026 FIFA World Cup Winner market on Polymarket has recorded more than $1.2 billion in trading volume. The market currently has over $280 million in liquidity. Daily trading volume has climbed close to $30 million amid interest in the tournament.
The 2026 FIFA World Cup will be hosted by the United States, Mexico, and Canada. It will be the largest World Cup in history, featuring 48 national teams and 104 matches played over 39 days.
The surge in trading shows interest in prediction markets, which let users bet on the outcome of real-world events using blockchain-based platforms.
As part of the promotion, Bitget Wallet is offering $200,000 in rewards on three World Cup campaigns.
The first campaign, World Cup Champion Pick, runs from June 4 to July 20. Users can predict the tournament winner, invite friends, and earn points. Those who correctly choose the champion will share rewards based on the points they collect.
The second campaign, 20-Day Prediction Streak, runs from June 11 to June 30. It rewards users who make predictions regularly and meet certain trading goals.
Bitget Wallet will also host community watch parties during the tournament, featuring live match discussions, fan streams, and extra rewards.
The launch expands on Bitget Wallet’s existing integration with Polymarket. Users can access prediction markets through a self-custodial wallet. They also get AI-powered event insights, tools to track market activity, and features that help them discover new markets.
To prepare for the increase in participation during live World Cup matches, Bitget Wallet has upgraded its prediction market system.
The improvements include an event data network and real-time score verification. The company has also added price-checking systems, monitoring tools, and cloud infrastructure built to handle large traffic surges during major games.
Alvin Kan, Chief Operating Officer of Bitget Wallet, said prediction markets are becoming a popular way for people to engage with major global events.
He said the World Cup brings billions of fans together, allowing them to follow matches, form opinions about outcomes, and respond to events in real time through on-chain markets.
Elden Mirzoian, Director of Growth and Partnerships at Polymarket, said the World Cup gives fans another way to participate in the tournament. He added that prediction markets make the experience more interactive by allowing supporters to engage directly with event-based markets.
