# MercadoLibre's Argentina loan delinquency rate rises sharply

> [!info] 2026-04-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇦🇷MercadoLibre's 90-day delinquency rate on Mercado Pago loans in Argentina jumped from 1.8% to 8.7% between December 2024 and December 2025 — a trend seen across the entire Argentine financial system — though still well below Brazil, where the rate fell from 15.9% to 11% over the same period.
