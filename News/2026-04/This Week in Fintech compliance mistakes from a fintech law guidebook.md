[This Week in Fintech] In the book “Fintech Law and Compliance: A History and Operator’s Guidebook,” fourteen contributors, all operators spanning decades of experience from bank sponsors to fintechs, helped me give some practical, inside tips on how the savviest fintechs approach that seemingly scary word: compliance. Taking some examples from that book, here are some common mistakes to avoid as a fintech founder or operator when it comes to approaching compliance.
