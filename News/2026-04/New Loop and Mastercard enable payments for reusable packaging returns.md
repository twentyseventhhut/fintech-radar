# New Loop and Mastercard enable payments for reusable packaging returns

> [!info] 2026-04-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇰 New Loop partners with Mastercard to enable seamless payments for a reusable takeaway packaging return system. Through this partnership with New Loop and the municipalities of Copenhagen and Frederiksberg, Mastercard is helping accelerate the shift from disposable to reusable packaging by simplifying how consumers receive their deposits.
