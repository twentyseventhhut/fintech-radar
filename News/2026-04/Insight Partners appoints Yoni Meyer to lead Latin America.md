Insight Partners has appointed Yoni Meyer as Director of Capital Raising for Latin America. Based in Mexico City, Meyer will lead the firm’s capital raising initiatives across the region, strengthening relationships with institutional investors and connecting Latin American investors with one of the most established software and artificial intelligence investment platforms in the world.
Yoni Meyer joins from HMC Capital , where he was partner and head of alternative investment distribution since 2018, developing and managing institutional alternative investment relationships across Latin America. Before HMC, he worked for nearly a decade at LarraínVial in Santiago, where he founded its corporate fixed income research unit, led institutional fund placements in the region, and co-founded the firm’s first derivatives trading desk. He later worked as an investment banker at Credit Suisse in New York and Santiago, advising on financing and mergers and acquisitions transactions in Chile, Latin America, and the Caribbean. In addition, Meyer holds a degree in Industrial Engineering and a master’s in Operations Management from the University of Chile, as well as an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
Founded in 1995, Insight Partners is a global venture capital and growth firm with more than $90 billion in regulatory assets under management and a 30-year track record of investing exclusively in software and AI companies. The firm has invested more than $55 billion in over 900 companies, from early-stage scaleups navigating their first inflection points to late-stage businesses requiring capital and operational expertise to accelerate global expansion, generating approximately $64 billion in realizations, including more than 55 IPOs.

# Yoni Meyer

Yoni Meyer is a Principal on the Capital Partnerships team, where he leads capital formation in Latin America & focuses on developing LP relationships. He joined the firm in April 2026.

Prior to Insight, Yoni spent several years at HMC Capital, where he developed deep expertise across institutional investing and alternative assets as Partner & Head of Alternatives Distribution.

Yoni began his career at Larrain Vial, one of Latin America's leading financial institutions, where he founded the corporate fixed-income research unit. He later transitioned to Institutional Sales, where he led the placement of proprietary and third-party investment funds, driving the consolidation of offshore fixed-income assets across institutional client portfolios throughout the region. He also played a founding role in the creation of Larrain Vial's first derivatives trading desk, significantly expanding the firm's offering of derivatives and structured products to corporate, institutional, and high net worth clients.

He subsequently joined Credit Suisse as an investment banker, based across New York and Santiago, where he advised on financing and M&A transactions spanning Chile, Latin America, and the Caribbean.

Yoni holds a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering and a master's degree in Operational Management, both from Universidad de Chile. He also earned an MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania.
