# HashKey and Shanghai Commercial Bank to Launch Co-branded Visa Card

Phemex News 2026/04/22 05:01

Share

HashKey, in collaboration with Shanghai Commercial Bank, is set to launch one of Asia's first co-branded Visa credit cards with a virtual asset exchange. Announced by HashKey's CEO, Ru Haiyang, at the 2026 Hong Kong Web3 Carnival, the card will be available for registration starting May 11. The card, approved by the Hong Kong Monetary Authority, requires no deposit or collateral and settles all transactions in fiat currency. Cardholders will receive a 2% cryptocurrency rebate on local purchases in Hong Kong and a 4% rebate on overseas spending.
