Starling set to expand business banking to US
Starling Bank has ruled out entering the “massively overserved” US retail market, targeting the expansion of only its business banking operations as it seeks to establish a presence in a second country. 
The UK challenger bank plans to target small and medium-sized companies through the acquisition of a US bank within the next two years, in a market segment that it says is underserved and highly profitable.
