[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] ➡️ New Data: The State of Mule Account Handovers in 2026. Incognia will present its 2026 report, highlighting a sharp rise in this type of fraud and the industry’s ongoing struggle to detect it proactively. Based on a survey of over 500 risk and AML professionals, the findings show most institutions still identify cases only after funds move, making improved early detection a top priority.
