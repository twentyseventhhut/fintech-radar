# Female borrowers in Dominican Republic rose 40% in seven years

> [!info] 2026-04-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Female borrowers in the Dominican Republic rose about 40% over the past seven years, surpassing 1.3 million and now making up nearly half of all borrowers. The progress is clear, but access remains uneven, with barriers like limited financial literacy and documentation still holding back more vulnerable women.Source: Superintendencia de Bancos
