Cifrato, a Bogotá, Colombia-based AI-powered accounting platform, has raised over $1 million in Seed funding. 
Investors 
The round includes backing from Y Combinator, along with Fen Ventures, Decelera Ventures, and Kuiper. 
Cifrato Use of Funds 
The funds will be used to strengthen operations in Colombia, expand integrations with ERP and POS systems, and develop new AI-powered features, such as bank reconciliation. 
About Cifrato 
Founded by Juan Pisco and Yerson Cacua, Cifrato is a B2B SaaS company focused on automating accounting processes through artificial intelligence. The company uses AI agents to handle end-to-end accounting workflows, including processing invoices, applying taxes, and managing inventory in real time. Its proprietary orchestration architecture integrates directly with Colombia’s electronic invoicing systems. Headquartered in Bogotá, Colombia, Cifrato primarily serves accounting firms managing multiple businesses.
