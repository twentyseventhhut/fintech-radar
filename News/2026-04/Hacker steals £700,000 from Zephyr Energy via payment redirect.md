# Hacker steals £700,000 from Zephyr Energy via payment redirect

> [!info] 2026-04-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Zephyr Energy, a London-listed oil and gas company, disclosed this week via a regulatory filing with the London Stock Exchange that a hacker stole £700,000 — close to $900,000 — from a U.S.-based subsidiary by intercepting a contractor payment and redirecting it to an attacker-controlled account. Classic business email compromise.
