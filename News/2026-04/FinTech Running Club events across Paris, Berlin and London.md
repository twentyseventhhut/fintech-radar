Fintech Fringe Friends: A New Monthly Fintech Social + FRC Fun Run
 ​ You must be a member of Fintech Fringe Forum to qualify for a free ticket: https://bit.ly/4sPnOIA 
​ A Collab between Fintech Fringe x FRC London
​ A MONTHLY SOCIAL GATHERING & FUN RUN 
​ Sometimes the best connections happen mid-stride, or over a relaxed drink with good people who share the same energy.
​ On April 15th, Fintech Fringe is teaming up with Fintech Running Club for a special double-launch event. Our first run together and the official debut of our brand-new monthly networking event.
​ All Are Welcome: Run or Relax 
​ Whether you’re here to clock a 5k or just to catch up with the community, there’s a place for you.
 ​ The Runners: Tobi John Oluwafemi (FRC) will lead a social 5km London run alongside our founder, Calypso Harland . We’ll be keeping a conversational pace - perfect for catching up while we tread some tarmac. 
 ​ Not a runner? No problem. Head straight to the pub to help us "warm the atmosphere" and have the drinks ready for when the runners return. 
​ The Hub: The Green Pub 
​ We’ll start and finish the route at The Green Pub in Clerkenwell Green , the official launch site for 'Fintech Fringe Friends' . This is our new monthly mixer: a relaxed social gathering with no agenda, no pitches, and no pressure - just a chance to hang out with fellow fintech fanatics.
​ The Schedule: 
 ​ 5:30 PM: Runners Arrival & Bag Drop (The Green Pub) 
 ​ 6:00 PM: Runners set off / Non-runners start the mixer 
 ​ 7:00 PM: The Full Crowd: Post-run drinks & socialising 
​ If you're in the London fintech scene, come run with the club or just come hang out and make some new friends.
​ About Fintech Fringe Fintech Fringe is the UK’s fastest-growing fintech ecosystem with a network of 14,000+ professionals and one mission: to help fintechs scale and grow. We support the industry through our events, community platform ( forum.fintechfringe.co ), the Scale & Grow consultancy, and Finclub - our new Central London home for fintechs to work, learn, and play. 👉 fintechfringe.co 
​ About Our Partner: Fintech Running Club (FRC) FRC is a global community bringing together people from FinTech, tech/finance, and beyond to connect, run, and have fun. Their runs are super casual and open to everyone, from first-timers to seasoned runners. No pressure, just good vibes and good people. 🌍💬 👉 Check out the FRC website
