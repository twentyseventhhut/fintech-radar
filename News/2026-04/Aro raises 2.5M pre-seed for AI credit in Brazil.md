# Aro raises 2.5M pre-seed for AI credit in Brazil

> [!info] 2026-04-23 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Aro raises $2.5M pre-seed round. Aro will use the funds to hire talent, improve its technology, and expand its credit operations. The company also plans to develop proprietary datasets to strengthen its credit models. Read more

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Aro, the AI-powered credit, raised a $2.5 million pre-seed round led by ONEVC and 17Sigma, with participation from Norte Ventures, Gilgamesh, and Grão VC. The company builds an AI agent that analyzes behavioral data to guide users toward the most suitable credit options, aiming to expand financial inclusion in Brazil.
