[This Week in Fintech] CrowdStrike's Latin America Threat Landscape Report 2025 provides a dedicated regional analysis of cyber threats across the region combining intelligence on targeted intrusions, cybercrime, and hacktivism. The report shows a 15% increase in Latin American victims named on ransomware and data extortion leak sites between 2023 and 2024. Financially motivated cybercriminal groups dominate the landscape, but highlights the growing role of geopolitics, with state-linked actors and hacktivists targeting government entities and critical sectors. The document shows how governments across the region are strengthening cybersecurity infrastructure and international cooperation, while navigating AI-driven cyber risks and the use of spyware in domestic political contexts.
