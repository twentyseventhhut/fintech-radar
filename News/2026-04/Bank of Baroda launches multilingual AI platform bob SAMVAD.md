‘bob SAMVAD’, an AI-powered multilingual conversational platform of Bank of Baroda to transform in-branch customer interactions, launched in Mumbai
 Press Information Bureau 
 April 8, 2026
‘bob SAMVAD’ is Bank of Baroda’s new AI-powered, multilingual conversational platform, the industry's first. bob SAMVAD was launched in Mumbai by Secretary of Department of Financial Services, Mr. M. Nagaraju. As an in-house platform, bob SAMVAD provides real-time, low-latency two-way communication in 22 languages, enabling customers and branch staff to communicate without language constraints. It is believed that the launch of bob SAMVAD will help improve inclusive banking, accessibility to customers, and efficiency in branch operations. By harnessing the capabilities of speech and language AI solutions, bob SAMVAD supports India’s overall efforts toward technology-based financial inclusion and customer-oriented banking.
Customers at the service counter can either voice or type their questions in the language they choose, with these inputs automatically converted into the preferred language of the staff member, making interactions swift, precise, and seamless. This conversation is made available in the form of on-screen texts, while a text-to-speech voice option allows for complete accessibility in case some customers prefer audio. bob SAMVAD will be rolled out in the first phase in 250 branches in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and Maharashtra, with its progressive rollout in other branches. The product is likely to set a new benchmark for AI-led multilingual banking services in terms of the performance of branches in improving customer satisfaction.

Bank of Baroda launches AI-based multilingual platform ‘bob SAMVAD’ 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
New Delhi: Bank of Baroda has launched ‘bob SAMVAD’, an AI-powered multilingual conversational platform aimed at transforming customer interactions at its branches. 
The platform was formally unveiled by M Nagaraju, Secretary, Department of Financial Services (DFS), Ministry of Finance, in Mumbai on March 28, 2026. 
Positioned as an industry-first initiative, bob SAMVAD is designed to eliminate language barriers by enabling seamless communication between customers and branch staff in their preferred languages. 
Developed in-house, it supports real-time, two-way conversations across 22 languages using AI-driven speech and language technologies, ensuring accuracy and natural fluency. 
Nagaraju lauded the initiative, saying it would promote inclusive and accessible service delivery while enhancing customer experience. He added that the platform sets a new benchmark for technology adoption in banking. 
At branches, customers can speak or type queries in their chosen language, which are instantly translated for staff, and vice versa. The system displays conversations as text and also offers an optional voice feature for greater accessibility. 
Commenting on the launch, Dr. Debadatta Chand, MD & CEO, Bank of Baroda, said, “With bob SAMVAD, Bank of Baroda (BoB) is leveraging AI to make our branches more inclusive and customer-friendly by enabling seamless real-time conversations in local languages.” The platform will initially be rolled out across 250 branches in five states, followed by a phased nationwide 
expansion.
