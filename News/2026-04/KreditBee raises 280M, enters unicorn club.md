# KreditBee raises 280M, enters unicorn club

> [!info] 2026-04-09 · 3 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 FinTech lender KreditBee enters unicorn club with $280 million pre-IPO funding at $1.5 billion valuation. The round, which includes $80 million in secondaries, will be deployed to expand the credit business, especially the newer verticals - secured and MSME lending.

[This Week in Fintech] Kreditbee, an unsecured digital lending startup, raised ₹2,590cr ($280M).

[This Week in Fintech] KreditBeejoined the unicorn club after securing $280 million in a funding round to expand its digital lending platform.
