 PayTic   has entered into a strategic partnership with Ecobank Côte d’Ivoire   aimed at transforming and automating back-office payment operations.
The collaboration is designed to modernise critical payment processes by leveraging PayTic’s intelligent components to streamline operations, improve efficiency, and enhance overall performance. By automating key back-office functions, both organisations seek to reduce operational complexities while ensuring faster and more reliable payment processing.
The agreement also underscores a shared commitment to optimising the cost of electronic payments, a key priority for financial institutions operating in increasingly digital and competitive markets. Through this partnership, Ecobank Côte d’Ivoire is expected to strengthen its operational capabilities while delivering improved service experiences.
For PayTic, the deal reflects its continued expansion across Africa, reinforcing its position as a growing player in payment operations automation and digital financial infrastructure on the continent.
