Citing Campaign’s Creator Pay Report published in September — which found that 87% of creators have been paid late despite the creator economy being valued at $250 billion — Visa and Lumanu have announced a partnership to ensure faster payments for creators. 
Lumanu, a payment platform designed for brands and agencies, revealed plans this morning to integrate Visa Direct for real-time payments from companies to creatives. The partnership will extend to creators in more than 195 countries and eliminate multiday delays and hidden fees, according to Lumanu’s announcement. 
Brands such as Warner Music Group, PepsiCo, Airbnb, WPP and Keurig Dr Pepper have utilized Lumanu for creator payments. As of December, the 2016-founded platform has powered over $1.5 billion in payments to more than 100,00 creators and contractors.
