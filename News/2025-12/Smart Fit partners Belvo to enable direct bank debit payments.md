# Smart Fit partners Belvo to enable direct bank debit payments

> [!info] 2025-12-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Smart Fit, the Brazilian gym chain operating in more than 420 locations and with 1.2 million users in Mexico, partnered with Belvo to digitize its payment system and enable direct bank debits. The update removes reliance on card payments and makes it easier for members to automate their monthly fees.
