# Brainfood: The future of AI-powered embedded accounting

> [!info] 2025-12-23 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] The future of embedded accounting is also powered by artificial intelligence. Beyond flashy features, AI acts as an engine for financial confidence by working in the background to radically improve data quality and automate workflows such as transaction categorization, like Xero customers can do with Just Ask Xero (or JAX). This technology shifts the focus from "looking back at the books" to intelligently "looking ahead at the business," allowing systems to surface insights so small businesses can make smarter, faster decisions.
