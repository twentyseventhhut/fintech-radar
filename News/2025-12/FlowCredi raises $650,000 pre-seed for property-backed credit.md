# FlowCredi raises $650,000 pre-seed for property-backed credit

> [!info] 2025-12-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 FlowCredi, the Brazilian marketplace connecting borrowers and lenders for property-backed credit, raised $650,000 in a pre-seed round led by Verve Capital and Norte Ventures. The company plans to expand distribution partnerships and offer white-label credit solutions in education and healthcare sectors.
