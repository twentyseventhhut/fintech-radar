December 2025 - Today, Vizmark announces both our launch and our $430k Pre-Seed funding round. This foundational capital will support our mission of making portfolio operations painless for emerging and mid-sized private market investors.
Vizmark is an AI-driven portfolio visibility platform that ingests and manages financial, operational, valuation, transaction and value creation data. Our highly automated system-of-record empowers investors to securely embed AI into core portfolio operations, underwriting and investor relations workflows via isolated, private models.
The pre-seed funding will accelerate our AI and agentic roadmap and fuel new platform capabilities, further catalyzing initial customer traction. Vizmark is proud to share that we will enter 2026 monitoring several billion dollars of assets under management.
The round includes several prominent angels, such as Jeff Parks and Francisco Alvarez-Demalde, co-founders of Riverwood Capital, Matthew Karle, co-founder of Cloudland Capital and Aviram Jenik, co-founder of Rabbit Ventures.
The Opportunity in Private Markets
Global private markets represent an immense, and growing, pool of capital, with assets under management totaling $22 trillion in 2025 per McKinsey, a 20x increase in size since 2000.
While portfolios have proliferated, the adoption of technology to manage them has lagged outside of the largest funds. Over 85% of mega-funds leverage technology solutions for portfolio monitoring, but usage has remained below 5% in the market for emerging and mid-sized funds.
We believe that this market has reached an inflection point, with converging trends pulling forward the pace of adoption. Limited Partners are more discerning than ever, increasingly demanding quantifiable value creation and competent fund management. Meanwhile investors are embracing lean operating models, necessitating that all resources be fully optimized to meet rising expectations. This places emerging and mid-sized investors in a difficult situation as they strive to efficiently navigate to rigorous performance standards.
Enter Vizmark
We help innovative investors achieve best-in-class portfolio practices, providing data-driven advantages through core differentiators, including our:
· Purpose-built platform for emerging and mid-sized private market investors
· AI-extraction engine to solve the cold start problem via automated data ingestion
· Expansive use cases for LP reporting, benchmarking and value creation
· Single pane of glass across financial, operational and investment data
· Flexible configuration tailored to funds' native data structures
· Intuitive UI with drag and drop dashboarding and point and click analytics
Vizmark is built for investors who recognize that winning in any market means outperforming peers in every way, including operations. Our AI and agentic capabilities cost-effectively automate complex workflows that previously required in-house resources or outsourced services. With Vizmark, investors can achieve excellence while staying lean.
Founded by Investors with Lived Experience
Our management team includes former senior investment professionals with expertise in venture capital, growth equity, private equity and lending. We know how painful manually managing information across disparate inboxes, excel files and PDFs can be, regardless of investment approach. We've also seen firsthand how this burden drains resources and how much more acute the impact is on lean organizations, where it pulls investment teams away from their core focus - sourcing great opportunities and building enduring businesses. That is why we created Vizmark.
---
This initial milestone belongs to the community that backed us from the outset. We are honored to serve the investment ecosystem and thrilled about what lies ahead.
Now back to building,
Pat and the Vizmark team
About Vizmark
Vizmark is an AI-driven portfolio visibility platform for private market investors that ingests and manages proprietary financial, operational, valuation, transaction and value creation intelligence on their assets. Our highly automated system-of-record empowers investors to securely embed AI into core portfolio operations, underwriting and investor relations workflows via isolated, private models. Vizmark focuses on emerging and mid-sized investors across venture capital, growth equity, private equity, private credit and alternatives approaches. Learn more at vizmark.io.
