# Brainfood: Startups that raised the most capital in LatAm in 2025

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] The startups that concentrated the most capital in Latin America in 2025 (ES) | Bloomberg Línea reviews the startups that attracted the largest funding volumes in Latin America in 2025, a year marked by fewer deals but much larger ticket sizes. The ranking highlights the growing role of non-dilutive financing alongside selective equity rounds. The list includes CloudWalk ($780 million debt round, Plata ($500 million) and Kavak ($400 million). Large equity rounds featured Klar ($190 million Series C) and Omie ($160 million Series D).
