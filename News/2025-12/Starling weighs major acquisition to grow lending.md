# Starling weighs major acquisition to grow lending

> [!info] 2025-12-15 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Starling Bank is weighing a major acquisition to accelerate lending growth. Chief Executive Raman Bhatia said that Starling aims to accelerate growth with more targeted acquisitions: “We have focused on high-quality acquisitions, but now we want to turbocharge that engine in the UK… there is so much to do here in terms of market share and growth expansion.”

[This Week in Fintech] Starling Bank explored plans to buy another UK lender, allowing the digital bank to put its £12bn worth of customer deposits to more profitable use.
