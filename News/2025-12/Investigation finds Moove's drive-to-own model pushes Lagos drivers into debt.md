## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] A new investigation revealed that Moove’s “drive-to-own” model is pushing Lagos drivers into debt. Many were promised new cars and fair financing, but ended up with used vehicles priced far above market rates and daily repayments that kept rising with Nigeria’s tough economy.
