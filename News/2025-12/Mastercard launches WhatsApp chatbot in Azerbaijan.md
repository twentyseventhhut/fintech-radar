**Mastercard Expands Silver Logo Benefits with Launch of WhatsApp Chatbot in Azerbaijan
**

*   _Mastercard launches**new WhatsApp chatbot** to simplify access to Silver Logo benefits in Azerbaijan._
*   _The new channel complements the existing**Telegram assistant**._
*   _Silver Logo cardholders enjoy exclusive cashback, airport transfers, lounge access, and lifestyle offers — with**no spending requirements.**_
*   _Aimed at consumers who value**seamless, secure, and time-saving digital experiences**._

**Baku, Azerbaijan — December 2025**. Mastercard has launched a new **WhatsApp chatbot** to provide cardholders with easier access to the benefits of its **Silver Logo benefits**, enhancing convenience and engagement for affluent consumers in Azerbaijan.

The new WhatsApp assistant complements Mastercard’s existing Telegram channel, offering users another fast and secure way to explore their card privileges. This expansion represents a new step in Mastercard’s strategy to connect with consumers through the platforms they use most, ensuring that valuable information about cardholder benefits is always at their fingertips.

Through the WhatsApp chatbot, Mastercard cardholders can instantly view detailed information about their Silver Logo benefits, receive tailored updates on new offers, and connect directly to assistance — all within a familiar messaging environment.

This innovation turns access to benefits into a seamless, interactive experience. Instead of searching across multiple platforms, cardholders can now discover everything in one place — from lifestyle perks to travel advantages — simply by chatting with Mastercard.

**Premium Benefits That Inspire Everyday Use**

The Silver Logo program rewards cardholders with experiences that go beyond payments, turning everyday moments into something exceptional.

Among the key advantages:

*   No spending volume requirement — cardholders enjoy premium privileges immediately, without needing to reach any threshold.
*   Exclusive cashback at leading restaurants and duty-free stores, including enhanced rewards when paying via digital wallets.
*   Complimentary airport transfers to and from Heydar Aliyev International Airport, ensuring a stress-free start and finish to every journey.
*   Priority check-in, fast-track services, and lounge access, designed for smoother, more comfortable travel.
*   Special discounts and access across fitness and wellness platforms such as Push30 and leading medical centers like Bona Dea Hospital.
*   Exclusive offers at Port Baku Walk, adding value to every shopping and dining experience.
*   VIP access to entertainment events via iTicket.az, offering early access and premium seating.

These benefits combine convenience, exclusivity, and personalization — motivating cardholders to use their Mastercard more frequently and confidently in their daily lives.

**Innovation for Premium Cardholders**

The launch of the WhatsApp chatbot is tailored for consumers who appreciate seamless, time-saving, and reliable digital solutions. By introducing this forward-thinking touchpoint, Mastercard continues to redefine premium service — combining cutting-edge technology with everyday convenience.

Consumers can now reach Mastercard’s Assistance Bot via:

*   WhatsApp: +994 10 312 50 66
*   Telegram: t.me/MCassistancebot

**About Mastercard Silver Logo**

The Mastercard Silver Logo program provides cardholders with access to exclusive privileges in dining, travel, and lifestyle categories. It’s designed to enhance everyday experiences and make life more rewarding — without any spending threshold.
