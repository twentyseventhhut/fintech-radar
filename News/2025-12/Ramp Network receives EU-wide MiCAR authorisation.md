Ramp Swaps (Ireland) Limited Receives EU-Wide MiCAR Authorisation
Today, we are excited to share a milestone we have been working toward for a long time.
Ramp Swaps (Ireland) Limited, trading as Ramp Network has been officially authorised as a Crypto-Asset Service Provider (CASP) under MiCAR by the Central Bank of Ireland. 
This approval places Ramp Network among the first companies operating fully under the European Union’s unified regulatory framework for crypto-asset services, a significant step not only for us, but for the broader ecosystem that depends on safe, compliant access between fiat and digital assets.
 Why this matters 
For years, Europe’s crypto landscape has been shaped by a patchwork of national regulations. MiCAR changes that. It introduces one clear, harmonised set of rules for all 27 EU countries, covering governance, operational resilience, transparency, and consumer protection.
With MiCAR authorisation, Ramp Network can now provide its on- and off-ramp services across the entire European Union under a single licence. No fragmentation. No uncertainty. One consistent regulatory framework for converting between fiat currencies and digital assets.
The authorisation covers the core activities that enable Ramp Network to facilitate secure and compliant fiat–crypto value transfers under EU-level supervision.
 Our mission continues stronger than ever 
Ramp Network is a financial technology company focused on connecting the crypto economy with today’s global financial infrastructure.
Our on- and off-ramp products already serve businesses and individuals across 150+ countries. MiCAR authorization strengthens the foundation we have built over the years and allows us to continue expanding responsibly across Europe.
 A word from our team 
“MiCAR gives Europe a clear and unified framework for building trustworthy crypto services. For us, this authorisation is more than a licence; it’s a commitment to making on-chain value work in everyday life. As a company founded in the EU, this is not a new entrance into the market. Europe has always been our home and one of our most important regions. This approval represents a level up and confirms that our standards, controls, and infrastructure meet Europe’s expectations. We’ve spent years building the rails between banks and blockchains, and this milestone brings us closer to giving people a simple, reliable account they can use with confidence across the EU.” — Przemek Kowalczyk, CEO of Ramp Network 
“MiCAR provides the regulatory clarity the crypto industry has long needed. For people using our product, that means higher standards for how we run things, more checks behind the scenes, and greater confidence that we are doing things the right way for them. This authorisation validates the strength of our governance, risk, and compliance programs, ensuring we operate to the highest standards across Europe. By choosing Ireland as the home for our MiCAR authorisation, we’re also recognizing the country’s emergence as a leading regulatory hub for digital assets in the EU. Under this unified framework, Ramp Network can scale responsibly while giving users and partners confidence in secure, compliant access to digital assets.” — Barry Coffey, CEO of Ramp Swaps (Ireland) Limited
 What comes next 
With MiCAR authorisation, Ramp Network can work with partners across Europe under stable, transparent, and predictable rules. It gives our product, compliance, and engineering teams a strong regulatory foundation to continue expanding secure and reliable on- and off-ramp solutions across the EU.
This approval is an important milestone in our mission to build infrastructure that connects traditional finance with the crypto economy, simply, safely, and compliantly.
