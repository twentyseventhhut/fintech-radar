## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Mastercard and Mubadala work on a deal for embattled Brazilian FinTech. Mubadala is negotiating a deal that would see the sovereign-wealth fund acquire control of Will Bank and inject capital into the FinTech firm. Will Bank also pays a fee to Mastercard for its services.
