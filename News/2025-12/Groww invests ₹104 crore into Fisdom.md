# Groww invests ₹104 crore into Fisdom

> [!info] 2025-12-01 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Groww pumps INR 104.4 Cr into wealthtech subsidiary Fisdom. Groww said that, as per the share purchase agreement it entered into with Fisdom’s parent company, Finwizard Technology Pvt Ltd, in May, it was to infuse additional capital to facilitate certain payouts and other working capital requirements.

[This Week in Fintech] Growwinvested INR 104cr ($12M) into Fisdom to diversify revenue streams and strengthen wealth management capabilities after its Oct 2025 acquisition.
