 ClearBank Appoints Angela Roberts as Group General Counsel 
 People Moves
 01.12.2025 08:35 am 
 ClearBank, the enabler of real-time clearing and embedded banking, today announced the appointment of Angela Roberts as Group General Counsel. Angela will lead the Group’s legal and governance functions, playing a key role in supporting ClearBank’s growth ambitions. 
 Angela brings over 20 years of legal and regulatory expertise, having held senior roles at private equity-backed companies as well as leading Fortune 500 and FTSE 100 firms. Angela’s regulatory experience includes serving as a working group chair of the FCA Financial Services Consumer Panel, providing advice and challenge to the FCA on policy development and how it acts in the interests of consumers and small businesses. 
 Angela joins ClearBank as the company enters an exciting phase of expansion, having acquired its European banking licence in 2024 and set ambitious growth plans across multiple jurisdictions. Her deep legal, regulatory and governance experience will be instrumental in navigating an evolving landscape and ensuring ClearBank continues to deliver innovative, compliant solutions for its clients. 
 Mark Fairless, Chief Executive Officer at ClearBank, said: “Angela’s extensive expertise and her passion for building high-performing, values-driven teams are a perfect fit for ClearBank’s vision. I am delighted to welcome her to the Group Executive team. Her strategic insight and commitment to excellence will be invaluable as we continue to grow and evolve.” 
 Angela Roberts, Group General Counsel at ClearBank, said: “I am excited to join ClearBank at such a transformative time for the business. The evolving regulatory environment across the UK and Europe presents both challenges and opportunities for innovation, and I look forward to working with the leadership team to support ClearBank’s ambition and help drive its next phase of growth.”

ClearBank appoints Angela Roberts as Group General Counsel
 Angela brings over 20 years of experience in senior leadership and board level positions in global financial services firms, and as a member of one of the statutory panels overseeing the UK Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)  
ClearBank, the enabler of real-time clearing and embedded banking, today announced the appointment of Angela Roberts as Group General Counsel. Angela will lead the Group’s legal and governance functions, playing a key role in supporting ClearBank’s growth ambitions.
Angela brings over 20 years of legal and regulatory expertise, having held senior roles at private equity-backed companies as well as leading Fortune 500 and FTSE 100 firms. Angela’s regulatory experience includes serving as a working group chair of the FCA Financial Services Consumer Panel, providing advice and challenge to the FCA on policy development and how it acts in the interests of consumers and small businesses. 
Angela joins ClearBank as the company enters an exciting phase of expansion, having acquired its European banking licence in 2024 and set ambitious growth plans across multiple jurisdictions. Her deep legal, regulatory and governance experience will be instrumental in navigating an evolving landscape and ensuring ClearBank continues to deliver innovative, compliant solutions for its clients.

 “Angela’s extensive expertise and her passion for building high-performing, values-driven teams are a perfect fit for ClearBank’s vision. I am delighted to welcome her to the Group Executive team. Her strategic insight and commitment to excellence will be invaluable as we continue to grow and evolve.” 
 

 “I am excited to join ClearBank at such a transformative time for the business. The evolving regulatory environment across the UK and Europe presents both challenges and opportunities for innovation, and I look forward to working with the leadership team to support ClearBank’s ambition and help drive its next phase of growth.”
