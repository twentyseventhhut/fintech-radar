# Bless Payments raises $3.75M seed for remittances

> [!info] 2025-12-09 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌎 Remittance FinTech Bless Payments raises $3.75 million Seed round. The funds will be used to support expansion into Canada, the UK, and the US. The company serves over 1,500 users, offers remittances to 60 countries, and has launched a no-fee travel card, with its mission focused on providing transparent, low-cost financial tools for migrants.
