_Small and micro businesses are driving Saudi Arabia’s economic growth, but access to capital remains a challenge. HalaFinancing is changing that with fast, flexible, and fully digital funding solutions tailored to local entrepreneurs._

Whether you’re a coffee shop in Riyadh ready to open a second location or a retailer in Al Khobar gearing up for peak season, Hala helps turn today’s momentum into tomorrow’s growth. In Saudi Arabia’s fast-evolving business landscape, small and micro enterprises (SMEs) are the backbone of economic diversification and innovation.

#### **Financing growth**

From independent retailers to service providers and home-based entrepreneurs, these businesses are making progress across the kingdom. Yet, many still struggle to access the capital they need to grow, restock, or seize new opportunities.

That’s where Hala Financing steps in — a modern, data-driven solution designed to empower Saudi businesses with fast, flexible, and fully digital funding based on monthly revenue.

Traditional financing often does not fit today’s small business. The process can be slow, complicated, and packed with paperwork, not to mention the collateral or credit history requirements that many businesses simply can’t meet. Hala takes a different approach. By using real-time sales data from a merchant’s Hala point of sale (POS) or payment history, the company offers pre-approved financing with zero collateral and no lengthy application process. Repayments are seamlessly deducted from your POS sales, saving you the hassle of logging in to make monthly payments.

#### **Fast and flexible**

Hala’s fully digital experience is designed to save time and eliminate complexity, making financing more accessible for first-time borrowers and informal businesses often excluded from traditional lending.

• Apply directly through the Hala app. No paperwork, no bank visits.

• Get approved in minutes and receive funds within three business days.

• Unlock Hala Financing after six months of being a Hala POS client.

#### **Built for SMEs**

Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 puts SMEs at the center of national growth, aiming to raise their gross domestic product (GDP) contribution to 35%. For this vision to become a reality, businesses need access to the right tools —and financing is a critical one. Hala’s financing model is uniquely suited to the Saudi market:

• Revenue-based approvals mean more businesses can qualify for funding.

• Flexible repayments that adjust with your sales help protect against seasonal slowdowns.

• An Arabic-first, locally built platform designed to understand the unique challenges of doing business in the kingdom.

#### **Real business impact**

Take the example of a grocery shop owner in Madinah. With strong monthly sales but no formal credit history, they struggled to access financing from traditional channels. Using Hala Financing, they received $6,666 (SAR 25,000) within days, which allowed them to restock ahead of a major holiday. Repayments were deducted automatically from daily sales, so cash flow remained stable, and the store saw record profits that month.

At Hala, the mission is simple: make business easier for everyone. That includes offering products that are clear, fair, and built for real-world business challenges.
