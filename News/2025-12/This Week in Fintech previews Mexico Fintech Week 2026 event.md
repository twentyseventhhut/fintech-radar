Stablecon Roadshow by Rain and VelaFi @ Mexico Fintech Week 2026
​ Calling stablecoin founders, banks, investors, operators, and the curious! 
​ Join us for Stablecon Roadshow at Mexico Fintech Week 2026, presented by Rain , VelaFi , Bridge , Kulipa , Persona and Ripple . ​Expect a curated afternoon of panels, fireside chats, and open networking with the people building real infrastructure in stablecoins from compliance to liquidity, payments, and beyond. We’ll explore: Cross-border payments & embedded finance The full stablecoin infrastructure stack How stablecoins + AI enable autonomous economies LATAM adoption trends Why VCs are betting big on this space
