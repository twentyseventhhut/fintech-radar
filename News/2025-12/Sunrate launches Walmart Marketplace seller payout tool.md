## Sunrate launches Walmart seller payment tool

Share

Add TIA as a preferred source

Sunrate, a Singapore-based payment and treasury management platform, has launched a payment solution for international sellers on Walmart Marketplace.

Sellers can now collect payments from Walmart directly into their Sunrate accounts.

The company said this new service enables faster onboarding for sellers and provides access to FX rates with currency risk protection, though withdrawal and transfer fees apply.

Sunrate said it currently enables businesses to send payments to over 190 countries and transact in more than 130 currencies.

The firm also offers global collection services in more than 30 currencies.
