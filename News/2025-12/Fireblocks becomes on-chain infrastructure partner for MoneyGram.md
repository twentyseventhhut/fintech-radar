# Fireblocks becomes on-chain infrastructure partner for MoneyGram

> [!info] 2025-12-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Fireblocks becomes an on-chain infrastructure partner for MoneyGram. MoneyGram and Fireblocks will attempt to bring faster, low-cost payments with real-time settlements based on stablecoin transfers. The partnership will connect over 200 countries and territories, 20,000 payment gateways, and nearly half a million retailers.
