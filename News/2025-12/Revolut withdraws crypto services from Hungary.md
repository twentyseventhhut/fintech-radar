# Revolut withdraws crypto services from Hungary

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇭🇺 Revolut withdraws from Hungary with its crypto services, citing the need to comply with local regulations; despite securing MiCA licensing through its Cyprus entity, the company says regulatory conditions in Hungary require a full shutdown, and customers must now close or withdraw their crypto holdings before access ends.
