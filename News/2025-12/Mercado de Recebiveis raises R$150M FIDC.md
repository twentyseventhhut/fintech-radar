# Mercado de Recebiveis raises R$150M FIDC

> [!info] 2025-12-01 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Mercado de Recebíveis concludes fundraising of its own R$150M FIDC. CEO Henrique Echenique said having its own FIDC is key to scaling credit, after previously relying solely on banks and funds, noting the structure provides a stable funding source to meet client liquidity needs.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Mercado de Recebíveis, the Brazil-based receivables-backed credit provider, issued a $28 million FIDC (credit rights investment fund) structured by Oriz Partners to secure permanent funding and scale its credit operations. The company reached break-even in May 2025 and expects rapid growth in 2026 as the receivables market expands.
