Buy Crypto with Revolut in the Uniswap Web App and Wallet
#Products
Buying crypto in the Uniswap Web App and Wallet just got easier. Uniswap Labs is partnering with Revolut , Europe’s largest finance app with over 65M customers, to offer a fast, seamless onramp. Users can purchase crypto using their Revolut balance or debit card, all within Uniswap apps.
This integration adds Revolut to our growing list of fiat onramping options, including Robinhood, Transak, and MoonPay, and gives users in 28 countries more choice in how they get onchain.
 Why use Revolut for onramping?
One of the most widely used and trusted financial platforms in the world, Revolut’s onramp product supports over a dozen fiat currencies (including USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, and JPY) and is available in 26 countries across EU member states ( not available to HU and UK customers ). It supports ETH and USDC on Ethereum, POL on Polygon, and other major cryptocurrencies. It offers:
 0% Revolut fee. When you pay with Revolut Pay, Revolut waives its fee and charges only network fees. 
 Fast, simple crypto purchases. Go from fiat to crypto in a few clicks. No extra identity checks for existing Revolut customers. 
 More tokens, more ways to pay. Access 40+ tokens, with flexible payment methods (Revolut Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cards). 
 Directly within Uniswap apps. No need to jump between tabs. Just tap “Buy” and select Revolut. 
 How to buy crypto with Revolut
It’s easy to turn your cash into crypto with onramping by Revolut:
 Tap "Buy" in the Uniswap Wallet or Uniswap Web App 
 Choose the asset you want to purchase 
 Enter the amount you wish to buy 
 Select "Revolut" as your payment method 
 Complete the fiat onramp flow with Revolut 
 Review transaction details and confirm 
 That’s it! Your crypto will be sent directly to your wallet 
Looking to sell your crypto for fiat? Revolut currently only offers onramp capabilities, with offramp functionality coming soon. Uniswap Web App and Wallet users can access other providers in the Uniswap Web App and Wallet products to sell their crypto for fiat .
 Go direct to DeFi with Revolut in the Uniswap Web App and Wallet
With Revolut now live, Uniswap users have another trusted way to onramp alongside options like Robinhood, Transak, and MoonPay. Whether you're using your bank, card, or Revolut balance, it's never been easier to get started with crypto.
To get started, download the Uniswap Wallet on iOS or Android , or connect to the Uniswap Web App directly in your browser.
