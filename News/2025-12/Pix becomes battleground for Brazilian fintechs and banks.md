> [!info] 2025-12-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Pix, Brazil’s instant payments system, has become a central battleground for fintechs and banks in the country, as players compete on FX rates, cross-border features, and value-added services. The system’s expansion into e-commerce, recurring payments, and international use cases reinforces Brazil’s leadership in real-time payments across LatAm.
