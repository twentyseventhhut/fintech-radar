# KuCoin Pay Integrates with Brazil's Pix for Instant Crypto Payments

KuCoin Announcement

Release Time: 11/21/2025 10:01:09

Share

 KCS $7.185 3.02%

As per Announcement, KuCoin Pay has officially integrated with Pix, Brazil's instant payment platform, allowing users to pay with crypto at millions of online and physical stores. The integration enables KuCoin Pay users in Brazil to scan Pix QR codes for seamless transactions, bridging cryptocurrency with everyday purchases. Pix, managed by the Central Bank of Brazil, processes R$2.5 trillion monthly and is used by over 150 million people. To celebrate the launch, KuCoin Pay is offering a 50% cashback on first transactions and a raffle for a Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra. The campaign runs from November 19 to December 18, 2025 (UTC+8).
