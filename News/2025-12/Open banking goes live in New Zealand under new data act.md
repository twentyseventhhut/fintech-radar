# Open banking goes live in New Zealand under new data act

> [!info] 2025-12-02 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇿 Open Banking goes live in New Zealand under the new Customer and Product Data Act, Minister Scott Simpson says it will enable faster loan approvals, easier bill management and more personalised budgeting tools. The regime is expected to boost competition, make switching banks easier, and give small businesses better, lower-cost financial tools.
