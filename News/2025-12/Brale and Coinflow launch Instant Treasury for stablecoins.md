Brale × Coinflow: Instant Treasury
Today, Brale and Coinflow are excited to announce a combined offering that lets platforms turn marketplace float and user balances into new revenue.
With the Brale × Coinflow stack, platforms can:
Accept card payments
Instantly settle balances in their own branded stablecoin
Earn treasury revenue on idle balances
Let users cash out to bank, card, or crypto—all from a single integrated stack
Most marketplaces and platforms already have balances. Very few have turned those balances into a programmable, revenue-generating asset they control.
That's what this partnership is designed to change.
 Make money on balances you already hold 
Instant Treasury turns that float into a revenue-generating asset while giving users instant payouts in a balance they control - your own branded stablecoin.
“ Our customers want instant payouts, programmable money, and better unit economics. Partnering with Coinflow lets us deliver all three in a single stack: card in, your own stablecoin as the balance, and treasury revenue on the float you already have. ”
“ Merchants come to Coinflow because they're tired of waiting days for payouts and losing control of their money flows to legacy processors. By teaming up with Brale, we can give them instant settlement in a money they actually control — their own stablecoin — and a way to share in the revenue that those balances generate. ”
How it works
Imagine a marketplace, betting app, or telecom platform using the Brale × Coinflow stack:

 User deposits with a card 
 A user adds $500 via Coinflow. Coinflow processes the card and calls Brale's API. Brale mints $500 of your stablecoin into that user's balance. 


 Your stablecoin becomes the in-app money 
 Inside your product, everything happens in that stablecoin: bets, purchases, transfers, rewards, fees. Brale manages issuance and reserves, and Coinflow manages card rails and KYC in the background. 


 Balances quietly earn revenue 
 While funds sit as stablecoin balances on platform, Brale manages reserves. A share of that revenue can go back to platforms. Idle balances become a new revenue stream instead of a cost center. 


 Users cash out however they want 
 When users or sellers are ready to withdraw, they can move funds to a bank account, receive a payout via supported rails, or go on-chain in stablecoins. The conversion and payouts are handled by Brale and Coinflow behind the scenes. 

For select customers, we can also enable an instant funding facility: Coinflow can mint your stablecoin immediately up to a pre-agreed limit, with up to 48 hours to settle back with Brale. Users experience instant payouts without you tying up your own capital.
