# Bancamia partners Pomelo to launch app-managed debit card in Colombia

> [!info] 2025-12-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Bancamía has partnered with Pomelo to launch a fully app-managed debit card. The card allows users to request, activate, block, and manage it entirely from their phones, reducing reliance on physical branches and improving security. Bancamía says the initiative strengthens financial inclusion and helps small businesses build a transactional footprint to support growth.
