# Finturu raises $1.3 million from angels for regional expansion

> [!info] 2025-12-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌍Finturu, the Colombia-based cross-border commerce platform, raised $1.3 million from angel investors to accelerate its regional expansion. With the funding, Finturu plans to develop one of the region’s first crypto-acquiring systems and launch multi-currency accounts to simplify cross-border revenue management, while expanding its presence in Mexico, Brazil, and Central America.
