# South Africa puts retail CBDC on hold, prioritises payment reforms

> [!info] 2025-12-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇿🇦 South Africa puts retail CBDC on Hold and prioritises payment system reforms. The South African Reserve Bank concluded that the country has no immediate need for a CBDC despite years of research. Instead, it plans to focus on strengthening the national payment system and improving access for non-bank financial participants.
