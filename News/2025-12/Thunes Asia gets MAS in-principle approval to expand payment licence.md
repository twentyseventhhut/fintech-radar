# Thunes Asia gets MAS in-principle approval to expand payment licence

> [!info] 2025-12-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 Thunes Asia receives in-principle approval to expand the scope of its Major Payment Institution Licencefrom the Monetary Authority of Singapore. This will allow Singapore merchants to accept payments via popular international methods, while also enabling global merchants in key markets across the Middle East, South Asia, Africa, and Europe to accept leading Singapore payment methods.
