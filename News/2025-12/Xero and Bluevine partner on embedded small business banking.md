# Xero and Bluevine partner on embedded small business banking

> [!info] 2025-12-23 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] A prime example of this in action is the partnership between Xero and Bluevine. Announced at Money20/20, this all-in-one solution with the largest small business banking platform in the US provides customers a single dashboard to understand and act on their finances from the place they spend the most time–their business checking account. This eliminates the need to toggle between apps or perform manual reconciliations, and increases credibility, accuracy and peace of mind for small business owners, allowing them the gift of time returned to focus on what matters: growth.
