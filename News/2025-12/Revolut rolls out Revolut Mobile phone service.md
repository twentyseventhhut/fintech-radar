[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Revolut’s newest product offering is a mobile phone service. The London-headquartered FinTech has begun rolling out Revolut Mobile, the company said in a statement. It will cost £12.50 ($16.73) a month for those who sign up before March 30, rising to £14.99 after that.
