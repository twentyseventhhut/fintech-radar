Groww Introduces Three New Commodities: Now Trade in Zinc, Copper & Aluminium
 Hello Traders, 
 We’re excited to share that Groww has expanded its commodities offering with the launch of three new base metals — Zinc, Copper, and Aluminium. These commodities are widely tracked, actively traded, and play a major role in global markets. Whether you actively track commodity markets or want to diversify your trading strategies, you can now access these widely traded base metals directly on Groww’s platform. 
 What’s New? 
 You can now trade: 
 Zinc Futures 
 Copper Futures 
 Aluminium Futures 
 All contracts are available as listed on MCX, and you can track prices, check margins, and place trades instantly through Groww. 
 
 Why We Added These Commodities? 
 Zinc, Copper, and Aluminium are among the most actively traded base metals in global and domestic markets. Their widespread industrial use and consistent demand make them essential components of the commodity ecosystem. Introducing these metals on Groww enables traders to access a broader and more representative set of commodity opportunities. 
 These base metals offer: 
 High liquidity, ensuring smoother entry and exit for traders. 
 Diverse market drivers, influenced by industrial production, global supply chains, and economic activity. 
 Effective hedging tools for managing price risk in volatile markets. 
 Strategic diversification, allowing traders to move beyond precious metals and energy commodities. 
 How to Get Started with these Commodities on Groww? 

 Activate F&O Trading on Groww Account 

 To start trading commodities, you first need to be F&O onboarded on Groww. If you are not already onboarded, complete the F&O activation process first. Once onboarded, you will start seeing the Commodities section under F&O Explore. 

 Activate Commodity Trading 

 Go to any Commodity Product Page, Option Chain, or the All Commodities page, and click on Activate Commodities. The activation usually takes 1 working day. Once done, you can trade commodities directly from your Groww account. 

 Explore the Commodities Dashboard 

 After activation, head to the commodities section within the Groww app or website. Here, you can view real-time prices, market trends, and detailed insights for commodities like Gold, Silver, Crude Oil, Natural Gas, Zinc, Copper, Aluminium. 
 

 Place Your First Trade 

 Select your desired commodity, choose the contract type (futures or options), and execute your trade. Use Groww's advanced charting tools and real-time data to make informed decisions. 

 Monitor and Manage Positions 

 Keep track of your open positions, set stop-loss orders, and stay updated with market news to manage your trades effectively. 
 Follow these above steps and explore these new commodities on Groww today. 
 Start building a well-rounded portfolio. 
 Happy Investing! - Team Groww 
 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) 
 Which new commodities are now available for trading on Groww? 
 Groww has added three new base metal futures: Zinc, Copper, and Aluminium. All contracts are available as listed on MCX. 
 Do I need F&O activation to trade these commodities? 
 Yes. Commodity futures fall under the Futures & Options segment. If you haven’t enabled F&O yet, you can complete the activation directly in the app. 
 Where can I find these new commodity contracts on Groww? 
 You can search for Zinc, Copper, or Aluminium in the search bar on the Groww app or web. You will see all available futures contracts for each commodity. 
 What are the charges for trading these commodities on Groww? 
 Groww charges the same brokerage for commodity trading as it is for equity futures and options i.e. Rs 20 per executed order. In addition to these, there are regulatory and statutory charges levied by exchange and the government. The details of all these charges can be found here .
