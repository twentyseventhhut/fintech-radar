LAS VEGAS & SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--MassPay Holdings LLC today announced that MassPay is integrating Visa Direct to enable faster payouts to cards, bank accounts, and digital wallets through MassPay's proprietary orchestration platform.

> “Visa Direct enables fast, secure, and transparent movement of funds, and our collaboration with MassPay extends those benefits to even more businesses and consumers,” said Vira Platonova, Global Head of Visa Direct.
> 
> Share

For businesses that need to pay people across borders - whether gig workers, marketplace sellers or independent contractors - the traditional process is complex and slow. Companies typically manage relationships with multiple banks, payment processors, and compliance providers across different countries. Payments can take days to arrive, and navigating different regulations in each market requires significant resources.

MassPay eliminates this complexity through a proprietary orchestration layer backed by an extensive network of established banks and financial institutions worldwide. The platform's AI-driven routing engine analyzes each transaction in real time, automatically selecting the most efficient path based on speed, cost, and compliance requirements. With billions of dollars already flowing through the platform annually, the collaboration enables MassPay to offer direct integration with Visa's global network. Businesses get a single connection that handles payouts across 200+ markets, with funds arriving quickly and in a compliant manner.

“Visa Direct enables fast, secure, and transparent movement of funds, and our collaboration with MassPay extends those benefits to even more businesses and consumers,” said Vira Platonova, Global Head of Visa Direct. “By integrating Visa Direct, MassPay can offer real-time 1 payout experiences across a wide range of domestic and cross-border flows, helping organizations operate more efficiently and deliver the speed their customers increasingly expect.”

"By joining forces with Visa Direct, we’re able to improve how businesses can access global payout infrastructure," said Ran Grushkowsky, Chief Executive Officer at MassPay. "We've built enterprise-grade technology that handles the operational complexity, regulatory requirements, scale and speed that global businesses demand. We're the bridge between Visa's trusted network and any business that needs to move money internationally."

The collaboration solves real operational challenges. Instead of building and maintaining infrastructure individually in each market, businesses can now access Visa Direct's network through MassPay's platform, which features built-in compliance, currency conversion, and identity verification. Companies maintain control of their data and relationships while gaining access to infrastructure that would otherwise require years and millions of dollars to develop.

MassPay supports direct payouts to debit cards, real-time 1 bank transfers via Visa Direct, and digital wallet funding - all through a single integration. The platform handles multi-currency transactions with transparent foreign exchange and includes enterprise-grade compliance capabilities.

**About Mass Pay**

MassPay is the leading global payout orchestration platform built for businesses that need to move money instantly at scale - anywhere, anytime. Processing billions of dollars annually, MassPay provides enterprise-grade infrastructure for cross-border payouts, combining instant settlement capabilities, embedded compliance, and white-label solutions. Learn more at www.masspay.io.
