OpenPayd to Power Altify’s Multi-Currency On/Off Ramps
Altify has partnered with OpenPayd to streamline fiat currency deposits and withdrawals for its global user base.
Posted on December 3, 2025
 OpenPayd Editorial Team 
Article navigation
 About Altify  
 About OpenPayd 
 London, UK, 3 December 2025 – Altify, the tokenised private market investing app built to help anyone invest like the ultra wealthy, has partnered with OpenPayd, a leading provider of financial infrastructure, to streamline fiat currency deposits and withdrawals in EUR, GBP and USD for its global user base. 
 Through the integration, Altify now offers seamless access to GBP, EUR and USD deposits and withdrawals via SEPA, Faster Payments and SWIFT, enabling fast and reliable money movement across its global ecosystem.  
 For Altify’s 80,000+ users, the partnership delivers faster access to and from digital asset markets, smoother cross-border flows and a more consistent investing experience across all asset classes. 
 Altify offers a unified investing platform that enables users to invest in digital assets and tokenised real-world assets (RWAs) across multiple asset classes, including private credit, stocks, commodities, crypto and more through a single, intuitive interface. As the platform scales, eliminating friction from the movement of value between assets and geographies is central to its mission. OpenPayd’s multi-currency infrastructure provides a robust, compliant foundation that supports the high-volume activity of a global user base, ensuring Altify can continue delivering a modern, borderless investing experience. 
 Sean Sanders, Chief Executive Officer at Altify, added, “We built Altify to make private market investing as simple as buying public stocks. But you can’t have a seamless investment experience if moving money is a headache. OpenPayd solves that. They provide the infrastructure that lets our users move between fiat and digital assets instantly. It’s the kind of invisible efficiency that builds trust.” 
 Lux Thiagarajah, Chief Commercial Officer at OpenPayd, stated, “Altify is building the next-generation investing experience, and that requires financial infrastructure that keeps pace with user expectations. By consolidating EUR, GBP and USD flows into one unified environment, we’re giving Altify the speed and reliability needed to deliver a truly seamless investing experience – wherever their customers are.” 
 As digital investing platforms evolve, seamless interoperability between fiat and digital assets is a competitive differentiator. With OpenPayd’s universal financial infrastructure, connecting domestic, international and blockchain payment rails, Altify can continue to innovate at pace while serving users across Europe, and beyond, where investors prefer to deposit in local currencies while accessing USD-denominated alternative investments. 
 About Altify  
 Altify , is a London-headquartered regulated investment platform that makes investing in tokenised private market opportunities as easy as buying shares. Altify is a self-directed investment platform, offering a mobile app and web platform for individual investors to access vetted, institutional-grade tokenised alternatives – across private credit, stocks, crypto, commodities, and more – without the high minimums or complexity. The fintech serves both institutional and retail clients with over 80,000 investors across EMEA. Altify is backed by leading global investors including listed specialist investment group Sabvest, CVVC, Emurgo, High-Tech Gründerfonds, 54Collective, and others. 
 About OpenPayd 
 OpenPayd is building the universal financial infrastructure for the digital economy. Their rails-agnostic platform enables businesses to move and manage money globally – across fiat and digital assets – through a single, powerful API. OpenPayd provides embedded accounts, FX, domestic and international payments, Open Banking, and stablecoin on/off ramps – delivering interoperability between traditional finance and digital assets. With one of the most comprehensive banking networks in the market, OpenPayd enables real-time money movement, everywhere.  
 Trusted by global brands including eToro, Kraken, OKX, and B2C2, they process more than €130 billion in annual volumes for over 800 businesses. OpenPayd is the infrastructure layer powering the next generation of financial services.
