# Nstech gets Central Bank of Brazil payment institution licence

> [!info] 2025-12-07 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Nstech, a supply chain software provider, secured authorization from the Central Bank of Brazil (BCB) to operate as a Payment Institution (IP) in the electronic money issuer category. The license allows the company to embed financial services and manage digital wallets directly within its logistics platform.
