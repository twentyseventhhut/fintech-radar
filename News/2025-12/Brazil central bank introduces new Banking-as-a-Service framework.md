# Brazil central bank introduces new Banking-as-a-Service framework

> [!info] 2025-12-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 The Central Bank of Brazil (BCB) introduced a new Banking as a Service (BaaS) framework, that some see as a turning point for the sector. The rules bring clearer oversight, higher service quality standards, and more defined responsibilities for licensed financial institutions offering BaaS. These providers now face stricter obligations in areas such as Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance, risk management, and internal controls.
