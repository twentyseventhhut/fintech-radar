Fintech startup MoneyView raises $75-M from Winter Capital, Evolvence India, others

My Big Plunge

Bengaluru-based fintech platform MoneyView has raised USD 75 million in a Series D round from Moscow-based Winter Capital and Evolvence India, with participation from existing investors Tiger Global and Accel. The round has taken the company's valuation to USD 625 million. Co-founded by Puneet Agarwal and Sanjay Aggarwal in 2014, MoneyView offers a full suite of personalised credit products like instant personal loans, cards, BNPL and personal financial management solutions. It will use the funds as growth capital to scale the core credit business, grow the team, and expand its product portfolio with services like digital bank accounts, insurance, and wealth management solutions.
