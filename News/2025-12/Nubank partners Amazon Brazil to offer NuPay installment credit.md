# Nubank partners Amazon Brazil to offer NuPay installment credit

> [!info] 2025-12-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Nubank, the Brazilian digital bank, partnered with Amazon Brazil to introduce “special credit” through its NuPay payment platform. The move lets customers pay for Amazon purchases in up to 24 installments, expanding access to credit in e-commerce and deepening the reach of digital payments in Brazil.
