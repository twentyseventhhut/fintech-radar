Another Milestone: Three New Stablecoins Strengthen Global Payments and Expand the xMoney Ecosystem
A look at the freshly minted whitepapers for EURXM, USDXM, and RONXM, and what to expect from the stablecoins’ launch in June 2026.
Contents
As more and more digital assets crowd the cryptocurrency ecosystem, the need for stable, reliable digital assets is becoming more and more pressing, especially given the unprecedented volatility currently shaking the market - which is why xMoney is once again disrupting the digital payments industry, this time by ushering in a new era of stablecoins.
Beginning with the publishing of whitepapers for stablecoins EURXM , USDXM , and RONXM - which will officially launch in June 2026 - our slate of stablecoins is a significant step forward in reaching our main goal of building compliant, scalable infrastructure for the global payments market, bridging the gap between the speed and efficiency of blockchain and the stability of traditional currencies.
Purpose of Our New Stablecoins
These aren’t just your ‘run of the mill’ stablecoins. With EURXM, USDXM, and RONXM, we’re expanding the boundaries of digital finance, where upon launch, they’ll be immediately integrated into the xMoney payment gateway and xMoney Card, allowing consumers to swap/hold the coins in their wallets with confidence.
By integrating our stablecoins within the xMoney card and crypto payment processing flows, we’re helping provide customers with a faster, more efficient payments experience, improving client flows and operations, and products by offering tailored payment rails.
We are strategically launching on Sui Network and MultiversX first. By leveraging the high-throughput capabilities of these two networks, our stablecoins will immediately offer near-instant settlement and negligible gas fees - providing a user experience far superior to legacy options. Following this initial rollout, we will expand to Ethereum and other ecosystems.
Stablecoins Built on Trust and Security
But speed and low gas fees aren’t the only traits of EURXM, USDXM, and RONXM.The stablecoins are MiCA compliant and fully regulated because xMoney followed all the necessary procedures and due diligence to ensure we respect the legislation. As a regulated entity, xMoney went through all the necessary steps to offer our clients - and the general public - fully compliant and secure stablecoins.
The stablecoins fully respect the MiCA regulation, being 1:1 fiat-backed and redeemable at any time at par value, and will undergo ongoing financial audits once publicly launched. The MiCA compliance ensures our stablecoins are some of the most trusted in Web3 finance, helping us strengthen the bridge between crypto payments and traditional finance.
Our stablecoin whitepapers go beyond a simple technical milestone and are a major step toward executing the vision that has guided xMoney: building a unified payment ecosystem where users and businesses can transact freely, securely, and without the bottlenecks of outdated financial systems.
By introducing regulated, highly efficient stablecoins directly into our payment gateway and card flows, we’re moving closer to a world where digital payments simply work, regardless of borders, currencies, or infrastructures.
The Path to EURXM, USDXM, and RONXM
The publishing of our stablecoins’ whitepapers is the latest milestone in our storied quest to bridge the gap between Web3 finance and traditional currencies.
In 2013, we received the first Electronic Money license issued by the National Bank of Romania, and have since built the xMoney infrastructure in line with European and national regulations in different areas, such as payments, AML, GDPR, and security.
In 2022, we were acquired by MultiversX group, and further rebranded to xMoney in 2025 - paving the way to being part of a group that offers extended financial services, such as payments processing, card issuing, crypto payments through the xMoney VASP licensed in Portugal, and now stablecoins EURXM, USDXM, and RONXM.
The Future of Stablecoins is on xMoney
It’s been an incredible journey so far, and we’re thrilled you’ve stuck with us because we’re just getting started. Check out our newly published whitepapers for more information on how you’ll be able to use EURXM , USDXM , and RONXM , and stay tuned for more innovations and unique use-cases to come from xMoney in the coming months.
