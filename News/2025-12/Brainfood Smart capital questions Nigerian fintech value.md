## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] “Smart capital is now asking whether fintechs are solving real problems that expand the economy or simply extracting rent from existing fragility,” said Kristin H. Wilson, Managing Partner at Innovate Africa Fund. It’s a brutal assessment, but one that explains why only 27 out of over 500 Nigerian fintech companies managed to raise funding of $100,000 or more this year.
