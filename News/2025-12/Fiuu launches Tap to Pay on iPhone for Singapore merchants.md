## Payment firm Fiuu unveils Apple’s Tap to Pay for SG merchants

Share

Add TIA as a preferred source

Fiuu has launched Tap to Pay on iPhone for Singapore merchants, allowing them to accept contactless payments through its Virtual Terminal app without extra hardware.

Fiuu, a digital payment processor with operations across Southeast Asia, said the feature supports contactless cards, Apple Pay, and other digital wallets using only an iPhone running iOS.

The company said merchants can set up the service within minutes on iPhone XS or newer devices.

Fiuu reported a total payment volume of over US$9.8 billion for FY2024.

Fiuu Now Offers Tap to Pay on iPhone for Merchants to Accept Quick Contactless Payments
December 02, 2025
 

 An easy, secure, and private way to accept contactless payments with only an iPhone and a supporting iOS app, no additional hardware required. 
 SINGAPORE – Fiuu , powering future payments in Southeast Asia, now enables its Singapore merchants to seamlessly and securely accept quick in-person contactless payments with Tap to Pay on iPhone through the Fiuu Virtual Terminal app. Tap to Pay on iPhone allows merchants to accept all forms of contactless payments, including contactless credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and other digital wallets, using only an iPhone and the Fiuu VT iOS app – no additional hardware or payment terminal needed.
Using Tap to Pay on iPhone is easy, secure, and private. With Tap to Pay on iPhone, at checkout, merchants will simply prompt the customer to hold their contactless payment near the merchant’s iPhone, and the payment will be securely completed using NFC technology. Tap to Pay on iPhone also supports PIN entry, which includes accessibility options.
Apple’s Tap to Pay on iPhone technology uses the built-in security and privacy features of iPhone to help protect business and customer data. Apple doesn’t store card numbers or transaction information on the device or on Apple servers, so merchants and customers can rest assured that their data stays theirs.*
“With the enablement of Tap to Pay on iPhone, payments are no longer tied to fixed locations. This gives businesses the flexibility to serve consumers wherever the interaction happens. As we keep pace with the growing demand for quick and seamless payments, we’re excited to introduce Tap to Pay on iPhone alongside three forward-thinking brands: Razer, a global lifestyle brand for gamers, is empowering its Singapore teams to accept payments directly on iPhone at its Funan stores and Southeast Asia HQ. IDS Aesthetics Clinic is elevating the customer journey by allowing beauty consultants to complete payments at the point of engagement. Beutea, a modern tea beverage brand is enhancing cashier efficiency through Zeoniq, our strategic POS partner, to deliver a seamless checkout experience,” said Eng Sheng Guan, CEO of Fiuu.
Tap to Pay on iPhone enables Fiuu’s merchants to use a contactless payment acceptance solution that is easy to set up and use. Merchants can unlock contactless payments within minutes using the Fiuu VT iOS app on an iPhone XS or later running the latest version of iOS. For more information on Fiuu Virtual Terminal, please visit https://fiuu.com/virtual-terminal .
*Encrypted card numbers are temporarily stored on iPhone only for transactions made in Store and Forward mode.
