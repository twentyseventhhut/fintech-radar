# SwissBorg and Mastercard launch crypto debit card

> [!info] 2025-12-11 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇭 SwissBorg has partnered with Mastercard to launch the SwissBorg Card, a crypto debit card enabling users to spend digital assets at over 150 million locations worldwide. Each payment converts crypto to fiat in real time through the SwissBorg Meta-Exchange to secure optimal rates.

The SwissBorg Crypto Card with Mastercard
CyBorg
SwissBorg expert
Today marks a major milestone in SwissBorg’s journey to bring crypto into everyday life by uniting seamlessly with traditional finance.
We’re proud to announce our official partnership with Mastercard for the forthcoming SwissBorg Card - a crypto debit card that lets you spend your digital assets at more than 150 million Mastercard accepting locations worldwide .
This is more than a card. It’s a new way to connect your crypto with the real world, built directly into the SwissBorg app . 
 The virtual card rollout will begin in Q1 2026 to multiple countries* - including the EEA and Switzerland.

Spending Your Crypto Has Never Been Easier
Powered by the SwissBorg Meta-Exchange (MEX)
Every time you make a payment with your SwissBorg Card, your chosen crypto is converted to fiat in real time .
 But Here’s the Magic: Your conversion happens via the SwissBorg Meta-Exchange , our smart aggregator that searches across 30+ centralised exchanges, decentralised exchanges, and multiple blockchains to secure the most efficient route.
In simple terms:
 When you pay, the MEX works to save you money. Automatically. 
You choose your primary spending asset - BORG , BTC, ETH, SOL, or a stablecoin, EUROP, USDC, USDG, or VCHF (depending on local availability) - and have the option to select backup assets that the app can use to fulfill the payment if your primary balance is too low.
 Transactions settle by default in your base currency (CHF, GBP, or EUR), depending on your country of residence.

Paid to Spend
Up to 99% Cashback in BORG - Real Rewards
Every transaction you make generates a small exchange fee from the crypto-to-fiat conversion. And just like with SwissBorg “ Paid to Trade ,” a portion of that fee comes straight back to you in BORG .
 Up to 99% cashback depending on your Loyalty Rank 
 BORG rewards are bought back from the open market , creating consistent buy pressure 
 As you accumulate cashback, you climb the Loyalty Ranks , unlocking boosted yields, airdrops, special earn strategies, and exclusive Alpha opportunities
With the SwissBorg Card, every payment moves you forward in the ecosystem.

Your Spending. Your Rules.
Total Control in a Single App
The SwissBorg Card is fully integrated into the SwissBorg app via an intuitive new interface where you can:
Choose and reorder spending assets
Freeze or unfreeze your card instantly
Block the card immediately in case of loss, theft or fraud
Track spending and see real-time conversions
Add to Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Curve (where supported)
Every feature is built for clarity, safety, and absolute control. 
Online payments are protected with 3D Secure , ensuring an extra layer of safety for e-commerce transactions.

A Practical Use Case for Digital Assets
 “The introduction of the SwissBorg Card is a big step in bridging crypto with real life experiences. We believe everyone should be free to spend the wealth they create in a seamless, simple way.” 
 Cyrus Fazel, Co-Founder and CEO of SwissBorg. 

For years, crypto cards have existed - but very few offer a genuine advantage to users. The SwissBorg Card will be unique by combining:
The routing power of the Meta-Exchange 
A real economic model that rewards spending
An ecosystem where the BORG token sits at the centre 
What Comes Next
The first release will come as a virtual card , rolled out during Q1 ’26 across Europe. Make sure you join the waitlist here to be amongst the first to receive it.
As previously promised, first priority will be given to eligible Series A investors and they have been included on the list by default.
 Next up: 
Additional features, including rewards, perks and real-life experiences
Physical cards

Our partnership with Mastercard is a significant step for the integration of cryptocurrency with everyday finance.

 Christian Rau, senior vice president, Global Fintech Enablers, Digital Assets & Blockchain at Mastercard 

Soon you’ll be able to spend your crypto anywhere Mastercard is accepted , and benefit from instant crypto-to-fiat conversion at the checkout.
