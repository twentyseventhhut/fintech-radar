Navro Activates International Stablecoin Payments With BVNK Integration
Aran Brown
CEO
‍
Navro, the pioneering fintech that simplifies international payments for businesses, today announces the introduction of stablecoin payments to its comprehensive range of international payment rails, thanks to its integration with BVNK, the leading stablecoin infrastructure provider. 
‍
Navro’s activation of stablecoin payment capabilities represents the next phase in its vision of providing access to the best payment services in every region of the world through one platform, one API, and one contract. 
‍
There is growing demand for payments in stablecoins from contractors and freelancers in countries with high inflation and unreliable local currencies. For organisations needing to pay these professionals, stablecoins can simplify multi-country workforce payroll and reduce the need for complex foreign exchange conversions.
‍
As a result, the integration of stablecoin payments is of particular interest to Employer of Record platforms – a key customer segment for Navro with its proficiency in managing large volumes of incoming and outgoing transactions in multiple jurisdictions alongside the associated compliance requirements. 
‍
 Aran Brown, CEO of Navro , said:
 "From the outset, Navro’s focus has been on developing fresh approaches, processes and infrastructure to remove some of the headaches that have historically plagued international payments – issues such as management complexity, opaque and excessive fees, or a lack of control over the end user’s experience. Introducing stablecoins as an alternative payment rail gives our customers even more choice about how they can best fulfil their specific requirements for cross-border transactions.” 
Navro opted for BVNK due to its expertise in payouts and a compelling product roadmap. 
‍
 Jesse Hemson-Struthers, Founder and CEO at BVNK , said: “We're excited to be partnering with Navro on this integration, which makes it easier for their clients to pay end customers, contractors and employees across borders with greater speed, transparency and reliability. 
 “Navro already delivers instant, global payroll and contractor payments, and by integrating BVNK’s fiat and stablecoin payout capabilities, they are adding another always-on rail and more choice, reinforcing their ability to move money worldwide in minutes, not days.” 
‍
The arrival of clear legal frameworks in the world’s leading financial jurisdictions – including the GENIUS Act in the US, MiCA in the EU, and the FCA’s roadmap for systemic stablecoins in the UK – has been the catalyst for growing adoption of stablecoins by mainstream financial institutions throughout the course of 2025. Offering almost instant settlement, the ability to transact outside normal banking hours, and reduced friction by eliminating chains of intermediaries, stablecoins have significant strengths in comparison to traditional payment rails and now power an estimated $9 trillion in annual transactions . 
‍
Navro’s addition of stablecoin payments comes two months after the firm announced the integration of 17 new realtime pay-to-bank corridors, meaning that it now offers direct, realtime payments to bank accounts in 61 countries.
