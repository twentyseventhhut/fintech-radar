# Mesa shuts down homeowners credit card program

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[TechCrunch — Fintech] A message on the Mesa website states that as of December 12, “all Mesa Homeowners Card accounts are closed,” adding, “All credit cards have been deactivated and you are no longer able to make any new purchases or earn Mesa Points.”

[This Week in Fintech] Mesa shuttered its credit card program that rewarded homeowners for making mortgage payments.
