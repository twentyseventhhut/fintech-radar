# Argentina's central bank weighs allowing banks to use crypto

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇷 Argentina's Central Bank is considering allowing banks to operate with cryptocurrencies. The publication reports that regulations are being drafted, but no date has yet been set for their presentation. A cryptocurrency brokerage operating in Argentina said that the measure could be approved in April 2026.
