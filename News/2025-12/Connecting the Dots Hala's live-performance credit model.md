## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] Rather than relying primarily on historical credit scores and static documentation, Hala’s model is based on live business performance. By using sales data from a merchant’s point-of-sale system or payment history, financing decisions are tied directly to how a business is performing in real time.
