Aspora Names Varun Sridhar CEO To Lead Global Fintech Expansion
 
 # Aspora 
 # Varun Sridhar 
 # CEO appointment 
 # Paytm Money 
 # fintech leadership 
 # global expansion 
 # wealthtech 
 Aspora has announced the appointment of Varun Sridhar as Chief Executive Officer for its wealth and lending business, signalling a strong push to strengthen its global fintech footprint and expand services for cross-border customers of Indian origin. 
 Sridhar, a seasoned financial-services leader, is best known for steering Paytm Money through a crucial phase of product modernisation and operational scale. He later headed Paytm Services, where he worked on strengthening the group’s financial ecosystem during a period of intense industry shifts. 
 Backed by over two decades of experience across multinational banks including BNP Paribas, Deutsche Bank and Citibank, Sridhar brings a deep understanding of retail finance, digital wealth, and regulated lending. His career has consistently centred on transforming traditional financial offerings through technology-led, consumer-first innovation. 
 At Aspora, his mandate will focus on accelerating the company’s entry into priority markets such as the US, UK, UAE, Germany, Italy and Ireland, alongside building tailored wealth and loan products for globally mobile Indian professionals and families. The company is also preparing for further geographical expansion, supported by a strong capital base and rising customer demand in the cross-border financial services segment. 
 Industry observers note that his appointment comes at a time when Aspora is positioning itself as a differentiated player in remittance-linked wealth services, an area gaining traction as global income flows and NRI investments grow. 
 Sridhar’s move marks his first major leadership role post-Paytm, reflecting his continued bet on technology-driven financial inclusion and global Indian consumer markets.
