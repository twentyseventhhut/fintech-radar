[This Week in Fintech] 🇨🇱 Visma, the Norwegian software group, bought the Chilean proptech Comunidad Feliz in a deal close to $70 million. The platform, which manages residential buildings across Latin America, generates about $11.5 million in annual revenue and employs more than 225 people in Chile and Mexico. The founders will remain in place as Visma accelerates the company’s regional expansion.

Visma acquires Comunidad Feliz for $70M
 by Araceli Dominguez 
December 5, 2025
 1 minute read
 Visma , a Norwegian business software company, acquired the Chilean proptech ComunidadFeliz for approximately $70M. 
Visma is one of Europe’s largest software providers and offers digital tools for accounting, payroll, and business operations.
ComunidadFeliz is an online property management platform that helps administrators and residents manage rental operations more efficiently. Its software streamlines workflows, automates payments, and improves transparency in daily building management.
The acquisition strengthens Visma’s strategy to expand in Latin America, following Visma’s 2025 acquisitions of Conta Azul and Rindegastos .
