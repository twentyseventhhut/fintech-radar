## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Tandem unveils new portfolio Cash ISA. The Portfolio Cash ISA brings instant access and fixed-rate ISAs under one easy-to-manage portfolio, allowing customers to tailor savings to their needs. Savers can open up to 20 ISAs across any combination of instant access and fixed-term accounts, offering flexibility or guaranteed returns.
