[This Week in Fintech] HashKey, Hong Kong's largest licensed crypto exchange, raised $206M through Initial Public Offering (IPO) on Hong Kong Stock Exchange.

[This Week in Fintech] HashKey Group went public yesterday—an important marker for Hong Kong’s regulated digital-asset push. It priced near the top of its range and raised about $206 million; shares opened soft and closed slightly down. The takeaway isn’t momentum but validation: cornerstone institutions, including UBS and Fidelity, backed a compliance-first model while the market stays risk-off near term.
