Mollie is ready for agentic commerce in Europe
Our payment infrastructure is ready for OpenAI's Agentic Commerce Protocol – and the future of AI sales.

Our payment infrastructure is ready for OpenAI's Agentic Commerce Protocol – and the future of AI sales.

 November 19, 2025 
Mollie is ready to help businesses accept payments through AI assistants like ChatGPT.
This move makes us one of the first European payment providers to be fully compatible with OpenAI's Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP). And it means our customers can accept payments securely through ChatGPT as soon as it launches in Europe.
Our product and engineering teams in our Lisbon hub drove this breakthrough, creating a full, end-to-end purchase made entirely within conversational AI – all powered by our compatibility with ACP’s Delegated Payment API, an essential building block that makes our payment infrastructure agentic commerce-ready. 
What this means for your business
While OpenAI hasn’t yet launched ACP in Europe, our systems are ready to help businesses get paid, securely and seamlessly, as soon as it does.
"This is another major step in our role as an AI commerce enabler for European businesses," says Bernardo Caldas, Mollie’s Director of Data and AI. "Our customers shouldn’t need to be experts in competing protocols. Instead, we handle that for them. Our infrastructure is ready, which means our customers can focus on their business, knowing we’ll be prepared to help them sell through AI from day one."
Mollie: Building for the future of AI-driven commerce
Our long-term focus is to make agentic commerce work for Europe. We aim to simplify the complexity of multiple agentic protocols under one integration, giving you a single, simple path to selling through agentic commerce. 
We’re also actively collaborating with Google on the development of the Agent Payments Protocol, and working with our ecommerce platform partners so that the European businesses we serve are ready to sell through agentic commerce from day one.
