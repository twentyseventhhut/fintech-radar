# Central Bank of Liberia launches instant payments system on Mojaloop

> [!info] 2025-12-17 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇱🇷 Central Bank of Liberia launches Transformational Inclusive Instant Payments System (IIPS) powered by Mojaloop. IIPS will principally enable mobile money transfers between two Mobile Network Operators, Lone Star Cell MTN and Orange Liberia, and represents a major milestone in the CBL’s mission to digitise the national economy and strengthen seamless interoperability.
