# Peru's BCRP authorizes seven wallets for instant payments pilot

> [!info] 2025-12-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇵🇪 The Central Reserve Bank of Peru (BCRP) authorized seven digital wallets and fintechs, including Yape, Niubiz, and Bitel, to participate in the pilot phase of the Retail Payments Platform (PPM), modeled on India’s Unified Payments Interface (UPI). Controlled testing of interoperable QR codes and real-time transfers is set to begin in December 2026.
