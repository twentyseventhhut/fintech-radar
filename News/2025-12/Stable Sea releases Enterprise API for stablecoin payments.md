Introducing Stable Sea Enterprise API
Julie Ingle
Dec 16, 2025
 The Fastest Way for Companies to Bring Stablecoins Into Their Global Payments and Treasury Systems at Scale 
Today we’re excited to announce the launch of the Stable Sea Enterprise API, a unified solution enabling businesses to embed stablecoin capabilities directly into their existing treasury and payment systems.
Until now, enterprises have struggled to take advantage of stablecoins due to fragmented networks, wallet sprawl, and the absence of infrastructure built for the scale and standards of traditional B2B payment flows. Stable Sea changes that by offering a unified API for global stablecoin operations and providing custom stablecoin workflows tailored to each enterprise's corporate structure and cross-border payments needs. 
“Enterprises are ready to adopt stablecoins, but they need infrastructure that is tailored to the scale, depth, and complexity of their global operations,” said Tanner Taddeo, CEO of Stable Sea. “Every business is different. With our new API platform, companies can get best-in-class infrastructure customized to their organizations specific cross-border payments needs."
Cross-border payments remain one of the most expensive and inefficient pain points in global finance. Businesses can still pay up to 200–300 basis points to move funds through traditional correspondent banking rails, with settlement times stretching into days. Stablecoins already represent over $15 trillion in annualized transaction volume, yet enterprise adoption lags due to fragmented networks, wallet sprawl, and lack of solutions to match the needs of enterprise-grade ticket sizes and volumes.
The Stable Sea API directly addresses these gaps, offering modular tools that unify digital asset operations across on/off ramps, wallets, and payment networks - all within a single enterprise control surface.
 On/Off-Ramps:  Seamlessly convert between stablecoins and fiat at industry-leading rates. Support for high-value transactions—up to $50 million per ticket—enables enterprise-scale payables, supplier funding, and treasury transfers. 
 Bespoke Stablecoin Support:  Custom workflows supported for stablecoin issuers. Get stablecoin off ramp capabilities without needing an exchange integration. 
 Multi-Wallet Management: Create, connect, and govern multiple wallets alongside traditional bank accounts with policy-driven controls and audit-ready transparency. 
 Multi-Network Management:   Operate across leading stablecoin networks and route payments by cost, corridor, or settlement speed. 
 Role-based User Access Controls: Manage multiple users and role-based permissions in our Ops Dashboard. 
 Direct ERP & Accounting Integrations: Sync stablecoin and fiat transactions directly with leading ERP and accounting systems. 
The release of the Stable Sea Enterprise API marks a major step in our mission to bring stablecoins into mainstream enterprise operations.
Stablecoins have proven their value as fast, programmable settlement assets. What’s been missing is a way for enterprises to use them with the same confidence and control they expect from traditional finance systems.
Stable Sea delivers that giving companies the ability to orchestrate and optimize global money movement across digital and traditional rails.
Visit StableSea.com/developers to learn more and explore documentation.
