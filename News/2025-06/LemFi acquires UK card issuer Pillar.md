[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 LemFi buys Pillar to expand credit services to immigrants. The company plans to offer Credit Cards to customers, starting with the UK, and roll out the full suite of LemFi Credit features publicly. LemFi secures access to Pillar's proprietary credit scoring technology, its FCA credit licence, and Bhatt and Lewis.
