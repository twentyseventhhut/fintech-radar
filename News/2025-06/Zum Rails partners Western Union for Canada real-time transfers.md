## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇦 Zūm Rails partners with Western Union, facilitating real-time transactions from Canada to the world. The partnership will, in turn, enable the company to drive real-time movement of funds for Western Union customers using Interac eTransfer. With Interac eTransfer, customers can instantly fund their money transfers in Canada, then send the money anywhere in the world.
