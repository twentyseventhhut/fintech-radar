# Mantra and Win Investments partner on sports asset tokenization

> [!info] 2025-06-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎 Mantra, the global blockchain platform focused on real-world assets (RWA), and Win Investments, the Argentinian digital sports assets startup, announced a strategic partnership to drive the tokenization of real-world assets in the sports sector. The collaboration will leverage Mantra’s Layer 1 blockchain technology and Win Investments’ expertise in creating digital sports assets within a regulatory framework.
