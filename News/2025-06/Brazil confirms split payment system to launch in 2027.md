# Brazil confirms split payment system to launch in 2027

> [!info] 2025-06-08 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Bernard Appy, Special Secretary for Tax Reform, confirmed on Thursday (May 29) that the split payment system will come into effect in 2027. The implementation will be gradual, beginning with business-to-business (B2B) transactions.
