Company Announcement
Hong Kong’s Securities and Futures Commission grants Airwallex licence to provide asset management services
 Airwallex Yield to be launched in early 2025, providing Hong Kong businesses with investment avenue for multi-currency funds 
 Airwallex becomes the first global payments group to receive this licence in Hong Kong 
 HONG KONG - Airwallex today announced that Airwallex Capital* is now licensed by the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong (SFC) to provide asset management services, paving the way for the upcoming launch of its popular Airwallex Yield in Hong Kong early next year. Airwallex Yield will enable customers to earn a return on multi-currency funds with no minimum lock-up period required. 
Airwallex Yield is designed to give businesses opportunities to:
 Maximise returns on their multi-currency funds via Airwallex Capital’s investments into highly-rated money market funds with decades of strong performance. 
 Grow their multi-currency balances without having to open a foreign bank account. 
 Avoid lock-up periods, hence easily move funds between cash balances and their Airwallex Yield account whenever needed to keep growing the business. 
Airwallex Yield was first introduced in Australia in November 2023 by Airwallex Capital’s sister company, and has since attracted over AU$100M in funds under management .
Celebrating the licence, Arnold Chan , Airwallex APAC General Manager said, "Airwallex is proud to have received a licence for asset management services in Hong Kong, via Airwallex Capital. This makes Airwallex the first global payments group to obtain such a licence in Hong Kong. This milestone allows us to prepare the launch of Airwallex Yield in Hong Kong, which will help customers earn a return on multi-currency funds. We remain committed to innovation and providing cutting-edge financial solutions."
Interested customers can join the waitlist to receive information about Airwallex Yield when available.
 END 
 *Remarks: Airwallex Capital Hong Kong Limited (CE Number: BUL570) (“Airwallex Capital”) is licensed by the Securities & Futures Commission of Hong Kong to provide Type 4 (advising on securities) and Type 9 (asset management) regulated activities. In Hong Kong, Airwallex Yield will be a discretionary portfolio management service provided by Airwallex Capital Hong Kong Limited. Airwallex Yield is provided in Australia by Airwallex Capital’s local sister entity.

Airwallex, a global payments and financial platform, has been granted a license by the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong to provide asset management services through its subsidiary, Airwallex Capital.
According to Airwallex , this makes the company the first global payments group to receive this type of license in Hong Kong.
The firm plans to launch Airwallex Yield , a service that allows businesses to earn returns on multi-currency funds, in early 2025.
Airwallex Yield will offer businesses a way to invest their funds in money market funds without a minimum lock-up period, providing flexibility and potentially higher returns.
The service has already seen success in Australia, where it has attracted over AU$100 million in funds under management since its launch in November 2023.
Airwallex Capital Hong Kong Limited is licensed to provide Type 4 (advising on securities) and Type 9 (asset management) regulated activities.
Businesses interested in Airwallex Yield can join a waitlist for more information ahead of the service’s Hong Kong launch next year.
