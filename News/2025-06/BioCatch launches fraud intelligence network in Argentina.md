# BioCatch launches fraud intelligence network in Argentina

> [!info] 2025-06-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇦🇷 BioCatch, the Israel-based behavioral biometrics fraud prevention platform, announced the launch of BioCatch Trust Argentina, a real-time fraud intelligence network now joined by Santander, Galicia, and Naranja X. The initiative aims to enable banks to share behavioral and device-based risk signals to block suspicious transfers before funds are withdrawn.
