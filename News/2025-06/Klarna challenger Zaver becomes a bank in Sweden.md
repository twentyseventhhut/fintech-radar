[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇪 Klarna challenger Zaver becomes a bank. The company has been granted a license for financing activities by the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority. This authorization allows Zaver to provide payment services under the Swedish Payment Services Act and the European PSD2 regulations.
