# CRDB Bank migrates card systems to BPC's SmartVista platform

The bank has also migrated its subsidiaries in Burundi and DR Congo to the platform.

CRDB Bank in Tanzania has partnered with Swiss payment technology provider BPC to migrate its switch and card management systems to the SmartVista platform.

The platform features multi-card issuance capabilities, including corporate and virtual cards, as well as acquiring services for processing transactions across POS networks, cardless withdrawals, and foreign exchange (FX) at ATMs, among others.

CRDB Bank has also connected SmartVista with its mobile banking and agency banking services, with the migration including its subsidiaries in Burundi and DR Congo, and affiliates Amana, Vision Fund, and the Kilimanjaro Co-operative Bank Limited (KCBL).

The partnership has so far enabled the issuance of 4.5 million cards across these operations, according to BPC, which, in Africa, also works with Vista Bank Group, Somalia Payment Switch and Egyptian start-up Taly.

Founded in 1996, CRDB Bank offers retail, corporate, SME, and capital markets services, and with a network of more than 260 branches, 36,000+ Wakala agents, 5,000 POS devices, and a balance sheet of $6.6 billion, operates as Tanzania's largest bank.
