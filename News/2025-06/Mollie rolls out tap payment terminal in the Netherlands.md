Mollie launches Tap: its smart payment terminal for seamless in-person payments
Mollie Tap: Smart, fast, affordable terminal for seamless in-person payments. Unify online & offline sales. Easy, affordable, ready in 30 secs.

Mollie Tap: Smart, fast, affordable terminal for seamless in-person payments. Unify online & offline sales. Easy, affordable, ready in 30 secs.

 June 18, 2025 
We’re thrilled to announce the launch of Tap , our smart, fast, and affordable payment terminal designed for modern businesses. 
Tap empowers Mollie customers to accept contactless payments with unparalleled ease, serving a wide range of businesses and entrepreneurs, from hair salons and cafés to market stalls and taxi companies. Tap is launching in the Netherlands first, and will then expand to other key Mollie markets including Belgium, Germany, and Austria in the coming weeks. 
Engineered for simplicity
Tap’s designed for the reality of running a small business, with smart functions and effortless ease of use. It runs on Android, which means it integrates with Android apps that fit in with business owners' day-to-day work. And thanks to its sleek, compact design, the terminal fits easily into any pocket. Plus, unlike traditional terminals, there’s no need to turn or tilt the device; customers can simply hold their payment card or smartphone against Tap’s front for a smooth, seamless transaction.
 Tap also enables entrepreneurs to seamlessly unify their online and in-person sales channels. By integrating physical payments into Mollie's powerful platform, business owners can manage all their transactions in one central dashboard. This simplifies their workflow, reduces the risk of errors, and provides clearer sales insights. The Mollie platform also offers a range of other financial services, including online payments, digital invoices, payment links, and business financing.
Simple, flexible pricing
Business owners can choose the pricing model that best fits their needs. The terminal is available from just €45. For businesses with higher transaction volumes, a subscription of €20 per month reduces the transaction rate significantly. Alternatively, businesses can opt for a one-off purchase price of €95, with no monthly fees and a clear, competitive transaction rate per payment.
 Here’s Koen Köppen, Mollie CEO, on Tap: "Many small business owners are looking for a payment solution that does not hinder their work, but rather makes it easier. However, many payment terminals are expensive or complicated, so business owners are currently still struggling with card payments. With Tap, we show that it can be done differently. The terminal is easy to use, similar to a smartphone, affordable and ready to use within 30 seconds. Moreover, Tap works anytime, anywhere, allowing business owners to grow their businesses both online and offline, and strengthen their omnichannel strategy.
