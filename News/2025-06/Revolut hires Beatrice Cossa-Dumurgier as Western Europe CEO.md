[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇫🇷 Revolut hires Béatrice Cossa-Dumurgier as Western Europe CEO. As part of her new role, Béatrice will oversee the development of Revolut’s activities in Western Europe, with a key focus on securing the company’s banking licence from the French banking regulator, the Autorité de Contrôle Prudentiel et de Résolution (ACPR), and the European Central Bank (ECB).
