Jupiter Partners with CSB Bank to Launch High-Reward RuPay Credit Card

 Jupiter and CSB Bank launch Edge+ RuPay Credit Card with no annual fee and  Rs 50,000+ in yearly rewards. 

 The card blends UPI functionality with traditional credit features for a seamless experience. 

 Offers 10% on Amazon, Myntra, Nykaa; 5% on travel; and 1% on most other spends. 

 Bangalore-based fintech firm Jupiter has partnered with CSB Bank to launch the Edge+ CSB Bank RuPay Credit Card, the company calls its most rewarding card so far. With zero annual fee and a rewards program that provides benefits in excess of  Rs 50,000 per year, the card promises to merge the convenience of UPI with the benefits of legacy credit cards.

 Exclusively accessed on the Jupiter app, the Edge+ card is aimed at digital-first, reward-smart consumers. It comes with seamless UPI functionality and provides users with financial information to better manage their money.

 It's not another credit card. Edge+ is the organic extension of Jupiter's philosophy simple, powerful, and rewarding money experiences," said Jitendra Gupta, Founder and CEO of Jupiter Money. He further noted that the new card will enrich customer interaction by providing high-value rewards for frequent spends.

 Rohit K Pandey, President of Jupiter Money, emphasized that the card has been designed to offer real value to consumers. “We saw an opportunity to create India’s leading rewards program one that isn’t built on gimmicks, but on real benefits for brands users already love like Amazon, Myntra, Nykaa, MakeMyTrip, and more,” he said. “By combining this with Jupiter’s intelligence and design approach, we’ve reimagined the credit card experience from the ground up.”

 The Edge+ card users will get 10% cashback at purchases made through popular shopping sites such as Amazon, Myntra, and Nykaa. Bookings made through MakeMyTrip and Yatra will get 5% cashback, while the majority of other online and offline spends will get 1% cashback. The rewards so earned can be redeemed through a host of flexible options ranging from cash to statement credit, bill payments, and even digital gold.

 Established in 2019, Jupiter has established itself rapidly within India's fintech landscape by providing digital banking products such as debit cards, UPI transactions, SIPs, mutual funds, customized savings plans, and expense management tools all centered around ease of use and transparency. The Edge+ card launch is another milestone moment for Jupiter's vision of revolutionizing how Indians perceive and manage money.

 If you are wondering what is cashback credit card, think of it as a specialized financial tool that provides a "rebate" on your purchases. Unlike traditional reward points that can be confusing to value, a cashback credit card returns a percentage of your transaction value directly back to you. This value is usually credited as brand vouchers, digital gold, or even direct cash. For example, with the Edge+ card, these rewards are called "Jewels," which you can redeem anytime through the Jupiter app for maximum flexibility.
 

 Yes, several banks offer these, but it is important to check the fine print. A "lifetime free" card means you never pay a joining fee or an annual renewal fee, regardless of how much you spend. Many of the best cash back credit cards in the market require you to meet a minimum annual spend to waive the fee. Currently, the Jupiter Edge+ card is offered as a lifetime-free cashback credit card, ensuring you get the best cash back credit card in India without the burden of recurring maintenance costs.
 

 Day to day payments are often excluded from high-reward tiers by most banks. However, because the Edge+ card is crafted specifically for the UPI ecosystem, it allows you to earn rewards on these daily essentials more effectively. By using the Edge+ card for your UPI transactions, you get a flat 1% back on (eligible) UPI spends. This consistent reward structure makes it a top contender for the best cashback credit card India has for managing monthly bills, recharges, and small-ticket daily expenses.
 

 Most of the best cash back credit cards implement "capping" to keep the reward program sustainable. These caps are usually monthly limits on how much total cashback you can earn or specific limits on high-reward categories (like 50% Jewels on shopping ). The key to choosing the best cashback credit card is finding one with high enough caps that they don't interfere with your regular spending habits.
 

 Jupiter provides total transparency here, allowing you to track your real-time earnings and see your remaining limits directly on the app's home screen. You can earn unlimited 25% Jewels on Jupiter Flights (5000 Jewels/ Cycle) and 5% Jewels on all eligible spends. For accelerated categories, you can earn up to ₹2,000 monthly on shopping (10% rate).
 

 While we aim to reward as many transactions as possible, standard industry exclusions usually apply to all best cash back credit cards. Common exclusions include ATM cash withdrawals, rental payments, wallet loads, and insurance premiums. It is also worth noting that while fuel spends don't usually earn cashback, the Edge+ card provides a 1% fuel surcharge waiver to ensure you still save at the pump. You can always find the most updated list of eligible and excluded categories within the "Terms and Conditions" section of your Jupiter app.
