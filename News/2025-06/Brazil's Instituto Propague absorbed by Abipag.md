# Brazil's Instituto Propague absorbed by Abipag

> [!info] 2025-06-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Instituto Propague, the Brazilian payments policy research institute, has been formally absorbed by Abipag, the Brazilian national association of payment institutions. Propague will continue operating as a research and education hub focused on financial inclusion, open banking, and regulation. No further of the transaction was disclosed.
