# PhonePe, HDFC Bank launch co-branded RuPay credit card with UPI integration

## The card is available in two variants—‘Ultimo’ and ‘UNO’. Both are designed to offer rewards across daily spending categories, with added convenience of UPI-based transactions using credit.

2 Min Read

[](https://www.google.com/preferences/source?q=cnbctv18.com "CNBCTV18 on Google")[](https://api.whatsapp.com/send?text=PhonePe%2C%20HDFC%20Bank%20launch%20co-branded%20RuPay%20credit%20card%20with%20UPI%20integration%20https%3A%2F%2Fwww.cnbctv18.com%2Fpersonal-finance%2Fphonepe-hdfc-bank-launch-co-branded-rupay-credit-card-with-upi-integration-19627249.htm "PhonePe, HDFC Bank launch co-branded RuPay credit card with UPI integration")

PhonePe and HDFC Bank have jointly launched the ‘PhonePe HDFC Bank Co-branded Credit Card’ on the RuPay network. This marks PhonePe’s entry into the co-branded credit card space.

 The card is aimed at digital-first consumers and offers UPI-linked credit payments.

 The card is available in two variants—‘Ultimo’ and ‘UNO’. Both are designed to offer rewards across daily spending categories, with added convenience of UPI-based transactions using credit.

**ALSO READ | Credit Card spending rises 15% YoY in May, driven by travel and e-commerce**

 The ‘Ultimo’ variant includes:

*   10% reward points on spends made through the PhonePe app, including bill payments, recharges, travel bookings, and purchases on Pincode (PhonePe’s hyperlocal delivery platform)

*   5% reward points on spends with select online merchants

*   1% reward points on UPI ‘Scan & Pay’ transactions

*   Two complimentary domestic airport lounge accesses per quarter

 The card integrates directly with UPI, allowing users to make payments using credit at millions of merchants accepting UPI QR codes. Cardholders can manage their card, track spends, and pay bills through the PhonePe app.

 Eligible users can apply for the card digitally within the PhonePe app. HDFC Bank will issue the card and enable UPI linkage upon approval. The rollout will take place in phases.

 The launch combines HDFC Bank’s credit issuance capabilities with PhonePe’s digital reach. It aims to widen credit card access among digital-savvy users and promote UPI-based credit usage.

 As of April 2025, PhonePe has over 61 crore registered users and a merchant acceptance network of over 4 crore outlets. The platform handles more than 34 crore daily transactions with an annualised total payment value (TPV) exceeding ₹150 lakh crore.
