## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] First Financial BancorpacquiredWestfield Bancorp (parent of Westfield Bank) in a $325 million cash-and-stock deal—paying $260 million in cash and issuing 2.75 million of its shares. The acquisition added around 12% to earnings and offered a 2.9‑year tangible book value earn-back.
