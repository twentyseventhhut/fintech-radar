# Kraken launches Bitcoin staking via Babylon protocol

> [!info] 2025-06-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Kraken launches Bitcoin staking through the Babylon Bitcoin staking protocol.
