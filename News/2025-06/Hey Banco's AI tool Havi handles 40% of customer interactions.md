# Hey Banco's AI tool Havi handles 40% of customer interactions

> [!info] 2025-06-15 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Hey Banco, the Mexican former Banregio fintech branch, revealed that their AI tool “Havi” handles 40% of customer interactions, with plans to expand its use and embed AI into everyday financial management. The bank is also developing a financial education feature to help clients understand their finances.
