SPAC 1RT Acquisition files for a $150 million IPO, targeting digital assets and blockchain

 1RT Acquisition, a blank check company formed by 1RoundTable Partners targeting digital assets and blockchain, filed on Wednesday with the SEC to raise up to $150 million in an initial public offering. The company plans to raise $150 million by offering 15 million units at $10. Each unit consists of one share of common stock and one-quarter of a warrant, exercisable at $11.50. 1RT Acquisition is led by CEO and Chairman Dan Tapiero , the Founding Partner, CIO, and CEO of both private equity firms 1RoundTable Partners and 10T Holdings, both of which are affiliates of the sponsor. It plans to target the digital assets and blockchain space, focusing on businesses with enterprise values $1 billion or more. The New York, NY-based company was founded in 2024 and plans to list on the Nasdaq under the symbol ONCHU . 1RT Acquisition filed confidentially on February 11, 2025. Cantor Fitzgerald is the sole bookrunner on the deal.

1RT Acquisition Corp. (ONCHU) Prices $150M IPO
 1RT Acquisition Corp. (NASDAQ:ONCHU) announced the pricing of its $150 million IPO and its units are expected to begin trading on the Nasdaq under the symbol “ONCHU”, Wednesday, July 2, 2025.
The new SPAC plans to target company in the digital asset ecosystem involved in the creation, storage, exchange or management of such assets, whether cryptocurrencies, NFTs or other decentralized finance protocols.
1RT’s management team is led by Chief Executive Officer and Chairman Dan Tapiero and Chief Financial Officer Joe Majocha. The Board also includes Jeffrey Nuechterlein, Eric Vincent, Jeffrey Blockinger, and Matty Frymier.
Total SPAC deal count for 2025 year-to-date is now 67. This offering is expected to close on Thursday, July 3, 2025.
 Cantor Fitzgerald & Co. acted as the sole book-running manager of the offering. Roberts & Ryan, Inc. acted as co-manager of the offering. Wilkie Farr & Gallagher LLP is serving as Issuer’s Counsel with Ellenoff, Grossman & Schole LLP serving as Underwriter’s Counsel. WithumSmith+Brown PC serves as auditor. Continental Stock Transfer & Trust Company is acting as trustee.
