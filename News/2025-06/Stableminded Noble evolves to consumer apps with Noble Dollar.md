# Stableminded: Noble evolves to consumer apps with Noble Dollar

> [!info] 2025-06-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] During the conversation, Jelena reveals Noble's evolution from pure infrastructure to consumer-facing applications. "We quickly realized it's really important to also actually be user facing and have experiences," she explains. This maturation led to Noble Dollar (USDN), built on M0's wholesale layer, which demonstrates their "composable yield" innovation—allowing apps to monetize or redistribute Treasury bill yields instead of charging fees.
