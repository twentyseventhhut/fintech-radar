Users can now seamlessly stake, pay, and transfer on the Solana blockchain, expanding Revolut’s offerings beyond basic crypto trading.

Dec. 3, 2025

Revolut, a digital banking platform that provides cryptocurrency trading services, today integrated Solana support for payments, transfers, and staking directly within its app.

The integration enables Revolut users to send, receive, or pay via Solana using USDT, USDC, and SOL while accessing the blockchain’s payment and transfer capabilities. Users can also participate in Solana staking through the platform.

Solana is a high-speed blockchain designed for scalable, decentralized applications, now supported alongside the wide range of crypto assets already tradable within Revolut’s banking app.

## Uniswap Labs integrates Revolut for seamless crypto purchases

Uniswap Labs integrates Revolut for crypto onboarding, allowing users to buy digital assets directly via mobile wallet and web app.

Dec. 2, 2025

Uniswap Labs integrated Revolut to enable direct crypto purchases through both its mobile wallet and web application. The integration allows users in supported countries to buy digital assets using their Revolut balances or debit cards.

The partnership streamlines the process of purchasing crypto assets like ETH and USDC directly within Uniswap’s platforms, eliminating the need for users to transfer funds from external sources before trading.

Uniswap Wallet provides mobile access to cryptocurrency management and trading across multiple blockchains. The web application serves as a browser-based interface for swapping cryptocurrencies on decentralized protocols.
