# valePay launches B2B travel consolidator Opere

> [!info] 2025-06-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 valePay, the Brazilian travel-focused digital banking platform, announced the launch of Opere, a B2B terrestrial travel consolidator offering NET pricing to over 3,000 agencies. The platform features 2M+ hotel, car rental, insurance, and ticketing options, enabling agencies to set margins and receive commissions within two business days.
