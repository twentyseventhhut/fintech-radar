## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) directed Unified Payments Interface (UPI) app providers to reduce transaction processing time from 30 seconds to 10-15 seconds, which took effect from June 16, 2025.
