# Jio Financial buys SBI stake to fully own Jio Payments Bank

> [!info] 2025-06-20 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Jio Financial Services takes full control of Jio Payments Bank with Rs 104.5 crore SBI stake buy. This follows RBI approval and coincides with Jio BlackRock's launch of mutual fund schemes, aiming to offer innovative investment products. The company reported a slight increase in net profit and revenue for Q4 FY25.
