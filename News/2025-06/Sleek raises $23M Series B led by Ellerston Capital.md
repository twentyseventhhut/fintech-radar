Digital corporate services provider Sleek has secured US$23 million in a Series B funding round led by Australian investment firm Ellerston Capital, with additional backing from SaaS company Money Forward.

Founded in 2017, Sleek offers a suite of digital solutions, including company incorporation, accounting, payroll, and tax compliance, tailored for SMEs.

The platform currently supports over 16,000 SMEs annually across Singapore, Hong Kong, the UK, and Australia.

The fresh capital will be used to advance Sleek’s automation-driven technology and broaden its digital service offerings.

[](https://lseg.group/49KObaI)

The company also aims to deepen its footprint in Australia and the UK, while continuing to strengthen its core markets in Singapore and Hong Kong.
