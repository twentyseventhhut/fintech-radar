# eToro UK debit card offers 4% back in stocks

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 eToro’s aggressive debit card deal, UK customers can get 4% back in stocks. According to the eToro Money website, the platform will allow debit card holders to earn up to £1,500 in stocks as monthly cashback rewards. They can either sell the awarded stocks or keep them as investments.
