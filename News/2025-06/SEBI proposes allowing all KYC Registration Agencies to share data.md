# SEBI proposes allowing all KYC Registration Agencies to share data

> [!info] 2025-06-21 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) proposed allowing all KYC Registration Agencies (KRAs) to function as accreditation agencies and enabling Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) to provisionally onboard investors, pending public consultation until July 8, 2025.
