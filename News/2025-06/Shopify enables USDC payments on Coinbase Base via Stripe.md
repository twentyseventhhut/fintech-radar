# Powering the future of ecommerce: Introducing Coinbase Payments

By Justin Gainsley Jun 18, 2025

**TL;DR:**_Coinbase Payments is a stablecoin payments stack designed for commerce platforms. Offer instant, 24/7 USDC payments to your merchants –globally, securely, and without blockchain complexity. Built on Base and already live with Shopify, it’s the fastest way for payment service providers (PSPs), marketplaces, and commerce infra providers to bring stablecoin payments to market._

* * *

## Commerce is changing

Stablecoins are reshaping global commerce, and Coinbase is leading the charge with a major move into payments. Stablecoins processed $30 trillion in settlements last year –3x growth year-over-year. They’re a faster, cheaper, more global way to move money – and the world is catching on.

More than half of the Fortune 500 is building onchain, and a third of small businesses already use crypto. And yet, despite growing demand, stablecoin payments remain out of reach for most platforms – held back by fragmented tooling, technical overhead, and a lack of production-ready infrastructure. Coinbase Payments changes that.

## Coinbase Payments: Stablecoin payments without the complexity

Coinbase Payments is the first full-stack stablecoin payment solution built for commerce platforms at scale. It abstracts away the blockchain so you can offer crypto-native payments without needing crypto-native teams. We built it to power seamless USDC payments with faster settlement, lower fees, and global reach.

This high-performance infrastructure is now live with Shopify, where we’re bringing USDC payments to millions of merchants with no additional setup required. Now, that same infrastructure is available to any platform that serves merchants.

## The Stack

Coinbase Payments is a modular stack to add stablecoin payments to your existing systems without friction.

### 1. Stablecoin Checkout: The consumer interaction layer

A fast, wallet-native checkout for USDC payments across hundreds of wallets like Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask, and Phantom. Optimized for conversion, it delivers a seamless, gasless user experience.

### 2. Ecommerce Engine: The merchant interaction layer

The API layer for merchant platforms and PSPs. Handles authorization, capture, refunds, ledgering, subscriptions, and key management –so you can offer stablecoin support without blockchain expertise.

### 3. Commerce Payments Protocol: The onchain execution layer

Open-source smart contracts that power secure, scalable onchain transactions –replicating traditional mechanics like escrow, delayed capture, and refunds. Runs on Base for sub-second, low-cost execution. Learn more here.

## How Coinbase Payments works for Shopify

Shopify is integrating Coinbase Payments to unlock 24/7 USDC payments across its ecosystem. Here’s how the stack performs under the hood:

*   **Stablecoin Checkout**delivers a fast, wallet-based payment UX to end customers. 
*   **Ecommerce Engine**lets Shopify manage merchant operations like auth and capture via APIs. 
*   **Commerce Payments Protocol**executes transactions securely onchain, using the Coinbase Developer Platform’s Wallet API to manage smart-contract-based escrow and settlement. 

This enables Shopify merchants to accept USDC payments from a global customer base with no additional setup and at a lower cost.

## Partner with us

Coinbase Payments is built for platforms that serve merchants –PSPs, marketplaces, ecommerce providers.

*   Add USDC support without rebuilding your stack 
*   Launch globally with 24/7, borderless payments 
*   Lower costs across the board 
*   Enable offramps to fiat 
*   Maintain trust with secure, audited onchain execution 
*   Incentivize adoption with programmable rewards (coming soon) 

You get the benefits of stablecoin infrastructure. Your merchants get the simplicity they expect. If you serve merchants and want to offer a faster, cheaper, programmable payment experience, we’d love to work with you. Get in touch.

Stablecoins Are Ready for Global Commerce: Accept USDC on Base With Shopify Payments.
Shopify partners with Coinbase and Stripe to enable fast, secure stablecoin payments from customers around the world.
Stablecoins are reshaping the way money moves around the world. Over the past few years, stablecoins like USDC have grown to over a trillion dollars in monthly transaction volume. But they haven’t seen mass adoption in mainstream commerce—until now. In partnership with Coinbase and Stripe, merchants can now accept stablecoins from customers globally on the Base network, using their existing payment capture and order fulfillment flows; no integrations or gateways required.
 Announcing USDC on Shopify Payments 
Customers can pay with USDC on Base, from hundreds of supported crypto wallets, on guest checkout and on Shop Pay¹.
 Merchants can choose how they want to receive their funds: 
By default, all USDC payments will be converted to the merchant’s local currency, with no foreign exchange or multi-currency fees. Payouts are deposited to their existing bank account connected to Shopify Payments. 
Merchants may alternatively elect to withdraw USDC funds on Base to their own crypto wallet.
 Coming soon, merchants and customers will enjoy further savings from USDC payments: 
Merchants with shops in the US and other select countries will receive a rebate of up to 0.50% on USDC orders¹.
In the United States, customers will receive a further rebate when they pay with USDC, coming later this year. 
 Ready for prime time: why stablecoins’ time has come 
Cryptocurrencies have come a long way since their early days. Three key innovations have made this progress possible: stablecoins, which hold a constant value relative to a currency like the US dollar; networks like Base that offer fast, cheap, and reliable transactions; and wallets that make digital payments effortless.
 Digital dollars, available everywhere  
Unlike cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ether, which have fluctuating values, USDC (issued by Circle) is backed 1:1 by US Dollar reserves, and is designed to maintain a constant value. The amount of USDC in circulation has accelerated faster than any other global stablecoin, growing by more than 78% year-over-year. It is increasingly accepted as an everyday currency. When customers pay in USDC, merchants receive funds in a currency that is secure, reliable, and easily exchangeable. 
 Faster checkouts
