# Multiplier raises $27.5M for AI accounting roll-ups

> [!info] 2025-06-18 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: TechCrunch — Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[TechCrunch — Fintech] Today, Multiplier, which is now called Multiplier Holdings, is announcing that it raised a total of $27.5 million in seed and Series A financing. Lightspeed Venture Partners led the Series A funding round for the startup, following Multiplier’s seed round, which was led by Ribbit Capital with participation from SV Angel.

[This Week in Fintech] Multiplier, founded by an ex-Stripe executive, secured $27.5 million to power AI-driven accounting roll-ups for SMBs.
