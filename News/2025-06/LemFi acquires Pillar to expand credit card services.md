# LemFi acquires Pillar to expand credit card services

> [!info] 2025-06-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] LemFiwas Founded in 2020, the company now serves over two million users globally and processes over $1 billion in monthly transaction volume. It raised $33 Million in a series A round in 2023, and a $53 Million series B round in 2023, giving it the capital base to pursue international acquisitions like this. After acquiring Pillar, which raised ~ $17 Million pre-seed in 2022, LemFi will expand credit card services to its two million users.
