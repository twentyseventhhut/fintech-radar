ABN AMRO launches neobank BUUT 
 About us 
Marieke Ziedses des Plantes
Sr Press Officer Corporate Banking, WM, Personal & BB
 After the summer, ABN AMRO will launch ‘BUUT’. BUUT offers everything you can expect from a bank, but a little bit different, like Tikkie. It is the first bank developed entirely around the world as young people experience it. Together with the Tikkie team, ABN AMRO built BUUT from the ground up in twelve months to create a bank that aligns seamlessly with the digital world inhabited by the new generation.
 ABN AMRO’s payment app Tikkie developed a full-service bank for a new generation in just 12 months 
 Annerie Vreugdenhil, Chief Commercial Officer Personal & Business Banking: “The world is changing, so we are too. There are more possibilities, we want more and we expect more. As a next-gen bank, BUUT understands this. It’s easy to use, interactive, 100% visually driven and totally at home in the world of the new generation. BUUT is everything you expect of a bank, but designed to Tikkie a different box. Built on the Tikkie platform, supported by ABN AMRO’s robust systems, with the fresh look & feel, tone of voice and user experience everyone knows from Tikkie. Developed with Tikkie’s creative drive, in an ABN AMRO environment where innovation, pioneering and entrepreneurship are leading. The result: innovation in record time.” 
 Young people manage their money differently 
 We’ve never had as many payment options as we do now. That opens up opportunities but also makes it harder to keep track, as we learned from a study by Nibud , the Dutch family finance awareness institute. Young people can lose control of their finances, thinking that they have less or more money than is actually the case. A matter of concern, because it is important to learn how to manage a budget at a young age, and realise that ‘when it’s gone, it’s really gone’. “As young people in this phase of life learn to save, keep within a budget and understand the impact of financial choices, BUUT is the solution this generation needs,” says Vreugdenhil. 
 Innovating with an eye to the future 
 Innovating from a foundation of trust, ABN AMRO has already launched several successful apps and brands including Tikkie, New10 and BUX. These innovations highlight the bank’s modern, flexible organisational structure. The bank is now harnessing this innovative power to respond to the changing expectations of new generations, who handle their money differently from previous generations. BUUT is ABN AMRO’s answer to the changing financial world young people live in. 
 BUUT will be available in app stores after the summer. To get updates, be first in line for access or even help test the app in our BUUT community, sign up via buut.com (live from 4 June).

ABN AMRO rolls out neobank BUUT 
Iulia Musat

After the summer of 2025, ABN AMRO is set to roll out BUUT, a neobank focusing on providing solutions that fit the demands and preferences of young people. Together with the Tikkie team, the bank developed BUUT to create a bank that aligns with the digital world inhabited by the new generation. Additionally, ABN AMRO seeks to offer a service that is user-friendly, interactive, as well as visually driven. Built on the Tikkie platform and supported by ABN AMRO’s systems, BUUT aims to be an advanced solution that can adapt to the current financial environment. 
Moreover, with this launch, ABN AMRO intends to support young individuals in managing their finances while also assisting them in understanding the impact of financial choices. Besides this new platform, the financial institution has already rolled out several apps and brands, including Tikkie, New10, and BUX. These moves underline the bank’s focus on creating a modern and flexible organisational structure. Also, ABN AMRO is currently leveraging these capabilities to respond to the evolving expectations of new generations, who manage their money differently from previous ones.
