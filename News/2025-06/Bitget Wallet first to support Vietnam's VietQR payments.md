# Bitget Wallet first to support Vietnam's VietQR payments

> [!info] 2025-06-18 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇻🇳 Bitget Wallet becomes the first crypto wallet to support national Vietnam QR payment, allowing users in the region to scan the VietQR code and directly pay in crypto. The integration makes Bitget Wallet the first self-custodial wallet to natively integrate itself into a country’s national QR code payment system.
