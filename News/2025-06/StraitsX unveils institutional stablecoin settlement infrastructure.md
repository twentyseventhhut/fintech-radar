Introducing DVA/+: Institutional-grade USD virtual accounts for fiat-stablecoin settlement
As stablecoins shift from the fringes of speculation to the heart of real-world value exchange, institutions need better infrastructure to operate at scale, without compromising compliance or control.
 What is DVA/+? 
 DVA/+ (Dedicated Virtual Accounts) is a next-generation solution that delivers:
 Named USD or SGD virtual accounts under your legal entity 
 Full COBO and POBO support to collect or pay on behalf of clients 
 Native stablecoin integration , starting with StraitsX’s USD-pegged stablecoin, XUSD 
 Real-time treasury visibility and programmable settlement flows 
 API-first design for seamless automation and integration 
Today, DVA/+ is offered across two options, tailored by risk profile but built on the same core infrastructure.
 Who is DVA/+ built for? 
DVA/+ is purpose-built for institutions operating at the intersection of fiat and stablecoins:
 Exchanges that need secure fiat rails without maintaining banking stacks 
 Wallets and custodians that want to automate fiat-stablecoin flows 
 Fintechs and payment platforms supporting Web3 clients and global treasury ops 
 Market makers and OTC desks seeking programmable liquidity across jurisdictions 
With DVA/+, institutions can streamline operations, meet regulatory expectations, and access stablecoin-native settlement rails with speed and security.
 Why now? 
Regulators worldwide are tightening oversight on fiat-backed stablecoins. Market participants are demanding greater transparency and operational scale. The digital asset ecosystem is maturing fast, and the infrastructure must keep up.
Our introduction of DVA/+ also reflects the next phase of StraitsX’s evolution: from a stablecoin issuer to a full-stack infrastructure partner for real-world digital finance.
