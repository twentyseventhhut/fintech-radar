# Nubank and Pibank disrupt Colombian credit market

> [!info] 2025-06-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Nubank and Pibank are disrupting traditional Colombian banking in the credit and debit card segment. Nubank has entered the top tier of savings account providers, offering high interest rates up to around 11–13 % E.A. Meanwhile, Pibank has surged into the market with savings accounts yielding approximately 12 % E.A.
