# Spain blocks BBVA's hostile takeover merger of Sabadell

> [!info] 2025-06-29 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria (BBVA)launched a hostile takeover of Banco de Sabadell, but the Spanish government blocked a legal merger for at least three—and up to five—years, requiring both banks to remain legally separate during that period.
