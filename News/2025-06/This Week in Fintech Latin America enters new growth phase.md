## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Latin America is entering a new growth phase, marked by resilient investment and a maturing tech ecosystem. While funding has moderated since the 2021 peak of $15 billion, levels remain well above pre-2021. In 2024, funding rounds surpassed previous counts, with strong early-stage activity and capital increasingly concentrated in later stages—signaling greater support for scaling startups. (Source).
