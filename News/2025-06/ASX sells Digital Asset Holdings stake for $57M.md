# ASX sells Digital Asset Holdings stake for $57M

> [!info] 2025-06-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 ASX sells out of Digital Asset Holdings for $57m. ASX announced it had completed the sale of its shareholding in Digital Asset Holdings, which will be recognised in its FY25 financial results. The sale represents a pre-tax gain of about $42 million. The gain will be recognised in the asset revaluation reserve in equity.
