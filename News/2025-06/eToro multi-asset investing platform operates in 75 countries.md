# eToro multi-asset investing platform operates in 75 countries

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] eToro is a multi-asset retail investing platform operating in 75 countries, with key markets being the U.K., the E.U., and Asia Pacific. Founded in 2007, eToro pioneered the concept of social investing. While only 3.6 million users have funded accounts, 38 million engage with its social feed and educational tools. You can think of eToro as X, but with built-in stock and crypto trading.
