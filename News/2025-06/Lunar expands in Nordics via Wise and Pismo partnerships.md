Lunar partners with Wise Platform, marking expansion into the Nordics
Lunar becomes Wise Platform's first partner in Denmark, offering seamless international payments directly within the Lunar-app. Over one million Lunar users can now send money internationally to more than 160 countries in over 40 currencies.
Today, Lunar announces a new partnership with Wise Platform - Wise’s global payment infrastructure for banks and global organisations - to bring fast, secure, and transparent international payments to over one million users across the region. The partnership builds on a successful relationship, with Wise already leveraging Moonrise, Lunar’s modern banking infrastructure platform, for seamless local clearing and payments across Scandinavia.
This next phase of the partnership brings Wise’s white-labelled solution to Lunar’s private and business users in Denmark, Sweden, and Norway, allowing them to send money abroad quickly, securely, in 40 currencies, within their app - in all at low cost. This experience is powered by Wise’s network that connects directly to 6 payment systems and 70+ licenses around the world, enabling 65% of transfers to arrive instantly - in 20 seconds or less*.
With this partnership, Lunar becomes Wise Platform’s first partner in Denmark and one of the Nordics' leading digital banks to offer this experience.
 Ken Villum Klausen, CEO and Founder of Lunar, said: 
“We’re happy to extend our collaboration with Wise by integrating their market-leading international payment infrastructure to facilitate faster and more transparent cross-border payments. This means our users can send money abroad easily, quickly, and at a fair exchange rate. It’s another step toward becoming the best everyday bank in the Nordics and a great example of how smart fintech partnerships can simplify banking for everyone.”
 Manuel Sandhofer, General Manager, EMEA, Wise Platform, said: 
“Digital banks, like Lunar, are setting a new standard for what customers expect from their bank - speed, transparency and convenience. This is especially true when it comes to moving money across borders. At Wise Platform, our mission is to power this experience and we’re proud to be partnering with Lunar to enable seamless international payments for more people and businesses across the Nordics.”
Wise Platform’s partnership with Lunar highlights the rising demand for faster, more transparent alternatives to traditional cross-border correspondents and the trust digital banks place in Wise to deliver that to their users. Lunar joins a growing list of top-tier digital banks globally already using Wise Platform to power seamless international payments for their users.
 The product is now available to customers in Denmark and will be rolled out across Sweden and Norway in the coming weeks. 
 *Transaction speed depends on individual circumstances and may not be available for all transactions.

 Lunar Goes Cloud-Native: First Nordic Bank to Process Visa Cards on Pismo 
 Cloud
 18.06.2025 10:25 am 
 Visa, a world leader in digital payments, and Pismo today jointly announced that the Nordic challenger bank Lunar will start Visa card processing with Pismo’s platform as the first bank in the Nordics.  The move to Pismo enables Lunar to develop and launch new products and solutions faster and more independently for their over one million private and business users across Denmark, Sweden and Norway. 
 Pismo’s technology makes it possible for customers like Lunar to launch innovative payments and banking products within a single cloud-native platform, regardless of network, geography or currency. Pismo also provides easy access to Visa’s suite of  Value-Added Services , from its trusted, AI-powered fraud fighting capabilities to state-of-the-art digital user experience. 
 ”Core banking and issuer processor capabilities are key offerings for many of Visa’s financial institutions and fintech partners. With Pismo under our wings, we have been able to broaden these offerings and serve the whole ecosystem even better as a result. We are very pleased to announce this deal with such an innovative, well established and successful challenger bank as Lunar,” says Claus Richter, Country Manager for Visa in Denmark. 
 Lunar was founded in 2015 as a digital alternative to traditional banks. With a Danish banking license since 2019, Lunar now also operates in Norway and Sweden and serves over one million private and business users across the Nordics. 
 “Switching to Pismo means we can move faster and deliver better experiences to our users across the Nordics. It allows us to accelerate service delivery, enhance the way our users interact with payments, and reduce our dependency on legacy systems. This partnership gives us the flexibility to build and launch new products independently, driven by the speed, simplicity, and control we believe modern banking should offer,” says Ken Villlum Klausen, CEO and founder of Lunar. 
 Vishal Dalal, CEO at Pismo, says, “Speed is a key market differentiator. By adopting Pismo to process its Visa cards, Lunar will deliver a secure, seamless, and cost-effective credit card experience, while also gaining the flexibility to develop innovative features that enhance customer engagement and set the brand apart.”
