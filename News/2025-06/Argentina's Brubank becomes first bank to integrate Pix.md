# Argentina's Brubank becomes first bank to integrate Pix

> [!info] 2025-06-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇦🇷Brubank, the digital bank in Argentina, became the country’s first and only bank to integrate Pix, Brazil’s instant payment system. This strategic move aims to simplify cross-border transactions for users and businesses operating between Argentina and Brazil, boosting regional financial inclusion and interoperability.
