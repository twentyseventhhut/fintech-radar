Aevi and Getnet Launch Global Partnership 
Latin America
Partnership
Global Partnership to enable international expansion for Enterprise Merchants and ISVs. First deployment in Mexico showcases a scalable global–local payments model combining orchestration and local acquiring expertise.
 Aevi, a leading provider of in-person payment orchestration, and Getnet, a global payment solutions provider and top 10 acquirer worldwide, today announced a global partnership aimed at simplifying international expansion for enterprise merchants and global ISVs.
The partnership introduces a scalable global–local payments model that removes one of the biggest barriers to cross-border growth: the need for local payment re-integration work in each new market. Through a single integration, existing Aevi clients and partners can access local acquiring in Mexico via Getnet, eliminating the need for multiple local integrations. 
Mexico marks the first deployment of this partnership, serving as a blueprint for further international rollout.
“Our strength lies in combining global capabilities with deep expertise across Latin America. Starting with Mexico, this partnership with Aevi allows us to extend that to Aevi’s existing clients and partners—enabling them to scale without the usual complexity of payments integration.”
 Fabrice Mendez , Head of Partnerships at Getnet. 
“Aevi is designed to remove the friction of managing payments across multiple markets,” said Victor Padee, Chief Revenue Officer at Aevi . “We’re starting with Mexico, but the partnership with Getnet is built for scale, combining strong in-market capabilities with regional reach and global expertise. This allows our clients to expand through a single integration model that can be replicated across markets.”
 
Aevi provides a multi-acquirer, omnichannel orchestration layer, while Getnet complements this with regional and local expertise including domestic schemes, regulatory alignment, and market-specific capabilities such as instalments.
 Key benefit s include:
Faster market entry
Lower integration costs
Reduced operational complexity
Scalable ISV deployment
 
 This global partnership reinforces both companies’ commitment to enabling frictionless commerce across borders.
