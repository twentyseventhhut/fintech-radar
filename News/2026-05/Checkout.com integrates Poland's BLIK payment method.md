Checkout.com deepens European reach with BLIK

 BLIK is one of Europe’s most popular payment methods, trusted by over 20 million users for fast, flexible, and secure payments. 
 BLIK, Twint, Swish, and Vipps MobilePay are the latest local payment methods to join Checkout.com’s growing European network. 
 London, UK - May 28, 2026 - Checkout.com, a leading global digital payments company, today announced its expanding presence in Europe by becoming a direct scheme member and official partner of BLIK, Poland’s leading mobile payment system.
This partnership positions Checkout.com as a key provider for merchants targeting Poland and the wider Central European region. With a direct integration to BLIK, Checkout.com can offer merchants sharper pricing, stronger performance, and access to a high converting customer experience.
Used by over 20 million people, BLIK is one of Poland’s most popular ways to pay. Shoppers generate a six-digit code in their banking app, enter it at checkout, and confirm the payment in-app. With instant confirmation and bank-grade security, the flow is fast, secure, and built to convert.
Checkout.com also supports BLIK's new recurring payments, which began rolling out across Polish banks in 2026. The partnership helps cement BLIK’s role in everyday spending, making it easier for consumers to pay for subscriptions, utilities, and more.
‍ Adam Kokoszkiewicz, Sales Department Director at BLIK, comments: “We’re excited to partner with Checkout.com to bring BLIK to a wider network of international merchants. This collaboration ensures that Polish consumers can pay with the method they know and trust, while giving merchants access to a high-converting flow built for the local market. Together, we’re shaping the future of payments in Central Europe.” 
 Matthieu Barral, Head of Global Partnerships at Checkout.com, comments: “Our approach to expanding payment method coverage is grounded in merchant demand and real-world usage. BLIK is a clear example of that, as it’s trusted by millions in Poland and central to the country’s digital economy. By integrating directly, we give our merchants better economics, improved reliability, and a local checkout experience that drives conversion. With BLIK Recurring Payments coming soon, we’re enabling new use cases that unlock long-term value in this high-growth market.” 
BLIK, alongside Twint, Swish, and Vipps MobilePay are the latest additions to Checkout.com’s European network of local payment methods. They form part of Checkout.com’s broader global network, which now includes over 40 payment methods worldwide, giving merchants access to the ways consumers actually choose to pay. 
Every integration is guided by customer behaviour, merchant demand, market trends, and performance data to ensure impact. Checkout.com continues to work closely with merchants to fine-tune their payment method mix, aligning local preferences with business goals to unlock growth where it matters most.
