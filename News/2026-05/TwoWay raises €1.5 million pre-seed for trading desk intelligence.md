TwoWay Raises €1.5M Pre-seed
TwoWay, a London, UK-based provider of institutional trading intelligence software for foreign exchange (FX) and fixed-income markets, has raised €1.5 million in a pre-seed funding round.
Investors
The funding round was led by welovefounders, with participation from Tenity, Plug and Play, SuperCapital, and Unorthodox Partners.
TwoWay Use of Funds
TwoWay plans to use the capital to expand its pricing models, grow deployments across Europe and the UK, and add support for rates derivatives, credit, and commodities markets.
About TwoWay
Led by CEO Chirine BenZaied-Bourgerie, TwoWay is a financial technology company developing institutional trading intelligence software for foreign exchange, fixed income, and over-the-counter (OTC) markets. The company helps banks, asset managers, and trading firms transform unstructured broker communications into structured, real-time pricing intelligence that enhances price discovery, market visibility, and trading performance. Designed to integrate seamlessly with existing trading infrastructure, TwoWay connects broker messaging platforms, market data feeds, and internal pricing systems to automate the collection, normalization, and analysis of market information
