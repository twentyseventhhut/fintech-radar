MENA DIGITAL COMMERCE 2026
In this exclusive report, we explore the rapid growth of digital commerce across MENA. Shoppers here are showing a stronger openness to AI-driven experiences, so expectations around convenience and security are evolving fast. This report looks at the trends shaping the future of ecommerce in MENA, and how you can make the most of the surge in online spending. 













Top insights in this report
97% say it's important to have an invisible payment process online
63% expect to shop online more in the next 12 months 
64% use digital wallets at least monthly for buying, budgeting, and managing finances
50% of consumers are willing to save their card details to reduce friction
62% say a safe and secure payment process is important when buying online
50% of consumers say they are comfortable to let an AI agent shop on their behalf
