# Samsung integrates Wallet with qlub for contactless bill payments

> [!info] 2026-05-26 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇪 Samsung Gulf Electronics has integrated Samsung Wallet with qlub to enable faster contactless bill payments at restaurants and hospitality venues. Galaxy users can scan a QR code, review their bill, and pay directly from their devices, with transactions secured through tokenisation and Samsung Knox technology.
