# MoonPay launches Headless Onramps for one-tap crypto checkout

We're excited to announce the launch of MoonPay Headless Onramps, the first native crypto checkout platform to deliver one tap purchases via Apple Pay, cards, and Google Pay across the US, EEA, and 100+ countries.
The ramp industry is undergoing a structural shift. Crypto applications with meaningful scale are moving away from hosted, branded checkout flows toward fully embedded experiences where they control the entire user journey. MoonPay Headless is built for exactly that. Partners own the checkout experience. MoonPay handles the payment rails, compliance, and identity verification underneath.
Any wallet, exchange, or application can now offer a checkout that looks and feels like their own product, with MoonPay's infrastructure running invisibly in the background. No MoonPay branding, no redirects, no breaks in the user experience.
Initial launch partners include Moonshot, Bitcoin.com, Bread, and Trust Wallet.
Getting onchain is nearly instantaneous. Apple Pay, the highest converting payment method in mobile commerce, can now be fully embedded inside partner applications for the first time. For verified users, a single tap through Apple Pay completes the purchase entirely inside the partner's app. No redirect. No re-authentication. One tap, done.
The platform launches with Apple Pay, card payments, and Google Pay, available globally across the US and EEA. For new users, a lightweight onboarding frame opens inline within the partner's app. Once a user completes that flow, every subsequent visit is frictionless.
While other headless onramp solutions remain limited to mobile payments in the US only, MoonPay Headless delivers native checkout across multiple payment methods and geographies from day one.
"The best payment experience is one the user never thinks about," said Ivan Soto-Wright, Co-Founder and CEO of MoonPay. "Headless Onramps give our partners full control over how their users buy crypto, while MoonPay powers everything behind the scenes. This is what infrastructure should look like: invisible, global, and instant."
MoonPay Headless Onramps is available now. To learn more or integrate, visit https://www.moonpay.com/business/ramps
