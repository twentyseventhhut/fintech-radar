# Shopify pursues US money transmitter licenses

> [!info] 2026-05-01 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Shopify is pursuing U.S. money transmitter licenses, already approved in 18 states and Puerto Rico, as it explores prepaid services. The move could reduce reliance on partners like Stripe, lower fees, and expand merchant financial tools while still requiring bank partnerships.

[This Week in Fintech] Shopifyhas been quietly adding new money transmitter licenses to expand its fintech footprint and gain more control over the payment processing services it offers merchants.
