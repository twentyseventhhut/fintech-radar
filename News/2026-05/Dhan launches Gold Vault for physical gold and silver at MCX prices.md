Frequently Asked Questions 
What is Gold Vault by Dhan?
Gold Vault by Dhan allows you to buy real gold and silver at live MCX prices, with secure vault storage and the option to take physical delivery anytime.
How is Gold Vault different from digital gold?
Unlike digital gold, Gold Vault is exchange-backed with transparent MCX pricing, no hidden spreads, and assured purity through regulated settlement.
What happens if I sell the same gold futures contract outside Gold Vault?
If you place a sell trade in the same contract as your Gold Vault position through the normal trading flow, your Gold Vault order will be cancelled. This happens because the sell creates a short position, and no physical delivery of gold will take place.
Where is my gold stored?
Your gold is securely stored in exchange-regulated vaults, ensuring institutional-grade safety without the need for personal storage.
Can I take physical delivery of my gold?
Yes, you can request doorstep delivery of your gold anytime, converting your holdings into physical form. You just have to initiate delivery from the Dhan app. 
What happens if I don't maintain the full delivery value in my account?
If the required full delivery value is not maintained before the staggered delivery period begins, your position will be squared off and the bullion purchase order will be cancelled.
When can I request home delivery of my gold?
You can request home delivery once your futures contract is successfully settled and the bullion is moved to the vault. After that, your gold is securely stored and can be delivered anytime you choose.
