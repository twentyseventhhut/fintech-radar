# Erada Finance and AgriCash launch digital loans for farmers

Erada Finance, a leading provider of financing solutions for micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs), has announced a strategic partnership with AgriCash, an agritech company specialized in developing integrated digital solutions for serving and financing the agricultural sector.
The partnership aims to introduce an innovative model that connects financing with agricultural services within a fully integrated digital ecosystem, contributing to the development of the agricultural sector and the promotion of financial inclusion.
 Company Strategy 
The partnership comes as part of Erada Finance’s strategy to expand financing for vital productive sectors, particularly agriculture, by adopting innovative financial solutions that go beyond traditional lending models. The company is focused on building strategic partnerships with specialized agritech and digital finance players to develop more flexible financing ecosystems tailored to the actual needs of customers.
 Empowering Farmers 
The new partnership introduces a different approach centered on empowering farmers rather than simply providing financing. Through the AgriCash platform, agricultural technology is leveraged to design flexible and innovative solutions that suit the nature of agricultural activities, while directly addressing key sector challenges, including limited access to traditional financing and rising production input costs.
The initiative targets various segments within the agricultural sector, including small and medium-sized farmers across different governorates, agricultural supply chain stakeholders, suppliers and distributors of agricultural inputs, as well as SMEs operating in the sector. This integrated approach supports agricultural value chains and enhances production efficiency and sustainability.
 Financing Solutions 
AgriCash offers a “Plant Now, Pay Later” financing model, enabling farmers to access agricultural inputs at the right time with flexible repayment plans of up to 12 months interest-free. This helps ease financial pressures and ensures smoother and more efficient production cycles.
The platform’s role extends beyond financing to include agricultural advisory services, guiding farmers toward reliable agricultural inputs, connecting them with markets and crop buyers, and enabling future expansion through additional sector partnerships. This contributes to building a fully integrated ecosystem that supports the entire agricultural value chain.
 Driving Innovation 
Through this partnership, Erada Finance aims to strengthen its role as a driver of innovation in specialized financing, particularly in underserved sectors such as agriculture. The company also seeks to raise awareness about agrifintech and digital agricultural financing while simplifying these concepts to accelerate market adoption.
The initiative reflects both parties’ vision that empowering farmers begins with providing smart, accessible financial tools that integrate financing, services, and knowledge simultaneously. It also contributes to advancing financial inclusion by bringing new segments of farmers into the formal financial system and supporting their ability to expand and increase productivity.
 Advancing Agritech in Egypt 
At a time when agritech and digital agricultural financing are still in their early stages in the Egyptian market, this partnership represents an important step toward building a scalable model that combines digital innovation, financing expertise, and agricultural infrastructure, paving the way for broader future partnerships within the sector.
Through this collaboration, Erada Finance and AgriCash are laying the foundation for a new agricultural financing model that goes beyond providing funding to become a true empowerment tool that supports farmers’ livelihoods and contributes to building a more sustainable, resilient, and growth-oriented agricultural sector.
 Important Step Forward 
Amr Abou El Azm, Co-Founder and CEO of Erada Finance, expressed his satisfaction with signing the agreement, stating: “This partnership represents an important step toward supporting and developing innovative financing solutions in the agricultural sector. We are committed to expanding our services to include the most impactful productive sectors within the Egyptian economy.”
He added: “The agricultural sector is one of the key drivers of economic growth, which requires the development of non-traditional financial solutions that support agricultural value chains at every stage, from securing inputs to reaching markets.”
 Innovative Financing Models 
He further explained: “At Erada Finance, we are committed to adopting more flexible and innovative financing models that integrate agricultural and financial technology with the real needs of farmers, helping address challenges related to traditional financing and rising production costs.”
