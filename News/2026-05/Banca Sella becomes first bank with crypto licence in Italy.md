## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇹 Banca Sella becomes the first bank to receive a crypto license in Italy to offer crypto services under Europe’s MiCA framework after receiving approval from the Bank of Italy. The bank plans to launch digital asset custody and transfer services for corporate and institutional clients later this year.
