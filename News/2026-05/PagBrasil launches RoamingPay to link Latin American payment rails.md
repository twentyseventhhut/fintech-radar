## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎 Brazilian fintech PagBrasillaunched RoamingPay, a platform that interconnects instant payment networks across Latin America — including Pix, Transferencias 3.0, and Bre-B — allowing travelers to pay abroad using their local banking apps while merchants receive settlement in their own currency. The company targets 10 countries and $600M in transactions in its first year.
