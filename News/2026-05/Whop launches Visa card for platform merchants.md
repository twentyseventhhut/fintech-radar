_Whop Cards enable businesses to earn, spend, and manage money entirely within the Whop platform, while earning up to 5% cash back_

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Whop, the fastest growing internet market where people can create, connect, and transact in one place, today announced the launch of Whop Cards that allow businesses on the platform the ability to spend directly from their Whop balance without withdrawing funds to an external bank. The launch completes the full business lifecycle on the platform: entrepreneurs can now launch, grow, and spend entirely within Whop.

Whop Cards operate on the Visa network and are accepted at more than 150 million merchant locations worldwide. Businesses can also earn up to 5% cashback on eligible purchases*.

“Whop’s strength lies in its fully integrated ecosystem - where storefronts, ads, wallets, and financial products like Whop Cards - work seamlessly together in one place,” **said Steven Schwartz, Whop CEO.**“Whop cards accelerate our vision for a true closed-loop economy, where businesses can earn and spend, while leveraging our built-in advertising product to run and manage campaigns directly from the same dashboard. Together, these products empower global users to build a living on the internet, entirely on their own terms.”

**Closing the Loop on the Internet Economy**

Whop was built with a single mission: to deliver everyone a sustainable income. Until now, Whop’s more than 40,000 monthly earners had only one way to access their funds: withdrawing to an external bank account. Whop Cards eliminate that friction, allowing businesses to move earnings directly to a card balance and spend them in real time, with no withdrawal required.

The cards are a cornerstone of Whop's product suite, giving businesses worldwide access to best-in-class payments infrastructure.

Whop Cards are funded through Whop's stablecoin infrastructure, with card payments processed through the Visa network. This interoperability enables global access from launch, allowing businesses to avoid currency fluctuation and limit their reliance on conventional payment networks.

The announcement follows the launch of Whop Finance, a suite of financial tools that allows Whop’s internet-native businesses the ability to spend their money earned on the Whop platform.

*****Businesses can also earn up to 5% cashback on eligible purchases, including Uber, Whop Content Rewards and Whop Ads, making it easier for them to reinvest directly into growth. _See terms for full details._

**About Whop:**Whop is a financial technology company on a mission to provide the world with sustainable income. Our vision is to create the world's largest internet market, where people can create, connect, and transact all from a single platform. Whop enables individuals and businesses to accept payments, launch ventures, and engage with others across the network. Today, people on Whop earn ~$4 billion annually across 145 countries. For more information, visit whop.com.
