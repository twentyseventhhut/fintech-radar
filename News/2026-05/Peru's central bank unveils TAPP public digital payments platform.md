# Peru's central bank unveils TAPP public digital payments platform

> [!info] 2026-05-06 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇵🇪Peru's Central Bank announced TAPP, a public digital payment infrastructure that will allow users to initiate real-time transfers from any authorized app — not just their bank's. A pilot is expected between June and July, with full launch by end of 2026. The move signals Peru is building open payment infrastructure at the state level, opening the door to fintechs and new players
