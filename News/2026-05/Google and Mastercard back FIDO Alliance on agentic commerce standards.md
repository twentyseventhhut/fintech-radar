## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Google and Mastercard get behind Fido Alliance on agentic commerce standards. The FIDO Alliance will leverage its track record of delivering standards at internet scale, building on its work to replace passwords with passkeys and advance digital credentials, to address emerging trust and interoperability challenges.

[This Week in Fintech] 🌍 Google and Mastercardbacked new FIDO Alliance standards for agentic commerce, as the industry looks to secure AI-led shopping and payment flows.

[This Week in Fintech] AI agents may soon be buying your stuff for you. The FIDO Alliance has teamed up with Google and Mastercard to try to ensure that shopping in the near future isn't a complete disaster.
