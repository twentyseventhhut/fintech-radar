# Mastercard and CAF partner to expand Latin America SME credit

> [!info] 2026-05-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎 Mastercard's Center for Inclusive Growth and CAF announced a partnership to expand credit access for micro, small and medium enterprises across Latin America and the Caribbean. CAF targets up to $100 million in financing over four years, with priority markets including Colombia, Peru, Panama, Ecuador, Chile, and El Salvador, and a focus on women-led businesses and climate-related projects.
