## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 PayPal plans job cuts as FinTech’s new CEO pursues turnaround strategy. PayPal employed around 23,800 people at the end of the year, meaning a workforce reduction of that size could eliminate more than 4,500 jobs. It’s part of the company’s plan to realize at least $1.5 billion in savings over the next two to three years, according to a statement.
