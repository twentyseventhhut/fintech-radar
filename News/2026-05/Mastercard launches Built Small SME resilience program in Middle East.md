# Mastercard launches Built Small SME resilience program in Middle East

> [!info] 2026-05-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇪 Mastercard has launched Built Small. Moving Strong, a regional resilience program across the Middle East designed to support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) as they navigate a challenging operating environment marked by supply chain disruption and tightening financial conditions. The initiative brings together banks, government entities, and ecosystem partners focused on digital tools, liquidity, and resilience.
