# Brazil central bank sets R$200 Pix limit on unrecognised devices

> [!info] 2026-05-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Pix will have a maximum limit of R$ 200 after a change announced by the Central Bank to combat fraud. Transfers from unrecognised devices will be temporarily limited, while banks like Nubank, Itaú Unibanco, and Caixa Econômica Federal can now apply precautionary blocks on suspicious transactions for up to 72 hours.
