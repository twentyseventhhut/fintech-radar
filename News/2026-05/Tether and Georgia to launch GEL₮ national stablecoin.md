# Tether and Georgia to launch GEL₮ national stablecoin

> [!info] 2026-05-26 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇪 Tether and the Government of Georgia to launch GEL₮, the official stablecoin of Georgia. GEL₮ is designed to function as a digital representation of the Georgian Lari, enabling lower transaction costs, near-instant settlement, programmable payments, and more efficient movement of value across digital financial systems.
