## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇬 FirstBank’s Digital Xperience Centres expand self-service banking, allowing customers to carry out a range of transactions through self-service kiosks, including account opening, card issuance, and other routine services.
