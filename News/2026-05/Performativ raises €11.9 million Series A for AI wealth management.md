## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Copenhagen-based Performativeraised an €11.9m Series A to scale its AI-native wealth management operating system for financial institutions — the round was led by FinTech Collective, with participation from BlackFin Tech, West Hill Capital and existing investors including Dreamcraft Ventures, Moonfire Ventures and Curiosity VC.
