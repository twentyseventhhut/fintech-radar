## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Plaid launches income verification tool in the UK and Europe. Plaid Income takes raw deposit activity and transforms it into structured income signals lenders can use to make more informed affordability assessments. It classifies earnings across income types, helping lenders more accurately assess borrowers whose income may fluctuate month to month.
