# Visa boosts Visa Pay app launch in DR Congo with AppsFlyer

> [!info] 2026-05-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇩 Visa improved the launch performance of its Visa Pay App in the Democratic Republic of the Congo by using AppsFlyer’s attribution and measurement tools to better track campaign effectiveness. The initiative resulted in a 155% increase in app downloads, an 85% reduction in customer acquisition costs, and a 114% improvement in registration conversion rates during a 12-week marketing campaign.
