## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 📊 Blue Dot Investors and FT Partners Report. Blue Dot Investors and FT Partners released a report highlighting a coming “FinTech liquidity supercycle,” driven by IPOs, M&A, and secondary markets. The analysis shows private FinTechs now outpacing public peers in revenue and scale, signalling a more mature and institutionalized phase for the sector.
