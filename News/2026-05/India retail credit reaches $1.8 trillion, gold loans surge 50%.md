# India retail credit reaches $1.8 trillion, gold loans surge 50%

> [!info] 2026-05-23 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] India's retail credit portfolio reached ₹170.2 lakh crore ($1.8tn) in March 2026, grew 16.6% YoY, with gold loans surging 50.4% while home loans expanded only 9.4%.
