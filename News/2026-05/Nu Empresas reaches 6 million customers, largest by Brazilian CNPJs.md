# Nu Empresas reaches 6 million customers, largest by Brazilian CNPJs

> [!info] 2026-05-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Nu Empresas reaches 6 million customers and becomes the largest financial institution in Brazil in terms of the number of CNPJs (Brazilian company tax IDs).
