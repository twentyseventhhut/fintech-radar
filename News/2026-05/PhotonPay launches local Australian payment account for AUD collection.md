PhotonPay Unlocks AUD Collection Capabilities to Catalyze B2B Trade Growth in Australia
HONG KONG, May 08, 2026 -- PhotonPay , a next-generation financial operating system, today announced the official launch of its Australian Local Account capabilities. This strategic expansion allows global businesses to open local AUD accounts online, enabling them to receive payments directly through Australia's local clearing networks.
The Australian Opportunity: A $50 Billion Frontier
PhotonPay Australian Local Accounts: Built for Efficiency at Scale
 Intermediary-Free Settlement: By utilizing Australia's local clearing networks, funds are transferred directly from the payer to the recipient. This eliminates the need for intermediary banks, ensuring faster settlement.
 
 Zero Intermediary Fees: Businesses no longer have to suffer from hidden deductions. Local accounts remove the traditional lifting fees and processing charges associated with SWIFT transfers, maximizing profit margins for global exporters.
 
 Enhanced Liquidity Management: Businesses can collect, hold, and manage AUD natively. This allows for more strategic FX timing and simplifies reconciliation by matching local currency inflows with Australian tax and vendor obligations.
 
 Enterprise-Grade Compliance: Built on PhotonPay's robust regulatory framework, the local account service integrates advanced KYB, KYC and anti-fraud protocols, ensuring every transaction meets both global and Australian financial standards.
