# LIC reports $6 billion profit, up 19.3% in FY26

> [!info] 2026-05-23 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Life Insurance Corporation (LIC)reported ₹57,419cr ($6bn) standalone profit after tax, growing 19.3% YoY in FY26, with value of new business surging 41.6% and total premium income rising 9.8% to ₹5,35,984cr ($56bn).
