PB Fintech reports Rs 2,061 Cr revenue in Q4 FY26; profit rises 54%
 
PB Fintech, the parent of PolicyBazaar, reported its Q4 FY26 results, with operating revenue up 37% year-on-year to cross the Rs 2,000 crore threshold, while profit rose 54% in the quarter.
 
 Follow Us
PB Fintech, the parent company of online insurance aggregator and brokerage platform PolicyBazaar, announced its Q4 FY26 results. The company reported a 37% year-on-year increase in operating revenue, crossing the Rs 2,000 crore threshold. Its profit also rose 54% during the quarter.
 PolicyBazaar’s revenue from operations surged to Rs 2,061 crore in Q4 FY26 in contrast to Rs 1,508 crore in Q4 FY25 , as per the firm’s financial results sourced from the National Stock Exchange (NSE). 
 
 The Gurugram-based company derived 92% of its operating revenue from insurance broking services, which grew 44% year-on-year to Rs 1,901 crore in Q4 FY26 from Rs 1,322 crore in the same quarter last year. The rest of 160 crore came from other services. 
 In addition to operating revenue, the firm earned Rs 104 crore from interest and gains on financial assets during the quarter, taking its total income to Rs 2,166 crore for the quarter ended March 2026. 
 For the full year ended March 2026, PB Fintech’s operating scale rose 37% to Rs 6,794 crore from Rs 4,977 crore in FY25. During the same period, its profit increased 90% to Rs 670 crore. 
 Employee benefit expenses accounted for 37% of the group’s total costs, amounting to Rs 699 crore, and increased by over 37% compared to the same quarter last year. Spending on advertising and promotion also rose 34% to Rs 371 crore in Q4 FY26. 
 The company did not disclose a detailed expense breakdown in its quarterly financials. Overall expenses rose 31% year-on-year to Rs 1,888 crore in Q4 FY26 from Rs 1,438 crore in the same quarter last year. 
 In the end, the firm’s profit rose 54% year-on-year to Rs 261 crore in the quarter ended March 2026 from Rs 170 crore in Q4 FY25, as revenue growth exceeded expense growth. 
 At the end of today’s trading session, shares of PB Fintech closed at Rs 1,703, taking its total market capitalization to Rs 78,797 crore (about $8.3 billion).
