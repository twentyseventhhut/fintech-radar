# Swiss campaign to force SNB Bitcoin holdings fails on signatures

> [!info] 2026-05-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇭 Bitcoin campaign to force SNB holdings has failed. Swiss crypto campaigners have abandoned a bid to require the central bank to hold Bitcoin after failing to collect enough signatures to trigger a plebiscite, Reuters reported. Organizers collected only around half of the 100,000 signatures required to force a vote of the electorate on the matter.
