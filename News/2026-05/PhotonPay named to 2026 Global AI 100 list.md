Pioneering Financial Intelligence, PhotonPay Honored with 2026 Global AI 100 Recognition
HONG KONG, May 22, 2026 — PhotonPay , a stablecoin-powered global financial operating system, today announced that it has won the "2026 Global AI 100" award initiated by Feifan Industrial Research . This recognition honors PhotonPay's continuous innovation in AI-integrated financial infrastructure and its pivotal role in architecting intelligent global commerce.

 Transaction Routing: Optimizes payment paths, clearing mechanics, and service responsiveness in real time, maximizing settlement velocity while eliminating frictions. 


 Treasury & Liquidity: Deploys predictive AI models to analyze FX movements, liquidity corridors, and capital flows, allowing businesses to manage global treasury with unmatched agility. 


 Risk & Compliance: Powers an adaptive risk engine that transforms complex multi-jurisdictional regulatory screening, anomaly detection, and AML auditing into a machine-readable, proactive defense mechanism.
