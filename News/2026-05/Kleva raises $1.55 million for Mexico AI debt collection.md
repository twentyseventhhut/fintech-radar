# Kleva raises $1.55 million for Mexico AI debt collection

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Kleva raised $1.55M in a seed round led by Wollef Ventures, with participation from Nascent, Kuiper VC, and Lerer Hippeau. The Mexico-based startup builds AI agents for debt collection, helping lenders automate contact centers and improve recovery rates while reducing costs.
