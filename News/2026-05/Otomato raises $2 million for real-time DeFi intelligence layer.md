# Otomato raises $2 million for real-time DeFi intelligence layer

> [!info] 2026-05-27 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇫🇷 Otomato raises $2M from Improbable to build a real-time intelligence layer for DeFi users. The French-founded startup helps DeFi users track risks and important changes across multiple crypto platforms through real-time personalized alerts. The funding will be deployed into product development, expanded multi-chain and multi-vertical coverage, and go-to-market.
