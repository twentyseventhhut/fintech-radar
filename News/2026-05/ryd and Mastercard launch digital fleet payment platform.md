# ryd and Mastercard launch digital fleet payment platform

> [!info] 2026-05-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇪 ryd launches ryd fleet in collaboration with Mastercard, a digital-first payment operating system for European fleet operators. ryd fleet combines ryd’s digital mobility platform with Mastercard’s global acceptance network and virtual card technology to offer a fully digital, tokenized fleet card.
