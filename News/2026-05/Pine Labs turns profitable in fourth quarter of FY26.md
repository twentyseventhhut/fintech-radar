Pine Labs posts Rs 700 Cr revenue and Rs 59 Cr profit in Q4 FY26
 
Fintech major Pine Labs announced its financial results for the quarter ended March 2026 on Monday. The company reported a 17% year-on-year increase in revenue during Q4 FY26 and turned profitable in the same period.

 
 Follow Us
 Fintech major Pine Labs announced its financial results for the quarter ended March 2026 on Monday. The company reported a 17% year-on-year increase in revenue during Q4 FY26 and turned profitable in the same period. 
 Pine Labs’ revenue from operations increased to Rs 700.5 crore in Q4 FY26 from Rs 598.6 crore in the same quarter last year, according to its financial statement sourced from NSE. 
 
 On a sequential basis, its operating revenue declined marginally by 6% during the quarter.  
 Other income contributed an additional Rs 40.9 crore, which took Pine Labs’ total income to Rs 741.4 crore during the quarter. For the full financial year ended March 2026, the company’s revenue increased 19% to Rs 2,710.6 crore from Rs 2,274.3 crore in FY25. At the same time, the fintech major also achieved full-year profitability, reporting a profit of Rs 112.5 crore 
 On the expense side, employee benefit was the largest cost centre, which accounted for over 36% of the total expense. This cost remained flat Rs  246.5 crore in Q4 FY26. Cost of material rose 18% to Rs 104.3 crore in Q4 FY26 from Rs 88.55 crore in Q4 FY25. 
 Transaction related cost also surged 27% to Rs 82.8 crore during the quarter. Finance cost, depreciation cost were other overheads which led to the total expense to increase by 8% to Rs 681.9 crore in Q4 FY26. 
 On an year-on-year basis, Pine Labs turned profitable during the quarter on the back of steady growth and disciplined cost control, reporting a net profit of Rs 59.4 crore in Q4 FY26 against a loss of Rs 28.9 crore in Q4 FY25.  
 Last month, Pine Labs acquired 100% stake in Shopflo Technologies, a direct to consumer D2C checkout platform, in a deal valued at up to Rs 88 crore. 
 At the end of today’s trading session, Pine Labs’ share closed at Rs 146.67, which valued the company with a total market capitalization of Rs 16,842 crore (around $1.77 billion).
