Mobikwik reports Rs 289 Cr revenue and Rs 4.4 Cr profit in Q4 FY26
 
Fintech platform MobiKwik reported its Q4 FY26 results on Tuesday. The company posted an 8% year-on-year increase in operating revenue and swung to a profit of Rs 4.4 crore in the quarter against a net loss of Rs 56 crore in the same quarter last year.

 
 Follow Us
 Fintech platform MobiKwik reported its Q4 FY26 results on Tuesday. The company posted an 8% year-on-year increase in operating revenue and swung to a profit of Rs 4.4 crore in the quarter against a net loss of Rs 56 crore in the same quarter last year. 
 MobiKwik’s revenue from operations grew to Rs 289 crore in Q4 FY26 from Rs 268 crore in Q4 FY25, its unaudited financial statements accessed from the National Stock Exchange (NSE) show. 
 
 For MobiKwik, commissions from recharges and loan servicing, along with income from payment gateway and technology platform services, were the key revenue contributors in Q4 FY26. The company, however, did not disclose a segment-wise revenue breakup in its quarterly filing. 
 For the full fiscal year ended March 2026, MobiKwik reported flat revenue at Rs 1,119 crore, while its losses halved to Rs 62 crore from Rs 122 crore in FY25. 
 For the Gurugram-based firm, payment gateway charges remained the largest expense in Q4 FY26, accounting for over 41% of total costs which stood at Rs 119 crore. Its Employee benefit expenses were recorded at Rs 46 crore, while lending operational costs dropped sharply by 95% to Rs 2 crore from Rs 41 crore in the year-ago quarter. 
 For MobiKwik, financial guarantee costs stood at Rs 30 crore in Q4 FY26, while legal, advertising and marketing, finance, and other overheads pushed total expenses to Rs 288 crore. The overall cost base declined 14% from Rs 335 crore in the corresponding quarter last year. 
 The firm reported a profit of Rs 4.4 crore in Q4 FY26, compared to a loss of Rs 56 crore in the same quarter last year, aided by controlled expenses, particularly lower lending operational costs. On a sequential basis, its profit increased 10%. 
 Recently, Mobikwik’s early backer Peak XV Partners fully exited the firm in a block deal worth Rs 130 crore, offloading its 7.7% stake in the firm. 
 Last month, MobiKwik received approval from the Reserve Bank of India for its NBFC licence application, paving the way for the launch of its in-house lending arm. 
 Mobikwik’s stock price is trading at Rs 220.9 (as of 11:18 AM) with a total market capitalization of Rs 1,740 crore or approximately $183 million.
