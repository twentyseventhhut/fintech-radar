New Research: The State of Mule Account Handovers in 2026
Mule account handovers are on the rise, and most institutions are only catching them after the damage is done. In this webinar, Incognia breaks down the findings of the 2026 State of Mule Account Handovers research report, a survey of 500+ fraud, risk, and AML professionals at financial institutions across the US and Europe. What's covered: → The scale of mule account handovers, which institutions are most at risk, and what's driving the increase → How institutions are currently detecting handovers, where signals are falling short, and what the institutions catching it early are doing differently → What a better approach to mule account handover detection looks like and how to make the case for it internally Speakers: – André Ferraz, CEO and Co-Founder, Incognia – Dave Laramy, Head of Group Fraud, Danske Bank – Dwayne Gefferie, Payments Strategist + Expert
