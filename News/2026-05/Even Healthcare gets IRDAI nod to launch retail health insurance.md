Even Healthcare Secures IRDAI Nod for Retail Health Insurance Entry 
Team Startupcity
 IRDAI approves Even for retail health insurance sales 
 Policy portability enables seamless customer transitions 
 2L+ members across 10 cities with 3X growth 
Even Healthcare, India's pioneering integrated healthcare provider, has received IRDAI approval to offer retail health insurance products directly to individuals and families. The regulatory milestone enables Even to move beyond group insurance , sell standalone policies, and facilitate policy portability while embedding its full-stack healthcare model.
Previously limited to group insurance, Even can now tap India's burgeoning retail health insurance market, estimated at Rs 1.2-1.3 lakh crore with 20% annual growth. The Bengaluru-based company serves over 2 lakh members across 10 cities, achieving 3X growth in two years through its unique model combining insurance with doctor consultations, care coordination, preventive support, and cashless hospitalization.
 "This approval is a defining milestone for Even", said Mayank Banerjee, Co-founder . "It allows us to bring our integrated healthcare model directly to individuals and families, moving beyond traditional insurance focused only on hospitalization. Our goal is to build a system where insurance and healthcare delivery work together to improve clinical outcomes". 
The approval enables policy portability, letting customers switch existing health policies while retaining benefits like waiting period continuity. This flexibility addresses India's high out-of-pocket healthcare spending (42% of total, per National Health Accounts) by offering comprehensive coverage integrated with managed care services.
