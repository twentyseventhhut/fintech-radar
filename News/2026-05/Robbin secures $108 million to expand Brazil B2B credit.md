# Robbin secures $108 million to expand Brazil B2B credit

> [!info] 2026-05-27 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷Robbin, the Brazilian B2B credit and payments fintech, announced a $108M financing package to expand credit operations between manufacturers and retailers in Brazil, including an $8M seed round co-led by Canary, Atlântico, and Caravela, and a $100M FIDC structured with Augme, XP's asset management arm. The company uses Pix infrastructure for real-time settlement and recently launched an AI assistant that approves credit directly on WhatsApp.
