Next steps
Thursday's hearing proceeded relatively quickly. The Senate Banking Committee and Agriculture Committee negotiators will meet and confer on how to merge their respective bills and try to find a compromise on ethics before moving to the Senate floor. 
White House adviser Patrick Witt said at CoinDesk's Consensus Miami conference last week that he saw a viable path to President Donald Trump signing the bill into law around July 4, but that leaves a compressed timeline for lawmakers to address their outstanding issues and bring the House on board. 
But still, the next thing to watch will be the agreement on ethics and what changes the Agriculture version of the bill might include.
