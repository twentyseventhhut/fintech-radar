# AppTech Payments names Albert Lord executive chairman, adds board member

> [!info] 2026-05-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 AppTech Payments appoints Albert Lord as Executive Chairman and adds Robert Lipstein to the Board. Under the expanded leadership structure, Lord will work closely with Chief Executive Officer Thomas DeRosa, who will continue serving as CEO and report to the full board. Lipstein currently serves on several corporate boards, including two banking companies.
