PhotonPay Answers ITU's Call for "Digital Lifelines" with 24/7 Dual-Rail Financial Infrastructure
HONG KONG, May 15, 2026 — On World Telecommunication and Information Society Day (WTISD) 2026, the International Telecommunication Union (ITU) has designated this year’s theme as “Digital Lifelines: Strengthening Resilience in a Connected World,” underscoring the critical importance of resilient digital infrastructure as global interdependence deepens.
 Dual-Rail Architecture: The Bedrock of Continuity 

 Global Accounts & Like-for-Like Settlement: Subject to KYC/KYB and compliance approval, businesses can open local accounts in minutes online across 19 major currencies, and receive funds in the same currency their customers pay in, eliminating unnecessary FX conversions and protecting profit margins. 


 Seamless Fiat-Stablecoin Ramps: The Photon Wallet acts as a liquidity command center, allowing users to move effortlessly between fiat and major stablecoins like USDT and USDC. This ecosystem removes the traditional "cut-off times" associated with legacy banking, ensuring that liquidity is accessible even during weekends and public holidays. 


 Global Payouts at Scale: Whether paying suppliers in 200+ countries or executing mass payouts to freelancers via API, PhotonPay provides unrivaled reach. By offering payouts in both fiat and stablecoins, the platform gives payees the flexibility they demand while allowing the sender to settle instantly. 


 Transactional FX & Risk Mitigation: PhotonPay provides 24/7 access to Spot FX, allowing businesses to convert currencies at transparent rates. Furthermore, the Scheduled Exchange feature offers a strategic tool for financial predictability, enabling users to lock in rates for up to 30 days and mitigate the impact of sudden market shifts. 

 Security and Compliance: The Essence of Resilience 
 

 Institutional-Grade Licensing and Oversight: Resilience is anchored by a robust legal framework. PhotonPay holds critical financial licenses in premier global hubs, including the US MTL, Hong Kong SFC (Types 1, 4, 9), and UAE DFSA (Category 3D). These credentials, supported by strategic partnerships with Tier-1 banking institutions, ensure that every transaction is shielded by rigorous international standards. 


 Real-Time Fraud Prevention and Information Security: The platform features an autonomous fraud prevention layer that monitors the payment lifecycle to detect anomalies at the millisecond level. Complementing this is the company's SOC 2 Type 1 certification, which validates a commitment to global standards for data protection and information security. 


 Comprehensive AML & Travel Rule Readiness : PhotonPay's framework includes in-depth KYC, KYB and KYT protocols. As a "Travel Rule Ready" provider, the platform also integrates with global VASP (Virtual Asset Service Provider) networks, ensuring that every stablecoin transaction is fully compliant with international anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing standards from day one.
