# NSE bars YES Securities from new clients for three months

> [!info] 2026-05-31 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] National Stock Exchange (NSE) penalisedYES Securities and barred it from onboarding new clients for 3 months for transferring regulatory margin penalties to clients, violating Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) norms.
