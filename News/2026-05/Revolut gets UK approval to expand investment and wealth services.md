## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Revolut gets UK approval to expand investments for customers. The firm’s trading entity is reworking its model to offer competitive pricing, according to a statement. It will also begin to offer portfolio management and private wealth services. Additionally, the company eyes a private bank arm for the UK and Europe to push into wealth. The move reflects the company’s strategy to expand revenue streams by offering premium financial services to high-net-worth individuals.
