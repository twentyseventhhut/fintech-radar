# Revolut launches Hungarian IBANs for customers

> [!info] 2026-05-20 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇭🇺 Revolut is launching Hungarian IBANs to all customers, allowing eligible customers in Hungary to receive local HU IBANs. The move is designed to support salary payments, direct debits, and utility bill payments while reducing friction often associated with foreign IBANs.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇭🇺 Revolut to start moving Hungarian users to local bank accounts within days. One of the biggest changes for users will be the introduction of Hungarian IBAN account numbers, allowing salaries to be paid directly into Revolut accounts. Read on
