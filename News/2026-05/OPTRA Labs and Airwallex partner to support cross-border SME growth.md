OPTRA Labs is proud to announce our new partnership with Airwallex, a leading global financial platform for modern businesses.

At OPTRA, we help SMEs identify, apply for, and secure government grants and other forms of non-dilutive funding. Over time, one pattern has consistently emerged: securing capital is a major milestone, but it is only one part of sustainable growth. As businesses expand across Southeast Asia and into global markets, day-to-day financial operations often become more complex around cross-border collections, foreign exchange, supplier payments, and spend control.

This partnership brings together two complementary capabilities for scaling companies: better access to growth capital through OPTRA, and modern financial infrastructure through Airwallex.

## Why this partnership matters

Many SMEs are ready to invest once funding is secured, but operational complexity can still slow execution. International transfers, fragmented banking relationships, reconciliation overhead, and limited real-time visibility can create unnecessary friction during growth.

By partnering with Airwallex, OPTRA can better support clients that need an integrated operating layer for cross-border finance. Airwallex provides capabilities across business accounts, transfers, spend management, online payments, and API connectivity in one platform.

## What this means for OPTRA clients

Through this partnership, eligible OPTRA clients can explore Airwallex solutions designed to support regional and international growth, including:

*   Multi-currency account capabilities built for cross-border operations
*   International transfer workflows across a broad global payout network
*   Foreign exchange functionality with transparent pricing controls
*   Corporate cards and spend controls for distributed teams
*   Payment acceptance and integration options for digital channels
*   API and automation support for finance operations at scale

For founders and finance teams, the practical objective is clear: reduce disconnected workflows, improve cash visibility, and strengthen control over how money is collected, moved, and managed across markets.

## Why Airwallex

Airwallex supports businesses operating across 150+ countries and regions through its global financial platform. Its infrastructure is built for companies that need to move from domestic operations to multi-market execution without relying on fragmented legacy systems.

For OPTRA clients, this is particularly relevant at the stage where grant-funded initiatives move into regional expansion and require stronger operational discipline across payments and treasury processes.

## OPTRA perspective: capital access plus execution infrastructure

Our mission is to help SMEs grow with confidence. That means helping businesses secure suitable non-dilutive funding and helping them deploy capital effectively after approval.

This partnership reflects that full-cycle view of growth:

*   Access capital intelligently
*   Operate capital efficiently
*   Scale with stronger systems from day one

By combining OPTRA's funding and grant expertise with Airwallex's global financial infrastructure, we aim to reduce execution risk and support faster, better-coordinated expansion for SMEs.

## Looking ahead

We see this as a long-term collaboration focused on measurable outcomes for clients. In today's operating environment, financial operations are no longer only a back-office concern. They are a growth function that directly affects speed, margin protection, and execution quality.

OPTRA and Airwallex are aligned on this reality, and we are excited to support ambitious SMEs through their next phase of regional and global growth.

If you are preparing to scale, we welcome a conversation on how OPTRA and Airwallex can support your plans.

## Important note

Product availability, onboarding requirements, and regulatory treatment can vary by market and entity. OPTRA does not provide regulated financial services. Financial products and services are provided by Airwallex group entities subject to local laws and licensing requirements.
