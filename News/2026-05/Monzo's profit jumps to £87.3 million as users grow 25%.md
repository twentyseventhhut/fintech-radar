# Monzo's profit jumps to £87.3 million as users grow 25%

> [!info] 2026-05-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Monzo’s profit rockets as the customer base grows by a quarter. The neobank’s pre-tax profit came in at £87.3m, 44% higher than the £60m scored in 2024. The increase came as revenue stormed 40% higher to £1.7bn. Monzo’s total users ballooned 25% in the period to 15.2m, which paved the way for a 55% surge in deposits, reaching £25.7bn.
