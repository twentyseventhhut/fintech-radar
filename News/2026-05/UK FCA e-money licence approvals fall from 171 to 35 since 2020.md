# UK FCA e-money licence approvals fall from 171 to 35 since 2020

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 FCA approvals for e-money licences plummet. New figures from Pathlight Associates show that approvals for UK electronic money institution (EMI) licences have fallen sharply, dropping from 171 in 2020 to just 35 in 2025. The decline highlights slowing momentum in the FinTech licensing pipeline.
