# Prestamype secures $27 million to expand Peru SME lending

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇵🇪 Prestamype, the Peruvian SME lending platform, secured $27 million in financing, including a $25 million credit line from international fund Lendable and a $2 million equity round. Noticia
La fintech peruana Prestamype cerró un financiamiento total de US$ 27 millones, integrado por una ronda de capital de US$ 2 millones y una línea de crédito de US$ 25 millones otorgada por el fondo Lendable.
Los recursos se destinarán a duplicar su cartera de préstamos para mipymes y fortalecer su presencia nacional mediante la apertura de oficinas en tres nuevas ciudades de Perú.
La compañía planea crear su propia sociedad administradora de fondos de inversión (SAFI) y proyecta levantar una ronda Serie A de hasta US$ 15 millones en el mediano plazo.

Prestamype raises $27M in equity and debt
 by Araceli Dominguez 
March 24, 2026
 1 minute read
Peruvian fintech Prestamype secured $27M in equity and debt. The funding includes a $2M bridge round from existing investors ALIVE Ventures , Oikocredit , and Salkantay Ventures , as well as a $25M credit line from Lendable .
Prestamype offers digital financial solutions to micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises in Peru, with a focus on loans and factoring. Its products also include a digital currency exchange platform, Cambio Seguro.
Prestamype will use the bridge round to create a fund management company (SAFI), which will allow it to raise capital from retail and institutional investors. The credit line will finance mortgage-backed loans through a trust structure with Peru’s development bank, Cofide.
In 2025, Prestamype reached $319M in factoring disbursements, up 163% from $121M in 2024. Since its launch in 2017, Prestamype has disbursed more than $650M to Peruvian SMEs.
