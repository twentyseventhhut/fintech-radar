[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Candex extends Series C to $40M+ to help enterprises easily onboard global vendors. The New York-based startup aims to help large companies pay small, one-time, or irregular vendors without the administrative headache or risk that comes with onboarding them.
