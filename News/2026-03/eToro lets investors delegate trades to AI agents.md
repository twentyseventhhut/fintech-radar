# eToro lets investors delegate trades to AI agents

> [!info] 2026-03-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇱 eToro lets investors delegate trades to AI agents as automation usage nearly doubles. Dubbed Agent Portfolios, the new offering acts as a separate sub-account within a user’s main profile. The agent can then open and close trades, check balances, and manage positions within that portfolio’s boundaries.
