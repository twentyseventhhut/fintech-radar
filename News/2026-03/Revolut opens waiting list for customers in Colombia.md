# Revolut opens waiting list for customers in Colombia

> [!info] 2026-03-04 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Neobank Revolut has opened a waiting list for customers and already has an app in Colombia. The European FinTech company has activated a pre-access mechanism on its local website that will allow Colombians to be notified when it begins formal operations in the country. The move confirms that it is already in an advanced stage of preparation, while the process to begin full operations in the country is being finalized.
