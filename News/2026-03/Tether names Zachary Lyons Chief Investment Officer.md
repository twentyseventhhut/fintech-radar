# Tether names Zachary Lyons Chief Investment Officer

> [!info] 2026-03-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇻 Tether has named Zachary Lyons as Chief Investment Officer, succeeding Richard Heathcote, who will step back from day-to-day duties and move into a non-executive advisory role. Lyons previously served as deputy CIO and will lead Tether’s investment strategy and capital deployment.
