# CBC Sri Lanka and Aurionpro go-live with modern cash management platform for corporate banking growth

CXOtoday News Desk4 months ago

[](https://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fcxotoday.com%2Fmedia-coverage%2Fcbc-sri-lanka-and-aurionpro-go-live-with-modern-cash-management-platform-for-corporate-banking-growth%2F)[](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=CBC+Sri+Lanka+and%C2%A0Aurionpro%C2%A0go-live%C2%A0with%C2%A0modern%C2%A0cash+management+platform+for+corporate+banking+growth&url=https%3A%2F%2Fcxotoday.com%2Fmedia-coverage%2Fcbc-sri-lanka-and-aurionpro-go-live-with-modern-cash-management-platform-for-corporate-banking-growth%2F&via=CXOToday.com)[](https://linkedin.com/shareArticle?mini=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fcxotoday.com%2Fmedia-coverage%2Fcbc-sri-lanka-and-aurionpro-go-live-with-modern-cash-management-platform-for-corporate-banking-growth%2F&title=CBC+Sri+Lanka+and%C2%A0Aurionpro%C2%A0go-live%C2%A0with%C2%A0modern%C2%A0cash+management+platform+for+corporate+banking+growth)

**Aurionpro Solutions Limited**today announced the successful go-_live_ of its next-generation cash management platform,iCashpro, at**Commercial Bank of Ceylon (CBC), Sri Lanka**.This marks a major milestone in CBC’s digital transformation journey to strengthen its corporate and institutional banking capabilities.

Under this partnership, Aurionpro has implemented its payments and portals stack within the iCashpro platform at CBC, setting the foundation for a comprehensive modernization of the bank’s cash management stack. Future phases will extend to liquidity management, post-dated cheque (PDC) reporting, and virtual account management, delivering a unified, end-to-end digital experience for corporate customers.

**Powering the next phase of digital corporate banking**

With the go-_live_ of iCashpro’s payments functionality, CBC can now offer enhanced speed, security, and control to its corporate clients while improving operational efficiency and scalability. The deployment reinforces the bank’s commitment to innovation and customer-centricity as it continues to lead Sri Lanka’s banking sector in digital capability and excellence.

Recognized as a leader at the _2025 Euromoney Transaction Banking Awards_, CBC continues to invest in modern infrastructure to support evolving corporate needs, improve resilience, and deliver superior client outcomes.

**S Prabagar, Chief Operating Officer, Commercial Bank of Ceylon,commented:**_“We are pleased to partner with Aurionpro Solutions as we continue to strengthen CBC’s digital banking capabilities.This go-live is an important step in CBC’s stated focus on digital transformation and customer-centric banking, strengthening its cash management foundation for scale. The seamless execution and delivery quality provided by Aurionpro have been exemplary, ensuring a smooth go-live. The timely deployment reinforces our confidence in the platform’s quality and its ability to support long-term growth.”_

**Poonam Puthran, EVP & Head,Transaction Banking Group,Aurionpro Solutions, commented**:_“We are proud to partner with Commercial Bank of Ceylon as they modernize and scale their cash management franchise. This go-live is a strong validation of Aurionpro’s vision to**lead the next**wave of cash management banking with intuitive tech and IP-led platforms. By bringing iCashpro’s payments capabilities live for CBC’s corporate clients, we are helping the bank deliver faster, more secure,and insight-rich experiences today.”_

She added,_“The successful go-live is a strong demonstration of Aurionpro’s ability to deliver large-scale, mission-critical transaction banking platforms for leading banks. This milestone showcases the strength of our superior product stack and our capacity to support high-volume, always-on corporate banking environments with speed, resilience, and reliability.”_

Aurionpro Solutions Ltd. is a global enterprise technology leader pioneering intuitive-tech through deep-tech IPs and scalable products. With a strong presence across Banking, Payments, Mobility, Insurance, Transit, Data Centers, and Government Sectors, Aurionpro is setting new benchmarks for AI innovation and impact. Its B2E (Business-to-Ecosystem) approach empowers entire ecosystems – driving growth, transformation, and scale across interconnected value chains. Backed by 3,000+ experts and a global-first mindset, Aurionpro is built to lead the next.

Aurionpro Solutions Limited (BSE: 532668 | NSE: AURIONPRO), a global leader in banking technology, has successfully gone live with its next-generation cash management platform, iCashpro, at Commercial Bank of Ceylon (CBC) in Sri Lanka. The development marks a major milestone in CBC’s ongoing digital transformation journey, aimed at strengthening its corporate and institutional banking operations.
Under the partnership, Aurionpro has implemented its advanced payments and portals stack within the iCashpro platform at CBC. This deployment lays the groundwork for a comprehensive modernization of the bank’s cash management infrastructure, enabling enhanced efficiency, scalability, and operational control. These additions are designed to deliver a unified, end-to-end digital experience for corporate and institutional clients, helping businesses manage their treasury functions more effectively through a single integrated platform.
Recognized as a leader at the 2025 Euromoney Transaction Banking Awards, Commercial Bank of Ceylon continues to invest in cutting-edge infrastructure to meet evolving corporate banking needs. The successful go-live of iCashpro underscores the bank’s commitment to innovation, resilience, and customer-centric digital solutions within Sri Lanka’s banking sector.
