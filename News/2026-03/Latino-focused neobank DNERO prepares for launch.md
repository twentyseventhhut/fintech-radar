# Latino-focused neobank DNERO prepares for launch

> [!info] 2026-03-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌎 Latino-focused neobank DNERO gears up for launch. DNERO will initially launch by conducting transactions between the US and Mexico, though it plans to branch out to El Salvador and Colombia. Following this, there will be a "phased rollout across up to 22 Latin American countries", according to a company statement.
