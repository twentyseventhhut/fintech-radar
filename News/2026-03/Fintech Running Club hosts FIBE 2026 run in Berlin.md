FRC x NAO for FIBE
​ AN EARLY MORNING RUN & COMMUNITY KICK-OFF 
​ As the energy builds around FIBE 2026, FinTech Running Club is teaming up with NAO to bring the community together in a more human way. This is more than just a run, it’s about creating space to connect beyond business cards and panels.
​ All Are Welcome: Run, Connect, or Simply Show Up 
​ Whether you’re ready to join a morning run or just want to meet like-minded people over coffee, there’s a place for you.
​ The Run: Robin Binder (CEO of NAO and FRC’s Berlin host) will lead a social run through the Tiergarten. Expect a relaxed, conversational pace, perfect for warming up both your body and your network before a full day at FIBE.
​ Not a runner? No problem. Come by for coffee, connect with the community, and be part of the atmosphere as the group gathers and returns.
​ The Perk: ☕ Free coffee provided by NAO to kickstart your morning
​ The Schedule: 
​ 7:30 AM | Meet-up & warm-up 7:45 AM | Group run begins (5 km at an easy pace) 8:15 AM | Free coffee, conversations & community time
​ If you’re in Berlin for FIBE 2026, this is your chance to start the day differently, moving, connecting, and building momentum with people from across the ecosystem.
​ 📅 April 16th ⏰ 7:30 AM ☕ Free coffee by NAO 👉 RSVP to join
​ About FinTech Running Club (FRC) FRC is a global community bringing together people from fintech, tech, and beyond to connect through movement. From first-time runners to seasoned athletes, everyone is welcome. No pressure, just good vibes and great people. 🌍💬
​ About NAO NAO makes investments in private and alternative markets accessible to everyone, markets that were previously reserved for a financial elite. Starting at just €1, you can invest in private equity, venture capital, infrastructure, and more – flexibly and in collaboration with renowned partners such as Partners Group, ARK Invest, and Goldman Sachs Asset Management.
