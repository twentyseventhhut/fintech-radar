# Stanbic, IFC and Mastercard back female-owned SMEs in Ghana

> [!info] 2026-03-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇭 Stanbic Bank, IFC and Mastercard collaborate to expand access to finance for female-owned SMEs and professionals in Ghana, under IFC’s Banking on Women programme. The companies have committed $600,000 to expand access to capital for female-owned SMEs, strengthen Stanbic Bank’s capacity to serve this segment, and promote gender-responsive banking to support inclusive growth in Ghana.
