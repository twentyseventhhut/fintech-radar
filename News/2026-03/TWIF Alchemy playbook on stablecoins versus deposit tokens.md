Which path fits your institution?
Stablecoin rails
Global reach & cost reduction
 ✓ Cross-border at 0.1-0.5% vs 6.6%
 ✓ No correspondent banking needed
 ✓ Capture $40-50M yield per $1B supply
 ✕ Cannot pay interest to holders
 ✕ No FDIC insurance
 Best for: Remittances, merchant settlement, closed-loop ecosystems, emerging markets
Deposit tokens
FDIC-insured efficiency
 ✓ Full FDIC coverage up to $250K
 ✓ Can pay interest to holders
 ✓ Native core banking integration
 ✕ Requires banking relationship
 ✕ Limited global reach
 Best for: Intra-bank liquidity, corporate treasury, wholesale banking, programmable credit
