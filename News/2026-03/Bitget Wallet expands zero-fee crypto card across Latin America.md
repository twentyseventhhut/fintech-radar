# Bitget Wallet expands zero-fee crypto card across Latin America

> [!info] 2026-03-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇸🇻Bitget Wallet, the self-custodial crypto wallet based in El Salvador, expanded its zero-fee crypto card across Latin America through a partnership with Immersve. The Mastercard-powered card allows users to spend USDC directly from their wallets and is rolling out in markets including Argentina, Mexico, Colombia, Chile, Peru, and Uruguay.
