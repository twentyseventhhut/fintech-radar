# BlockFills Seeks Restructuring Amid Legal Challenges

Phemex News 2026/03/07 01:23

Share

BlockFills, a cryptocurrency trading and lending firm, is pursuing restructuring options following a suspension of client withdrawals and involvement in legal proceedings. The company has engaged Berkeley Research Group (BRG) for restructuring advice and appointed BRG's Mark Renzi as Chief Transformation Officer. This move comes after a U.S. federal judge issued a temporary restraining order on February 11, halting client withdrawals.
