# Vipps sells MobilePay Checkout merchant contracts to Kustom

> [!info] 2026-03-30 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇪 Vipps sells its cash register solution, Vipps MobilePay Checkout's merchant contracts, to Kustom. The acquisition means that Kustom may add over 3,000 additional merchants and increase transaction volume of more than NOK 7 billion. As part of the deal, Kustom will also become the recommended checkout partner for Vipps MobilePay.
