# Bunq ordered to pay €315,000 to unblock bankrupt customer

> [!info] 2026-03-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Bunq must pay 315,000 euros to unblock a bankrupt customer. That person then received payments to withdraw or transfer them to other accounts. The bank must also pay €8,477.04 in legal costs. The judge rejected Bunq's argument that the entire course of events is attributable to the actions of the trustee.
