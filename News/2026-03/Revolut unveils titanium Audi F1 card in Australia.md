Revolut unveils titanium Audi F1 card for fans in Australia
 
Revolut has launched a limited-edition payment card linked to the Audi Revolut F1 Team, featuring a titanium coating that matches materials referenced in the team's new Formula 1 car.
The card is available via the Revolut app in Australia for AUD $209.99, plus delivery. Availability is limited and will be sold while stocks last. It is only available to customers aged 18 and over.
Revolut is positioning the release as the official card for the Audi Revolut F1 Team, which is set to begin its first season on the grid. Customers can also join a waitlist to be notified when the card becomes available.
Design and materials are central to the launch. The card is coated in the same type of high-grade titanium used in the R26, the team's 2026 car. Revolut describes it as "lightweight, sharp and streamlined", with styling that draws on aerodynamic cues.
The launch is part of a broader commercial push spanning payments, loyalty and marketing. Revolut is offering enhanced rewards on team merchandise purchases made with Revolut Pay, its online checkout option.
 Rewards tie-in 
Under the promotion, Revolut customers can earn 20x RevPoints on official merchandise purchased through the team's online store when they check out using Revolut Pay. The points sit within Revolut's RevPoints loyalty programme, which the company has been expanding across markets to drive engagement beyond banking services.
Revolut is framing the fan rewards as an immediate benefit of its Formula 1 partnership. The offer covers products including caps, T-shirts and jackets, according to the announcement.
The launch also includes an in-person activity in Melbourne around race weekend. Revolut will host a club night called "OverTake: The After Party", featuring a headline set from DJ Baby J. Customers in Melbourne will also be able to access events hosted by both the team and Revolut.
The campaign extends into out-of-home and digital advertising. Revolut has launched a new above-the-line campaign, "Overtake", in Australia, including tram wraps and digital installations.
 Australia push 
The card and fan programme arrive as Revolut highlights its Australian user base and local growth plans. Revolut says it has more than 1 million customers in Australia, within a global base of more than 70 million.
Revolut has been using partnerships and consumer-facing features to increase transaction volume and encourage customers to use its app as a primary money-management tool. Payments and loyalty initiatives, including Revolut Pay and RevPoints, are central to that strategy.
The tie-up also gives the fintech a platform to build brand awareness in a high-profile sports setting. Formula 1 continues to attract global audiences and significant sponsorship spend, with teams seeking partners that can fund operations and provide marketing reach.
For the Audi Revolut F1 Team, the card extends branding beyond trackside assets and merchandise, using a daily-use financial product as another touchpoint with fans. Revolut says the design incorporates the team's visual identity.
The announcement also highlighted the team's 2026 driver line-up, naming Nico Hulkenberg and Gabriel Bortoleto as pilots of the R26.
Antoine Le Nel, Revolut's Chief Growth and Marketing Officer, said: "From day one, our ambition for this partnership has been clear: put the fans first, and we're excited to reveal the Official Audi Revolut F1 Team titanium-coated card. Designed for racing enthusiasts who value performance, precision and premium craftsmanship, it offers a tangible way to commemorate a new era of racing.
"Melbourne is set to be an unforgettable start to the season, both on and off the track and across the city, and our new campaign captures that energy. We couldn't be more excited for lights out and kicking off the season in true Revolut style," he added.
"Our partnership with Revolut is built on a shared ambition to deliver performance-driven experiences with precision and purpose. Seeing our design language translated onto the Revolut card is a strong expression of that alignment. The Audi Revolut F1 Team skin captures key elements of our visual identity, extending our philosophy beyond the car and into everyday life for our fans," said Stefano Battiston, Chief Commercial Officer, Audi Revolut F1 Team.
