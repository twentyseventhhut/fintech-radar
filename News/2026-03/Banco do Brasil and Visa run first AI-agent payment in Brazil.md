**Banco do Brasil concluded yesterday (11) the first agency transaction in the country, using the Visa Intelligent Commerce (VIC) platform. This achievement marks a turning point in Brazilian e-commerce, which is celebrating 30 years, and paves the way for a new model of digital consumption.**

The operation was carried out in a controlled production environment. An artificial intelligence agent executed a payment on behalf of the client, using the BB Visa card. The process utilized tokenization, that is, the replacement of the real card data with encrypted codes, and integrated authentication, with real-time risk monitoring by the Visa network.

**What is agent commerce?**

In agent commerce, the consumer pre-authorizes an AI agent to perform steps of the purchase within limits defined by themselves. The agent can search for products, compare prices, and initiate payments autonomously, without the user needing to intervene at each step.

Visa Intelligent Commerce offers APIs, that is, programming interfaces that connect different systems, so that these transactions occur securely and in compliance with regulatory standards.

**Security at the center of the process**

Authentication, tokenization, and security controls of the operation were managed by Visa's global infrastructure. The model allows issuers like Banco do Brasil to offer transactions initiated by agents without exposing sensitive cardholder data.

Pedro Bramont, director of Payment Methods and Services at Banco do Brasil, highlighted that the initiative enhances convenience for customers without sacrificing the security and reliability of the bank.

Rodrigo Cury, president of Visa Brazil, stated that the transaction demonstrates, in practice, how the Brazilian market is being prepared for the next generation of digital commerce.

**Next steps**

With the transaction completed, authorized agents and merchants will be able to operate with Visa Intelligent Commerce in Brazil. The expectation is for broader adoption of the technology in the country's payment ecosystem.
