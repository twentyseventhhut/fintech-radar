In a strategic move to enhance its market position, Global Payments Inc. GPN +3.47% 83, a Georgia-based corporation, has announced the completion of its acquisition of Worldpay Holdco, LLC from Fidelity National Information Services, Inc. and affiliates of GTCR LLC. Concurrently, Global Payments has divested its Issuer Solutions business to FIS. This simultaneous acquisition and divestiture mark a significant restructuring effort by the company.
