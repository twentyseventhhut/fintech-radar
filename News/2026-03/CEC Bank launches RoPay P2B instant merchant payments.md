CEC Bank launches RoPay P2B for instant payments to merchants
 CEC Bank has expanded its RoPay payment solutions with the launch of RoPay P2B (Person-to-Business), a feature that allows individuals to make instant payments to merchants directly from the CEC mobile banking app, without using a card or cash. 
The new functionality enables customers to complete payments by scanning a QR code displayed in stores, with funds transferred instantly from account to account.
“Through RoPay P2B, our customers can pay quickly and securely by scanning the QR code displayed in stores. The solution stands out through ease of use and instant transfers, giving merchants immediate access to funds (account-to-account transfer). At CEC Bank, legal entities are exempt from fees until 30.06.2026. We continue to invest in digital technologies that make banking more intuitive and more user-friendly for all users,” said Mugur Podaru, Director of the Remote Operations Directorate at CEC Bank. 
RoPay P2B is designed for small merchants operating a single point of sale who use the CEC mobile banking application. Unlike traditional card payments, where settlement can take one or two days, payments made through RoPay P2B are processed instantly.
To receive payments, the merchant must enrol in the CEC mobile banking application and generate a QR code that can be displayed to the payer. The payer then opens their mobile banking app, scans the QR code, and confirms the payment amount. Read more about CEC Bank finances strategic investment in Romania’s pig farming sector read more 
CEC Bank customers using RoPay as payers currently have several payment options available, including scanning QR codes displayed at POS terminals or on stickers, QR codes generated within mobile banking applications, and QR codes printed on invoices.
Payments can also be made on online merchant websites by scanning a QR code, through redirection to a mobile banking application, or by using a phone number. The system also allows users to send or request payments using phone numbers. 
RoPay is part of Romania’s national instant mobile payments system developed by Transfond together with financial institutions. The service enables users to perform instant transfers without fees and to pay merchants directly from their mobile banking applications.
