# This Week in Fintech: Why fintechs compete for bank sponsors

> [!info] 2026-03-24 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] For financial institutions, the “Traditional banks vs. Neobanks” narrative often frames competition to offer banking services as a zero-sum game. This ignores an underlying reality: It may seem that most fintechs need sponsor banks (i.e. traditional financial institutions) to provide the banking & money movement services that power their digital financial experiences. While the opportunity to embed financial products in new solutions is still massive– eighty percent of the $185 billiontotal addressable potential for embedded finance is still untapped– one of the biggest constraints to building embedded finance is that demand for sponsor banks now far exceeds supply. As a result, banks are now increasingly selective about their fintech partners.
