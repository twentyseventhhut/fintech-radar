Mastercard has elevated Anoop Jose as Director, Marketing Planning and Analysis, APAC.

He will collaborate with regional and market teams to steer large-scale initiatives and marketing programmes across the region.

Anoop Jose previously served as B2B Marketing Manager for Mastercard ANZ and has been associated with the company for close to three years.

Jose has also previously worked with Dentsu International as an Account Director.

**Anoop Jose** has been elevated to _Director, Marketing Planning and Analysis_, APAC at **Mastercard**, where he will lead planning, marketing strategy, and analytics initiatives across the region.

He announced this move via alinkedin post.

He previously served as B2B Marketing Manager at Mastercard ANZ from August 2023 to March 2026, where he led marketing strategy, planning, and campaign execution across B2B segments.

Before Mastercard, Jose was Senior Account Director at Green Hat in Sydney from August 2022 to August 2023, spearheading strategic marketing initiatives for global clients, including Nestle and Starbucks, with a focus on account-based marketing, digital performance, and social media campaigns.

He also worked with Dentsu International as Account Director for Merkle B2B APAC from January 2022 to July 2022, managing the Mastercard APAC B2B account and delivering data-driven media, creative, and technology integration for over a hundred clients globally. During September 2021 to January 2022, he served at Dentsu Mcgarrybowen overseeing communication development and client management for American Express across consumer and commercial services.

From 2005 to 2019, Jose was Group Account Director at BPG Group, WPP Group, Dubai, UAE, responsible for strategic marketing planning, digital strategy, and go-to-market execution for major clients.
