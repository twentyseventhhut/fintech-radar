FinTech Running Club ft. Rain
​ We’re a global community bringing together people from FinTech, tech/finance, and beyond to connect, run, and have fun . Our runs are super casual and open to everyone, from first-timers to seasoned runners. No pressure, just awesome vibes and good people. We'll plan to get coffee after at Cafe Ambrosia (on W 23rd St) on Rain!
​ Run details
​ Meeting point: Pier 66 at Hudson River Park . 
​ Distance: 3.1 miles (5km)
​ Route: We will head south from Pier 66 all the way to Pier 40 and back. Then we'll walk together to Cafe Ambrosia to enjoy some coffee with the Rain team!
​ Pace: We’ll have three pace groups, each led by a designated pace leader: 8 min/mile, 9 min/mile, and 10 min/mile.
