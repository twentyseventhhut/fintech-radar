# Brex launches ChatGPT integration for expense data

> [!info] 2026-03-09 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Brex has introduced a ChatGPT integration that allows users to access and analyze their expense data through natural language queries. The feature enables employees and admins to quickly check spending details, policy requirements, limits, and reimbursement status without leaving their workflow.

Brex in ChatGPT: Your expenses, one question away 
 AI-native finance, now in ChatGPT 
Brex is now available in the ChatGPT app marketplace. It's part of how we're bringing intelligent finance to the tools you already use. Need to check a limit balance, find an expense, or look up a policy? Now you can just ask ChatGPT. Connect your account to get started.
 Compliance in fewer clicks 
Staying on top of expenses means juggling a lot. Which expenses need documentation? How much is left on my limit? What's our policy on client dinners? The answers are easy to find in Brex, but when you're in the middle of something else, even a quick lookup is a context switch. For managers and admins, that friction is only multiplied.
 Get instant answers by using Brex in ChatGPT 
Now you have conversational access to your Brex data in ChatGPT based on your Brex permissions, so you can easily get answers about your spending.
 For everyone: 
Check which expenses need receipts or memos
See your limits and available balances in real time
Find expenses by merchant, amount, or date
View your cards, their statuses, and shipping details
Track reimbursement status and expected payout dates
Ask policy questions without reading entire docs
 For admins: 
Analyze company-wide expense data with natural language queries
 This is only the beginning 
Today's release is read-only by design: you can safely get answers about your spending in ChatGPT, and take any required actions in Brex. We're working on expanded functionality, including write capabilities, as part of our broader intelligent finance vision.
Inside Brex, we already automate receipt matching and expense memo generation, and answer policy questions instantly. Plus, our integrations with Slack , Uber , and thousands of other apps reduce friction across workflows. Brex in ChatGPT extends this idea: meet people where they already are, so they can spend smarter and move faster.
 Use Brex in ChatGPT today 
Brex is available now in ChatGPT for ChatGPT Enterprise users. Connect your account to start getting answers to your Brex questions today.
 Brex is proud to be one of the first financial apps in OpenAI's ChatGPT app marketplace.
