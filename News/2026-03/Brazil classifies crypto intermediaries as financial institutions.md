# Brazil classifies crypto intermediaries as financial institutions

> [!info] 2026-03-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷Brazil’s Central Bank and the National Monetary Councilruled that crypto intermediaries, custodians, and brokers will be treated as financial institutions under Brazilian law. The classification brings virtual asset service providers under the country’s bank secrecy rules and other financial system obligations.
