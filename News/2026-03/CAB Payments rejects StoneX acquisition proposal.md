# CAB Payments rejects StoneX acquisition proposal

> [!info] 2026-03-17 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 CAB Payments rejects StoneX's possible acquisition offer. CAB Payments said today that the Independent Board, together with its advisers, has carefully evaluated the StoneX Possible Offer and concluded unanimously that it significantly undervalues CAB Payments and its prospects.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 StoneX makes a cash proposal to acquire CAB Payments. StoneX believes that its Proposal would provide a compelling opportunity for CAB Payments’ shareholders to monetise their holdings in CAB Payments for cash at an attractive valuation, which is also at a significant premium to its recent and undisturbed share price and the Helios Consortium’s firm offer.
