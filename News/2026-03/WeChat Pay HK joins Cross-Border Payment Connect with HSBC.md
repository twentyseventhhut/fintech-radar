# WeChat Pay HK becomes the first e-wallet to participate in the 'Cross-Border Payment Connect,' partnering with HSBC to provide real-time transfers to bank accounts in mainland China.

AASTOCKS·Mar 2 04:28

TCEHY-0.20%HSBC HOLDINGS+1.26%TENCENT+0.04%TENCENT-R 0.00%

WeChat Pay HK has participated in the 'Cross-Border Payment Connect' jointly launched by the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) and the People's Bank of China (PBOC), becoming the first e-wallet to offer this service. Under the newly added 'Cross-Border Payment Connect' feature, its 'Faster Payment System' (FPS) Hong Kong dollar settlement service is provided by HSBC, meaning that WeChat Pay HK users can now transfer funds to mainland bank accounts anytime and anywhere via their mobile phones.

The Hong Kong-to-Mainland transfer service provides 7x24 real-time small-amount remittances to accounts at participating mainland banks, with the system using real-time exchange rates for Hong Kong dollar settlements. Users holding mainland identity cards can use the 'Cross-Border Payment Connect' instant transfer to Hong Kong through the mobile applications of participating mainland banks between 7:00 AM and 11:00 PM daily.

Tencent Fintech Vice President Daniel Hong stated that WeChat Pay HK becoming the first e-wallet to participate in the 'Cross-Border Payment Connect' will help promote financial interconnectivity between the two regions and facilitate regional economic and trade exchanges. The company has been actively collaborating with various financial institutions, leveraging cutting-edge technology to provide secure, reliable, and convenient electronic payment experiences.

 WeChat Pay HK has become the first e-wallet to offer cross-boundary instant payments via Payment Connect, the linkage between Mainland China’s Internet Banking Payment System (IBPS) and Hong Kong’s Faster Payment System (FPS).
The service enables users to transfer funds from Hong Kong to Mainland bank accounts instantly using their mobile devices.
 HSBC has supported this launch, building on its long-standing collaboration with WeChat Pay HK to settle funds in Hong Kong dollars through FPS.
With Payment Connect, users can now make transfers at any time, with real-time settlement in both Hong Kong dollars and renminbi.
The service operates without service fees and utilises established banking infrastructure to provide secure and reliable transactions.
The service helps business professionals, families, and travellers manage cross-boundary funds more easily.
The new feature is available through the “Transfer to Chinese Mainland” option in the WeChat Pay HK wallet.
