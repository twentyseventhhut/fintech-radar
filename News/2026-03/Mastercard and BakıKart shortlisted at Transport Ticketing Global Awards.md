# Mastercard and BakıKart shortlisted at Transport Ticketing Global Awards

> [!info] 2026-03-06 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇿 Mastercard and BakıKart have been named finalists at the 2026 Transport Ticketing Global Awards for their project transforming Baku’s transit system into an EMV-enabled open-loop ecosystem handling around 60 million monthly journeys. Developed with K Group and local partners, the initiative enables passengers to pay seamlessly with bank cards.
