## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 VALT Bank gets a conditional OCC charter. The bank must raise $25 million before opening and maintain a 9% tier 1 leverage ratio for its first three years thereafter. The newcomer will serve “digitally demanding” small to midsize businesses with $2 million to $10 million in sales.
