# Alive Ventures closes $55 million second impact fund

> [!info] 2026-03-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎Alive Ventures, the Colombian impact investment firm, closed a $55M second fund (ALEG II), backed by investors including Proparco, FMO, and Impact Fund Denmark. The fund targets early growth-stage companies, particularly pre-Series A and Series A, with a focus on financial inclusion and climate solutions in Colombia and Peru.❝
