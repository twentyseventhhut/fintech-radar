# Federal Reserve orders OakNorth to set up UK holding company

> [!info] 2026-03-24 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Federal Reserve orders Oaknorth to set up UK holding firm. OakNorth has been asked by the U.S. Federal Reserve to move its holding company from Jersey to the UK as a condition for approving its acquisition of Community Unity Bank. The change would support regulatory approval and the bank’s broader expansion in the U.S. market.
