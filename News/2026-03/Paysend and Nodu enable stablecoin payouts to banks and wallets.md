# Paysend and Nodu enable stablecoin payouts to banks and wallets

> [!info] 2026-03-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Paysend and Nodu bring stablecoin payouts to bank accounts & wallets. The integration allows institutions to convert stablecoins into 43 currencies and initiate payouts across 62 countries via Paysend’s global network. The companies said the offering is among the first off-ramp solutions at this scale.
