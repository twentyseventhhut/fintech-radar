Richard Davies
CEO
 Allica Bank 

 Richard is CEO of Allica, a digital bank focused on established SMEs that has lent over £4bn since launching in 2020, and named by Deloitte in 2023 and 2024 as the fastest growing technology company in the UK, and by the FT in 2025 as the second fastest growing company in Europe. 
Previously he was COO at Revolut, as it scaled from 5 million to 15 million customers and launched in various new geos, and he was the inaugural CEO at OakNorth.
Divya Bhardwaj 
COO
 Uber Payments 

 Divya is a seasoned FinTech leader bringing over 16 years of industry experience. Her work with various global organizations across the payments value chain, including Acquirers, Issuers, Money Remitters, Merchants, and Marketplaces, has equipped her with hands-on knowledge in payment operations, product innovation, regulatory strategy, and payment partnerships. Currently, Divya serves as the Chief Operating Officer at Uber Payments UK, where she is responsible for executing robust first-line operations to ensure consistently positive customer outcomes.
 
Miguel Angel Alcala
Managing Director, Head of Europe
 BBVA Spark 

 With over 25 years of experience in financial services, Miguel Alcalá has developed his professional career in the areas of equity and debt investments, strategy, and operations. Since 2025, he has been Head of BBVA Spark in Europe. His career at BBVA began in 2006 and has spanned various positions, including his role as Head of the Financial Sector in the Equity Research team at Global Markets, Deputy Director in the Office of the Chairman (2011-2017), and Executive Director in Client Solutions (2017-2022). Before joining BBVA, Miguel worked in Corporate Finance, M&A, and Audit. His experience also includes more than a decade as a member of the board of directors in companies within the technology sector as well as the real economy. 
 
Clare Gambardella
CCO
 Zopa Bank 

 Clare is responsible for Zopa’s brand, marketing, communications and product design. She leads customer lifecycle – driving customer experience and value in cross-product channels like the App, AI Assistant and CRM. Her and her team also shape Zopa’s wider contribution to the industry through ESG programs like the Fintech Pledge and Jobs 2030. Prior to Zopa, Clare was the Group Chief Marketing Officer at Virgin Active, overseeing brand and marketing for the group's international network. She began her career at Procter and Gamble and has also worked in Strategy Consulting at The Boston Consulting Group. 
 
Pol Navarro
Managing Director
 Banco Sabadell 

  
 
Heidrun Luyt 
Chief Growth Officer 
 Qonto 

 Heidrun Luyt is the Chief Growth Officer at Qonto, the leading European business finance solution for SMEs and freelancers. Graduated from the University of Mannheim, Luyt has almost two decades of experience in driving growth in the international SaaS, fintech, and retail industries. She has a proven track record in brand development, go-to-market strategies and customer experience, having held leadership roles at IG Group, Pipedrive, David Lloyd Leisure, Le Creuset, and Peek & Cloppenburg. 
At Qonto, she leads a team of over 300 people spanning local and global Sales, Acquisition, Client Development, Brand & Communications, as well as Growth Projects & Operations teams. Her strategic vision and leadership will be instrumental in Qonto's mission to make day-to-day business finance management easier for SMEs and freelancers across Europe. 
Manuel Costescu
Managing Director
 JP Morgan 

 Mr. Costescu is based in London and leads the global JPMorgan Fintech franchise for the corporate bank. Until recently, he led the New Economy team, helping EMEA’s fastest growing Fintechs, SAAS, and e-commerce companies scale internationally. 
Previously, Mr. Costescu served as Member of the Romanian Parliament and State Secretary for Trade and Investment. During his tenure, he reformed Romania’s Investment Agency and acted as investment and economic advisor to the Prime Minister during international diplomatic missions. 
Throughout his career, Mr. Costescu served in various corporate and financial leadership positions managing an investment portfolio in Emerging Markets, running an equity raising platform for start-ups in London, and setting the European expansion strategy for a UK based Credit fund. 
Mr. Costescu started his career spending more than a decade as a strategy consultant for McKinsey and Company in NY and a banker for JPMorgan, covering European Public Sector clients. 
Mr. Costescu is a trustee of the 5Rights Foundation which safeguards child online safety. 
Bianca Zwart
Chief Strategy Officer
 bunq 

 As bunq’s Chief Strategy Officer, Bianca keeps the company laser-focused on its mission: building the first bank people actually love to use. She shapes bunq’s long-term strategy and steers the teams that keep the company centered on users’ wants and needs.  
  
From entry level employee to CSO in less than 10 years – and all before the age of 33 – Bianca’s journey at bunq started in 2016 on the frontlines of user support. After rising to Head of PR & Communications, she left to launch and scale two successful companies – Black & White Communications, and Pioneer Academy – before rejoining bunq as its first Chief of Staff in 2022. Now, as CSO, she drives the strategy that powers bunq’s global growth and leads the company’s largest division, focused on making life easy for users everywhere.  
  
Having lived an international lifestyle herself, Bianca brings the same borderless perspective as bunq’s users. That experience fuels her passion to redefine banking: so that people don’t have to slow down their international lifestyle to fit a bank, but have one that empowers it. 
 
Christos Chamberlain
General Manager, UK & Europe
 Airwallex 

 Christos Chamberlain is General Manager of Airwallex for UK and Europe. Airwallex is a leading financial platform building the future of global banking for modern businesses. By combining proprietary infrastructure with software and AI, Airwallex is reimagining how businesses manage accounts, access capital, control spend, and embed financial services. Prior to Airwallex, Christos served as General Manager at Flexport, where he partnered with businesses to overcome the regulatory, financial and operational complexities of cross-border trade. Earlier in his career, he held senior operational roles in high-growth ecommerce companies, giving him first-hand experience of the financial bottlenecks that constrain global expansion. 
 
Keith Grose
UK CEO
 Coinbase 

 Keith is the UK CEO at Coinbase, based in London, leading product and business strategy in one of the world’s most dynamic fintech markets. A seasoned executive with a passion for building financial infrastructure, prior to joining Coinbase Keith led the establishment of Plaid’s UK and European operations, driving its growth as one of the largest private fintech companies globally, and served as COO of SequenceHQ, focusing on B2B payments. Keith began his career in Silicon Valley as a consultant at Bain & Company before spending many years at Google, where he played a key role in scaling the payments and mobile hardware divisions. As an investor, Keith supports ambitious founders driving financial freedom through technology with a focus on fintech & crypto startups.
 
Yorick Naeff
CIO
 ABN AMRO 

 Yorick Naeff is a Dutch fintech leader best known as CEO and co-founder of BUX, the European mobile-investing platform serving over half a million clients. With a background at ING in strategy and innovation, he helped scale BUX across multiple markets and eventually sold BUX to ABN AMRO and Coinbase. 
Following BUX’s acquisition, Yorick recently stepped into the role of Chief Innovation at ABN AMRO Bank, where he’s driving breakthroughs in AI, digital assets, and next-generation financial services. He is also an entrepreneur in residence at Techleap, he serves in the ETF advisory board of JPM’s Asset Management division, and he's a member of the International Advisory Board of the VU University in Amsterdam.
