# GoTyme Bank shifts to phygital model with mall customer hubs

> [!info] 2026-03-23 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇿🇦 GoTyme, Africa’s first profitable digital bank, gets physical. GoTyme Bank is shifting to a more “phygital” model by replacing its in-store kiosks with mall-based Customer Hubs, offering services like account opening, card issuance, and support. The move reflects a growing need for physical presence, even among digital-first banks in South Africa.
