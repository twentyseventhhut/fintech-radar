# Japan plans to reclassify XRP as financial product by 2026

> [!info] 2026-03-17 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇵 Japan plans to reclassify XRP as a financial product by 2026. If implemented, the move would transfer XRP from the current “crypto asset” category under the Payment Services Act to the more stringent Financial Instruments and Exchange Act (FIEA) framework.
