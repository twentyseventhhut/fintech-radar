## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇪 Main Irish banks to launch mobile payments service. Customers of AIB, Bank of Ireland, and PTSB will be able to send, request, and split payments under the new system this week. The Zippay service will be rolled out on a phased basis, with the potential to reach more than five million eligible customer accounts of the three retail banks.
