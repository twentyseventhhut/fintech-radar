# Getnet expands Tap on Phone to Tap on Everything in Brazil

> [!info] 2026-03-17 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Getnet is expanding its Tap on Phone concept to Tap on Everything,aiming to enable contactless payments on any NFC-enabled Android device, from kiosks and tablets to potential future integrations in cars, TVs, and other smart devices. The company is currently testing the solution with 150 kiosk devices.
