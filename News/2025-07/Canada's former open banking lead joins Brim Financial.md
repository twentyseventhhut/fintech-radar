[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇦 Canada’s former open banking lead, Abraham Tachjian, joins FinTech startup Brim Financial. Tachjian said the role will focus on growing Brim’s business and leading its regulatory matters. He added it’s an opportunity to bring out his “payments nerd side,” and draw on his experience in the legal field, capital markets, and digital banking.
