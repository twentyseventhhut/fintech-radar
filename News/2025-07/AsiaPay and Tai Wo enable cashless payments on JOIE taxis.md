**AsiaPay**, an Asia-Pacific region **digital payment solutions** leader, has partnered with **Tai Wo Management** to implement an **integrated electronic payment system** on the **JOIE taxi booking platform**. The tie-up is expected to enhance convenience for taxi drivers while enhancing passengers' booking and paying experience, complementing the city's drive toward smart mobility.

**Joseph Chan, AsiaPay CEO,** said, "_We are pleased to see AsiaPay leading the digital economy with innovative, user-friendly payment solutions that enrich the spending and travel experience. With Southeast Asia's fintech industry ongoing development, we are dedicated to providing localized, simple, secure, and trustworthy payments_.”

**Key Highlights-**

*   AsiaPay partners with Tai Wo to enable cashless taxi payments on JOIE app
*   System supports credit cards UnionPay AlipayHK WeChat Pay HK and Apple Pay
*   Collaboration aims to boost smart mobility and improve passenger convenience

The system will accommodate various **cashless payment channels** such as credit cards, UnionPay, AlipayHK, WeChat Pay HK, and Apple Pay, with additional options to follow. The solution is also capable of real-time processing and reconciliation, minimizing driver friction and offering passengers flexible, **secure payment** options.

Passengers are now able to book and settle payments directly within the JOIE app, skipping the process of handling cash and offline payments. AsiaPay's platform includes **fraud prevention technologies** and adheres to Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) standards, further enhancing user trust.

**Tai Wo Management** added, _"This collaboration addresses**increasing demands** among drivers and passengers for electronic services. We will continue to work with AsiaPay to identify more innovations, such as dynamic pricing integration."_

With more than 25 years of expertise, AsiaPay's PayDollar solution has developed a **strong reputation** for secure, scalable services, cementing its status as a market leader in digital payments throughout the region.

HONG KONG , July 4, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- AsiaPay, a prominent provider of digital payment solutions in the Asia-Pacific region, announced today a partnership with Tai Wo Management to implement an integrated electronic payment system within the JOIE booking platform. The collaboration is designed to offer taxi drivers more flexible and accessible payment options while streamlining ride-booking and payment processes for passengers, supporting the city's broader transition to smart mobility.
 Helping Fleet Operators Expand Cashless Payment Options 
As digital payment becomes the norm, passenger expectations for cashless transactions continue to climb. AsiaPay has integrated a secure and efficient payment system into the taxi booking platform, supporting a wide range of popular payment methods including credit cards, UnionPay, AlipayHK, WeChat Pay HK, and Apple Pay, with additional options planned. The solution also enables real-time transaction processing and reconciliation. The update simplifies payment for drivers and appeals to passengers who prefer digital payments, enhancing operational efficiency and driving revenue growth.
 Improving the Passenger Experience and Encouraging Sustainable Mobility 
Passengers using JOIE can now book and pay directly through the app, avoiding the inconvenience of cash handling or offline payments. The platform incorporates AsiaPay's fraud prevention technologies and complies with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), ensuring secure transactions and building customer trust. Tai Wo Management stated, "This partnership meets the growing expectations among both drivers and passengers for digital services. We plan to further collaborate with AsiaPay to explore additional innovations, including dynamic pricing integration."
PayDollar, AsiaPay's secure online payment platform, has earned a strong market reputation over more than 25 years. Renowned for its scalability and robust security, the platform remains a leader in the field. Joseph Chan , CEO of AsiaPay, said, "We are proud to see AsiaPay driving the digital economy forward with innovative, easy-to-use payment solutions that enhance the travel and spending experience. As Southeast Asia's fintech landscape continues to evolve, we are committed to delivering localized, simple, secure, and trusted payment services. With the integration of our system into Tai Wo Management's taxi app, we aim to optimize the journey experience for both drivers and passengers while helping merchants grow revenue and expand market reach."
About AsiaPay
Established in 2000 in Hong Kong , AsiaPay Limited is a premier provider of digital payment solutions, technologies, and services, delivering advanced, secure, comprehensive, and cost-effective payment systems to banks, businesses, and e-commerce companies worldwide. Its offerings cover a wide range of payment methods, including international credit and debit cards, e-wallets, QR code payments, and prepaid card systems.
AsiaPay delivers enterprise-grade payment processing and gateway services to financial institutions and is a certified Internet Payment Service Provider (IPSP). Since 2003, the company has been certified by Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and JCB as a 3-D Secure provider, and has maintained compliance with PCI DSS for eight consecutive years. AsiaPay has earned numerous industry accolades by combining its PayDollar platform—which supports multiple currencies, languages, card types, and channels—with an advanced online fraud detection system and a deep commitment to service excellence. Currently, the company works closely with nearly 100 banks and serves top-tier merchants across the APAC region.
To support localized service delivery and expert consulting, AsiaPay has expanded its presence to 17 offices throughout the region, including Hong Kong ; Australia ( Sydney , Melbourne ); Mainland China ( Guangzhou , Shanghai ); Indonesia ( Jakarta , Bali ); Malaysia ; the Philippines ( Manila , Cebu); Singapore ; Taiwan ; Thailand ; Cambodia ; Vietnam ( Ho Chi Minh City , Hanoi ); and India .
For more information, visit http://www.asiapay.com and http://www.paydollar.com .
About JOIE
JOIE is a next-generation taxi fleet in Hong Kong operated by Tai Wo Management. Committed to improving passenger service and updating fleet operations through innovative technology, JOIE seeks to raise the professional standing of drivers and introduce a new vision for urban mobility.
