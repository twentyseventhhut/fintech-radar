Pinterest selects Checkout.com to strengthen global payment performance for its clients

Checkout.com, a leading global digital payments company, today announces that Pinterest, used by 570 million people worldwide, is a new customer of its global digital payments platform. The company will help Pinterest to deliver a better payments experience for its advertisers, supporting its mission to bring everyone the inspiration to create a life they love.  Checkout.com’s global digital payments platform is built to deliver fast, reliable, and secure transactions across global markets. Powered by AI, machine learning and real-time network data, Checkout.com boosts transaction routing to increase acceptance rates and reduce payment failures.  Andrea Acosta, Chief Accounting Officer at Pinterest said: “As Pinterest continues to scale its business, it’s crucial that we offer a global payments experience for our advertising clients that is seamless and reliable. Checkout.com gives us the infrastructure to grow with confidence, supporting our mission of helping our half a billion users create a life they love.” ‍ ‍ Antoine Nougué, Chief Revenue Officer at Checkout.com said: “At Checkout.com, we’re digital by design, with a platform built to help innovative businesses like Pinterest succeed in the digital economy, using payments as an enabler for trust.Working with a visionary platform like Pinterest allows us to demonstrate the scalability and intelligence of our platform, ensuring consistent performance, globally. ”
