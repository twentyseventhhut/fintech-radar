# Standard Chartered launches institutional crypto spot trading

> [!info] 2025-07-16 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 StanChart launches bitcoin and ether spot trading for institutional clients. Institutional clients, including corporates, investors, and asset managers, can now trade digital assets through familiar FX interfaces, and will soon be offered non-deliverable forwards trading, StanChart said in a statement.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇬🇧 StandardChartered has launched digital asset trading for institutional clients allowing clients to custody with their chosen custodian which includes their own custody solution.

[This Week in Fintech] Standard Charteredlaunched spot-trading services for Bitcoin and Ether to institutional clients through its UK branch, becoming the first globally systemically important bank to offer deliverable spot crypto trading.❝
