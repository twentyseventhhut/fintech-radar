## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Coinbase brings leverage to the masses with perpetual futures. The digital asset announced that American individuals can now access “perpetual-style” futures contracts for trading Bitcoin and Ether. These are the most popular ways for offshore investors to gain exposure to crypto without holding the digital assets directly and to add leverage that can be as high as 100 times.

[This Week in Fintech] Coinbaselaunched perpetual futures trading in the U.S., enabling customers to trade crypto derivatives natively on its platform.
