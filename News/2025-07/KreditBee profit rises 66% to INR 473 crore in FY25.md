# KreditBee profit rises 66% to INR 473 crore in FY25

> [!info] 2025-07-12 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Kreditbee, a consumer lending startup, reported INR 473 crore ($55M) profit, growing 66% YoY, and INR 2,712 crore ($317M) revenue, growing 40% YoY, with Assets Under Management (AUM) growing at 72% CAGR over the last 3 years.
