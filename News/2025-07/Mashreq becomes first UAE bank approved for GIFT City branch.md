# Mashreq becomes first UAE bank approved for GIFT City branch

> [!info] 2025-07-06 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Mashreq, a UAE-based bank, received in-principle approval from the International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA) to open a branch in Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City), becoming the first UAE bank to do so; branch expected to open by Q4 2025.
