# Brazil's central bank suspends Pix participants after C&M hack

> [!info] 2025-07-07 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 BC suspends Pix participants Transfeera, Nuoro Pay, and Soffy. The decision was taken as a result of the hacker attack that began over the weekend, with the invasion of C&M Software, which affected six institutions. The suspension was based on Article 95-A of BCB Resolution No. 30 of October 29, 2020.
