## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Shareholders of a private market startup vow to fight bankruptcy. Managers of Linqto claimed former executives mismanaged the firm's business by misleading customers into believing they owned shares in privately held companies. Sapien Group said its objectives are to preserve the value of Linqto and protect the value of the shareholders' investments.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Linqto, a new firm that opened the private market to the little guy, is in big trouble. The CEO, William Sarris, was attempting to sell shares of Ripple to Linqto’s 11,000 users at a price at least 60% higher than Linqto originally paid. The SEC prohibits markups above 10% but Sarris pushed ahead, not disclosing the price hike to customers.
