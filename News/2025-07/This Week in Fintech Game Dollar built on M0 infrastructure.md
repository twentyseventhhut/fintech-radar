## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Game Dollar addresses gaming's specific economic challenges using M0's programmable infrastructure. Publishers can turn payment mechanisms into profit centers, using treasury yield for player rewards, game bundles, and discounts impossible with traditional rails. This contrasts sharply with Web3 gaming's failures: "Web3 gaming has mostly focused on financialization of games versus fun. People play games because they're fun."
