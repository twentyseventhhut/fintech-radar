# HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank post double-digit profit growth in Q1 FY26

> [!info] 2025-07-26 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] HDFC Bank and ICICI Bankreported YoY earnings growth in Q1 FY26, with HDFC Bank's net profit rising 12.2% to INR 18,155cr ($2.1bn) and ICICI Bank's net profit increasing 15.4% to INR 12,768cr ($1.5bn).
