## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] PayPalhas been revolutionizing commerce globally for more than 25 years. PayPal empowers consumers and businesses in approximately 200 markets to join and thrive in the global economy, creating innovative experiences that make moving money, selling, and shopping simple, personalized, and secure. They are the largest, two-sided, open commerce platform in the world with 430M+ consumer and merchant accounts. In 2024, PayPal processed more than 26 billion transactions and nearly $1.7 trillion in total payment volume - more than a quarter of the world’s annual ecommerce volume. Learn more at PayPal.com.
