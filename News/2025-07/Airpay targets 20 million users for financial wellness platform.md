## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇳 Airpay targets to onboard 20 million users for a financial wellness platform. Airpay has launched Airpay Money, which is a free financial wellness platform, designed specifically for the underserved middle-class earning between Rs 20,000-75,000 monthly, the company's founder and CEO, Kunal Jhunjhunwala, said.
