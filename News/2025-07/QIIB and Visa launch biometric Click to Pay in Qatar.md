## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇶🇦 QIIB and Visa launch Biometric Click to Pay. Users can employ a unique identifier, such as their email address or phone number, to swiftly complete transactions with participating merchants. Integrated with Visa’s Payment Passkey Service, this solution leverages biometric authentication, eliminating the need for manually entering card details or one-time passcodes.
