# JN Bank launches JN Pay digital wallet in Jamaica

> [!info] 2025-07-28 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇲 JN Bank releases a new digital pay wallet. JN Pay Wallet allows users, whether they have a bank account or not, to access the Central Bank Digital Currency, JAM-DEX, to transfer funds, pay bills, buy mobile credit, and make deposits and withdrawals from a JN Bank account and JN Bank Smart ATM.
