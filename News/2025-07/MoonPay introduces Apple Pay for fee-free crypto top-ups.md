 MoonPay Introduces Apple Pay Integration For Instant, Fee‑Free Crypto Top‑Ups 
 Payments
 08.07.2025 04:15 pm 
 MoonPay, a global leader in crypto payment solutions, today announced the launch of Apple Pay as a new way for users across the EU, UK, and US to top up their MoonPay Balance — offering faster, more secure, and fee-free access to the crypto economy. 
 This new integration marks a significant step in MoonPay’s mission to simplify and democratize access to digital assets. Apple Pay joins the lineup of trusted payment methods available on MoonPay, delivering seamless usability and enhanced flexibility for users, whether they’re taking advantage of market movements or building long-term strategies. 
 “Our goal is to remove friction from the crypto experience,” said [Spokesperson Name], [Title] at MoonPay. “With Apple Pay, we’re giving users a fast, familiar, and secure way to top up their MoonPay Balance — instantly and without fees. It’s all about helping people move at the speed of the market.” 
 Why Apple Pay? 
 Apple Pay is one of the most trusted digital wallets in the world. Now, MoonPay users can enjoy: 
 Zero Fees – No top-up fees, just like with bank transfers and PayPal. 
 Instant Access – Say goodbye to long bank transfer delays. 
 Recurring Buys – Stay consistent with automated crypto purchases. 
 Trusted Security – Leverage Apple’s secure infrastructure and biometric authorization. 
 Streamlined Experience – High acceptance rates and minimal KYC friction. 
 Getting Started is Easy 
 To top up using Apple Pay: 
 Log into your MoonPay account. 
 Tap Deposit, then choose Top up your MoonPay Balance. 
 Select Apple Pay. 
 Confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode. 
 Funds are instantly available, giving users immediate access to purchase crypto, earn yield, or explore the broader digital economy.
