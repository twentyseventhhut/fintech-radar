[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇫🇷 Stripe and TrueLayer partner to launch Pay by Bank integration in France and Germany. With Pay by Bank payments, consumers bypass entering card details and instead authorise payment directly from their bank accounts, typically via biometric methods.
