 UK fintech firm Wise opens global office in Hyderabad, plans major hiring
 
 Currently employing 70 people locally, Wise aims to hire hundreds more across product, engineering, and operations. 
 By BL Hyderabad Bureau 
Share

 Smrithi Ravi, APAC Head of Engineering, Wise
 
The UK-based fintech company Wise has opened its new global office in Hyderabad as part of its expansion plans. The company, which currently has 70 employees on its rolls in Hyderabad, plans to expand its workforce over the next few years. 
“Together with the APAC leadership team, we’ve found immense value in speaking directly with our customers this week to understand their unique cross-border payment needs. We’re truly excited to bring these insights to life with our growing team in Hyderabad and help shape the future of global money movement,” Smrithi Ravi, APAC Head of Engineering at Wise, told the newspersons on Tuesday.

Wise would continue to hire for hundreds more roles across product, engineering, operations, and more in Hyderabad over the next few years. The local team will play a key role in Wise’s mission to become the network for moving and managing the world’s money, building products and features for India, as well as contributing to the company’s global roadmap, she said. 
“The opportunity to be on ground connecting directly with the incredible talent in Hyderabad has been valuable. The caliber of tech and operations professionals we’ve met is exceptional, reinforcing why Hyderabad is the perfect place for us to build our hub,” said Richa Dirani, APAC Head of Talent at Wise.
