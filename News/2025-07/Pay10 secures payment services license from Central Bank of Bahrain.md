# Pay10 secures payment services license from Central Bank of Bahrain

> [!info] 2025-07-09 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇭 Pay10 secures a payment services license from the Central Bank of Bahrain. The license, issued under the Ancillary Services Provider framework, enables Pay10 to integrate directly with Bahrain’s domestic payment systems, including the Fawri+ real-time payments network.
