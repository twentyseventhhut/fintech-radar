# MPS partners Salt Edge on PSD2 open banking APIs

> [!info] 2025-07-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 MPS partners with Salt Edge to launch PSD2-compliant open banking APIs for retail and corporate accounts. Initially available in the Netherlands and France for both retail and corporate accounts, these APIs will be accessible to all licensed third-party providers (TPPs) throughout the European Economic Area.
