# Finzly appoints first CFO and head of partnerships

> [!info] 2025-07-22 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Finzly has appointed Adam Carson as its first Chief Financial Officer, while Robert Coakley has been named as the new Head of Partnerships. Carson brings over two decades of financial expertise to the company. Coakley will focus on expanding Finzly's client portfolio.

Highly experienced industry veterans Adam Carson, CFO and Robert Coakley, Head of Partnerships join executive team 
 I’m excited to join Finzly as its first CFO during this pivotal moment as financial institutions accelerate their payment modernization initiatives.  The company’s innovative platform and solutions set combined with a strong market position present tremendous opportunities for continued growth and value creation. 
 Adam Carson, CFO, Finzly 
 In today’s fintech landscape, success depends on building robust partnership ecosystems that deliver integrated solutions. Finzly’s platform, combined with the right strategic alliances, will accelerate our customers’ modernization and expand our collective market impact. 
 Robert Coakley, Head of Partnerships, Finzly 
 Adam and Robert bring the strategic vision and operational expertise we need as we scale.  Adding our first CFO marks an important milestone in our growth journey, while Robert’s partnership expertise will be crucial as we help more financial institutions modernize and thrive in the real-time economy. 
 Booshan Rengachari, Founder and CEO, Finzly
