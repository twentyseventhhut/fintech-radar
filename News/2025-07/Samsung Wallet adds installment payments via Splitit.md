# Samsung Wallet adds installment payments via Splitit

> [!info] 2025-07-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 New Samsung Wallet feature offers more flexible payment options, powered by a partnership with Splitit. The new feature offers greater flexibility and convenience when paying in-store with Samsung Wallet, allowing customers to separate their purchases into smaller payments. Users in select states beginning July 25, with expansion to all states planned by the end of 2025.
