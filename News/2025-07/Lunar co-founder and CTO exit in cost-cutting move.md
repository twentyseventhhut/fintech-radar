## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇩🇰 Lunar co-founder and CTO exit, in a cost-cutting move. Peter Andreasen, Lunar’s Co-Founder and CIO, and Kåre Kjelstrøm, the CTO of Lunar, have both left. Ken Villum Klausen, Lunar’s CEO and co-founder, said: “These changes reflect a deliberate shift as we move from a phase of rapid growth and expansion into one of operational maturity and profitability”.
