[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Tether expands access to gold-backed stablecoins and continues to assist law enforcement agencies in combating crime. The company has expanded access to its gold-backed stablecoin, Tether Gold, by listing it on Mobee, a regulated digital asset exchange in Indonesia, and has earned recognition for its role in assisting Brazilian authorities during a major anti-money laundering operation.
