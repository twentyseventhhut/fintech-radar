# India mutual fund assets reach ₹65 lakh crore as retail grows

> [!info] 2025-07-26 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Mutual fund assets in India hit INR 65 lakh crore ($751bn), with retail participation growing from 26% to 28% and SIP contributions reaching INR 2.9lakh crore ($33.5bn) in FY2025, driven by fintech platforms and strong equity fund performance.
