> [!info] 2025-07-15 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] In return, Circle gains Coinbase, one of the largest crypto exchanges globally and the largest exchange in the U.S., as a key distribution partner. While USDC is available on many exchanges, Circle only shares revenue in select cases. For example, it has partnered with Binance and is reportedly in talks with Ant Group about a potential distribution deal.
