# Cloudflare rolls out pay-per-crawl micropayments for AI crawlers

> [!info] 2025-07-06 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Cloudflare is rolling out a new pay per crawl feature that will enable content owners to charge AI crawlers for access via micropayments. The micropayment creator compensation product never really took off with human users, but with AIs, it's much simpler to enable micropayments so that creators get paid for their work and models can continue to get better.
