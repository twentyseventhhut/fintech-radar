# SK Finance reports INR 13,261 crore loan book, gets SEBI IPO nod

> [!info] 2025-07-19 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] SK Finance, a Jaipur-based Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC), reported INR 13,261cr ($1.54bn) loan book in FY25, growing 27% YoY, and received SEBI approval for INR 2,200 crore (USD 25.4M) Initial Public Offering (IPO).
