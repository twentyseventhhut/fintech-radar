# Australia advances CBDC with Project Acacia

> [!info] 2025-07-11 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech, This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 Australia advances digital currency exploration with Project Acacia. The Reserve Bank of Australia will collaborate with three of the four major banks and other participants to pilot various digital currency use cases. These include stablecoins, bank deposit tokens, and a wholesale CBDC designed to facilitate credit and liquidity risk-free settlement.

[This Week in Fintech] Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) launched 24 use case trials for tokenised asset markets (crypto and CBDC) involving banks and fintech firms in Project Acacia's next phase.
