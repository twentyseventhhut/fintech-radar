# HSBC completes e-HKD CBDC trials on public blockchains

> [!info] 2025-07-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇳 HSBC completes e-HKD trials of CBDC on public blockchains. The bank said it explored e-HKD payments on various public blockchains, including Arbitrum, Ethereum, Linea, and Polygon. Additionally, within the bank, it developed a private DLT solution using Hyperledger Besu, presumably to act as a tokenization platform before public issuance.
