[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 Monoova, SuperAPI, and Payroo partner to launch one-click payroll and super solution. The new solution combines Monoova’s payments infrastructure, SuperAPI’s onboarding and compliance tools, and Payroo’s cloud-based payroll software to streamline the process and reduce risk. The offering replaces error-prone legacy systems and is already live for Payroo customers.
