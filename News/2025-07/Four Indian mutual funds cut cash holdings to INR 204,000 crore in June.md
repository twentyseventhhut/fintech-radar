# Four Indian mutual funds cut cash holdings to INR 204,000 crore in June

> [!info] 2025-07-19 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 4 Indian mutual funds, including SBI Mutual Fund, ICICI Prudential, HDFC Mutual Fund, and PPFAS Mutual Fund, reduced cash allocation to INR 204,000 crore ($23.7B) in June, representing 5% of total Assets Under Management (AUM).

4 mutual funds had over Rs 20,000 crore cash in their portfolios in June
Cash Pile
MFs reduced their overall cash allocation by Rs 13,000 crore on a monthly basis to Rs 2.04 lakh crore and the cash allocation as a percentage of total AUM also went down to 5.08% in June. Here is breakup of AMCs having over Rs 20,000 crore cash in their portfolios (Source: ACE MF) 
SBI Mutual Fund
SBI Mutual Fund, the largest fund house, had the highest cash allocation in its portfolio in June of Rs 34,654 crore which was 4.68% of the total AUM. The fund house had an equity AUM of Rs 7.06 lakh crore. 
ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund
ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund had Rs 28,196 crore cash in its portfolio in June which was 6.02% of the total AUM. The fund house had an equity AUM of Rs 4.40 lakh crore. 
HDFC Mutual Fund
HDFC Mutual Fund had Rs 27,012 crore cash in its portfolio which was 6.43% of the total AUM. The fund house had an equity AUM of Rs 3.93 lakh crore.
PPFAS Mutual Fund
PPFAS Mutual Fund had Rs 22,778 crore cash in its portfolio which was 19.65% of the total AUM in June against 21.62% in May. The fund house had an equity AUM of Rs 93,171 crore.
Cash as a percentage of AUM
Unifi Mutual Fund had the 25.46% cash as a percentage of AUM because of a small base as the fund house is a new entrant in the mutual fund industry. PPFAS Mutual Fund had 19.65% cash as a percentage of AUM. 
Cash allocation
Fund managers are allowed to keep a part of their portfolio in cash to meet events like redemptions or make investments when they see a possible opportunity in the market.
