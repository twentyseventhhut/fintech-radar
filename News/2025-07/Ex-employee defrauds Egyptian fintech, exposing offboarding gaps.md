## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇬 Ex-employee defrauds Egyptian FinTech and exposes gaps in offboarding practices. The suspect, identified only as a former staff member, contacted clients while posing as a representative of the company, claiming he needed to update or reactivate their wallets. He was able to extract login credentials and one-time passcodes, which he then used to withdraw funds.
