# PayPal mobile money portal shuts down in Kenya

> [!info] 2025-07-30 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇰🇪 PayPal’s mobile money portal shuts down on 16 August 2025. The shutdown arrives just weeks after Safaricom rolled out a dedicated PayPal mini-app inside the M-PESA Super App, allowing instant in-app withdrawals and deposits without browser redirects. By steering users to the Super App, Safaricom can keep transactions inside its growing ecosystem.
