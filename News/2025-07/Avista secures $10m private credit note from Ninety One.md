# Avista secures $10m private credit note from Ninety One

> [!info] 2025-07-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇨🇴 Avista, the Colombian payroll-lending startup for pensioners, secured a $10 million private credit note from Ninety One, arranged by The Rohatyn Group. This marks Ninety One’s first private credit investment in Colombia, enabling Avista to expand lending to underserved senior citizens.
