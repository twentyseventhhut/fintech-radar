**The Future of Payment is Right in Your Palm (Literally).**

Biometric payments are transforming the way we pay, and palm payment technology is taking this transformation to the next level—offering unprecedented speed, convenience, and security. No longer confined to wallets or even smartphones, the future of payment is quite literally in the palm of your hand. Following successful rollouts in countries such as China and the U.S., this technology is rapidly gaining global momentum. Now, with FOMO Pay's newest launch of the Palm Payment solution backed by Tencent's biometric recognition technology, that future has arrived in Singapore—marking one of the country's earliest deployments of its kind.

FOMO Pay's Palm Payment solution has recently been implemented at the Adventure Cove Waterpark in Resorts World Sentosa (RWS), where visitors can pay by simply showing their palm print. In situations where visitors want to enjoy various water activities without carrying their wallets or phones, Palm Payment offers a frictionless alternative—no tapping, scanning, or physical contact required. Just present your palm, and you're done.

This seamless experience is made possible with a one-time enrollment at any designated registration counter. Once set up, users can complete transactions using Weixin (WeChat) Pay by placing their palm over a designated terminal, facilitating an effortless check-out that can be completed within seconds.

As Singapore's leading fintech company, FOMO Pay is committed to advancing the landscape of cashless payments across the country. In this digitally native era, FOMO Pay always strives to provide the most comprehensive and convenient financial and payment services for the market. With the integration of the Palm Payment solution, FOMO Pay revolutionizes everyday transactions, offering users a seamless, simple, and no-mobile-required payment option suitable for diverse settings. Meanwhile, FOMO Pay's robust system enables merchants to process a full suite of payment methods with minimal operational effort and comprehensive reporting.

With the successful launch in Singapore, FOMO Pay will explore opportunities to expand this Palm Payment solution to its merchants across different sectors, such as retail, F&B, hospitality, entertainment, and beyond. The company also plans to broaden the range of payment options compatible with palm recognition—including the integration of credit cards and additional digital wallets—to further enhance flexibility.

As SG60 approaches, FOMO Pay’s Palm Payment solution launch signals more than just a fintech milestone—it marks a deeper commitment to driving innovation in the digital economy, building future-ready infrastructure, and reshaping what the next decade of payments can look like.

**About FOMO Pay**

Founded in 2015, FOMO Pay is a Major Payment Institution licensed in Singapore, Hong Kong and the United Arab Emirates (UAE).
