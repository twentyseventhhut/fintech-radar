# Soft Space and Hokkoku enable stablecoin payments on SoftPOS in Japan

> [!info] 2025-07-02 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇯🇵 Soft Space and Hokkoku enable stablecoin payments on SoftPOS in Japan. The solution will allow merchants in the country to accept both contactless cards and stablecoin payments using only an NFC-enabled Android smartphone or tablet. It is scheduled for launch in Q4 2025.
