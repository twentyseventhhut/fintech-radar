Taktile has once again been recognized as a Category Leader in the G2 Summer 2025 Report for Decision Management Platforms, marking our fourth consecutive quarter earning this distinction.
This recognition is powered by the voices of our valued customers. As risk teams seek more opportunities for automation, self-service, and experimentation, it’s clear that agile decision infrastructure has become mission-critical. At Taktile, we’re excited to help drive that transformation with our AI-powered Decision Platform.
Taktile’s Summer 2025 accolades
We’re proud to have earned top honors across multiple categories, showcasing the tangible value our platform delivers across the full customer lifecycle in risk management. Our key recognitions include:
 Category Leader: Rated highly by G2 users, with substantial Satisfaction and Market Presence scores.
 Momentum Leader: Ranked in the top 25% of decision management platforms by our users.
 Most Implementable: #1 in Implementation, showcasing our seamless onboarding process.
 Easiest Setup: Leading in ease of setup, highlighting the accessibility and simplicity of our platform.
 Highest User Adoption: Recognized for empowering teams with tools they love to use.
 Best Relationship: Top-rated for customer relationships and trust.
 Best Support: Delivering industry-leading customer support to help teams succeed.
 Best Results: Helping customers achieve measurable, impactful results.
 Best Usability: Simplifying complex decision-making with intuitive tools.
 Easiest to Use: The most user-friendly platform in the category.
 Best Meets Requirements: Delivering exactly what teams need to excel.
 Users Love Us: Collected 20+ reviews with an average rating of 4.0 stars.
These accolades reflect the deep collaboration between our product, engineering, and customer teams. Most importantly, they reflect the ongoing partnership we share with the forward-thinking financial institutions we serve. As Maik Taro Wehmeyer, CEO & Co-Founder of Taktile, puts it:

 “These milestones are made possible by the strong relationships we’ve built with our customers, the true catalysts behind our innovation. At Taktile, we’re proud to be building an AI-powered Decision Platform that empowers risk teams to move faster and make smarter business-critical decisions.” 
What do G2 users say about Taktile's Decision Platform?
We love seeing how our platform enables risk teams to drive impact at scale. Here’s a peek at what users are sharing on G2:
 "A versatile platform that bridges strategy to live credit‑decision rules: 
Taktile is helping us automate and streamline our underwriting decision-making. Before, our logic was hard-coded and slow to adapt - every change required engineering time. Now, we can build and iterate on rules ourselves, run A/B tests, and deploy improvements in hours instead of weeks. This flexibility has been key as we scale and refine our risk strategy.”
 "Intuitive, reliable and non-technical friendly: 
Integration was smooth, and its intuitive UI has allowed us to iterate quickly on our credit decision strategies. One of Taktile’s greatest strengths is that it empowers non-technical team members to adjust and refine the decision flows without needing engineering support. Their support team has also been very responsive and helpful whenever questions came up.”
 "Taktile for risk mitigation: 
Taktile solves the challenge of fragmented data and complex decision-making by allowing me to gather information from multiple data sources and consolidate it into a unified risk profile. Its flexible logic engine enables me to build custom decision flows based on the data we collect, significantly improving the efficiency and accuracy of our risk evaluation process.”
