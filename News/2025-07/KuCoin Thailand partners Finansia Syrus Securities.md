KuCoin Thailand Partners with Finansia Syrus Securities to Advance Thailand’s Digital Asset Market, Showing KuCoin’s Global Vision
 We are pleased to highlight a recent strategic partnership between KuCoin Thailand, operated by ERX Company Limited, and Finansia Syrus Securities Public Company Limited (FSS), a leading financial services firm in Thailand. This collaboration marks a pivotal milestone in strengthening Thailand’s digital asset ecosystem by combining FSS’s financial expertise with KuCoin Thailand’s innovative, fully regulated digital asset platform, licensed and supervised by the Securities and Exchange Commission of Thailand.
 Expanding Access to Digital Assets in Thailand and Beyond 
This partnership positions KuCoin Thailand as the preferred platform for FSS clients seeking to explore digital asset investments. By referring investors to KuCoin Thailand’s secure and compliant platform, FSS enhances its service offerings, providing seamless access to the rapid growing world of digital finance. This initiative aligns with KuCoin’s global mission to drive innovation, foster regulatory compliance and empower investors in Thailand’s emerging role as a regional hub for digital assets across all markets where it operates. 
 Mr. Chuangchai Nawongs, Chief Executive Officer of Finansia Syrus Securities, added: “Partnering with KuCoin Thailand enables us to broaden our service offerings and provide our clients with access to a trusted digital asset platform.” 
 A Commitment to Innovation and Compliance 
This partnership between KuCoin Thailand and Finansia Syrus Securities underscores KuCoin’s dedication to supporting localized innovation while adhering to the highest standards of regulatory compliance. By combining cutting-edge technology with trusted financial expertise, this partnership empowers investors to confidently participate in the evolving digital finance landscape.
