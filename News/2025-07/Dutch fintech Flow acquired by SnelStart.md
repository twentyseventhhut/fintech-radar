# Dutch fintech Flow acquired by SnelStart

> [!info] 2025-07-09 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Flow has been acquired by Snelstart. The two companies had previously collaborated on integrating Adyen’s payment infrastructure into Snelstart’s accounting platform. The acquisition is described as a strategic move, with both companies aiming to benefit from each other’s networks. Flow will continue to operate under its name as part of the Snelstart Group.
