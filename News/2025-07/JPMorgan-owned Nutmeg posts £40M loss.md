JPMorgan’s digital wealth manager Nutmeg posted a £40m loss for 2024, despite a jump in assets under management.

The robo-adviser, which was launched in 2012, has not yet turned a profit. However, losses at the company have narrowed slightly from £44m in 2023, according to results for the year ended 31 December 2024.
