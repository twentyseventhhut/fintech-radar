Hey PH Community! 👋

I’m Rishabh, head of product at Skydo, and today we’re excited to share Skydo Payouts with you for the very first time.

**Why we built it**

Running Skydo’s cross-border receivables product put us on weekly calls with founders who rely on talent in India. Every conversation sounded the same:

*   Hidden FX fees: 1-2 % vanishing on every transfer

*   Compliance headaches: no FIRC, endless bank e-mails, month-end panic

*   "Bulk" payouts that weren’t bulk: one wire, one invoice, on repeat

It shouldn’t be harder to pay engineers in Bengaluru than to buy coffee in SF. So we decided to fix payouts once and for all.

**What Skydo Payouts does**

One bulk transfer -> fully compliant INR salaries in every teammate’s bank account the next morning.

*   Transparent pricing – zero per-payee fees, no subscriptions

*   Pay 10 or 10,000 people in one go – upload a CSV, review, hit send

*   Invoices & FIRCs generated automatically – every payout is audit-ready

*   Real-time settlement & QuickBooks sync – funds land fast and ledgers stay tidy

*   Built-in compliance – RBI PACB approval + ISO 27001 & SOC 2 safeguards

Since our private beta opened in February we’ve already moved $1 M+ in monthly payroll, saving customers 100s of hours of payroll processing time and ~60 % on payout costs

**Who it’s for**

*   Founders & finance teams paying Indian contractors or employees

*   Marketplaces and agencies with hundreds of freelancers

*   Payroll / EoR platforms that need a faster India rail

**PH-only perk 🎁**

Sign up with code through product hunt and we’ll waive the platform fee for your first payout month (offer lasts through 31 July 2025).

Check out the link in the hunt, give Skydo Payouts a try, and let us know what you think. I’ll be in the comments all day- ask me anything about RBI rules, FX, or scaling global ops.

Huge thanks to hunting us and to every founder who kept nudging us to build this. 🚀

- Rishabh & the Skydo Crew
