# Pix data of 11 million people leaked in Brazil

> [!info] 2025-07-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Pix data of 11 million people leaked; access to accounts was preserved. The CNJ states that the incident that occurred between Sunday (20) and Monday (21) caused the leak of data from 11,003,398 people. Data such as the person's name, Pix key, bank name, branch number, and account number was accessed.
