KB Financial Group steps up to lead South Korea’s best-performing banks
Leading scores for operational efficiency and asset quality helped KB Financial Group take the top place in South Korea’s best-performing ranking. Second places for growth, profitability and return on risk have helped the bank to an overall performance just ahead of its closest rivals. 
This result comes despite difficult conditions in South Korea, with KBFG seeing a 6.8 per cent decline in Tier 1 capital, a 7.1 per cent fall in assets and a 1 per cent decline in pre-tax profits.
