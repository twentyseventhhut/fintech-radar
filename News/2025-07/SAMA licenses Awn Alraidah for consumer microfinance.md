SAMA Licenses Awn Alraidah Company to Engage in Consumer Microfinance Activity

Tuesday 13/01/1447

Riyadh

Voice reader

Riyadh, July 08, 2025, SPA -- Saudi Central Bank (SAMA) has licensed Awn Alraidah Company to engage in consumer microfinance activity through financial technology. With Awn Alraidah's licensing, the number of companies authorized to offer consumer microfinance solutions in Saudi Arabia has risen to nine. This brings the total number of finance companies licensed by SAMA to 68.

 SAMA’s focus on microfinance reflects its commitment to supporting the financial sector, enhancing the efficiency of financial transactions, and promoting innovative solutions that foster financial inclusion across the Kingdom.

 The bank reiterates the importance of dealing exclusively with authorized financial institutions. A full list of licensed and permitted financial entities is available on SAMA’s official website.

 -- SPA
