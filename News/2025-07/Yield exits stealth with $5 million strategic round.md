# Yield.xyz Secures $5 Million in Strategic Funding Led by Multicoin Capital

Phemex News 2025/06/28 07:01

Share

Yield.xyz has successfully closed a $5 million strategic funding round, with Multicoin Capital leading the investment. The company, previously known as Omni, was acquired by Echo Base earlier this year. Yield.xyz aims to deliver a comprehensive DeFi yield infrastructure for various applications, enhancing its capabilities in the decentralized finance sector.
