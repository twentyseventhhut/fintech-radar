## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇸🇬 Monetary Authority of Singapore fines five payment firms S$960,000 over AML/CFT control failures. The enforcement action follows MAS examinations that found the firms lacked adequate AML/CFT controls, leading to multiple violations of MAS Notice PSN01, which sets out requirements for specified payment services.
