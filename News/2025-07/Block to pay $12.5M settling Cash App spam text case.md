## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Block Inc. settles $12.5 million cash app referral lawsuit. The settlement is specifically relevant to Washington state residents who received the disputed spam messages, with eligibility dependent on having received such texts during the settlement period, which began on November 14, 2019, and will conclude at a date yet to be determined.
