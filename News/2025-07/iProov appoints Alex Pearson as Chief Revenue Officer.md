 iProov Appoints Alex Pearson As Chief Revenue Officer 
 People Moves
 03.07.2025 10:15 am 
 iProov , the world's leading provider of science-based biometric identity verification solutions, has appointed Alex Pearson as Chief Revenue Officer and a member of the Senior Leadership Team. In this role, Alex will be responsible for iProov’s global sales, account management, and customer success functions, and driving the company's revenue growth strategy. 
 With over 27 years of experience in the IT and software industry, including 18 years in international leadership roles, Alex brings a wealth of expertise in building and scaling high-performing revenue organizations in fast-growth enterprise technology environments. Before joining iProov, he was at GRC and then Ideagen, where he ran its global commercial functions through complex restructuring and aggressive growth periods across EMEA, APAC and the US. Prior to that, Alex spent over seven years at Appgate, a cybersecurity SaaS company, where he created and ran the international business across EMEA and APAC since its inception, quadrupling growth across his regions. His career also includes leading CSC’s (now DXC) EMEA Cyber Security business. 
 "Alex is an experienced and talented leader with a sharp commercial instinct and a proven track record in driving growth in the enterprise software and cybersecurity sectors," said Andrew Bud, founder and CEO of iProov. "His expertise in leading teams across diverse markets, regulatory landscapes, and cultural nuances is precisely what we need as we enter our next phase of expansion. He is a great addition to our talented top team, and his appointment will accelerate our growth and solidify our global leadership. He will make a huge impact." 
 Newly appointed Chief Revenue Officer Alex Pearson said, "iProov is a leader in the rapidly advancing biometric identity verification market, processing millions of remote and in-person identity verifications daily. The market opportunity is immense, and the company's commitment to enhancing digital and physical access with unparalleled identity assurance and reliability is unmatched. I’m thrilled to join this talented team and look forward to building a seamless, scalable engine that will accelerate our growth and deliver our market-leading solutions to even more organizations globally.”
