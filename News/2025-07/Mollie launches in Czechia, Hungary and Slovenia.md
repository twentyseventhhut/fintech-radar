Mollie enters the Czech payments market, offers Czech localization and support
Mollie expands into Central Europe expands to support local businesses as the company reduces financial bureaucracy across Europe.
Mollie expands into Central Europe expands to support local businesses as the company reduces financial bureaucracy across Europe.
 July 15, 2025 
 Prague, July 15, 2025 - Mollie, one of the fastest growing financial services providers in Europe, is today launching into the Czech market. This is another significant milestone in the company's mission to make payments and money management effortless for businesses across Europe. With the expansion, Czech businesses have access to a single platform that enables easy payment processing, reporting, and fraud protection.
The expansion into the Czech Republic represents a strategic move for Mollie. While the Czech market is one of the most advanced European ecommerce markets, there’s still lots of potential for improvement in terms of payment methods. Mollie arrives to fix that, offering a single, effortless platform that turns payments into an accelerant for growth, with local payment methods and Czech localization. Mollie’s commitment is the same in every market: build a team of local experts. That’s why they're assembling a dedicated Czech team of payment specialists and sales professionals to work directly with businesses, helping them turn payments into a growth engine.
With the official entry into the market, Mollie's Czech customers can count on a localised platform, easy and fast onboarding, and great support – all in Czech.
Czech customers can also take advantage of existing integrations with major ecommerce platforms such as Shopify, Magento, WooCommerce, PrestaShop,and Shopware for a quick and seamless implementation.
Mollie’s Czech launch is part of their expansion into multiple European countries. Already, Mollie simplifies payments and money management for more than 250,000 businesses.
One of the first Czech clients, founder of Padel Power Vojtěch Vašek, says: "As a fast growing Czech company, we were looking for a payment solution that would grow with us. Mollie proved to be the best choice, their platform is easy to use in day-to-day operations and has greatly simplified our payment processing. Deployment and onboarding was also seamless. We also really appreciate their transparent pricing and great support, which responds very quickly to any of our questions. Mollie is simply a great partner that supports us in our growth."
Koen Köppen, Mollie CEO, says: "We saw the incredible ambition of Czech entrepreneurs and thought that we could offer them the payments and money management services they deserved.
"Like all our customers across Europe, they didn’t start a business to get caught up in financial admin. They started it to create value and build something new. So, our promise is that we'll help them simplify conversion, sales, and cash flow, so they can focus on what they do best. That way we can help the next generation of Czech businesses compete and win, not just at home, but across all of Europe."
About Mollie
Mollie offers a single platform for businesses to get paid and manage their money. One that makes payments, reconciliation, reporting, fraud prevention, and financing simple for all – from startups to enterprises.
Founded in 2004, Mollie’s mission is to make payments and money management effortless for every business in the UK and Europe. Their 850-strong team works from offices across the continent, including Amsterdam, Ghent, Lisbon, London, Maastricht, Milan, Munich, and Paris.
Today, more than 250,000 businesses use Mollie to drive revenue, reduce costs, and manage funds.
