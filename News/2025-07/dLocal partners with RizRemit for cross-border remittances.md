> [!info] 2025-07-25 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 dLocal and RizRemit team up to accelerate cross-border remittances across Africa and Asia. This collaboration empowers faster, more reliable delivery through popular eWallets and local bank rails. Through the integration with dLocal, RizRemit is gaining the tools to make delivery simpler and more in tune with how people move money.
