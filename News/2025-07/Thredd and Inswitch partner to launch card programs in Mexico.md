# Thredd and Inswitch partner to launch card programs in Mexico

> [!info] 2025-07-28 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇲🇽 Thredd, the UK next-generation global payments processor, and Inswitch, the Miami-based regulated BIN sponsor and embedded finance provider operating across LatAm and the U.S., announced a strategic alliance to enable international companies to launch local card programs in Mexico.
