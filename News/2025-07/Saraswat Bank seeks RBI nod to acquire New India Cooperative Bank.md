# Saraswat Bank seeks RBI nod to acquire New India Cooperative Bank

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Saraswat Cooperative Bank, India's largest cooperative bank, sought RBI approval to acquire New India Cooperative Bank Ltd., which has INR 2,399cr ($280M) deposits and INR 1,162cr ($136M) advances, aiming to protect depositors after RBI imposed moratorium in February 2025.
