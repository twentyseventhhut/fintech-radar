Gemini IPO Set to Launch Soon — Save the Date!
 Jakarta, Pintu News – The financial world is abuzz with the latest news from the crypto market . According to a Wall Street expert, the IPO of the Gemini crypto exchange is scheduled to debut at the end of the third quarter of this year.
 Goldman Sachs , J.P. Morgan, and Citi have been appointed as lead underwriters. This information adds heat to the competition among crypto companies planning to go public.
Check out the full information in this article!
IPO Details and the Role of Underwriters
Andrew Parish, a leading expert on Wall Street and the crypto world, announced through a post on X on July 16 that Gemini’s IPO would be the next highlight.
According to the company, it will soon issue an S-1 filing and roadshow to reveal more details about the number of shares and the price range offered. Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan, and Citi, as underwriters, have important roles in the IPO process.
They are responsible for ensuring that all legal and financial aspects meet the standards set by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) before shares are offered to the public.

Comparison with Other IPOs and Market Potential
Gemini is not the only crypto company planning to list on an exchange. Galaxy Digital and eToro have also filed documents for an IPO. In addition, crypto fund manager with $33 billion AUM, Grayscale, has also filed a confidential IPO application on July 14.
 Circle Internet, which just had an IPO, has seen their market capitalization jump to over $42 billion, with a share price increase of over 636% in a month.
This indicates a huge demand from institutional investors, and it is expected that Gemini and Grayscale Investments will experience the same if the SEC approves their S-1 filings.

Pro-Crypto Regulations and Their Impact
The crypto market is currently getting a breath of fresh air thanks to a more supportive regulatory environment. The SEC has shown a more flexible attitude towards the crypto industry, allowing more companies to consider IPOs. This is a positive step for the overall growth of the crypto industry.
With favorable regulations, investors have more confidence to invest their capital in crypto assets. This not only impacts the companies doing IPOs, but also generally increases investor confidence in the crypto market.
Conclusion
With an IPO scheduled for Q3 and backing from several major banks, Gemini’s future looks bright. It also signals positive momentum for the growing crypto industry. Investors and market watchers will certainly be looking forward to further developments with enthusiasm.
