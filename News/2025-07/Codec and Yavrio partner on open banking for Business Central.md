[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇮🇪 Codec and Yavrio announce strategic partnership. The partnership enables seamless integration of Yavrio’s open banking platform into Business Central, allowing users to access real-time banking data, initiate payments, and automate reconciliation, all within their ERP system.
