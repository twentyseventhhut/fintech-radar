## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 IFX Payments may terminate the £3M Argentex acquisition on insolvency grounds. The company is considering withdrawing its takeover offer for the troubled FX broker after the target firm entered administration. The original deal involved escape clauses, and IFX is now consulting regulators about invoking them.
