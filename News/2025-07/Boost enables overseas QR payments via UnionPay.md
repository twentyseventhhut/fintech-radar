# Boost enables overseas QR payments via UnionPay

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇳 Boost users can now make QR payments overseas via UnionPay. The update enables Boost users to scan QR codes, including static ones, at retail stores in China through Weixin Pay, which is also known internationally as WeChat Pay. Read on

[This Week in Fintech] Boost, a Malaysia-based regional fintech player, expanded cross-border payment capabilities through integration with UnionPay International (UPI) and direct acceptance at Weixin Pay QR merchants in China.
