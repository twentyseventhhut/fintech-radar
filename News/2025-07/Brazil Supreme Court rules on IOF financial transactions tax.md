[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazil’s Supreme Federal Courtupheld the validity of the May decree that increases the Financial Transactions Tax (IOF). The decision preserves the federal government’s authority to adjust tax rates on financial operations, despite legal challenges raised against the measure.

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazil’s Supreme Federal Court (STF) suspended the effects of presidential and congressional decrees concerning the Financial Transactions Tax (IOF), halting recent regulatory changes related to its application.
