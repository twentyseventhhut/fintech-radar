# Green-Got raises $9M in 52 minutes to build sustainable banking infrastructure

● a day ago

French sustainable finance startup Green-Got has raised €8 million in 52 minutes via crowdfunding platform Crowdcube, with 5,286 investors participating. This marks a record for the platform and follows previous crowdfunding rounds of €1.9 million in 2023 and €5.2 million in 2024. The fundraising comes after Green-Got obtained its definitive payment institution licence from French regulator ACPR, enabling it to offer payment services directly without third-party partners. Founded in 2020, the company will use the funds to build proprietary banking infrastructure and launch new products including instant transfers, virtual cards and automated savings features. Green-Got reports over €3 billion in transactions and nearly €200 million collected in savings products since 2024. The company aims to achieve profitability within 18 to 24 months.
