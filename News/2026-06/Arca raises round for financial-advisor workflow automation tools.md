# Arca raises round for financial-advisor workflow automation tools

> [!info] 2026-06-27 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: StrictlyVC

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[StrictlyVC] Arca, a one-year-old New York startup that provides financial advisors with tools and automated workflows to manage client portfolios, planning, and administrative tasks while delivering personalized wealth management services, raised a $48.5 million Series A round led by General Catalyst, with Index Ventures and previous investor Venrock also participating. The company has raised a total of $64 million. The Wall Street Journal has more here.
