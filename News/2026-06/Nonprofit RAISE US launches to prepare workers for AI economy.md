## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[MTS] Nonprofit RAISE US is launched to prepare workers for the AI economy. Its co-chairs are Democrat Gina Raimondo, Biden’s Commerce Secretary and former Governor of Rhode Island, and Republican Eric Holcomb, former Governor of Indiana. Founding partners include Anthropic, the OpenAI Foundation, Amazon, Microsoft, Eli Lilly, Coefficient Giving, and the Schwarzman, Ford, Rockefeller, Arnold and Walton foundations. Worth keeping an eye on as an elite organization, like the World Economic Forum, that will help create AGI-era labor policy.
