# MTS: US government backs nuclear power with $17.5B in loans

> [!info] 2026-06-23 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: MTS

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[MTS] The US government is backing nuclear power with up to $17.5 billion in loans available from the Department of Energy to utilities to purchase nuclear reactors, helping to ease pressure on the electric grid as demand for power to supply the AI infrastructure buildout skyrockets. This follows decades of Chinese industrial policy offering cheap loans and other incentives to companies that build nuclear power. After decades of stagnation, new nuclear projects (like Vogtle 3 and 4) are finally coming to fruition.
