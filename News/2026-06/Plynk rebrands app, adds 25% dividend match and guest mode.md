_The award-winning investing app unveils a refreshed visual identity and app upgrades, along with its new Income Hub and dividend match offer, designed to empower investors._

JERSEY CITY, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Plynk®, the award-winning investing app designed to uncomplicate the investing experience and empower users with confidence-boosting tools, announced its app upgrade and rebrand alongside the launch of the dividend match, a first-of its-kind offer. These updates mark a significant milestone in the Plynk teams’ mission to uncomplicate investing and help users grow through intuitive tools.

> "We redesigned the Plynk app for people who are curious, intentional, and ready to grow — not just their money, but their confidence, too."
> 
> Share

The rebrand introduces a refined visual identity that reflects the evolving needs of investors today. More than just an aesthetic update, the new interface prioritizes seamless navigation and searchability. Now, users can expect:

*   **An Enhanced User Experience****:** A streamlined navigation providing instant access to portfolios, discovery tools, and news. Users can now search from any screen, and switch to dark mode for comfort and accessibility. The news feature now brings real-time updates not just on stocks but also on funds, cryptocurrencies, and broader market developments. 
*   **More Visualizations:**New asset allocation graphs help users understand their diversification at a glance, as well as easy-to-understand data visualizations to quickly assess market performance. 
*   **Improved Money Movement:**Higher deposit limits (from $5k to $10k) and deposit/withdraw movements on the home screen. 
*   **Increased Speed and Reliability:**Revamped technology to make the app faster and more intuitive, as well as reduce glitches and bugs. The upgraded technology allows for rapid innovation to continuously deliver new features that align with the evolving needs of users. 
*   **Guest profiles:**A low-barrier entry point to the app, allowing users to explore select features without sharing particular details typically required for investing app access. Designed to help new users build familiarity and confidence before opening a brokerage account. 
*   **Dividend match and enhanced Income Hub:**The dividend match offer gives all users a **25% match on dividends earned**which can be tracked in the Income Hub on the Plynk app. This offer is launched in an effort to help users’ money go further. 

“We redesigned the Plynk app for people who are curious, intentional, and ready to grow — not just their money, but their confidence, too,” said Kristin Kanders, Head of Marketing and Engagement for the Plynk app. “This upgrade is about listening to what our users really need: a platform that’s reliable, easy to use, and smart enough to grow with them. We’ve made the tech faster, the experience smoother, and the brand more like them: modern, grounded, and ambitious. At the end of the day, Plynk is here to help users invest their way — clearly, confidently, and on their own terms.”

A prime example of listening to customers is the launch of the Plynk app’s recent feature: the Income Hub. After hearing the pain points people face when trying to track their dividend and interest income, the Plynk team designed a central hub that calculates and tracks these streams for them. It gives users a clear view of upcoming interest and dividend payment dates, estimated payouts, match bonus income, and a way to browse other dividend earning opportunities in a single, intuitive dashboard.

These enhancements, new visual identity, and dividend match are available today. Download the Plynk app and open an account in minutes to experience the newly improved platform.

**About Plynk**Plynk® is an investing app that aims to help individuals get started, build confidence, and gain knowledge along the way. Created by a team passionate about empowering investors, the Plynk app provides an easy-to-use experience that is free of complexity and confusion. With straightforward language and clear explanations, the app helps tackle the big things that prevent people from investing and the little ones that keep them from enjoying it.
