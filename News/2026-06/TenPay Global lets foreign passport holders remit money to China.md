TenPay Global lets foreigners send remittances to China via WeChat
 
 TenPay’s cross-border remittance platform has over 60 partners globally. 
 TenPay Global is now allowing foreign passport holders to send remittances directly from their home country to China .
Under the “Remit to China for Non-Chinese citizens” feature, foreigners residing in China can receive remittances using their mobile numbers directly linked to WeChat.
Services are available 24/7, and funds are promised to arrive within minutes.
TenPay Global’s cross-border remittance platform has partnered with over 60 major international banks and remittance institutions, including DBS, Panda Remit, LemFi, Remitly, Wise, Gmoneytrans, Hanpass, and GME, supporting remittances to China from over 100 countries and regions worldwide.
TenPay Global also has over 40 overseas wallet partners globally, enabling these wallets to make payments in mainland China through WeixinPay QR codes. Of this, 36 e-wallets from 13 countries and regions have gone live, including LiqidPay and ChangiPay from Singapore, ZaloPay and MOMO from Vietnam, PayPal from the US, and HiPay from Mongolia, amongst others.
