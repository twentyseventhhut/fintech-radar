# Founders of 3S Money invest in Botswana VASP Express Minds to expand stablecoin payments in Southern Africa

No text provided

5 minute read

Deal is expected to enable businesses to settle cross-border transactions using USD-denominated stablecoins as regional demand grows

)

Authors

**The founders of 3S Money, Ivan Zhiznevsky and Eugene Dugaev, have invested in Express Minds, a licensed Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) in Botswana, to support the expansion of its stablecoin payments infrastructure across Southern Africa.**

The investment is made in a personal capacity by the founders and is separate from 3S Money’s regulated EMI activities.

The deal is expected to help businesses in the region manage USD-denominated stablecoin liquidity and settle cross-border transactions more efficiently, as demand grows alternatives to traditional banking rails.

Botswana, which borders major regional economies including South Africa, Namibia, Zambia and Zimbabwe, is positioning itself as a gateway for digital financial services, supported by an established regulatory framework for virtual assets.

Sub-Saharan Africa is one of the fastest-growing crypto markets globally, with transaction volumes exceeding $200 billion in the past year, according to Chainalysis, driven largely by payments, remittances and currency stability use cases.

The investment is expected to support the development of Express Minds’ platform, including capabilities designed to provide predictable, near-instant settlement, improved access to USD-denominated liquidity and reduced reliance on correspondent banking in cross-border payments.

3S Money, separately, focuses on cross-border payments infrastructure for internationally operating businesses and does not provide virtual asset services in Botswana.

“The investment supports the development of more efficient cross-border payment solutions for businesses in the region,” said Zhiznevsky, speaking in a personal capacity as an investor.

“The challenge in cross-border payments is reliability at scale,” said Dugaev. “Stablecoin-based rails can reduce settlement friction, but only when built on robust, compliant infrastructure.”

“This is an extremely exciting opportunity,” said Goabaone Watlala, known as Brose, Founder of Express Minds. “It allows us to accelerate platform development and expand access to regulated digital asset services across the region.”

**About 3S Money**

**About Express Minds**

Express Minds is a Botswana-based and regulated VASP, providing compliant digital asset infrastructure and enabling businesses to transact and manage digital assets across Southern Africa.
