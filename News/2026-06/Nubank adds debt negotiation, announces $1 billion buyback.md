# Nubank adds debt negotiation, announces $1 billion buyback

> [!info] 2026-06-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Nubank now allows negotiating debts from other institutions in the app. The feature allows customers to track outstanding financial obligations from different institutions. Additionally, Nu Holdings announces a US$1.0 billion share repurchase program. The buyback will be executed over the next 12 months while Nu continues to fully fund its growth initiatives and regulatory capital requirements across Brazil, Mexico, Colombia, and the United States.
