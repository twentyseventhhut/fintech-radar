**Madrid, 17 June 2026** – N26 today announced the launch of N26 SIM in Spain, marking the company’s second local telecommunications market after Germany where it will offer its monthly mobile plans. Customers in Spain can now subscribe to a local mobile plan directly through the N26 app and gain instant access to Orange’s 5G network, all without the need for a physical SIM card.Customers with compatible devices can activate their N26 SIM plan directly in the N26 app – without paperwork, waiting times, or a visit to a local telco store. N26’s local mobile plan comes with unlimited data*, national calls and text messages, as well as 20 GB of free roaming within the EU and EEA** for the flat rate of €11.99 per month. Customers who sign up before July 31, 2026 will be able to benefit from a limited introductory offer, paying just €9.99 per month for their first year using N26 SIM. Customers may choose between porting in an existing phone number, or getting a new number provided by N26. For maximum flexibility, customers can cancel their plan at any time, without having to worry about long fixed-term contracts or binding commitments.eSIM technology also enables customers to switch between multiple numbers and active mobile plans on a single device, making it ideal for those who want to separate work and personal connections or stay flexible with traveling abroad.

> **Antón Díez Tubet, General Manager of N26 Spain and Portugal, said:** “Customers value simplicity, flexibility, fair pricing and a seamless experience. Modern banking is fundamentally a mobile experience, meaning seamless connectivity and personal finance now go hand in hand. With N26 SIM, we are leveraging our scale to offer mobile connectivity at a fair price directly within our app. This launch reflects our core focus: delivering exceptional value and making everyday life easier for our customers.”

N26 SIM is available to eligible customers*** in Spain with a personal or business N26 account, in collaboration with its connectivity partner, 1GLOBAL. The launch of N26 SIM builds on the introduction of N26 Travel eSIM in 2025, further expanding N26’s telecoms offering and helping customers stay connected at home and abroad. Please find more information about N26 SIM on the N26 website.*_Includes 150 GB of data at 5G speeds. Once this allowance is used, speeds may be reduced._** _National calls, SMS, and EU/EEA roaming are subject to reasonable use policies under EU regulations._*** _Customers must have an N26 account with a Spanish IBAN and Spanish Terms and Conditions in order to be eligible for N26 SIM_.
