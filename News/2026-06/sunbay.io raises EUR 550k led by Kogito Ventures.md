Warsaw-based sunbay.io has raised €550,000 in funding from Kogito Ventures, with participation from s20 and angel investors including Kahoot CTO Jostein Håvaldsrud.

The startup builds software for finance teams to automate the collection of overdue invoices through email, SMS and AI voice calls. Its platform connects directly to invoice data, manages escalations and routes cases to finance teams only when human input is needed.

Sunbay.io says it already works with more than 20 businesses across five countries and collects payments from customers in 30 markets. The new funding will be used to expand the platform with predictive insights, additional automation and future AI agents aimed at helping teams identify risk earlier and make faster decisions.

The round highlights continued investor interest in finance operations software, particularly tools using AI to automate manual workflows tied directly to cash flow.
