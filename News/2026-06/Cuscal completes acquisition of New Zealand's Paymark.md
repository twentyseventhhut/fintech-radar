# Cuscal completes acquisition of New Zealand's Paymark

> [!info] 2026-06-01 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇿 Cuscal has completed its acquisition of New Zealand payments provider Paymark, which will now operate as Cuscal Paymark. The deal expands Cuscal’s presence across Australia and New Zealand, strengthening its payments infrastructure capabilities while allowing Paymark to continue operating as a standalone business.
