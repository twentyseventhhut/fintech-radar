# MTS: OpenAI, Anthropic and Google delay model launches

> [!info] 2026-06-23 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: MTS

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[MTS] OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google are all delaying launches. Per anonymous leaker Leo, GPT-5.6 and Gemini 3.5 Pro, originally scheduled for June, be delayed at least into July. While you can never know what’s going on inside of the labs, one reason to suspect a delay is that OpenAI and Google’s internal models are not as good as Fable, and they’re scrambling for more time to catch up. Fable itself has been gone for over a week, and its return is nowhere in sight — Polymarket traders estimate that there’s only a 22% chance it will be restored for US customers by July 1. The era of the public getting a new frontier model every month may be over.
