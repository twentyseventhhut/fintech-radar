Mollie launches in Slovakia to power the next wave of European e-commerce growth
The fast growing Dutch financial services provider will equip Slovakia’s successful online brands, with the tools for seamless international expansion.
The fast growing Dutch financial services provider will equip Slovakia’s successful online brands, with the tools for seamless international expansion.
 June 11, 2026 
 Bratislava, June 11, 2026 - Marking another key step in its accelerated European expansion, Mollie, one of Europe’s fastest-growing financial service providers, today announces its official launch in Slovakia. The expansion into the strategic heart of Central Europe brings a modern, effortless payment platform to the market, empowering Slovakian businesses to accelerate their cross-border growth and solidifying Mollie's drive for a dominant presence across the continent. Mollie currently supports over 250,000 merchants across the UK and Europe.
A partner for the next stage of growth
Slovakia is home to a rapidly maturing e-commerce market, with many successful online brands poised for international expansion. However, as these businesses look to enter competitive Western European markets, they often face new challenges: managing multiple currencies, offering diverse local payment methods, and dealing with complex cross-border reconciliation.
Mollie is designed to solve this. The platform provides a single integration to access all essential payment methods, including major credit and debit cards and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay, as well as a full suite of Europe's leading local payment methods. This allows Slovakian businesses to offer a truly localized checkout experience in every market, simplifying operations and boosting conversion rates.
From local success to European leader
By providing a single, powerful platform, Mollie acts as a growth partner for Slovakian businesses ready to scale. The solution removes the financial borders that often hinder international expansion, allowing merchants to focus on product innovation and customer experience rather than payment administration. This enables a smooth transition from being a local leader to becoming a competitive pan-European player.
" Slovakia's position in Central Europe makes it a critical market for us as we accelerate our expansion across the region. This launch is another milestone in our journey to become the leading payment partner for businesses continent-wide, " says Koen Köppen, CEO of Mollie . " We see many successful Slovakian businesses ready to take the next step, and our mission is to provide them with a single, effortless platform that removes the barriers to international success. By simplifying cross-border payments, we empower Slovakian merchants to compete and win anywhere in Europe. "
About Mollie
Mollie is a fast growing financial services platform that helps businesses across Europe and the UK get paid and manage their money effortlessly: online, in person, and across borders. With a single, powerful integration, companies can accept payments, streamline reconciliation and reporting, prevent fraud, and access flexible financing to fuel their growth.
Through Mollie Connect , SaaS and platform partners can embed payments directly into their products and unlock new revenue streams.
Today, over 250,000 businesses, from fast‑growing startups to large enterprises rely on Mollie’s hyper‑local solutions in 30+ UK and European markets , supported by local payment methods, multi‑language support and integrations with local business software solutions.
Founded in 2004 in Amsterdam, Mollie’s mission is to make payments and money management effortless for every business in Europe and beyond.
