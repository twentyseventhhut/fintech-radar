# BTG Pactual receives approval to acquire HSBC Uruguay

> [!info] 2026-06-15 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇾 BTG Pactual has received approval from Uruguay’s central bank to acquire HSBC Uruguay, completing the regulatory authorization process for the deal. The transaction, valued at about $175m, supports BTG’s international expansion strategy and strengthens its presence across Latin America.
