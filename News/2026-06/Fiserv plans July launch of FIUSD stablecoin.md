# Fiserv plans July launch of FIUSD stablecoin

> [!info] 2026-06-05 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Fiserv plans to launch its FIUSD stablecoin in July, according to CEO Mike Lyons. FIUSD aims to keep stablecoin-related deposits within financial institutions while providing an alternative to dominant stablecoins such as Tether’s USDT, Circle’s USDC, and PayPal’s PYUSD.
