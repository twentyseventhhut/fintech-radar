# BLIK enables payments at Tatry Mountain Resorts hotels

> [!info] 2026-06-26 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇵🇱 Polish users will pay with BLIK for accommodation in hotels of the Tatry Mountain Resorts group. From now on, users from Poland who book a stay via the website: tmrhotels.com, will pay for it in euros, using BLIK payments in the same way as they do domestically. The system automatically converts the amount into Polish zloty and displays the final value before the transaction is approved.
