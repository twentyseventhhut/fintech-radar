## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇱 Digital bank bunq fined for late response to fraud complaints. The main reason for the fine is bunq’s failure to respond on time to seven complaints from customers who were victims of online fraud. Customers who filed a complaint only received feedback from bunq several weeks after the deadline had expired.
