# Ecommpay launches express checkout for Google Pay and Apple Pay

> [!info] 2026-06-10 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Ecommpay introduces express checkout for Google and Apple Pay users,enabling merchants to offer faster, wallet-based payments directly within their checkout flow. The new feature reduces friction for guest shoppers by minimising manual data entry and streamlining the path to purchase, helping merchants improve conversion rates and checkout efficiency.
