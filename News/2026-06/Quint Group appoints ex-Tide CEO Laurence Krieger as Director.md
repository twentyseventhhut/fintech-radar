# Quint Group appoints ex-Tide CEO Laurence Krieger as Director

> [!info] 2026-06-25 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Quint Group has appointed FinTech leader Laurence Krieger as a Director, former CEO of Tide and COO of Revolut.
