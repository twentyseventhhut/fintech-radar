# H2site raises round for ammonia-to-hydrogen membrane reactors

> [!info] 2026-06-27 · 1 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: StrictlyVC

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[StrictlyVC] H2site, a seven-year-old startup based in Bilbao, Spain, that builds membrane reactor and separation systems that produce and purify hydrogen from ammonia and other feedstocks for industrial and energy applications, raised a $6.8 million Series B extension from EIC Fund and an unnamed private investor. Renewables Now has more here.
