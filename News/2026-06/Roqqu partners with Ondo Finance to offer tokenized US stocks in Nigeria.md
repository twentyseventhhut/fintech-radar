# Roqqu partners with Ondo Finance to offer tokenized US stocks in Nigeria

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇬 Roqqu has partnered with Ondo Finance to offer tokenized US stocks and ETFs to retail investors in Nigeria.
