# The Capital Market Authority Licenses Brookfield Arabia for Business Services to Conduct Managing Investments and Operating Funds Activity in the Securities Business and its Completion of the Commencements of Business Requirements

Publishing Date:11-June-2026

Page Content

​In accordance to the Capital Market Law issued by Royal Decree No. (M/30) dated 02/06/1424H and its Implementing Regulations, the Capital Market Authority announces that Brookfield Arabia for Business Services has completed the commencements of business requirements to conduct Managing Investments and Operating Funds Activity in the Securities Business licensed as per CMA resolution dated 14/04/1447H corresponding to 06/10/2025G.
