# Yuh CEO says Revolut's Swiss tactics border on unfair competition

> [!info] 2026-06-08 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇨🇭 Revolut's Swiss tactics "Border on unfair competition," Yuh CEO Jan De Schepper says. He stated that competitors such as Revolut benefit from an uneven regulatory environment in Switzerland, arguing they avoid the licensing costs faced by locally regulated banks. The comments came as Yuh outlined plans to grow its customer base through AI-driven automation.
