# Qvik payment requests go live on Revolut in Hungary

> [!info] 2026-06-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇭🇺 Qvik and payment requests are available on Revolut, enabling instant account-to-account transfers, payment requests, and QR-code payments directly within the Revolut app. The addition strengthens Revolut’s local payments offering following the launch of its Hungarian branch and provides users with access to Hungary’s domestic instant payment infrastructure.
