AIVA Secures $1.5 Million in Funding
AIVA, a New York-based AI company building an intelligence layer for hotels, has secured $1.5 million in funding.  
Investors  
Backed by Comma Capital and 645 Ventures.  
AIVA Use of Funds  
AIVA will use the capital to expand its engineering team, accelerate product development, and deploy its technology across its initial hotel customer base.  
About AIVA  
Founded in 2026 by Lucía Marí Sanguino and Paula Rodríguez Jou, AIVA is building an intelligence layer that helps hotels understand guest behavior and property operations in real time. Using contextual AI and autonomous agents, AIVA enables hotels to make smarter decisions, improve guest experiences, and increase ancillary revenue through personalized recommendations. Based in New York and incorporated as a Delaware C-Corp, AIVA's long-term vision is to become the operating system for hotels: the layer that helps every property decide what should happen next across service, operations, and revenue.
