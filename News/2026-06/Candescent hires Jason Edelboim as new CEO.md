> [!info] 2026-06-29 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇺🇸 Candescent hires Jason Edelboim as new CEO. Edelboim joins the company from iCIMS, a recruitment software specialist which he has led as CEO since January 2024. On LinkedIn, he said he will work to further integrate AI into the Candescent platform, used by around 1,300 banks and credit unions.
