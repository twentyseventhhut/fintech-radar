# Revolut said to plan $750M share sale

Business

Christian Baha / DJ

7 June

1 min read

C O U R T E S Y O F R E V O L U T

Revolut Group Holdings Ltd. is planning a secondary share sale worth at least $750 million, the Information reported on Sunday, citing a person familiar with the matter. The source added that the company recorded enough interest to sell $2 billion worth of shares, but that the final size depends on "how many employees and early shareholders are willing to sell their stock at the proposed $109 billion valuation."

The sale is reportedly set for June 15 and will not be finalized until July or August. Some companies have allegedly "already indicated that they are likely to write checks north of $100 million," according to the source.
