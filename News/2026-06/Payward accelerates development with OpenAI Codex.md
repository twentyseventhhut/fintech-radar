Payward Accelerates Development with OpenAI Codex
Payward's Chief Data Officer, Kamo Asatryan, discusses how OpenAI Codex has accelerated development, enabling the company to prioritize speed and innovation in fintech.
In a move that highlights the growing impact of AI on financial services, Payward, a company operating the Kraken cryptocurrency exchange, is accelerating its development processes through the use of OpenAI Codex. Kamo Asatryan, Chief Data Officer at Payward and Kraken, explains how the integration of this AI-powered coding assistant has transformed their workflow.

Asatryan states that without Codex, Payward would likely be six months behind its current development schedule. He emphasizes that speed is the number one value for his team, enabling them to delegate tasks and have 50 agents running concurrently to review merge requests. This efficiency allows for faster releases.

The Importance of Speed in Fintech
The financial technology sector is characterized by rapid innovation and intense competition. Companies that can deliver new features and services quickly gain a significant advantage. Asatryan articulates Payward's commitment to this principle: "The number one value for my team is speed." This focus on velocity is not just about releasing features faster, but also about the ability to iterate and respond to market demands effectively. The ability to delegate tasks and have multiple agents working in parallel, as facilitated by Codex, is crucial for achieving this speed.
Leveraging AI for Enhanced User Experience
Payward's broader vision extends to integrating inference and personalized user experiences across all its products. Asatryan highlights the company's ambition: "We want to be at the cutting edge of finance and there's no way to do that without being at the cutting edge of technology." This statement underscores the strategic importance of adopting advanced technological solutions, such as those offered by OpenAI, to maintain a competitive edge. By partnering with OpenAI, Payward aims to ensure it remains at the forefront of both financial innovation and technological advancement, ultimately enhancing the experience for its users.
