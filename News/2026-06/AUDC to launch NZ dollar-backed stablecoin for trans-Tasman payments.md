## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇦🇺 AUDC to launch NZ dollar-backed stablecoin for faster trans-Tasman payments. Pegged 1:1 to the fiat currency, the digital asset is designed to facilitate real-time, 24/7 cross-border settlements, reducing processing times from several days to near-instant while addressing inefficiencies in the $30 billion Trans-Tasman trade corridor.
