# a16z: Space warfare and the new orbital arms race

> [!info] 2026-06-22 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: a16z

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[a16z] Russia is jamming GPS satellite receivers across most of Eastern Europe. Iran has launched hundreds of ballistic missiles through outer space. China has openly contemplated destroying Starlink satellites. Plainly: our adversaries are waging wars in space.

[a16z] Christian spent seven years as a satellite manufacturer and operator, working on both commercial and military missions and closely tracking America’s adversaries in space. Alex Oliver was a military officer for 16 years. Both of us are now at Andreessen Horowitz as partners on the American Dynamism team

[a16z] Russia just mobilized a fleet of satellites to surround a commercial Earth-imaging satellite: ICEYE-X36, a Finnish satellite that sells imagery to Ukraine.
