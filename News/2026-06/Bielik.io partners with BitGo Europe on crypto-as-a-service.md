# Bielik.io partners with BitGo Europe on crypto-as-a-service

> [!info] 2026-06-22 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌎 Bielik.io has partnered with BitGo Europe to integrate its Crypto-as-a-Service infrastructure, supporting regulated crypto trading and custody across the EEA.
