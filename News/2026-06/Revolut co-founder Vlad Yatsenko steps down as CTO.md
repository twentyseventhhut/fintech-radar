# Revolut co-founder Vlad Yatsenko steps down as CTO

> [!info] 2026-06-05 · 2 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] Vlad Yatsenko, co-founder, first-ever employee, and the person who's been under the hood since before Revolut even had a name, is handing over the CTO keys in July.

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇬🇧 Revolut Co-Founder and CTO Vlad Yatsenko to step down from the role and transition to a non-executive director role on the company’s board. He will be succeeded by Donato Lucia, who will take on the newly titled position of Vice President of Technology.
