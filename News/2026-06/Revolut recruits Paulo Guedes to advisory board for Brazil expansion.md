# Revolut recruits Paulo Guedes to advisory board for Brazil expansion

> [!info] 2026-06-15 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Revolut recruits Paulo Guedes to support its expansion in Brazil. The former Brazilian Economy Minister has joined Revolut's newly created advisory board, which will also include Ana Novaes and Luiz Henrique Lobo. Revolut said Guedes will contribute macroeconomic expertise and international relationships as it expands in one of its fastest-growing markets.
