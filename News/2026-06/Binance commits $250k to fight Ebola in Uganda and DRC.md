Binance Donates $250K to Support Ebola Response in DRC and Uganda
 Humanitarian Funding Commitment : Binance is committing $250,000 to support Ebola response efforts in the Democratic Republic of Congo and Uganda, aiming to enhance frontline medical operations, infection control, and community awareness, thereby strengthening the capacity to combat this public health emergency.
 Funding Allocation : The donation will be evenly split between the Uganda Red Cross Society and Médecins Sans Frontières, directly supporting emergency medical services in affected and high-risk communities, ensuring healthcare workers receive necessary protective equipment, and improving overall response efficiency.
 Current Outbreak Status : As of now, the Democratic Republic of Congo has recorded 635 confirmed Ebola cases and 127 deaths, with the outbreak expanding to 26 health zones, particularly in Ituri province where over 94% of cases are concentrated, highlighting the severity and urgency of the situation.
 Increased International Support : Alongside Binance's donation, the U.S. State Department announced an additional $20 million for Ebola preparedness in the region, demonstrating the international community's strong commitment to combating the outbreak and the necessity for collaborative efforts.
