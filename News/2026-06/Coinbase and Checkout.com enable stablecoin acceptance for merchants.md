# Coinbase and Checkout.com enable stablecoin acceptance for merchants

> [!info] 2026-06-05 · 2 упоминаний · 1 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] Checkout.comlaunched stablecoin acceptance via Coinbase Payments, letting enterprise merchants take stablecoins alongside cards and local methods.

[This Week in Fintech] Coinbasepartnered with Checkout.com to enable stablecoin acceptance across the latter's network of 1,000+ enterprise merchants, letting consumers pay in USDC or USDT while merchants continue to settle in USD through existing rails.
