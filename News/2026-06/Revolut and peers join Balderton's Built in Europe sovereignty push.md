[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍Revolut, Wayve and Elevenlabs join European tech sovereignty push. More than 100 European founders and CEOs have joined Balderton Capital’s new “Built in Europe” initiative, aimed at strengthening the continent’s technology sovereignty and highlighting the scale of its startup ecosystem. The campaign includes FinTech leaders such as Revolut, Cleo, Scalable Capital, Lendable, and GoCardless.
