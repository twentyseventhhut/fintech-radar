# Chainsmokers' Mantis VC raises fourth fund targeting $100 million

> [!info] 2026-06-20 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: StrictlyVC

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[StrictlyVC] The Chainsmokers are apparently better at venture capital than most people expected from a DJ duo. Their firm, Mantis VC, is raising a fourth fund targeting $100 million, which would push the firm past $300 million in assets and officially graduate Alex Pall and Drew Taggart from "emerging managers" to – as The Information puts it – "just regular old VCs" on the strength of bets like Rogo and Factory AI, both now valued north of $1.5 billion.
