# Brazil's central bank requires audit reports for crypto authorization

> [!info] 2026-06-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🇧🇷 Brazil's Central Bank now requires crypto companies to submit an independent audit report before receiving operating authorization. The rule, effective June 1, aims to strengthen anti-money laundering controls and align Brazil with international crypto oversight standards.
