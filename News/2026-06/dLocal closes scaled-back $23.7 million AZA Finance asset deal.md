# dLocal closes scaled-back $23.7 million AZA Finance asset deal

> [!info] 2026-06-03 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: This Week in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[This Week in Fintech] 🌎 dLocal, the Uruguayan payments processor, closed a $23.7M asset deal with Kenya-based AZA Finance — a sharp retreat from what was initially announced as a ~$150M full acquisition. Legal complications derailed the original deal, leaving dLocal with AZA's Cameroonian payments license, IP, and African client relationships.
