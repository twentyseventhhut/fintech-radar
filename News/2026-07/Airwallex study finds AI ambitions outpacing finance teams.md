[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 📰 Airwallex research finds AI ambitions outpacing finance teams’ readiness. A Forrester Consulting study commissioned by Airwallex found that 74% of finance leaders rank AI as a top priority, while 90% plan to increase AI investment over the next year.
