# ASML to speed up shipments of EUV lithography machines

> [!info] 2026-07-16 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: MTS

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[MTS] ASML will work to ship their EUV machines faster. The Dutch company’s extreme ultraviolet lithography machines are critical for all advanced semiconductor manufacturing, and cost hundreds of millions of dollars each. Its cycle time, the time between beginning production and shipping to a customer, currently takes about 22 weeks, but the company is working on reducing it to 15-16. Thanks to the AI boom, ASML’s 2027 capacity is already close to booked, and they’re working on increasing capacity 30% year-over-year for the next two years. Intel is already using ASML’s most advanced machine, the EXE:5200B High NA EUV, to manufacture some chips. They also may raise prices.
