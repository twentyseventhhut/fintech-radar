## [0] What exactly happened (de-PR'd)
- **The de-PR'd fact:** The Information (2026-07-15) reports DeepSeek's **annualized revenue run-rate nears ~$500M** (reported as a **$400M–$500M range**), attributed **"largely to sales of cloud-based API access to its models"** — i.e. overwhelmingly the paid API, NOT consumer subscriptions (the DeepSeek app/web chat is **free**). Downstream (PYMNTS, Crypto Briefing, Reuters/Bloomberg funding follow-ups) all trace to that single report. **DeepSeek did not disclose or confirm this.** It is a leak/estimate released *into* the ≥$1.5B raise + STAR-IPO news cycle.
- **"Run-rate," not revenue.** ~$500M = a **monthly pace of ~$33M–$42M ×12**, explicitly NOT audited annual revenue (Crypto Briefing/Winbuzzer flag this directly). The honest figure is the **$400–500M band, low end more credible**. It is roughly **double** the ~$200–220M implied by DeepSeek's own Feb-2025 disclosure.
- **The one real, defensible new margin number:** The Information also reports **~70–80% gross margin on V4 API access** — distinct from (and far more credible than) the infamous **"545% theoretical cost-profit ratio"** DeepSeek self-published in Feb-2025 and *itself disclaimed* (actual revenue "substantially lower" due to free tiers, off-peak discounts). Don't confuse the two; the 70–80% is gross (excludes R&D, training compute, free-tier serving).
- **→ Why structured / released this way and what it reveals:** A revenue number surfacing on the exact day of the $71–74B raise report is **not coincidence — it is valuation support.** At ~$74B on a ~$0.5B run-rate the mark is **~148× revenue**, a multiple only defensible on national-champion/strategic grounds, so the leak exists to give a fundamentals-shaped anchor to an otherwise narrative-driven mark. Second order: the more important disclosure the number *implies* is the **monetization paradox** (see [3]) — DeepSeek is a top-4 global chatbot / #2 on Vercel's gateway yet posts ~1/50th of Anthropic's revenue. The revenue leak, read skeptically, actually **highlights how little of DeepSeek's enormous usage converts to cash.**

Read: strong top-line acceleration used to justify a fundraise/IPO, but the number is soft (run-rate, not trailing) and the underlying pricing model is deflationary by design.

**Key figures (as disclosed / reported).**
- Annualized revenue run-rate: **~$400–500M** (The Information; corroborated by PYMNTS, Cryptobriefing), vs **~$200–220M** in 2025 → **~+100% YoY** on a run-rate basis (analysis: doubling of run-rate, not audited FY revenue).
- Revenue mix: predominantly **API/cloud access** to its models; enterprise contracts were ~22% of the 2025 revenue share (Cryptobriefing/Business of Apps). No official 2026 API-vs-subscription split disclosed → **[UNSOURCED]** at the current run-rate.
- Gross margin: frontier-inference providers cited at **70–80%**; DeepSeek's own Feb-2025 GitHub disclosure claimed a *theoretical* **545%** cost-profit margin on V3/R1 over a 24h window (~$562k theoretical daily revenue vs ~$87k daily H800 inference cost, ≈$200M annualized theoretical) — but DeepSeek itself flagged actual revenue is "substantially lower" (only a subset of services monetized; off-peak discounts).
