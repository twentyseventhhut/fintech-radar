# Mastercard expands airport Fast Track to Madrid and Barcelona

> [!info] 2026-07-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇪🇺 Mastercard has expanded its airport Fast Track network in Europe, adding Madrid and Barcelona to existing locations in Athens and Istanbul. The expansion gives eligible cardholders access to faster security lanes as part of The Mastercard Collection, which now includes more than 200 Fast Track lanes worldwide.
