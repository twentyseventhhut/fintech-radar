# WB Bank names Svyatoslav Emelyanov acting board chairman

> [!info] 2026-07-07 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Финтехно

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Финтехно] WB Банк назначил Святослава Емельянова временно исполняющим обязанности председателя правления — после того, как Георгий Горшков снова стал зампредом ВТБ. Со стороны кадровая конструкция выглядит так: Горшков — архитектор партнёрства со стороны ВТБ, Емельянов — операционный и риск-ориентированный руководитель WB Банка внутри RWB. Емельянов — внутренний кандидат. Он в WB Банке с 2025 года, был зампредом и участвовал в его трансформации. А ранее работал на руководящих позициях в «Хоум Кредит», «Связном» и «Почта Банке», где входил в правление как куратор рисков, а с 2020 года был зампредом. Всё это массовая розница — как раз то, что строит WB Банк, трансформируя данные маркетплейса, кошельки, расчёты селлеров и клиентский трафик в кредитные, депозитные и платёжные продукты. Показательно, что «Эксперт РА» одновременно с ростом банка прямо фиксирует будущую зону риска: качество активов может быть пересмотрено по мере вызревания корпоративных ссуд и масштабирования необеспеченного розничн

### [0] What happened (one-liner)
On **2026-07-07**, WB Bank (Wildberries Bank, the credit institution of the merged Wildberries&Russ / RWB group) named **Svyatoslav Emelyanov** acting chairman of the management board, after prior chairman **Georgy Gorshkov** left to become deputy president–chairman of the management board at **VTB**, where he will curate the VTB–WB Bank strategic partnership.

### [1] Timeline (dated)
- **2025-07-02** — Expert RA first assigns WB Bank rating **ruBBB / stable** (collateral coverage 26%, 3 sectors = 99% of portfolio, corporate loans 17% of balance sheet, capital H1.0=260.9%). [raexpert.ru](https://raexpert.ru/releases/2025/jul02f)
- **2025-08-12** — Ex-VTB deputy Georgy Gorshkov appointed chairman of WB Bank's board. [fontanka.ru](https://www.fontanka.ru/2025/08/12/75822651/)
- **2026-05-26/28** — VTB and Wildberries&Russ announce strategic partnership: VTB to buy **5%** of WB Bank (option to increase), gain WB Bank access to VTB branches/ATMs/mortgages; Kostin targets closing "over the summer"; material economics not expected before 2027 (Pyanov). [fontanka.ru](https://www.fontanka.ru/2026/05/26/76442350/), [frankmedia.ru](https://frankmedia.ru/282054)
- **2026-06-05** — VTB + Wildberries surface at SPIEF under a "WTB" co-brand; VTB forms a Wildberries strategic-partnership committee. [investfuture.ru](https://investfuture.ru/articles/vtb-i-wildberries-usilivayut-strategicheskoe-partnerstvo-i-vykhodyat-na-pmef-s-brendom-wtb-1181044082)
- **2026-06-22** — WB Bank launches retail savings accounts (12% p.a.) and consumer online loans up to ₽500k, 24–60 mo (see [[WB Bank launches savings accounts and consumer loans]]).
- **2026-06-29** — Expert RA **upgrades** WB Bank to **ruBBB+ / stable**, citing strengthened corporate procedures and risk management. [finance.mail.ru](https://finance.mail.ru/article/kreditnyij-rejting-wb-banka-povyishen-do-urovnya-rubbb-69215469/)
- **2026-06-30** — VTB sells former Otkritie office (Vivaldi Plaza, ~24.4k m²) to Wildberries&Russ (see [[VTB to sell former Otkritie office to Wildberries-Russ]]).
- **2026-07-07** — Gorshkov moves to VTB; **Emelyanov named acting chairman** of WB Bank board. [kommersant.ru](https://www.kommersant.ru/doc/8798165), [adindex.ru](https://adindex.ru/news/hr/2026/07/7/346971.phtml), [interfax.com](https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/118324/)

### [2] Who is Svyatoslav Emelyanov / key actors
- **Emelyanov** — internal candidate; at WB Bank since **2025** as deputy chairman, involved in the bank's transformation. Prior senior roles at **Home Credit**, **Svyaznoy Bank**, and **Post Bank** — where he joined the board in **2017** and was **deputy chairman from 2020** as risk curator. Career is anchored in **mass retail lending**, the exact model WB Bank is building. [interfax.com](https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/118324/), [new-retail.ru](https://new-retail.ru/novosti/retail/svyatoslav_emelyanov_vozglavil_wb_bank/)
- **Georgy Gorshkov** — architect of the VTB side of the partnership; ex-VTB, chaired WB Bank since Aug 2025, now returns to VTB as (first) deputy president–chairman responsible for retail business + the VTB–WB Bank partnership. [forbes.ru](https://www.forbes.ru/finansy/564027-vtb-soobsil-o-kadrovyh-izmeneniah-v-sostave-pravlenia)
- **RWB / Wildberries&Russ** — parent group; **Tatyana Kim (Bakalchuk)** founder. WB Bank monetizes marketplace data, WB wallets, seller settlements and client traffic into credit/deposit/payment products. [ru.wikipedia.org](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/Вайлдберриз_Банк)

### [3] Numbers / hard facts
- Leadership split: **Gorshkov = VTB-side partnership architect; Emelyanov = operational/risk-focused WB Bank chief inside RWB.**
- VTB stake being acquired: **5%** (option to raise). Deal targeted to close summer 2026; economics ≥2027.
- Rating trajectory: **ruBBB (Jul-2025) → ruBBB+ (Jun-2026)**, stable.
- 2025 asset-quality snapshot (Expert RA): collateral coverage **26%**, top-3 sectors **99%** of loan book, corporate loans **17%** of balance sheet, overdue **1%**, capital H1.0 **260.9%**.
- Consumer products (Jun-2026): savings **12% p.a.**; consumer loans up to **₽500k**, **24–60 mo**.

### [4] Why it matters / stakes
- Signals the **operationalization** of the VTB–Wildberries partnership: the partnership architect (Gorshkov) moves to VTB's side of the table, while a **risk-and-retail operator** (Emelyanov) runs the bank day-to-day — a clean division consistent with a bank scaling unsecured mass-retail credit.
- Expert RA flagged that despite the June upgrade, **asset quality is a forward risk zone** as corporate loans season and unsecured retail scales; Emelyanov's risk pedigree (Post Bank/Home Credit) reads as a deliberate hedge against exactly that.
- "Acting" status leaves open whether Emelyanov is confirmed permanently or is a placeholder pending CBR approval / partnership close.

## Market Research

<!-- enrichment:market_research -->
**Sector & drivers.** Russia's "marketplace-bank" (BigTech-neobank) subvertical — banks embedded in e-commerce platforms (Wildberries, Ozon, Yandex) that convert marketplace data, seller settlements and buyer traffic into credit/deposit/payment products. Structure: highly concentrated at the platform layer but the fintech arms are new challengers to state incumbents (Sber, VTB) — the combined share of Wildberries/Ozon/Yandex fintechs in the electronic-payments market reached ~40% by mid-Oct 2024, ~20x higher than three years earlier (per TAdviser reporting, as of Oct 2024) `(analysis on trend)`. Drivers/"why now": (1) captive distribution — WB Bank monetises the marketplace's user base rather than paying for CAC; (2) the VTB partnership (announced May 2026) gives WB Bank access to VTB branches, ATMs, mortgages and investment products in exchange for a 5% stake — a de-facto rails-sharing deal signed as WB scales beyond seller settlements into unsecured retail lending (per Interfax / Moscow Times, May–Jun 2026).

**Competitive landscape.** Sector KPIs: assets, active customers, deposit base, ROE, and (increasingly) unsecured retail loan quality / cost-of-risk. Key players and basis of competition (captive traffic + data, not price):
- **WB Bank** (protagonist): assets ~₽125bn (~$1.8bn), ranked ~45th by assets in Russia; balance sheet grew >3x May-2024→May-2025 (per cbonds / Expert RA, 2025). Historically a settlements/commission business — ROE reportedly exceeded 200% off risk-free commission income with very high capital ratios (H1.0=261% as of May 2025). Now transforming into full retail lending (savings accounts + consumer loans launched Jun 2026 — see [[WB Bank launches savings accounts and consumer loans]]).
- **[[Ozon Bank registers digital financial assets platform with CBR|Ozon Bank]]**: universal license, >31.7m active customers, ~35th by assets (Q1 2025); fintech deposits ₽348.6bn (Jun 2025); fintech segment pre-tax profit ₽13bn in Q1 2026 and 37.5% of group EBITDA (per bne IntelliNews / AKM, 2026). Materially ahead of WB Bank on scale and lending maturity.
- **Yandex Bank**: third BigTech fintech in the ~40% payments cohort.
Protagonist's position: **niche/catching up** — WB Bank is smaller and later into lending than Ozon Bank; its moat is the Wildberries traffic/data funnel and, now, VTB's balance sheet and infrastructure (network effects + distribution), not standalone banking scale `(analysis)`.

**Comps & multiples.** No valuation/round is attached to this leadership-change news, and WB Bank is a private subsidiary of RWB (65% Wildberries LLC / 35% Stinn LLC) — no listed equity, so market-cap multiples = **no data**. VTB's deal to buy 5% implies a valuation reference but the headline consideration/post-money was not disclosed in sourced reporting → **[UNSOURCED]**. Scale comparison instead:
- WB Bank assets ~₽125bn vs Ozon Bank (~35th) larger; WB deposits: no reliable figure → no data. Ozon fintech deposits ₽348.6bn (Jun 2025).
- A ₽34.6bn "Q1 2026 net profit" figure surfaced in one search summary but is inconsistent with a ₽125bn asset base and unverified against a primary source → **[UNSOURCED], excluded**.
Internal comps: [[WB Bank launches savings accounts and consumer loans]] · [[Ozon Bank registers digital financial assets platform with CBR]] · [[VTB takes large Ozon share stake as collateral]] · [[Ozon Bank to double ATM network to 2,000 by end-2026]]. Distribution not computed (no ≥3 comparable public multiples) — qualitative comparison only.

**Risk flags.**
1. **Asset-quality deterioration as loans season.** Expert RA (which upgraded WB Bank to ruBBB+ post-VTB) explicitly flags future risk: quality may be revised as corporate loans mature and unsecured retail scales. Why: the bank's historic ~200% ROE rests on *risk-free commission* income; pivoting to unsecured retail imports credit risk it hasn't cycled through a downturn — cost-of-risk could compress the return profile sharply.
2. **Dependence on VTB's rails.** Growth beyond settlements now leans on VTB branches/ATMs/mortgages/investments (5%-stake partnership). Why: a strategic minority owner that is also a state incumbent controls key infrastructure — WB Bank captures marketplace economics but disintermediation risk sits with whoever owns the banking rails.
3. **Governance/key-person transition.** Acting chairman (Emelyanov, internal) installed only because Gorshkov — the VTB-side architect — returned to VTB as deputy chairman. Why: "acting" leadership during a scale-up into a riskier lending book raises execution risk; the arrangement signals VTB steering strategy while an operational/risk manager runs the bank day-to-day.

**What this changes (idea-lens).** `(analysis)` This is a consolidation/co-optation signal, not a re-rating: Russia's largest state bank is embedding into the fastest-growing BigTech-fintech via a 5% stake plus a risk-focused operating chief — the marketplace-bank thesis is shifting from "challenger disrupts incumbents" toward "incumbent captures the challenger's distribution." Falsifiable thesis: if WB Bank's next Expert RA action or CBR reporting shows rising NPLs/cost-of-risk in unsecured retail within 2–4 quarters, the high-ROE story breaks; watch the Emelyanov-era loan-book mix and any disclosed WB Bank standalone results (currently opaque) as the trigger.
