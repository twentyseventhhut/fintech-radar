# Wero goes live on Airwallex for European bank payments

> [!info] 2026-07-17 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Connecting the Dots in Fintech

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🌍 Wero is now available on Airwallex, enabling merchants worldwide to accept instant bank payments from European shoppers without requiring a European entity. The integration offers lower processing costs, same-day settlement, and support for features including one-click and recurring payments.
