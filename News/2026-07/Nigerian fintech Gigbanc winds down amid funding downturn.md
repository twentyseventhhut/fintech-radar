# Nigerian fintech Gigbanc winds down amid funding downturn

> [!info] 2026-07-13 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇳🇬 Nigerian FinTech Gigbanc is winding down operations, becoming the latest casualty of the funding downturn affecting early-stage startups across Africa. The company, which set out to build cross-border financial infrastructure for African freelancers, creators, entrepreneurs and businesses, confirmed the decision in a statement signed by its co-founders.
