# Linas Newsletter: Who actually makes money from AI

> [!info] 2026-07-13 · 3 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: Linas Newsletter

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Linas Newsletter] Who Actually Makes Money When AI Eats the World? 💸

[Linas Newsletter] The four biggest hyperscalers plan to spend more than $700 billion on AI infrastructure this year, over double the entire global telecom industry’s capex. NVIDIA just posted a record $81.6 billion quarter, up 85% year over year. Anthropic crossed a $47 billion revenue run-rate and raised a $65 billion Series H at a $965 billion valuation. OpenAI’s annualized revenue topped $25 billion.

[Linas Newsletter] And yet roughly 95% of ChatGPT’s 900+ million weekly users pay nothing. The cost of frontier-grade intelligence fell 128x in a single year. The top 15 models now sit within about 3 percentage points of one another on major benchmarks. Frontier-class capability is now even free to download: Z.ai's open-weight, MIT-licensed GLM-5.2 goes head-to-head with the top closed models and runs on a single Mac. In other words, the most capital-intensive buildout in the history of technology is racing toward a product whose price is collapsing and whose differentiation is shrinking.
