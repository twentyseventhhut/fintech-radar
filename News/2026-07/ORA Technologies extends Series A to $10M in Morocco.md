# ORA Technologies extends Series A to $10M in Morocco

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇲🇦 ORA Technologies extends Series A to $10m, betting on Morocco’s consumer FinTech market.
