## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Linas Newsletter] How to Build a Second Brain with Claude Fable 5 🧠

[Linas Newsletter] That knowledge base is a second brain 🧠, and in 2026, the term means something specific. It’s not a note-taking method. It’s a folder of plain markdown files on your own disk that the model reads, writes, links, and maintains largely on its own. You drop in sources; the agent compiles them into an interconnected wiki, cites where every claim came from, and gets measurably smarter with every file it gains. Anthropic’s Andrej Karpathy formalized the pattern in his April 2026 “LLM Wiki” gist, which inverted the usual approach: the second brain should not be written by you; it should be written by the AI model.

[Linas Newsletter] Claude Fable 5 is the first model that demonstrably rewards this setup harder than anything before it.

[Linas Newsletter] Fable 5 launched on June 9, 2026 as Anthropic’s first public Mythos-class model, positioned above Claude Opus 4.8 and built for long-horizon autonomous work. It is included in paid Claude plans for now: Pro, Max, Team, and premium Enterprise seats can spend up to 50% of their weekly usage limits on Fable 5 at no extra cost through July 19, 2026 at 11:59 PM PT, a window Anthropic has already extended twice. After that, Fable 5 moves to usage credits at the top of Anthropic’s price list.
