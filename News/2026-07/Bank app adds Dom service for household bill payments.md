РСХБ запустил сервис «Дом» в мобильном приложении банка, который упрощает управление жилищно-коммунальными платежами по всем объектам недвижимости клиента. В разделе «Мой Дом» пользователи могут объединить счета по одному адресу, находить не только дом и квартиру, но и другие виды недвижимости, включая гаражи, кладовки, и оплачивать все услуги в одном окне – воду, электричество, капитальный ремонт и другие коммунальные сервисы.
 
Пользователи могут добавлять дополнительные лицевые счета для своевременной оплаты услуг, при этом счета за коммунальные услуги подтягиваются автоматически – клиенту остается только проверить данные и оплатить их одним нажатием. В настройках раздела доступно управление домами: добавление новых объектов, изменение названия и оформления, управление подписками и удаление дома при необходимости. Кроме того, клиенты могут оплачивать счета, сканируя QR-коды на квитанциях, и следить за всеми начислениями по своему адресу в одном месте.
 
«Мы последовательно развиваем цифровые сервисы, которые помогают клиентам комфортно решать повседневные задачи. Раздел «Мой Дом» позволяет видеть все счета по конкретному адресу в одном месте и оплачивать их за несколько кликов, без необходимости искать квитанции и реквизиты. Следующим этапом мы добавим возможность учитывать расходы в разрезе каждого объекта недвижимости, что даст клиентам полный контроль над бюджетом на содержание недвижимости. Более 80% клиентов РСХБ уже используют  цифровые сервисы банка. Объем розничного бизнеса в цифровых каналах по итогам 2025 года вырос на более чем 40% по сравнению с 2024 годом», –  отметила Елена Анатольевна Батурова, заместитель Председателя Правления РСХБ.
 
Подключение сервиса «Дом» осуществляется в мобильном приложении банка. Для этого клиенту необходимо открыть раздел «Платежи», нажать на виджет «Создать Дом» в левой верхней части экрана, выбрать опцию «Создать Дом», подтвердить предложенный адрес либо изменить его и задать объекту название. Если у клиента уже есть сохраненные счета, он может отметить те, которые относятся к выбранному дому.

# Context-enrichment: Bank app adds «Дом» service for household (ЖКХ) bill payments
_Analytical notes (not a post). Importance: 1/5._

**⚠️ ATTRIBUTION CORRECTION (load-bearing):** Despite the ingest channel label "News & Trends by Sber", the bank here is **NOT Sberbank**. The primary source is **rshb.ru** and the Russian text reads "РСХБ запустил сервис «Дом»". The bank is **Rosselkhozbank / Россельхозбанк / RSHB / Russian Agricultural Bank** — a state-owned, agri/rural-focused mid-tier retail bank. The "Sber" channel is merely the digest that carried the item, not the actor. Ironically, **Sberbank already runs an identically-named «Дом» section** with the same functionality (see [1]), so RSHB is following, not leading.

## [0] What exactly happened (de-PR'd)
On **2026-07-09** RSHB announced it "launched" («запустил») a «Дом» / «Мой Дом» section inside its mobile banking app: a single-window place to consolidate and pay ЖКХ (housing-and-communal) bills across all of a client's real-estate objects at one address (apartment, house, garage, storage). It auto-pulls accruals, supports QR-scan of paper payment slips, lets users add extra line-item personal accounts (water, electricity, capital-repair), and manage/rename/delete objects. A roadmap item — per-property expense tracking — is **future, not shipped**.
- **+ Why framed this way / what it reveals:** The PR is worded as a live launch, but the only numbers cited (">80% of RSHB clients use digital services"; "retail digital-channel volume +40% in 2025 vs 2024", per Deputy Chair Elena Baturova) are **bank-wide stats that predate and are unattributable to this feature**. There is **zero feature-level adoption data** (no users, no transactions). This is the classic "announcement dressed as traction" pattern — de-PR'd, it is a table-stakes retail-app feature shipped without proof of uptake.

## [1] Competitors / peers
Centralized, multi-address, in-app ЖКХ payment is **standard-issue** in the Russian market and has been for years:
- **Sberbank** — a «Дом» section in SberBank Online (same concept, same name): address-based ЖКХ since **2022**, dedicated «Дом» section since **~21.10.2024**; QR/barcode + autopay.
- **T-Bank/Tinkoff** — «Оплата ЖКУ (ГИС ЖКХ)» by single personal account, autopay per service, **fee-free** (long-standing).
- **VTB** — 200k+ ЖКХ services catalog with ЖКУ autopay.
- **State «Госуслуги Дом»** (Minstroy/ГИС ЖКХ) — one-window ЖКХ, meters, bills, multi-property; **>20M users by Jul 2026** ("every 7th resident"), free.
- **+ Second-order:** RSHB is at parity-catch-up, not ahead. It even reused the "Дом" name Sber has used since 2024. The competitive question is not "does RSHB have this feature" but "why would a client use RSHB's version over Sber's larger app or the free state app that already aggregates the same ГИС ЖКХ data" — RSHB's only plausible edge is its rural/agri base (dachas, multiple rural objects), which the PR does not lean on.

## [2] Company history / fit
RSHB is migrating onto a new ДБО/IT platform (updated mobile/internet banking; new ДБО terms effective 2025-08-13 and 2026-01-26) and running a broader retail-digitization push (a senior-citizen app version in Apr 2026, digital-ruble onboarding). «Дом» fits that logically.
- **+ Why RSHB acts this way:** As a mid-tier, agri-anchored retail player far behind Sber and T-Bank in super-app depth, RSHB is closing obvious functional gaps to reduce churn/attrition of its retail base — defensive parity-building, not differentiation. ЖКХ payment is a high-frequency, sticky utility hook, so it is a rational retention feature even if commercially thin.

## [3] Novelty / value-add / traction
**Novelty: essentially none.** The mechanism (auto-pull ЖКХ accruals + QR + multi-address) is years old at Sber/T-Bank/VTB and available free via «Госуслуги Дом». **Traction: unproven** — no feature-specific numbers; roadmap items unshipped.
- **+ Who captures the margin / what breaks it:** ЖКХ payments carry thin economics (~0.2–2% commissions; SBP ЖКХ fee capped at 0.2%; льготники/pensioners exempt from ЖКХ commission since 2024-07-01). The bills' data rails run through **ГИС ЖКХ**, the same source feeding the free state app — so the bank is a substitutable front-end over commoditized data. Value-add is engagement/retention, not revenue; the multiple such a feature "supports" is negligible.

## [4] What's next / market sentiment
RSHB says the next step is per-object expense tracking for "full budget control." Market backdrop: state «Госуслуги Дом» scaling fast (20M+ users), fee restrictions tightening on ЖКХ, digital-ruble ЖКХ tariff set at 0.2% from 2027.
- **+ Structural / counterintuitive:** The free, state-backed «Госуслуги Дом» app is the real competitive gravity well for multi-address ЖКХ. Bank ЖКХ-payment features increasingly become **loss-leader engagement hooks** rather than fee earners. RSHB shipping this in mid-2026 signals it is defending its retail flank, not opening a new revenue line. Materiality to RSHB's P&L or the market: **negligible**.

## Market Research

<!-- enrichment:market_research -->
**Correction upfront (de-PR).** The protagonist is **RSHB / Rosselkhozbank (Russian Agricultural Bank)**, NOT Sberbank. The primary source is rshb.ru; "News & Trends by Sber" is only the digest channel that re-reported it. The feature is RSHB's «Мой Дом» / «Дом» section in the RSHB mobile app — a centralised ЖКХ (utility) bill aggregation-and-payment hub across all of a client's real-estate objects (flats, garages, storage units), with auto-pulled accruals, QR-scan of paper slips, and multi-account management. This is an incremental ecosystem/UX feature, not new capability: the bill data flows from the utility providers / ГИС ЖКХ and RSHB could already process ЖКХ payments; the add is packaging + a single-window «Дом» view. Announced ~2026-07-09.

**Sector & drivers.** The Russian ЖКХ (utility) payments market is very large and near-saturated on collections: households paid an estimated **~RUB 4.2tn** for ЖКХ in 2025, with a payment/collection discipline of **~98%** (per finance.mail.ru / gosuslugi search, as of 2026). Household ЖКХ arrears run **~RUB 415bn+** (Minstroy, via senatinform, 2025). Structure: payment RAILS are commoditised (any bank app, Госуслуги, QIWI-era terminals, marketplaces all move the same regulated bills), so value migrates to the FRONT-END aggregation/UX layer, not the transaction itself — margins on ЖКХ acceptance are thin-to-zero (RSHB advertises "from RUB 0"). Why now: (i) Kirill Tsarev (Sber First Deputy Chairman) publicly framed the next retail battle as an **"interface war" for the client** (2026-07-04, www1.ru) — convenience, not deposit rate, as the differentiator; (ii) a **new law (Kommersant, 2026): from Sept 2027 ЖКХ receipts auto-deliver into Госуслуги personal accounts**, with ГИС ЖКХ integrated into a state bill-issue-and-pay service — i.e. the state is building the same «Дом»-style hub for free.

**Competitive landscape.** Sector KPIs: MAU / DAU of the app, ЖКХ-payment attach rate, share of bills auto-pulled, cost of acceptance (basis points, here ~0). Key players and basis of competition (product/UX + distribution, NOT price):
- **Sber** — already runs an identical «Дом» ЖКХ section inside SberBank Online (sberbank.ru/ru/person/payments/zhkh/home) PLUS the Domklik house-community layer; SberBank Online MAU **84.7m** ([[Sberbank H1 2026 net profit rises 20.4% to RUB 995bn]]). Sber is clearly AHEAD; RSHB is a fast-follower copying an existing Sber feature.
- **State: Госуслуги / Госуслуги.Дом (Minstroy)** — the structural competitor; free, authoritative bill source, mandated auto-delivery from 2027.
- **T-Bank / VTB / Alfa, Yandex/marketplace banks** — all field super-app ЖКХ payment flows; marketplaces (Ozon/WB/Yandex banks) held ~17% of active cards by end-2024 (Moscow Times, 2025).
RSHB's own position/moat: NARROW. Retail is only **~16% of RSHB assets**; the bank is ~5th by assets (**~RUB 5.9tn**, +7.2% in 2025) with ~7–10m clients and a top-1 franchise in AGRICULTURE (33% APK share), not retail super-apps `(analysis)`. Stated traction claim (management, unverified): >80% of RSHB clients use digital services; retail digital-channel volume +40% YoY in 2025. Position: **catching up / defensive parity**, not a moat.

**Comps & multiples.** Not a valuation/round/deal event — no multiples apply; RSHB is 100% state-owned and unlisted (P/S, EV/Rev = **no data / N/A**). Internal comps = same "thin front-end over government/existing data, packaging as value-add" pattern already logged for Sber:
- [[SberBank Online adds FSSP travel-ban check service]] — Sber wraps free FSSP/Gosuslugi data + push alerts (same playbook).
- [[SberBank Online adds remote escrow for secondary property deals]] — Sber real-estate ecosystem UX add.
- [[Sberbank H1 2026 net profit rises 20.4% to RUB 995bn]] — Sber super-app scale anchor (84.7m MAU) the peer set is chasing.
Distribution not computed (no comparable financials for a feature launch); qualitative comparison only.

**Risk flags.**
1. **State disintermediation (structural).** From Sept 2027, ГИС ЖКХ/Госуслуги auto-deliver and let users pay ЖКХ bills for free (Kommersant 2026) — the exact job RSHB's «Дом» does. Second-order: the state can commoditise the aggregation layer banks are now racing to own, capping any RSHB (or Sber) engagement moat.
2. **Fast-follower with no differentiation.** RSHB is copying Sber's existing «Дом»; on a ~RUB 0-margin, commoditised rail with a much smaller, non-retail-centric base (~16% of assets), the feature is unlikely to shift share vs Sber's 84.7m-MAU app. Execution/relevance risk, not upside.
3. **Data-dependency on ГИС ЖКХ / providers.** Auto-pulled accruals rely on external registries RSHB doesn't control; coverage gaps by region/provider limit the "one-window" promise, and the same rails are open to every rival.

**What this changes (idea-lens).** `(analysis)` Nothing structural — this is a defensive feature-parity move in the Russian bank "interface war," not a re-rating or new-entry catalyst. Falsifiable thesis: RSHB's «Дом» will NOT move retail-engagement share; watch (a) whether RSHB discloses any «Дом» MAU/attach uptake (it won't, likely), and (b) the 2027 Госуслуги auto-billing rollout — if the state hub ships on time, bank «Дом» clones (Sber's and RSHB's alike) lose their reason to exist. Trigger to revisit: any RSHB retail-share disclosure or a Госуслуги.Дом integration deal.
