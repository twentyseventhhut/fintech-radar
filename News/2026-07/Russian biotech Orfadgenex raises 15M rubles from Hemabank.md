It is **PJSC MMCB (ММЦБ, MOEX: GEMA)**, a biotech operating the "Gemabank" personal cord-blood / stem-cell storage brand, part of the **Artgen Biotech** group (MOEX: ABIO, ex-Human Stem Cells Institute / HSCI). ## [0] What exactly happened (de-PR'd)
Gemabank (MMCB) committed a **total of RUB 15m** to **Orfadgenex** (Орфадженекс / orphagenex.ru), an early-stage Russian startup developing **AAV-vector gene-therapy drugs for rare neuromuscular (orphan) diseases, primarily spinal muscular atrophy (SMA)**. The round is **not fully funded**: per pharmprom.news, **RUB 10.5m is already deployed**, with the residual ~RUB 4.5m due **by end-2026**. Use of proceeds: **preclinical development, production scale-up and preparation for clinical trials** — i.e. Orfadgenex is **pre-clinical**, no candidate in trials, no data, no approval. Total size ≈ **$190k** at ~RUB 79/USD — angel/seed scale, not a venture "round" in any Western sense.

**+ Why this way / what it reveals:** This is **staged, milestone-gated in-group capital** inside the Artgen Biotech ecosystem, not independent VC — which is why "15m" is disclosed as a to-be-completed total (10.5m in, 4.5m contingent) rather than a closed round. Artgen buys cheap pipeline optionality on the SMA/neuromuscular space from the cash-generative cord-blood arm.

## [1] Competitors / peers
**(a) Therapeutic frame — SMA gene therapy — dominated by incumbents Orfadgenex cannot touch:** Novartis **Zolgensma** (AAV9, onasemnogene abeparvovec) was **registered in Russia Dec 2021** at ~RUB 160m (~$2m)/dose; plus **Spinraza** (Biogen) and **Evrysdi** (Roche). State access runs via the **"Circle of Kindness" (Круг добра)** foundation. Domestically, **BIOCAD** already has Minzdrav clearance for an SMA gene-replacement (SMN1) candidate in clinical trials — i.e. a Russian rival **ahead of Orfadgenex in the clinic** `(analysis)`. So Orfadgenex is a late pre-clinical entrant, an **import-substitution play**, not a science lead. **(b) Small-round funding frame:** corpus comps [[Russian AI startup Mymeet.ai raises 16.5m roubles]] (~$210k) and [[Russian automation platform Cue raises 20 million rubles]] (~$255k); nearest medtech is [[Russian prosthetics maker Motorica raises 1bn rouble soft loan]] (60x larger, debt).

**+ Second-order:** Post-2022 sanctions create the import-substitution pull AND remove the means (globally-controlled AAV vectors, plasmids, know-how, capital). A RUB 15m pre-clinical program is orders of magnitude below the hundreds of millions Novartis spent — the gap between the ambition and $190k of staged funding is the central tension.

## [2] Company history / fit
Gemabank/MMCB IPO'd on MOEX **July 2019**; Russian leader in personal cord-blood storage (>48,000 samples), 2025 rev ~RUB 377m, net profit ~RUB 158m. Parent Artgen (ABIO) posted 2024 consolidated rev ~RUB 1.52bn and positions as a gene-therapy + regenerative-medicine ecosystem (Neovasculgen was among Russia's first gene-therapy drugs). Just weeks earlier (mid-June 2026) Gemabank ran an oversubscribed ~RUB 400m SPO — [[Gemabank places shares unsold under pre-emptive rights]].

**+ Why the company acts this way:** Sequencing is telling — raise ~RUB 400m equity (June), deploy a tiny RUB 15m into gene-therapy pipeline (July). Storage is a mature cash annuity; to justify a biotech (not utility) multiple, MMCB/Artgen must show it recycles storage cash into higher-growth gene-therapy optionality. Orfadgenex is a low-cost narrative asset supporting that re-rating; its signaling value to ABIO equity holders may exceed its scientific value at this stage `(analysis)`.

## [3] Novelty / value-add / traction
Novelty is **thesis-level, not product-level**. **No traction** that matters: no clinical data, no candidate in trials, no regulatory milestone — the money funds *preparation*. Concrete facts are limited to the staged capital (10.5m/4.5m) and the SMA target; everything else is forward-looking PR.

**+ Deeper:** In gene therapy, value is captured by whoever controls the **vector platform + manufacturing + regulatory approval** — none owned by a $190k pre-clinical startup. Even a future Orfadgenex SMA candidate must clear Russian registration in a class where Zolgensma is already registered/reimbursed via Круг добра and where BIOCAD is ahead — so the addressable win is **domestic import-substitution economics**, contingent on state procurement, not on beating Zolgensma clinically.

## [4] What's next / market sentiment
Near term: Gemabank deploys residual ~RUB 4.5m by end-2026; Orfadgenex attempts to move from "preparation" into actual preclinical studies. Watch: (a) any disclosed **equity stake / valuation** (undisclosed — red flag), (b) whether Artgen consolidates Orfadgenex, (c) any **Круг добра / Minzdrav** signal on domestic SMA gene therapy — the only thing that makes the economics work.

**+ Counterintuitive:** Sanctions simultaneously create the demand (import-substitution) and remove the means (global vectors/know-how/capital), so tiny in-group tickets like this are more a **signal of intent than of capability**. For a Fintech Radar audience, the durable takeaway is the *pattern* — a listed Russian biotech holding recycling SPO proceeds into staged in-ecosystem seed bets — not the drug.
