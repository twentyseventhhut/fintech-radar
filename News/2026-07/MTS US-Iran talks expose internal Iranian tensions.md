# MTS: US-Iran talks expose internal Iranian tensions

> [!info] 2026-07-11 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом
> Каналы: MTS

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[MTS] Negotiations between the US and Iran are also bringing to the surface internal tensions in Iran. The most significant external sign of these tensions was last week’s strikes on ships transiting the Strait. If you belong to Iranian factions that don’t want to see a deal with the US under any circumstances, flagrantly breaching the ceasefire is obviously one way to accomplish that. It’s also possible, of course, that there was less strategy involved. In the clip from our conversation at the start of July that starts this piece, Daniel Saedi, one of the founders of Minerva and, more importantly, our ongoing Iran correspondent (from New York), says that the IRGC had clearly prepared for a war with the US by dramatically decentralizing command. Maybe this was just a sign that re-centralizing during the ceasefire has its own challenges.
