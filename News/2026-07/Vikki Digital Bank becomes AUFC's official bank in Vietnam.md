# Vikki Digital Bank becomes AUFC's official bank in Vietnam

> [!info] 2026-07-14 · 1 упоминаний · 0 источника(ов) с текстом

## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[Connecting the Dots in Fintech] 🇻🇳 Vikki Digital Bank becomes the official bank in Vietnam in the first domestic market partnership for AUFC. The partnership will promote the bank's digital financial services through fan engagement initiatives and integrate its banking platform with VietJet's ecosystem to combine travel, payments and loyalty benefits.
