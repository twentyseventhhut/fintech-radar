## Агрегированный текст (из дайджестов)

[MTS] China establishes an international AI organization. The World AI Cooperation Organization (WAICO) has 29 founding members, including Russia, Indonesia, Pakistan, Brazil, South Africa, and Vietnam. WAICO seeks to promote international cooperation on AI governance and standards, much like the hypothetical US-led consortium proposed by people including Sam Altman and Demis Hassabis³.

[MTS] If the US-China AI race is the second Cold War, perhaps this is the Warsaw Pact to the US’ NATO.
