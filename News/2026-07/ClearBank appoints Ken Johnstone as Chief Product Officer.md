## Контекст

<!-- enrichment:context -->
**[0] What happened.** ClearBank (UK real-time clearing / embedded-banking infrastructure provider) appointed **Ken Johnstone as Chief Product Officer**, joining the Group Executive team. He will own the product proposition across payments, accounts and embedded banking, serving fintech, financial-services and corporate clients across the UK and Europe. Announced 2026-07-10 via Connecting the Dots in Fintech / [Finextra](https://www.finextra.com/pressarticle/110386/clearbank-appoints-ken-johnstone-as-chief-product-officer).

**[1] Who is Johnstone.** ~20+ years across fintech, banking and telecoms. Most recently **CPO for NatWest Boxed and Mettle** — NatWest's cloud-native BaaS / embedded-finance venture and its SME neobank — i.e. a direct competitor-adjacent embedded-finance operator. Earlier: FreeAgent, Quikkly, MessageMedia; telecoms roots (INQ Mobile co-founder, 3/Hutchison Whampoa, Motorola). Source: [FF News profile](https://ffnews.com/people/ken-johnstone/), [LinkedIn](https://uk.linkedin.com/in/kenjohnstone).

**[2] Why it matters / pattern.** This is the latest in a sustained ClearBank C-suite build-out over 2025-H1 2026, each hire framed around "expansion" and "embedded banking": Group General Counsel Angela Roberts (Dec 2025, [[ClearBank appoints Angela Roberts as Group General Counsel]]), CTO Neil Drennan ex-Visa (Feb 2026, [[ClearBank hires Visa's Neil Drennan as CTO]]), EU CEO Tristan Kirchner ex-Uber (Feb 2026, [[ClearBank names former Uber executive Tristan Kirchner EU CEO]]). Adding a dedicated CPO signals productisation/scaling of the platform under Group CEO Mark Fairless as the company pushes embedded banking into corporates and Europe.

**[3] Business backdrop (real figures, FY2025).** ClearBank posted its third consecutive year of UK profitability: Group normalised revenue +34% to £121.6m, UK pretax profit +53% to £12.2m; deposits £17.8bn (+65%); 262m payments processed (+57%); 279 Group clients; UK Investment Grade (BBB-) from S&P. But statutory Group pretax **loss of £16.7m** reflects European investment. See [[ClearBank posts third year of UK profitability, revenue up 34%]] and PASS C below. Johnstone's product mandate directly targets the high-quality recurring fee income (now ~3/4 of revenue) that this thesis rests on.

**[4] Product surface being scaled.** Recent 2025-2026 product/commercial moves the CPO inherits: embedded banking with Coinbase/LemFi savings, Revolut, Capital on Tap (£1bn deposits), Tide (~800k SMEs), first corporate partner PayCaptain; ClearBank Europe stablecoin/digital-asset rails (Circle Mint, EURC/USDC, [[ClearBank Europe launches Digital Asset Rails for settlement]], [[ClearBank Europe to roll out Circle's Mint stablecoin platform]]); new agency/transaction-banking accounts & payments services (Sept 2025).

Sources: [Finextra](https://www.finextra.com/pressarticle/110386/clearbank-appoints-ken-johnstone-as-chief-product-officer) · [ClearBank leadership](https://clear.bank/leadership) · [FF News](https://ffnews.com/people/ken-johnstone/) · [Finance Magnates](https://www.financemagnates.com/executives/clearbank-announces-new-cpo-amid-expansion-plans/)
